Bill & Boz &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Bill & Boz''''' is an Australian sports television show currently broadcast on Fox Sports News four nights a week from Sunday to Wednesday. It is hosted by journalist Bill Woods and former Socceroo goalkeeper Mark Bosnich, along with a rotating panel of guests. &&&&& &&&&& The show features news and discussion of the day's sporting events with the panelists and home viewer feedback. It covers all major Australian sports as well as international sports. &&&&& &&&&& Regular panelists &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The show debuted on 22 January 2017 at 9.30pm as a 1 hour show.
