Compsodrillia urceola &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Compsodrillia urceola''''' is an extinct species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Pseudomelatomidae, the turrids and allies. &&&&& &&&&& Description &&&&& The length of the shell attains 13.3 mm; its diameter 4.2 mm. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Distribution &&&&& Fossils have been found in Pliocene strata of the Bowden Formation of Jamaica; also on Saint Thomas; age range: 3.6 to 2.588 Ma &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& * W.
