Bon Aqua Springs Historic District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Bon Aqua Springs Historic District''' near Bon Aqua, Tennessee is a historic district which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1990. The listing included six contributing buildings, seven contributing structures, and a contributing site. &&&&& &&&&& It was developed as a mineral springs resort, the Bon Aqua Springs Spa, located southeast of the unincorporated community of Bon Aqua. The district includes remnants of the resort, including houses, spring houses, a cottage, a corn crib, bridges, a dam, a swimming pool, and a reservoir. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
