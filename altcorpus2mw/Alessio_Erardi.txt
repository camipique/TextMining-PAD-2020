Alessio Erardi &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Alessio Erardi''' (1669–1727) was a Maltese painter. He was the son of the artist Stefano Erardi and his wife Caterina Buttigieg. He initially studied art with his father, and eventually spent five and a half years in Rome between 1695 and 1701.
