47	Austwickia
Austwickia &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Austwickia''''' is a genus of bacteria from the family of Dermatophilaceae with one known species (''Austwickia chelonae''). ''Austwickia'' is named after the botanist Peter K.C.
