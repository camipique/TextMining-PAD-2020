AEC 761T &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''AEC 761T''' was a two-axle double deck trolleybus chassis manufactured by AEC. Based on the AEC Q-type bus chassis, only seven were built; a demonstrator that was later purchased by Bradford, one in Southend-on-Sea and five exported to Sydney, Australia. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
