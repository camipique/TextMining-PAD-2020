Chapel and Lovelace Hall, Marion Military Institute &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Chapel and Lovelace Hall, Marion Military Institute''' were listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1978. &&&&& &&&&& They are the two surviving buildings from the 1854-58 Howard College campus. Howard College moved in 1887 to Birmingham, Alabama and the Colonel James Thomas Murfee stayed behind to open Marion Military Institute. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
