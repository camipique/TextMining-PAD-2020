Crown Cement &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Crown Cement Group''' is a manufacturer and supplier of cement, ready mix concrete and other building materials in Bangladesh. M. I. Cement Factory Ltd. is the parent company of Crown Cement Group. The Company was founded in 1994 in Dhaka. The company won the national export trophy thrice. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& &&&&& Crown Cement was founded on December 31, 1994. Initially, the company had the capacity of producing only 600 tons Portland cement per day which they increased day by day. Now the company produces more than 3.3 million metric tons per annum. Crown Cement started exporting cement in 2003. The company has its factory located at West Mukterpur, Munshiganj on the bank of Dhaleswari river. &&&&& &&&&& The company won the national export trophy (Gold) twice Crown also ended up being the runner-up of 2016 Best Brand Award- hosted by Bangladesh Brand Forum. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
