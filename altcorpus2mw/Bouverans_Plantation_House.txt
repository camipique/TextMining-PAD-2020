Bouverans Plantation House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Bouverans Plantation House''', also known as '''Arialo''', is a historic house on a former plantation in Lockport, Louisiana. It was built in 1860 for M. J. Claudet. It was one of the most productive sugarcane plantations in the parish in 1871-1872. &&&&& &&&&& The house was designed in the Creole and Greek Revival architectural styles.
