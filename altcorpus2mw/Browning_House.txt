Browning House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Browning House''' is a historic house in Milan, Tennessee. It was built in 1873. It was the childhood home of Gordon Browning, who served as the Governor of Tennessee from 1937 to 1939. In 1941, the house became one of the buildings of the Milan Army Ammunition Plant. It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since June 28, 1974. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
