Chiou Jiunn-rong &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Chiou Jiunn-rong''' () is a Taiwanese politician. He was the Deupty Minister of National Development Council in 2017-2018. &&&&& &&&&& Education &&&&& Chiou obtained his bachelor's, master's and doctoral degree in economics from National Taiwan University in 1989, 1991 and 1995 respectively. &&&&& &&&&& Education careers &&&&& At the Department of Industrial Economics of Tamkang University, Chiou was the associate professor, department chair and professor in August 1995 - July 2000, August 1998 - July 2000 and August 2000 - July 2003 respectively. &&&&& &&&&& Political careers &&&&& Chiou started his political career when he joined the Taipei City Government in March 2015 as an adviser. He then subsequently became the committee member of Mainland Affairs Council in 2016. &&&&& &&&&& ===National Development Council=== &&&&& Chiou was appointed as the Deputy Minister of National Development Council on 7 September 2017 by Premier-appointed William Lai and took office the day later. On 28 January 2018, Choiu was appointed as one of the ten members of New Southbound Policy task force.
