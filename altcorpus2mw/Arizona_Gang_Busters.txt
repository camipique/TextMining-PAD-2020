Arizona Gang Busters &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Arizona Gang Busters''''' is a 1940 American Western film directed by Sam Newfield and written by William Lively. The film stars Tim McCoy, Pauline Haddon, Lou Fulton, Forrest Taylor, Julian Rivero and Arno Frey. The film was released on September 16, 1940, by Producers Releasing Corporation. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& A ring of saboteurs headed by Carl Schmidt is building an arsenal along the US-Mexican border.
