Bill Crutchfield &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Bill Crutchfield''' (born May 7, 1926) is a former American football player and coach. He served as the head football coach at Atlantic Christian College from 1949 to 1950 and Presbyterian College from 1954 to 1956.
