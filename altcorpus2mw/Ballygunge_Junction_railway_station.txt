It is under the jurisdiction of Eastern Railway zone. Ballygunge Junction railway station is one of the busiest railway stations of Sealdah railway division. More than 150 pairs of E.M.U. Local Trains ply through the railway station on a daily basis. It is situated in Kolkata in the Indian State of West Bengal.
