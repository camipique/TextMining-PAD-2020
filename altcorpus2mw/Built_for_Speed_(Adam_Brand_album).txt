Built for Speed (Adam Brand album) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Built for Speed''''' is the third studio album by Australian recording artist Adam Brand. The album was released in January 2002 and peaked at number 24 on the ARIA charts.
