BUT 9611T &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''BUT 9611T''' was a two-axle double deck trolleybus chassis manufactured by British United Traction between 1947 and 1951. It was based on the AEC Regent III bus chassis, with a total of 138 manufactured for 11 operators in England at Leyland's Ham and AEC's Southall factories. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
