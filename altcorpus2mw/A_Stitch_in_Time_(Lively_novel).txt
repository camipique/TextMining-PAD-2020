A Stitch in Time (Lively novel) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''A Stitch in Time''''' is a 1976 children's novel by Penelope Lively. It was the winner of the 1976 Whitbread Award for children's book. 40 years later, it was re-published by Collins under the modern classics range. &&&&& &&&&& The book follows Maria Foster during a summer holiday in Lyme Regis.
