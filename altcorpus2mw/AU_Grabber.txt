AU Grabber &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''AU Grabber''' (pronounced as: '''''Gold Grabber''''') is an self-propelled barge excavator dredge used to mine Bering Sea placer gold deposits in the region around Nome, Alaska, USA. It is owned and operated by Richard Schimschat and featured in the Discovery Channel USA mining reality TV show ''Bering Sea Gold''. It cost $600,000 to buy.
