Adria Santana &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Adria Santana''' (29 August 1948 – 30 September 2011) was a Cuban actress best known for her work in the theater. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Trained at the National School of Art, Adria Santana soon joined Havana's Teatro Estudio company, where she remained for twenty years. She worked with the directors Héctor Quintero, Armando Suárez del Villar, Berta Martínez, , and Jorge Alí Triana. Her best-known roles were ''Medea'', Bernarda in ''The House of Bernarda Alba'', Lalita in ''Contigo, pan y cebolla'', Camila in ''Santa Camila de La Habana Vieja'', the Cuban play ''Vagos rumores'' (in which Santana considered that she "developed as an actress"), and especially, her work with Abelardo Estorino.
