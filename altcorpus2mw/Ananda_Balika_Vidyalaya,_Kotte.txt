Ananda Balika Vidyalaya, Kotte &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Ananda Balika Vidyalaya''' is a public girls school located in Kotte., Sri Lanka. It was established in 1971 as the girls school of Ananda Sastralaya, Kotte. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& In the 1960s, Stanley Thilakaratne, a former MP from Kotte (who was selected as the speaker of the parliament later) pointed out the need for a girls' college in Kotte and reserved an unused land plot in the 13 acre land in the Pagoda Ananda Sastralaya Junior School No. 2.
