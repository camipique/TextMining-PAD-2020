Artern Subcamp &&&&& Introduction &&&&& The '''Artern Subcamp''' in Artern was a subcamp of the Nazi concentration camp Buchenwald. &&&&& &&&&& From 20 November 1944 until 6 April 1945 the camp was used for approx. 250 male inmates (as of December 1944). This subcamp of Mittelbau-Dora was known as "Adorf" or "Rebstock neu" by the camp SS. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
