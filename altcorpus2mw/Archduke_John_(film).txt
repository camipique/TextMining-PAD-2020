Archduke John (film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Archduke John''''' (German: '''''Erzherzog Johann''''') is a 1929 Austrian silent historical drama film directed by Max Neufeld and starring Igo Sym, Xenia Desni and Paul Biensfeldt. It portrays the life of Archduke John of Austria, a nineteenth century member of the Habsburg Dynasty. &&&&& &&&&& It was shot at the Schönbrunn Studios in Vienna and on location in Styria.
