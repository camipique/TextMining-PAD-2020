Central Service Station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Central Service Station''' at 534 Whitman St. in Rosalia, Washington was built in 1923. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2007. &&&&& &&&&& It is a brick commercial building, with brick laid in common bond, on a poured concrete foundation. It was deemed notable as an "intact example of an early Texaco gas station in eastern Washington. The building embodies the distinctive characteristics of its type (House with a Canopy), and period of construction." &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
