Bausa (rapper) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Julian Otto''' better known as '''Bausa''' (born in Saarbrücken, Germany in 1989) is a German rapper and singer known for his many collaborations and hits. Bausa also plays the guitar and piano, and composes his own songs in a Bietigheim recording studio. &&&&& &&&&& In 2014, Bausa released his EP ''Seelenmanöver'' on Capo's label Hitmonks. His 2017 album ''Dreifarbenhaus'' reached number 10 on the German Albums Chart also charting in Austria and Switzerland. His single "Was du Liebe nennst" peaked at number one on German Singles Chart for eight consecutive weeks. It was certified 3× Platinum in Germany.
