Astana Zhas Ulan Republican School &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Zhas Ulan Republican School named after Sagadat Nurmagambetov''' ( / ) also known as the '''Astana Zhas Ulan Republican School''' is a military boarding school in Kazakhstan's Armed Forces which specializes in the training of Kazakh youth who want to join the military. It came into existence in 1999, and is named after Army General Sagadat Nurmagambetov, the first Defence Minister of Kazakhstan and one of the most notable Kazakh soldiers who took part in the Battle of Berlin. The school was founded as a secondary educational institution with in-depth study of the Kazakh language and foreign languages, with enhanced military and physical discipline. Many of the graduates of the school have come to higher military educational institutions of Kazakhstan, Russia, Belarus, Germany, Turkey and other countries. &&&&& &&&&& Description &&&&& On March 27, 1999, the Government of Kazakhstan passed a resolution on the creation of a republican school in Astana. The school was considered to be a way to educate future members of the Kazakh Armed Forces. The school was officially opened on November 4, 1999 in the presence of president Nursultan Nazarbayev and Belarusian president Alexander Lukashenko. In December 2011, the school expanded into a new building. Since 2010, 80 pupils of the school have taken part in every Constitution Day and every Defender of the Fatherland Day parade in Kazakhstan as part of the Corps of Drums. &&&&& &&&&& Leadership Structure &&&&&  &&&&& The following people make up the leadership structure of the school: &&&&& &&&&& * Assistant director of the school - Lieutenant Colonel Gabit Zhusupaliev &&&&& * Head of the educational studies - Lieutenant Colonel Amantai Zharbosynov &&&&& &&&&& Requirements &&&&& In order be a cadet at the school, young men must be at least 15 years old at the year of admission. Cadets must also go through a 10-day competition in which they meet the physical requirements of the school.
