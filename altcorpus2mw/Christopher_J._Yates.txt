Christopher J. Yates &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Christopher J. Yates''' is a British-American fiction writer and the author of two novels, ''Black Chalk'' and ''Grist Mill Road''. ''Black Chalk'' was first published in the UK by Harvill Secker in September 2013 and in the US by Picador in August 2015. In December 2015, NPR named ''Black Chalk'' one of the best books of the year. ''Grist Mill Road'' received a Kirkus Star and praise from ''The New York Times'' for a narrative "full of pleasantly unpleasant surprises." He was born in Kent, England on February 4, 1972, and graduated from Oxford University with a law degree in 1993.
