He reached an agreement with the Finnish Veikkausliiga's TP-47 previous to the 2005 season. He was recruited by Östersunds FK in summer 2005, the defender-cum-midfielder was first fielded in a 1-1 deadlock with Kiruna FF, starting away to Friska Viljor. He set to strengthen the Maltese Premier League's Valletta for the second leg of 2005/06, he was slapped with a red card and suspension on his debut at home to Pietà Hotspurs.
