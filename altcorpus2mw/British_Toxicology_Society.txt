British Toxicology Society &&&&& Introduction &&&&& The '''British Toxicology Society''' is the main British professional association for toxicologists. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& It was formed in 1979. &&&&& &&&&& Structure &&&&& It is headquartered in Staffordshire. &&&&& &&&&& Function &&&&& In 1993 it worked with the Royal Society of Biology to produce the UK Register of Toxicologists; this is added to the Register of Toxiclogists of EUROTOX (European Society of Toxicology). &&&&& &&&&& It holds the annual British Toxicology Society Congress in April, held at Newcastle in 2018.
