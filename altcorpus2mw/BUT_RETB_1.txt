BUT RETB/1 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''BUT RETB/1''' was a two-axle single deck trolleybus chassis manufactured by British United Traction between 1950 and 1964. Glasgow purchased one in 1950, followed by two batches of 10 in 1952 and 1958. Wellington purchased 109, the last 38 being assembled by Scammell's Watford plant in 1964. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
