Antoine Dignef &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Antoine Dignef''' (3 October 1910 in Velm – 9 April 1991 in Sint-Truiden) was a Belgian cyclist. He is best known for finishing 3rd overall and winning two stages of the Vuelta a España, making him the first ever winner of a stage in the Vuelta.
