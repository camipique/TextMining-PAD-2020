Béla Uitz &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Béla Uitz in 1922 &&&&& '''Béla Uitz''' (8 March 1887, Mehala – 26 January 1972, Budapest) was a Hungarian painter and communist activist. &&&&& &&&&& In 1907 he studied at the Hungarian National School of Applied Arts before moving on to the Hungarian Academy of Fine Arts in 1908. &&&&& &&&&& He was the editor of ''Egység '', a radical art journal published in Vienna and Berlin, 1922-1924.
