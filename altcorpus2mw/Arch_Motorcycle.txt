Reeves was interested in having Gard customize a motorcycle for him, and the two eventually got talking about their ideas and love of motorcycles. This mutual understanding led to the two forming a company together. &&&&& &&&&& Models &&&&& Their custom production manufacturing consists of three models: KRGT-1, 1s and The Method 143.
