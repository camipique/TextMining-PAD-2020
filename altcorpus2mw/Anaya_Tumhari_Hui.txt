Anaya Tumhari Hui &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Anaya Tumhari Hui''''' is a Pakistani romantic drama series, produced by Asif Raza Mir and Babar Javed under their production banner A&B Entertainment. The drama airs twice weekly on Geo Entertainment every Wednesday and Thursday. It stars Sami Khan and Rabab Hashim in lead roles.
