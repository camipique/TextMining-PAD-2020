59	Bythitoidei
Bythitoidei &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Bythitoidei''' is a suborder of the order Ophidiiformes, the cusk eels. They are disnguished form the other Ophidiform suborder, the Ophidioidei, by being largely viviparous. &&&&& &&&&& Families &&&&& The following families make up the suborder Bythitoidei: &&&&& &&&&& * Family Bythitidae Gill, 1861 — viviparous brotulas &&&&& * Family Aphyonidae Jordan & Evermann, 1898 —aphyonids, blind cusk-eel &&&&& * Family Parabrotulidae Nielsen, 1968 — false brotulas &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
