Aoi Matsuda &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& is a Japanese badminton player. Born in Osaka prefecture, Matsuda graduated from the Toyama University of International Studies High School. She was a former Tricky Panders team and later affiliated with the Amer Sports Japan since April 2018. Matsuda was part of the national junior team that won the bronze medal at the 2013 Asian Junior Championships.
