Brett Plowman &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Brett Plowman''' (born 13 June 1969) is an Australian former rugby league footballer who played in the 1980s and the 1990s.  &&&&& &&&&& Playing career &&&&& Plowman began his career at Brisbane where he spent six seasons but was not apart of Brisbane's premiership successes of 1992 and 1993.  &&&&& &&&&& In 1994, Plowman moved to Parramatta where he spent two seasons.
