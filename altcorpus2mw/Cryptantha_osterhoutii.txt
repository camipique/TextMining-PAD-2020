Cryptantha osterhoutii &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Cryptantha osterhoutii''''' (Payson) Payson, common name '''Osterhout's cryptantha''', is a rare herbaceous perennial occurring in the U.S. states of Colorado, Utah, and Arizona. It is also known by the synonym ''Oreocarya osterhoutii'' Payson, and common name Osterhout cat's-eye. It is named in honor of George Everett Osterhout. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
