Cindy Ostmann &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Cindy Ostmann''' (born December 9, 1938) was an American Republican politician who served in the Missouri House of Representatives. &&&&& &&&&& Born in St. Louis County, Missouri, Ostmann graduated from Lindenwood College with a bachelor's degree in education in 1963. She taught in the Fort Zumwalt School District and served on the Fort Zumwalt board of education.
