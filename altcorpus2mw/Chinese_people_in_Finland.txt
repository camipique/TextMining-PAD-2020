Chinese people in Finland &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Chinese people in Finland''' form one of the largest immigrant groups in Finland. As of 2018 there were 12,407 speakers of Chinese in Finland. About 60% of them reside in Capital Region (Helsinki, Espoo, Vantaa and Kauniainen). As of 2018 there were 9,230 Chinese citizens living in Finland.
