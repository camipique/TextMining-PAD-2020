Clay Central-Everly Community School District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Clay Central-Everly Community School District''' ('''CCE''') is a school district headquartered in Royal, Iowa. The district is mostly in Clay County with portions in Dickinson and O'Brien counties, and it serves Royal, Everly, Greenville, and Rossie. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& It was formed on July 1, 1993 by the merger of the Clay Central School District and the Everly School District. &&&&& &&&&& On July 1, 2010, the South Clay Community School District was dissolved and portions went to the Clay Central Everly district. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * Clay Central-Everly Community School District &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
