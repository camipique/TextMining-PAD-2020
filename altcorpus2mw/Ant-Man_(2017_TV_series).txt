Ant-Man (2017 TV series) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Marvel's Ant-Man''''', or simply '''''Ant-Man''''', is an American animated television series based on the ''Ant-Man'' comics published by Marvel Comics. It premiered on June 10, 2017 on Disney XD. Josh Keaton voiced Ant-Man and Melissa Rauch voiced The Wasp.
