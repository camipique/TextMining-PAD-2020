Artigisa nigrosignata &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Artigisa nigrosignata''''' is a moth of the family Erebidae first described by Francis Walker in 1864. It is found in Sri Lanka, Borneo and India. &&&&& &&&&& Its wings are straw colored with medium brown variegated stripes. There are fine wavy, black postmedials. There is an oblique, diffusely edged black bar at apex. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
