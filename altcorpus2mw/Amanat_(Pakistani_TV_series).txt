Amanat (Pakistani TV series) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Amanat''''' is a Pakistani social drama series, produced by Amna Nawaz Khan under their production banner ANK Production while she also directed and written this serial. The drama airs weekly on Urdu 1 every Tuesday.
