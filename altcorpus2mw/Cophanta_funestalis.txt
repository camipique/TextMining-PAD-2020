Cophanta funestalis &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Cophanta funestalis''''' is a moth of the family Noctuidae first described by Francis Walker in 1864. It is found in Indo-Australian tropics of India, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Borneo, New Guinea and Australia. &&&&& &&&&& Forewings brownish with variable pale spots and dark bands.
