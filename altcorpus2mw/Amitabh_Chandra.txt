Amitabh Chandra &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Amitabh Chandra''' is an Indian-American academic and healthcare economist who is the Malcolm Wiener Professor of Social Policy at the John F. Kennedy School of Government at Harvard University. &&&&& &&&&& Chandra received his BA and PhD in economics from the University of Kentucky; his dissertation was titled ''Labor Market Dropouts and the Racial Wage Gap, 1940-1990''. &&&&& &&&&& He is a recipient of the Eugene Garfield Award and the American Society of Health Economists (ASHE) medal. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
