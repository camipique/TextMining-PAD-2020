Cairnie Junction railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Cairnie Junction railway station''' served the village of Cairnie, Aberdeenshire, Scotland from 1897 to 1968 on the Great North of Scotland Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened in 1897 by the Great North of Scotland Railway.
