Barstow Bridge &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Barstow Bridge''' was a historic Pratt pony truss bridge, at US 395 and Co. Rd. 4061, over the Kettle River near Kettle Falls, Washington. It spanned between Ferry County, Washington and Stevens County, Washington. &&&&& &&&&& It was a pre-fabricated steel bridge that was a surplus World War II bridge. It was bought from the U.S. War Assets Administration in 1946 and was installed near the unincorporated community of Barstow in 1947. &&&&& &&&&& The historic bridge was removed and replaced in 2010. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
