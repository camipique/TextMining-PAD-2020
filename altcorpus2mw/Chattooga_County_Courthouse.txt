Chattooga County Courthouse &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Chattooga County Courthouse''', on Courthouse Sq. in Summerville, Georgia, was built in 1909. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1980. &&&&& &&&&& The courthouse has three pedimented Corinthian tetrastyle entrances; the fourth entrance, also pedimented, has just four Corinthian pilasters. It has a domed clock tower. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
