Aleksandar Lukman &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Aleksandar Lukman''' (; born April 29, 1967) is a Serbian professional basketball strength and conditioning and alpine skiing coach and former pole vaulter. He currently serves as a strength and conditioning coach for Crvena zvezda of the Basketball League of Serbia, the ABA League and the EuroCup. &&&&& &&&&& Early life and education &&&&& Lukman was born in Belgrade, SR Serbia, SFR Yugoslavia, where he finished an elementary and a high school. He earned his bachelor's degree in physical education from the University of Belgrade in 1991 and earned his doctor's degree from the Faculty of sport and tourism – TIMS in Novi Sad in 2008. &&&&& &&&&& Coaching career in basketball &&&&& Lukman started his strength and conditioning coaching career in 1995.
