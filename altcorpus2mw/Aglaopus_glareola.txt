Aglaopus glareola &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Aglaopus glareola''''' is a moth of the family Thyrididae. The species was first described by Cajetan Felder, Rudolf Felder and Alois Friedrich Rogenhofer in 1874. It is found in Sri Lanka, and Borneo. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
