California Historical Radio Society &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''California Historical Radio Society''' is a museum about the history of radio broadcasting in the San Francisco Bay Area. It is located in Alameda, California. &&&&& &&&&& David F. Jackson founded the museum in 2003, as the "Bay Area Radio Museum" in Berkeley, California. In 2014, it moved to Alameda. &&&&& &&&&& The museum annually hosts its "Radio Day by the Bay" each summer where old-time radio shows are performed, and each fall inducts notable local broadcasters into the Bay Area Radio Hall of Fame. &&&&& &&&&& The museum also hosts the Bay Area Radio Museum, an online collection of airchecks. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * Bay Area Radio Museum &&&&& * California Historical Radio Society &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
