Clinical Autonomic Research &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Clinical Autonomic Research''''' is a bimonthly peer-reviewed medical journal covering research on the autonomic nervous system and its disorders. It was established in 1991 and is published by Springer Science+Business Media. The editors-in-chief are Horacio Kaufmann (New York University) and Jens Jordan (German Aerospace Center).
