Brieuc Vourch &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Brieuc Vourch''' (born in 1995) is a French classical violinist. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Born in Paris, Vourch started playing the violin when he was 3. At the age of 13, he auditioned for the conductor Louis Langrée in Tchaikowsky's Violin concerto and went to New York to study with Itzhak Perlman at the Juilliard School and with Aaron Rosand. At 17 he became a student of Boris Kuschnir in Vienna. Brieuc was also guided by David Kim, concertmaster of the Philadelphia Orchestra. &&&&& &&&&& He has performed in numerous concert halls in Europe and the United States, including the Carnegie Hall, the Alice Tully Hall, the Peter Jay Sharp Theater of the Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts in New-York, the Théâtre des Champs-Élysées, the Philharmonie de Paris, La Seine Musicale, the Salle Cortot, and the Palais des Congrès de Paris. In 2008, he performed as soloist for Shimon Peres, President of Israel, during a prestige concert organized on the occasion of his state visit to Paris, and more recently for the President of the French Republic Emmanuel Macron. &&&&& &&&&& As a chamber musician, Brieuc Vourch played with Roman Simovic, Svetlin Roussev, Abdel Rahman El Bacha, Elena Rozanova, François Salque, Ingmar Lazar, Ismaël Margain, Nathanaël Gouin, Manuel Vioque-Judde, and Ella van Poucke. Brieuc is also a member of the Centre de Musique de Chambre de Paris under the artistic direction of Jérôme Pernoo and Ensemble en Résidence of the Festival de La Roque-d'Anthéron 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Brieuc has taken part in numerous masterclasses of violin and chamber music with Zakhar Bron, Ana Chumachenco, Gábor Takács-Nagy, Augustin Dumay, Svetlin Roussev, Renaud Capuçon and Lawrence Power. He also received advice from Frank Peter Zimmermann, James Ehnes and Glenn Dicterow. He has participated in numerous academies and festivals in the United States and Europe, including the Summit Music Festival, the Bowdoin International Music Festival, the Verbier Festival Academy and the Schleswig-Holstein Musik Festival masterclasses for young soloists. He also received advice from Philippe Jaroussky, being a member of his Academy. &&&&& &&&&& During the 2017/2018 season, Brieuc performed in recitals and concertos in France, Germany, England, Switzerland and the United States in venues such as St Martin-in-the-Fields in London, La Seine Musicale (accompanied by the Ensemble Appasionnato conducted by Mathieu Herzog, and the Salle Cortot in Paris. He was also broadcast by France Musique, Medici.tv, Radio Classique and DakApp. &&&&& &&&&& In 2019, Brieuc will make his debut in Shanghai during the Hantang International Music Festival. &&&&& &&&&& He plays a modern violin, made for him in 2013 by Kurt Widenhouse, after the Guarneri del Gesù Kochansky of his teacher Aaron Rosand, and a violin by Goffredo Cappa. &&&&& &&&&& In addition to his regular artistic activities, Brieuc puts his talent at the service of citizen engagement organizations such as "Le Printemps de L'Optimisme" and "Le Projet Imagine". &&&&& &&&&& Brieuc is also a close friend of Jean-Laurent Cochet whom he considers one of his mentors. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * Official website &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
