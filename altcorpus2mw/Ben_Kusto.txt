Ben Kusto &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Ben Kusto''' (born 23 February 1976) is an Australian former professional rugby league footballer. &&&&& &&&&& Kusto started his career off with St George where he played for 3 seasons before moving to Parramatta. Kusto spent two seasons at Parramatta and was part of The 2000 Parramatta side which the media dubbed "The Baby Eels". Kusto left Parramatta at the end of the 2000 season to join English side Huddersfield. Kusto was part of The Huddersfield side which were relegated from The Super League. Kusto then signed a contract to rejoin Parramatta.
