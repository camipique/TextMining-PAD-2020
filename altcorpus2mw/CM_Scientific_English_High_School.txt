CM Scientific English High School &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''CM Scientific English High School''' is a school of Imphal West district of Manipur, India. It is situated in Iramsiphai Leirak Ashangba. The Chandreshwor Memorial Scientific English High School came into existence in 1995. It was founded by Chongtham Kober Singh.
