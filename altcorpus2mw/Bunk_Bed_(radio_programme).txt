Bunk Bed (radio programme) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Bunk Bed''''' is a comedy radio programme hosted by Peter Curran and Patrick Marber. The programme features the hosts lying in bunk beds, discussing various topics. It was described as "slightly weird" by Kate Chisholm, and was praised by Miranda Sawyer as being "strange, funny, silly, enchanting, beautifully put together".
