Captain Fantastic (album) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Captain Fantastic''''' is the tenth full-length studio album by German hip hop band Die Fantastischen Vier. It was released on 27 April 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Release and reception &&&&& The album was published on 27 April 2018. From the album, the title ''Zusammen'' was released as a single. It was chosen as the official song for the 2018 FIFA World Cup by the German public broadcaster ''Das Erste''. &&&&& &&&&& The album peaked at number two of the German charts, number one in Switzerland and number three in Austria. &&&&& &&&&& On the web page ''laut.de'' ''Captain Fantastic'' was rated with 3 out of 5 stars.
