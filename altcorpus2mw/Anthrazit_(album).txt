Anthrazit (album) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Anthrazit''' is the fifth studio album by Austrian musician RAF Camora. Released on 25 August 2017, by RAF Camoras own label Indipendenza, the album debuted at number one on the German, Swiss and Austrian album charts. ''Anthrazit'' is certified Gold by IFPI Austria and by Bundesverband Musikindustrie (BVMI). The album produced six singles, including "Kontrollieren", "In meiner Wolke", "Alles probiert", "Bye Bye", "Andere Liga" and "Primo". All of the singles reached the charts in German-speaking europe, which exception of "Bye Bye", which didn't enter the Swiss charts. &&&&& &&&&& Background &&&&& Following the release of his fourth studio album ''Ghøst'' (2016), RAF Camora released his fourth collobarative studio album, ''Palmen aus Plastik'' (2016), alongside German rapper Bonez MC. RAF Camora announced in April 2017 his fifth studio album, ''Anthrazit'', without giving a specific release date.
