Anubha Sourya Sarangi &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Anubha''' or Anubha Sourrya is an Indian film actress who is known for her acting in Ollywood and Bollywood industry. She is the daughter of actress Puspa Panda. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& &&&&& Anubha was born on 16 July 1992 at Bhubaneswar, Odisha to actress Pushpa Panda and engineer Lalatendu Narayan Sarangi. She belongs to a small hill side village, Ranapur in Khurda, She completed her schooling at DAV Chandrasekharpur and pursued her B.Tech course from Silicon Institute of Technology, Bhubaneswar. She is a trained Odissi dancer and learnt Oddisi dance from late Guru Gangadhar Pradhan. She has performed in many festivals in Konark and Puri and also at Dhauli. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& &&&&& Anubha began her career as a professional actress with the movie Mun Premi Mun Pagala alongside Harihar Dash. She made her debut in Bollywood with the movie Kaun Kitne Paani Mein directed by Nila Madhab Panda. She made her comeback in Ollywood after 5 years in 2016 and appeared in Samaya Bada Balaban(2016), Revenge(2016), Sweet Heart(2016). However, she rose to prominence after she started working as a judge and mentor in Rajo Queen from 2014 onwards. 2017 release Bajrangi was a hit and she was appreciated for her performance. &&&&& &&&&& In 2018,she appeared against Jyoti Ranjan Nayak in the movie ''Saathi Tu Pheria''. &&&&& &&&&& Filmography &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Year &&&&& &&&&& Cinemas &&&&& &&&&& Language &&&&& &&&&& Notes &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2011 &&&&& &&&&& Mun Premi Mun Pagala &&&&& &&&&& Odia &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2014 &&&&& &&&&& Kaun Kitne Paani Mein &&&&& &&&&& Hindi &&&&& &&&&& Debut in Bollywood &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2016 &&&&& &&&&& Samaya Bada Balaban &&&&& &&&&& Odia &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2016 &&&&& &&&&& Sweet Heart &&&&& &&&&& Odia &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2016 &&&&& &&&&& Revenge &&&&& &&&&& Odia &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2017 &&&&& &&&&& Bajrangi &&&&& &&&&& Odia &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2018 &&&&& &&&&& Saathi Tu Pheria &&&&& &&&&& Odia &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
