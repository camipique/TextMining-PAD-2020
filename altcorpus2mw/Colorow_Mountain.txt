Colorow Mountain &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Colorow Mountain''' is a summit in the Indian Valley area in Rio Blanco County, Colorado. The '''Colorow Mountain State Wildlife Area''' is located on the mountain. &&&&& &&&&& The Colorow Mountain State Wildlife Area is located near White River City and is north of Rio Blanco Lake and State Highway 64. There is camping, hunting, hiking, horseback riding, and wildlife viewing on the 1500-acre park. It has access to some Bureau of Land Management areas north of the wildlife area.
