Bert Ogden Arena &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&&  &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Bert Ogden Arena''' is an indoor arena in Edinburg, Texas, United States. The arena officially opened in August 2018 and currently houses the Rio Grande Valley Vipers of the NBA G League. The arena seats nearly 7,700, which can be expanded up to 9,000 for concerts. &&&&& &&&&& Location and design &&&&& The arena was mainly built for basketball, but also hosts concerts and other events.
