C Spire Ferriss Trophy &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''C Spire Ferriss Trophy''' was created in the fall of 2003 by the Mississippi Sports Hall of Fame to honor the Mississippi Collegiate Baseball Player of the Year. The trophy bears the name and likeness of Dave Ferriss.
