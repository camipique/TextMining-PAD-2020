Brex &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Brex''' is a credit card company that issues corporate credit cards to technology companies in the United States. &&&&& &&&&& Inception and funding structure &&&&& Brazilians Henrique Dubugras and Pedro Franceschi founded the company . In January 2017, Brex received seed funding from Y Combinator. Brex received additional capital from Peter Thiel, Max Levchin, Ribbit Capital and Yuri Milner.
