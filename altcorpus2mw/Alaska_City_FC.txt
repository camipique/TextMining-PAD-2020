Alaska City FC &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Alaska City FC''' is an amateur soccer club based in Palmer, Alaska. The club began playing in the United Premier Soccer League in 2019. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The club was founded in 2011.
