Aberdeen Stakes &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Aberdeen Stakes''' was an American Thoroughbred horse race held annually from 1913 through 1947 at Havre de Grace Racetrack in Havre de Grace, Maryland.
