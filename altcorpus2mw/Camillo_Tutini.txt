Camillo Tutini &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Camillo Tutini''' (1594-1667) was an Italian historian. &&&&& &&&&& Life &&&&& Tutini initially studied at the Certosa di San Martino in Naples and - between 1628 and 1630 - lived in Naples with his mother and two of his sisters. Tutini later became part of the city's scholarly and literary circles.
