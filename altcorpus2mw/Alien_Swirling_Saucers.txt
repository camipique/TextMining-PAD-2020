Alien Swirling Saucers &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Alien Swirling Saucers''' is a spinning ride at ''Toy Story'' Land in Disney's Hollywood Studios & Walt Disney Studios Park. First announced at the 2015 D23 Expo, it opened on June 30, 2018 along with Slinky Dog Dash.
