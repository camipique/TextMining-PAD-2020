Arjun - Prince of Bali &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Arjun - Prince of Bali''''' is an Indian animated television series produced by Green Gold Animation. It premiered on 1 June 2014 on Disney Channel India. It is a spin-off of the show ''Chhota Bheem''. The main character of the show, Arjun, was first featured in the film ''Chhota Bheem and the Throne of Bali''. The show features the adventures of Arjun, the prince of Bali.  &&&&& &&&&& Characters &&&&& *'''Arjun''': Arjun is the 11-year old prince of Bali. He is skilled in warfare and archery. &&&&& *'''Zimboo''': Zimboo is Arjun's 6-month old loyal pet tiger. &&&&& *'''King Sahadev Verma''': King Sahadev Verma is the 36-year old king of Bali. &&&&& *'''Queen Suvarna''': Queen Suvarna is the 30-year old queen of Bali. &&&&& *'''Tanya''': Tanya is Arjun's 6-year-old sister and the princess of Bali. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
