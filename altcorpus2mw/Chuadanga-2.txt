Chuadanga-2 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Chuadanga-2''' is a constituency represented in the Jatiya Sangsad (National Parliament) of Bangladesh since 2008 by Md. Ali Azgar of the Awami League. &&&&& &&&&& Boundaries &&&&& The constituency encompasses Damurhuda and Jibannagar upazilas, and two union parishads of Chuadanga Sadar Upazila: Begampur and Titudaha. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The constituency was created in 1984 from a Kushtia constituency when the former Kushtia District was split into three districts: Meherpur, Kushtia, and Chuadanga. &&&&& &&&&& Members of Parliament &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Election &&&&& &&&&& Member &&&&& &&&&& Party &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 1986 &&&&& &&&&& Mirza Sultan Raja &&&&& &&&&& Jatiya Samajtantrik Dal (Siraj) &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 1988 &&&&& &&&&& Habibur Rahman &&&&& &&&&& Bangladesh Jamaat-e-Islami &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 1996 &&&&& &&&&& Md.
