Anton Herashchenko &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Anton Yuriiovych Herashchenko''' (, , born 10 February 1979) is a member of the Ukrainian parliament (Verkhovna Rada). &&&&& &&&&& Herashchenko was elected to parliament in the 26 October 2014 Ukrainian parliamentary election, representing the People's Front in a multi-mandate nationwide district. He is secretary of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine Committee on Legislative Support of Law Enforcement. &&&&& &&&&& Herashchenko was one of the founders of a database called "Peacemaker". He is also known for anti-Russian views. &&&&& &&&&& In January 2017, it was announced that the Security Service of Ukraine (SBU) had prevented an assassination attempt against Herashchenko. The SBU had had the two assassins under surveillance for over a month and arrested them in the act with an explosive device in their possession. The SBU stated that the assassins had been in prison in the Russian-annexed Crimea, until they agreed to assassinate Herashchenko. The SBU claim that the assassins were coordinated by Andrey Tikhonov.
