Abraham Lowenstein House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Abraham Lowenstein House''' is a historic house in Memphis, Tennessee. It was built in 1901 for Abraham Lowenstein, a Jewish German immigrant who co-founded a department store in Memphis with his brothers. It belonged to the Beethoven Club from 1922 to 1946. &&&&& &&&&& The house was designed in the Queen Anne architectural style.
