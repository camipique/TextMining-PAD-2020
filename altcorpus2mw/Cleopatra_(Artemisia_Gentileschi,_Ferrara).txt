Cleopatra (Artemisia Gentileschi, Ferrara) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Cleopatra''''' is a c.1620 painting by Artemisia Gentileschi, now in the Fondazione Cavallini-Sgarbi collection in Ferrara. It was formerly misattributed to Guido Cagnacci. It shows Cleopatra committing suicide with an asp. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
