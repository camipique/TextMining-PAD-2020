Catherine de la Tour du Pin &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Catherine de la Tour du Pin''' (about 1290 - 1337), was the tenth child of Humbert I of Viennois and Anne of Burgundy. She married Philip I of Piedmont, with who she has eleven children, including James of Piedmont. &&&&& &&&&& Notes and references &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
