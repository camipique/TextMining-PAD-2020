Baleng Tower &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Baleng Tower''' () is a tower in Fuxing District, Taoyuan City, Taiwan. &&&&& &&&&& Architecture &&&&& The 29.5 meters high tower was constructed in a former fort built during the Japanese rule of Taiwan. It was constructed on an altitude of 1,314 meters above sea level. The structure is equipped with LED lamps that will shine during night time.
