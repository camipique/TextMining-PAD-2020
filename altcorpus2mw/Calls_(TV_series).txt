Calls (TV series) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Calls''''' is an upcoming American-French drama web television series based on the French series of the same name created by Timothée Hochet. The series was created by Hochet and is a co-production between Apple TV+ and French network Canal+. &&&&& &&&&& Premise &&&&& ''Calls'' will allow "audiences to experience short stories through real-life audio sources and minimal visuals." &&&&& &&&&& Production &&&&& On June 21, 2018, it was reported that Apple had given the production a series order for a first season consisting of ten episodes. The series, an English-language adaptation of original French series ''Calls'', was created by original series creator Timothée Hochet. The show is a co-production between Apple TV+ and French network Canal+.
