Congressional Soccer Match &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Congressional Soccer Match''' is an annual soccer game played each summer by members of the United States Congress. The game began in 2013 and in its current format in 2014. In the match, the Republicans and Democrats form separate teams and play against each other. The event is hosted by the United States Soccer Foundation and generally features members of the Congressional Soccer Caucus, and former professional players.
