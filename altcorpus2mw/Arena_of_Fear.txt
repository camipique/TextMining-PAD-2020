Arena of Fear &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Arena of Fear''''' (German: '''''Geliebte Bestie''''') is a 1959 Austrian drama film directed by Arthur Maria Rabenalt and starring Gerhard Riedmann, Margit Nünke and Willy Birgel. &&&&& &&&&& The film's sets were designed by the art directors Alexander Sawczynski and Werner Schlichting. Location filming took place at the Circus Busch-Roland. &&&&& &&&&& Cast &&&&& * Gerhard Riedmann as Ruda, Raubtierdompteur &&&&& * Margit Nünke as Beatrix &&&&& * Willy Birgel as Cameron, Kunstschütze &&&&& * Fred Bertelmann as John &&&&& * Mady Rahl as Marianne &&&&& * Gustav Knuth as Carl de Vries &&&&& * Massimo Giuliani as Willi, Dodys Sohn &&&&& * Gretl Schörg as Olga, Sekreträrin &&&&& * Sigrid Marquardt as Anita.
