Atul Gupta &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Atul Gupta''' (born 14 June 1968) is a South African businessman and a member of the Gupta family, well-known in the country for their corrupt business dealings. He started the family's first business in South Africa, Sahara Computers, in 1993.
