BLC Leather Technology Centre &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&&  &&&&& '''BLC Leather Technology Centre''' or '''BLC''' is a testing, auditing and consulting business that specialises in chemicals, leather, footwear and other associated products and related materials, and is based in Northamptonshire . An additional specialism is the safe use and management of chemicals in the supply chain; a concept from which BLC developed the ZDHC approved BLC Chem-MAP certification process for chemicals, chemical companies, brands and their suppliers. &&&&& &&&&& A significant item of note is that BLC is the official facilitator of the Leather Working Group (LWG) which is a significant environmental stewardship programme within the leather industry.
