Comitas allani &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Comitas allani''''' is an extinct species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Pseudomelatomidae. &&&&& &&&&& Description &&&&& The length of the shell attains 26.7 mm. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Distribution &&&&& This extinct marine species was found in Lower Pleistocene strata off Hawke’s Bay, New Zealand.
