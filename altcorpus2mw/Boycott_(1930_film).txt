Boycott (1930 film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Boycott''''' (German: '''''Boykott''''') is a 1930 German drama film directed by Robert Land and starring Ernst Stahl-Nachbaur, Lil Dagover and Rolf von Goth. &&&&& &&&&& The film's sets were designed by the art director Ludwig Reiber.
