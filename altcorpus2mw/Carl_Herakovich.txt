Carl Herakovich &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Carl T. Herakovich''' (born August 6, 1937) is an American retired engineering professor, college football player, coach and official. He was the founding director of the NASA-Virginia Tech Composites Program at Virginia Tech, Director of Applied Mechanics and the Henry L Kinnier Professor of civil engineering at the University of Virginia. He led the nation in scoring in 1958 with 168 pts in 8 game, second all-time to Barry Sanders |title=NCAA.
