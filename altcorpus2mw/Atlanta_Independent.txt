Atlanta Independent &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''The Atlanta Independent''''' was an African American weekly newspaper published in Atlanta, Georgia from 1903-1928. It was one of the first African American newspapers in Atlanta. A Republican newspaper, it was started by Benjamin J. Davis, father of the civil rights activist Benjamin J.
