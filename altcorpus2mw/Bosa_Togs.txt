Bosa Togs &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Bosa Togs''' is a senior IT manager from Papua New Guinea. In 2016 she won the Westpac Outstanding Woman of the Year Award and the PricewaterhouseCoopers Private Sector Award. &&&&& &&&&& Togs is the general manager for IT at Telikom Papua New Guinea. She successfully campaigned within the company for equal pay for women employed by Telikom, resulting in a change in remuneration policy by the corporation. &&&&& &&&&& She holds two masters degrees, one in IT and one in business administration. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
