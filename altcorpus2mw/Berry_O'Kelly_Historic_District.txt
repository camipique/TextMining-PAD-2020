Berry O'Kelly Historic District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Berry O'Kelly Historic District''' is a historic district in Raleigh, North Carolina, which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2017. &&&&& &&&&& It includes the '''St. James African Methodist Episcopal Church''', built in 1923 and extended to the rear in 1999. It is a red brick Gothic Revival building, with brick laid in running bond. &&&&& &&&&& It includes the grave of Berry O'Kelly, with a gravestone giving March 14, 1931 as date of death. &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * List of Registered Historic Places in North Carolina &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
