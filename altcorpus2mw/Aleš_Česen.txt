Aleš Česen &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Aleš Česen''' (born 26 April 1982, in Kranj) is a Slovenian climber, mountaineer, mountain guide and entrepreneur. &&&&& &&&&& In 2015 Česen was awarded Piolet d’Or for ascent of the north face of Hagshu along with Marko Prezelj and Andrej Lindič. &&&&& &&&&& He was awarded Alpinist of the year award in 2017, given by the Alpine Association of Slovenia. &&&&& &&&&& Česen has a PhD in Civil Engineering and is a IFMGA/UIAGM mountain guide and climbing instructor.
