Andalusia Star News &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''''Andalusia Star-News''''' was formed in 1948 out of the merger of the Andalusia Star and the Covington News, both of Andalusia, Alabama. It has a circulation of 3,372 and is owned by Boone Newspapers, Inc. &&&&& &&&&& In 1972, it was sold to Tuscaloosa Newspapers by Ed Dannelly and Byron Vickery, who had operated it since 1948. Tuscaloosa Newspapers would later become Boone Newspapers, Inc. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
