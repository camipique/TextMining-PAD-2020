Boule de Suif (1934 film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Boule de Suif''''' () is a 1934 Soviet drama film directed and written by Mikhail Romm. It is an adaptation of eponymous short story by Guy de Maupassant. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& A group of French capitalists leave Rouen, occupied by Prussia.
