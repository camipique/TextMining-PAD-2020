Andrew Vollert &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Andrew Joseph Vollert''' (born March 15, 1995) is an American football tight end for the Cincinnati Bengals of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Weber State in Ogden, Utah. &&&&& &&&&& College career &&&&& Vollert played one season at San Jose State, followed by year at San Francisco City College, before transferring to Weber State. He was an FCS All-American in 2016 and 2017 for the Wildcats, totaling 123 receptions for 1,613 yards and 12 touchdowns. &&&&& &&&&& Professional career &&&&& ===Arizona Cardinals=== &&&&& Vollert signed with the Arizona Cardinals as an undrafted free agent on April 30, 2018. He was waived on September 1, 2018 and was signed to the practice squad the next day. He was released on October 31, 2018. &&&&& &&&&& ===Cincinnati Bengals=== &&&&& On November 5, 2018, Vollert was signed to the Cincinnati Bengals practice squad.
