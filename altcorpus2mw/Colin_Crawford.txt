Colin Crawford &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Colin Crawford''' is an American academic and the 24th dean of the University of Louisville School of Law. &&&&& &&&&& Dean Crawford began as dean of the law school on January 1, 2018. Prior to his current position, he was the Robert C. Cudd Professor of Environmental Law at Tulane University Law School in New Orleans, Louisiana. He has also taught at Georgia State University College of Law, Brooklyn Law School, and Thomas Jefferson School of Law. &&&&& &&&&& Dean Crawford earned his Bachelor of Arts from Columbia University, a Master of Arts degree from the University of Cambridge, and a Juris Doctorate from Harvard Law School.
