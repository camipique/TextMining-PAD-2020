Barton Wallop &&&&& Introduction &&&&& The Hon. Barton Wallop (5 November 1744-1 September 1781) was Master of Magdalene College, Cambridge from 1774 until 1781. &&&&& &&&&& The third son of John Wallop, Viscount Lymington, he was educated at Eton and Magdalene. He held livings in Portsmouth and Cliddesden. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
