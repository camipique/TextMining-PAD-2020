Canon Commercial Historic District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Canon Commercial Historic District''' in Canon, Georgia is a historic district which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1985. The listing included eight contributing buildings. &&&&& &&&&& It includes the Canon Hotel, a two-story Georgian Revival-style building. &&&&& &&&&& It includes the W.F. Bowers Building, a one-story brick building built in the early 1890s, which, in 1985, had a "deteriorated Greek Revival-style porch at the Depot Street level that is supported by square brick columns. The central portal has a glass transom and a segmental arch and is flanked by a window on each side and four square brick wall pilasters.
