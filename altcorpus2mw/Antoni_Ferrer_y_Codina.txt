Antoni Ferrer y Codina &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Anton Ferrer y Codina''' (Barcelona, 1837 - October 11, 1905) was a Spanish playwright and newspaper writer. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Born in Barcelona in 1837, he was editor of the ''Barcelona Alegre'' and ''La Tomasa'' weeklies. He died on October 11, 1905. &&&&& &&&&& Major works &&&&& * 1867. ''Les relíquies d'una mare'', premiered at the Teatro Romea, on 20 January 1867. &&&&& * 1867. ''Un gefe de la coronela'' patriotic drama in three acts, premiered at the Teatro Romea, on 19 December 1867. &&&&& * 1868. ''La perla de Badalona'', premiered at the Teatro Romea, on 4 January 1868. &&&&& * 1868. ''Ocells d'Amèrica'', premiered at the Teatro Romea, on 3 February 1868. &&&&& * 1868. ''El gat de mar'', premiered at the Teatro Romea, on 21 November 1868. &&&&& * 1902. ''Els calaveres'', comedy in three acts, originally by A.
