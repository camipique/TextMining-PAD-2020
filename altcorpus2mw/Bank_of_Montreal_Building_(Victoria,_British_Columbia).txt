Bank of Montreal Building (Victoria, British Columbia) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Bank of Montreal Building''', or '''Bank of Montreal, Government Street Branch''', is an historic building in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada. Designed by architect Francis Rattenbury, the building was constructed in 1897. &&&&&  It is located on Government Street, at the entrance of Bastion Square, a few blocks north of the British Columbia Parliament Buildings and The Empress.
