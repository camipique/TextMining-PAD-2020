As the Old Sang, So the Young Pipe (Jordaens, Valenciennes) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& 300px &&&&& '''''As the Old Sing, So the Young Pipe''''' is a 1638–1640 painting by Jacob Jordaens, now in the musée des beaux-arts de Valenciennes.
