Alexios Ntanatsidis &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Alexios Ntanatsidis''' is a Greek judoka. In 2013, he won the gold medal at the U21 World Championships. In 2014, he won the bronze medal at the European Judo Open Championships.
