Benfleet F.C. &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Benfleet Football Club''' is a football club based in Benfleet, Essex. They are currently members of the and play at Park Lane, Canvey Island. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& Founded in 1922, the club was known as Benfleet United until a merger with a team based in nearby Thundersley called Parkfields Athletic, with the merged club being known as Benfleet Rangers. The club played in the Southend and District League before changing its name again to Benfleet and joining the Essex Olympian Football League in 1972.
