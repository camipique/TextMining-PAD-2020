An Episode from the Russian Campaign &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& 300px &&&&& '''''An Episode from the Russian Campaign''''' (French - ''Épisode de la campagne de Russie'') is an 1836 oil on canvas painting by Nicolas-Toussaint Charlet, now in the Musée des Beaux-Arts de Lyon.
