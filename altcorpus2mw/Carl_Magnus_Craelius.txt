Carl Magnus Craelius &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Carl Magnus Craelius''' (1773-1842), was a Swedish opera singer (tenor) and voice teacher. He was a member of the Royal Swedish Academy of Music (1822). He was engaged at the Royal Swedish Opera in 1795-1806.
