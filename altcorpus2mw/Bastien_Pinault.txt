Bastien Pinault &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Bastien Pinault''' (born October 18, 1993) is a French professional basketball player for Élan Chalon of LNB Pro A. &&&&& &&&&& Born in Tarbes, he was trained at the Élan Béarnais Pau-Lacq-Orthez and then played for La Rochelle. Pinault played the 2016-17 season with ALM Évreux. He averaged 8.8 points, 1.6 rebounds and 2 assists per game in Pro B.
