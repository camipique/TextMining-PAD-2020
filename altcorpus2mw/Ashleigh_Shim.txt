Ashleigh Shim &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Ashleigh Jerise Shim''' (born 11 November 1993) is a Jamaican footballer who plays as a forward for the Jamaica national team. She also holds British citizenship of the Cayman Islands. &&&&& &&&&& Playing career &&&&& ===College career=== &&&&& Shim played as a forward with FIU Panthers during 2011-2014. During her time there, she scored 17 goals and made 13 assists. &&&&& &&&&& ===Club career=== &&&&& &&&&& In 2016, Shim played for VfL Sindelfingen and FC Djursholm. &&&&& &&&&& ===International career=== &&&&& Shim, who is of Caymanian descent through her grandmother, represented Cayman Islands at the 2009 Caribbean Football Union Women's Under-17 Tournament. &&&&& &&&&& Shim switched allegiance to Jamaica in 2015 and competed at the 2016 CONCACAF Women's Olympic Qualifying Championship qualification and the 2018 CONCACAF Women's Championship qualification.
