Al Sharq Forum &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Al Sharq Forum''' () is an independent pro-democracy, non-partisan, non-profit, think-tank focusing on the greater Middle East. Established in 2012, Wadah Khanfar, the former director general of Al Jazeera Network, is the co-founder and President.
