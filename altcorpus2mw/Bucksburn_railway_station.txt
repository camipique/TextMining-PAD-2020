Bucksburn railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Bucksburn railway station''' served the suburb of Bucksburn, Aberdeenshire, Scotland from 1854 to 1956 on the Great North of Scotland Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened as Buxburn on 20 September 1854 by the Great North of Scotland Railway. It was situated north of Bankhead and south of . The name changed to Bucksburn on 1 January 1897.
