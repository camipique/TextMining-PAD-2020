Caesar Rodney (Baker) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Caesar Rodney''''' is a marble sculpture depicting the American lawyer and politician of the same name by Bryan Baker, installed in the United States Capitol's crypt, in Washington, D.C., as part of the National Statuary Hall Collection.
