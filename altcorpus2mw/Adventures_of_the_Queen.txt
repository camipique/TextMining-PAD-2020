Each was made through 20th Century Fox for $1 million but was done for different networks. &&&&& &&&&& "I missed television," said Allen. "There's a hysteria and an excitement in television that exists nowhere else in business." &&&&& &&&&& Allen called the film "Son of ''The Poseidon Adventure''."
