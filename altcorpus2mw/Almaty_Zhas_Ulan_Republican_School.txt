Almaty Zhas Ulan Republican School &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Zhas Ulan Republican School named after Baurzhan Momyshuly''' ( / ) also known as the '''Almaty Zhas Ulan Republican School''' is a military boarding school in Kazakhstan's Armed Forces which specializes in the training of Kazakh youth who want to join the military. The school was opened on February 28, 1983 on the basis of a decree of the Council of Ministers of the Kazakh SSR.
