Ancient and Primeval Beech Forests of Albania &&&&& Introduction &&&&& The '''Ancient and Primeval Beech Forests of Albania''' () encompasses the beech forests of Gashi in Valbonë Valley National Park and Rajcë in Shebenik-Jabllanicë National Park.
