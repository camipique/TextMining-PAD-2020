Bankhead railway station (Aberdeen) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Bankhead railway station (Aberdeen)''' served the area of Bankhead, Aberdeenshire, Scotland from 1887 to 1937 on the Great North of Scotland Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened on 1 July 1887 by the Great North of Scotland Railway. It was situated north of and south of station.
