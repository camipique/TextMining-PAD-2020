Almost a Husband &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Almost a Husband''''' is a 1919 American comedy film directed by Clarence G. Badger and written by Robert F. Hill. It is based on the 1897 novel ''Old Ebenezer'' by Opie Read. The film stars Will Rogers, Peggy Wood, Herbert Standing, Cullen Landis, Clara Horton and Ed Brady. The film was released on October 12, 1919, by Goldwyn Pictures. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& A New England schoolteacher moves to a small Southern town and comes to the aid of many local people, including a young woman who faces unwanted romantic advances from another man.
