Abraham de Broen &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Abraham de Broen &&&&& '''Abraham de Broen''' (1759-1804), was a Swedish stage actor. He belonged to the elite of the pioneer generation actors of the Royal Dramatic Theatre. He was also the founder and first director of the ''Djurgårdsteatern''. He was a very popular character actor and mainly played elder male roles in so called burgher plays, often tragedies. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& * Nordensvan, Georg, Svensk teater och svenska skådespelare från Gustav III till våra dagar.
