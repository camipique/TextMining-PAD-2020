Adaffa Formation &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Adaffa Formation''' is a Geological formation in Saudi Arabia, and the lowermost unit of the Suqah Group. It dates back to the Campanian of the Late Cretaceous. The Lithology consists of Sandstone, Siltstone and Marl, with a basal granitic conglomerate with phosphatic nodules. Also present are ferricrete horizons. It was deposited in marginal marine conditions close to land.
