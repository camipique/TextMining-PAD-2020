Colonial Mutual Life building, Dubbo &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Colonial Mutual Life building''' is a heritage-listed former life insurance office located at 118 Macquarie Street, Dubbo in the Dubbo Regional Council local government area of New South Wales, Australia. It is also known as '''CML Building''' and '''116-120 Macquarie Street'''. The property is privately owned and was added to the New South Wales State Heritage Register on 2 April 1999. &&&&& &&&&& Description &&&&& The three-storey building has been repurposed and, in 2013, the ground floor was used as a cafe, travel agent, and other mixed retail.
