Cetobacterium somerae &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Cetobacterium somerae''''' is a Gram-negative, microaerotolerant, non-spore-forming and rod-shaped bacterium from the genus of ''Cetobacterium'' which has been isolated from human feces. ''Cetobacterium somerae'' occur in intestinal tracts of freshwater fish. &&&&& Cetobacterium somerae produces cobalamin. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
