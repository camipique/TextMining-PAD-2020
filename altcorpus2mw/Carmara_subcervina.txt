Carmara subcervina &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Carmara subcervina''''' is a moth of the family Noctuidae first described by Francis Walker in 1864. It is found in Sri Lanka, Japan, Taiwan, Borneo and Australia. &&&&& &&&&& Forewings brownish with a pale broken zigzag submarginal line. There is a pale mark on the hind margin of hindwings. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
