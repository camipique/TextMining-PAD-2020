Admiralty Central Metallurgical Laboratory &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Admiralty Central Metallurgical Laboratory''' was a specialist research unit of the British Royal Navy from 1936 to 1956. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The Central Metallurgical Laboratory was based at Emsworth in England. The organization was a specialist research unit as part of the Portsmouth Dockyard's engineering department. In 1956 the unit was closed and its staff and work was divided between two other Admiralty organizations the Materials Laboratory and Chemical Department. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * Admiralty &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Sources &&&&& *Archives, The National.
