Congo Express (film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Congo Express''''' (German: '''''Kongo-Express''''') is a 1939 German adventure film directed by Eduard von Borsody and starring Marianne Hoppe, Willy Birgel. and René Deltgen . &&&&& &&&&& The film's sets were designed by the art director Franz Koehn.
