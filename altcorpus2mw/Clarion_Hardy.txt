Clarion Hardy &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Clarion DeWitt Hardy''' (October 2, 1877 – June 27, 1936) was an American football and baseball coach. He served as the head football coach at Dakota Wesleyan University in three separate stints (1902, 1907–1908, and 1913–1914) compiling a record of 16–14–2. Hardy also had two stints at the head baseball coach at Dakota Wesleyan, from 1905 to 1910 and again in 1913.
