Andrew Douglas (squash player) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Andrew Douglas''' (born 12 August 1998 in New York) is an American professional squash player. As of February 2019, he was ranked number 124 in the world. He won the 2018 Barbados Open professional tournament &&&&& &&&&& Douglas currently studies at the University of Pennsylvania and is expected to graduate in the class of 2021.
