Capital Development Authority &&&&& Introduction &&&&& A '''Capital Development Authority''' is a kind of entity found in various countries that is concerned with the municipal development of the capital city or capital territory of that country. Examples include: &&&&& &&&&& *The Capital Development Authority of Islamabad, the capital of Pakistan &&&&& *The Capital Development Authority of Dodoma, capital of Tanzania, &&&&& *Rajdhani Unnayan Kartripakkha, or RAJUK, the Capital Development Authority of Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh &&&&& *The Federal Capital Development Authority of Abuja, the capital of Nigeria &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
