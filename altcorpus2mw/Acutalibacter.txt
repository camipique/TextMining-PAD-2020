36	Acutalibacter
Acutalibacter &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Acutalibacter''''' is a genus of bacteria from the family of Ruminococcaceae with one known species (''Acutalibacter muris''). ''Acutalibacter muris'' has been isolated from the feces of a mouse from Munich in Germany. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
