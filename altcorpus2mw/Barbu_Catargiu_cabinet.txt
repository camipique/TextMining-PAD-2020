Barbu Catargiu cabinet &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Barbu Catargiu &&&&& The cabinet of Barbu Catargiu was the first government of the United Principalities of Romania, after formal union of Wallachia with Moldavia, and lasted from 22 January to 24 June 1862.
