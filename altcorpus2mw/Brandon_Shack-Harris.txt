Brandon Shack-Harris &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Brandon Shack-Harris''' is a professional poker player who has won two World Series of Poker bracelets. He was born in Racine, Wisconsin and is based in Chicago. Shack-Harris developed his skills in cash games at the Horseshoe Hammond. &&&&& &&&&& World Series of Poker &&&&& His first bracelet happened in the 1128-player 2014 Event #3: $1,000 Pot-Limit Omaha for $205,634. It was the first $1,000 buy-in Pot Limit Omaha event and the largest non-No Limit Hold'em field in the history of the World Series of Poker.
