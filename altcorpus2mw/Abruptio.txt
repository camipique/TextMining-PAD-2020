Abruptio &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Abruptio''''' is an upcoming American independent horror film written and directed by Evan Marlowe. Produced by Kerry Finlayson and Kerry Marlowe, the film stars James Marsters, Christopher McDonald, Hana Mae Lee, Robert Englund, Jordan Peele, and Sid Haig. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& Les Hackel (James Marsters) is a guy down on his luck who wakes to find an explosive device has been implanted in his neck.
