Black-spotted dwarf gecko &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''black-spotted dwarf gecko''' (''Lygodactylus nigropunctatus'') is a species of gecko endemic to the northern South Africa (Gauteng, Limpopo, Mpumalanga, and North-West Provinces). The subspecies ''L. n. incognitus'', '''cryptic dwarf gecko''', and ''L. n. montiscaeruli'', '''Makgabeng dwarf gecko''', are sometimes considered distinct species (e.g., in the IUCN Red List of Threatened Species). Both of these species has been assessed as "data deficient" by IUCN. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
