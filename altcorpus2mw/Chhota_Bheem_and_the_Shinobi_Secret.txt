Chhota Bheem and the Shinobi Secret &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Chhota Bheem and the ShiNobi Secret''' is an indian animated film featuring Bheem the main character of Chhota Bheem series. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& Bheem and his friends unite and travel to Japan with their Samurai friend Sam to help him save his clan from an unknown enemy called Shinobis. In the way they face several troubles like getting attacked by bear. Before they reach Sam's village Sam gets kidnapped by the Shinobis.
