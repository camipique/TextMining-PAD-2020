Clocksbriggs railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Clocksbriggs railway station''' served the town of Forfar, Angus, Scotland from 1848 to 1964 on the Arbroath and Forfar Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened in 1848 by the Aberdeen Railway. It was situated north of and south of station. The station closed temporarily on 1 January 1917, reopened on 1 June 1919, closed to passengers again on 5 December 1955 and to goods traffic on 15 June 1964.
