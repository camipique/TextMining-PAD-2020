Crystal Swan &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Crystal Swan''''' is a 2018 Belarusian drama film directed by Darya Zhuk. It was selected as the Belarusian entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 91st Academy Awards, but it was not nominated. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& In the 1990s, Velya hopes to leave Belarus and become a DJ in the United States.
