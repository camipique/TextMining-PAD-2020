Bibliography of Holyoke &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& Pulitzer Prize winner Constance McLaughlin Green's case history of Holyoke, among the earliest academic works of urban history, Green's doctoral thesis was widely published by Yale University Press upon receiving the university's Eggleston Award in History &&&&& This is a '''bibliography of Holyoke''', a city in Massachusetts, with books about the area's history, culture, geography, and people. Due to the area's proximity to a number of industrial developments and the numerous cultures of different waves of immigrant workers, a wide number of books, dissertations, and comprehensive articles have been written about Holyoke throughout its history in several languages.
