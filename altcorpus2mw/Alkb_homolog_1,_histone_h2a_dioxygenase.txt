Alkb homolog 1, histone h2a dioxygenase &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''AlkB homolog 1, histone H2A dioxygenase''' is a protein that in humans is encoded by the ALKBH1 gene. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Function &&&&& &&&&& This gene encodes a homolog to the ''E. coli'' alkB gene product. The E. coli alkB protein is part of the adaptive response mechanism of DNA alkylation damage repair.
