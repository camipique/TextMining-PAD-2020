Caroline Bancroft House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Caroline Bancroft House''' is a historic house in Denver, Colorado. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on August 29, 1990. The house named for Caroline Bancroft, who was born there in 1900 and wrote about Colorado history from 1929 to 1968.
