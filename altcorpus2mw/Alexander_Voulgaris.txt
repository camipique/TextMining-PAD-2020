Alexander Voulgaris &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Alexander Voulgaris''' () is a Greek film director, screenwriter, composer and actor. Films Voulgaris wrote, directed and scored include ''Thread'', ''Roz'' and ''Higuita''. His composing credits include ''The Last Note'', ''Park'' and ''The Sentimentalists'', the latter of which garnered him a Hellenic Film Academy Award for "Best Music." &&&&& &&&&& Voulgaris is frequently credited as "The Boy."
