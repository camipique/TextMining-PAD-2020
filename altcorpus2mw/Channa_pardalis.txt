Channa pardalis &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Channa pardalis''''' is a species of dwarf snakehead in the genus ''Channa''. It was first described in 2016 from Khasi Hills, Meghalaya of northeastern India. Prior to its scientific description, it was known as ''Channa'' sp. "True Blue" or ''Channa'' sp. "Meghalaya" in the aquarium trade, although some claim that "Meghalaya" is another, still undescribed species. ''C. pardalis'' is closely related to ''C. bipuli'' and ''C. stewartii''. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
