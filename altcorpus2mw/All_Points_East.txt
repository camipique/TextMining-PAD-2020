All Points East &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''All Points East''' is a music festival held over two weekends once a year in London's Victoria Park, run by AEG Presents. The 10 day event comprises one festival weekend, four days of free entry and community activities known as "In the Neighbourhood" and finally three standalone headline shows in their APE Presents series. The first year of All Points East was held in 2018 with LCD Soundsystem, The xx and Björk headlining the festival weekend and Catfish and the Bottlemen, The National and Nick Cave headlining the three standalone shows the following weekend. Other supports included Yeah Yeah Yeahs, Phoenix, Glass Animals, Lorde, Sampha, Beck, Father John Misty, Blossoms, The War on Drugs (band), Future Islands and Patti Smith. &&&&& &&&&& On November 6th 2018, it was announced that The Chemical Brothers would headline the first day of All Points East 2019, with Hot Chip, Primal Scream, Spiritualized and more. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& All Points East Festival
