54	Bezdědice_(Bělá_pod_Bezdězem)
Bezdědice (Bělá pod Bezdězem) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Bezdědice''' is a village in the Mladá Boleslav District, Czech Republic. It is an administrative part of Bělá pod Bezdězem, and located about 7.9 km west of the town.
