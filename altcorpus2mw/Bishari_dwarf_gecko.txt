Bishari dwarf gecko &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Bishari dwarf gecko''' (''Tropiocolotes bisharicus'') is a species of gecko of the genus ''Tropiocolotes''. It is found in Egypt.
