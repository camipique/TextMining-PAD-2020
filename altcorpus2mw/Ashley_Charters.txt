Ashley Charters &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Ashley Deon Charters''' (born March 13, 1986) is an American former softball infielder. A three-time collegiate All-American, she played college softball at Washington and was part of the 2009 Women's College World Series championship team. &&&&& &&&&& Charters played for USSSA Pride of National Pro Fastpitch from 2011 to 2013, helping the Pride win the 2013 Cowles Cup championship before retiring from professional softball in February 2014.
