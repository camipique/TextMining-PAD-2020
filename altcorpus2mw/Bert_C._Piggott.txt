Bert C. Piggott &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Bert Cody Piggott''' (March 5, 1920 – January 10, 1999) was an American football player and coach. He served as the head football coach at North Carolina A&T University from 1957 to 1967, compiling a record of 56–39–4.
