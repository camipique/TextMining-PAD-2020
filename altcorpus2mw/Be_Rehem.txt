Be Rehem &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''BayReham''''' is a 2018 Pakistani soap drama serial directed by Abid Rahim, produced by Babar Javed and written by Samina Ejaz. The drama stars Omer Shahzad and Sumaiyya Buksh in lead roles, and was first aired on 17 July 2018 on Geo Entertainment, where it aired Monday to Friday followed by Umm-e-Haniya.
