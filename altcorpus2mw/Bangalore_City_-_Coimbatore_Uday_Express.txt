Bangalore City - Coimbatore Uday Express &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Bangalore City - Coimbatore Uday Express''' is a Double-decker AC chair train of the Indian Railways connecting in Karnataka and in Tamil Nadu. It is currently being operated with 22665/226668 train numbers on Five Days a week basis. &&&&& &&&&& Coach Composition &&&&& &&&&& Utkrisht Double Decker Air Conditioned Yatri Express will be Double-decker train trains with 40% additional passenger capacity. They will run overnight on busy route by Indian Railways with features of LED screen display to show information about stations, train speed etc. and will have announcement system as well, Vending machines for tea, coffee and milk, Bio toilets in compartments as well as CCTV cameras. &&&&& &&&&& UDAY Express has a total of 10 coaches; 3 coaches with Dining facility with seating capacity of 104 Seats each and 5 coaches without dining with seating capacity of 120 seats. The remaining two will be power cars. &&&&& &&&&& == Service== &&&&& &&&&& It averages 62 km/hr as 22665 Uday Express starts from covering 419 km in 6 hrs 45 mins & 61 km/hr as 22666 Uday Express starts from covering 419 km in 6 hrs 55 min. &&&&& &&&&& Traction &&&&& &&&&& Both trains are hauled by a WAP-7 of Royapuram or Erode Locomotive Shed on its entire journey. &&&&& &&&&& Route and halts &&&&& &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& &&&&& * Bangalore City - Chennai Central Uday Express &&&&& * Uday Express &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& &&&&& * 22665/KSR Bengaluru - Coimbatore Uday Express &&&&& * 22666/Coimbatore - KSR Bengaluru Uday Express &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
