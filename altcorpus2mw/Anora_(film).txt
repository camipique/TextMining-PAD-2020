Anora (film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Anora''''' () is an upcoming Sri Lankan Sinhala drama thriller film directed by Hector Kumarasiri and co-produced by Chris Wickramasinge, Don Premadasa, Pulasthi Bethmage, Sarath Kumara Podinilame, Priyantha Kaluarachchi, and Sunil Dissanayake for A U M Films. It stars Menaka Madhuwanthi in debut cinema acting with Mahendra Perera and W. Jayasiri in lead roles along with Wijeratne Warakagoda and Dayadeva Edirisinghe.
