Andrija Gavrilović &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Andrija Gavrilović''' (; born January 13, 1965) is a Serbian-Italian professional basketball coach. He currently works as an assistant coach for Crvena zvezda of the Basketball League of Serbia, the ABA League and the EuroCup. &&&&& &&&&& Coaching career &&&&& Gavrilović moved to Bormio, Italy in September 1992. Until 2003, he was a coach in several academy teams in the Milan area. He also coached the U16 team of Roseto Sharks and Progresso Castelmaggiore. Later, he spent three seasons as an assistant coach for Virtus Bologna. Further, he was an assistant coach for Unicaja of the Spanish ACB League. &&&&& &&&&& In 2007, he signed as an assistant coach for UNICS of the Russian Super League. Next season, he had moved to Khimki where he stayed for six seasons. &&&&& &&&&& In July 2018, Gavrilović was named an assistant coach for the Belgrade team Crvena zvezda mts. On February 2, 2019, Gavrilović led Crvena zvezda to a 89–61 win over Petrol Olimpija.
