Ararat Wind Farm &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Ararat Wind Farm''' is wind farm in western Victoria, Australia. It was officially opened on 27 June 2017 and was the third-largest wind farm in Australia at that time, with capacity to generate 240MW from 75 turbines. &&&&& &&&&& Of the 75 turbines, 70 are in the Rural City of Ararat and five are in the Shire of Northern Grampians. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
