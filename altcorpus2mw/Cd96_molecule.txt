Cd96 molecule &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''CD96 molecule''' is a protein that in humans is encoded by the CD96 gene. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Function &&&&& &&&&& The protein encoded by this gene belongs to the immunoglobulin superfamily. It is a type I membrane protein. The protein may play a role in the adhesive interactions of activated T and NK cells during the late phase of the immune response. It may also function in antigen presentation.
