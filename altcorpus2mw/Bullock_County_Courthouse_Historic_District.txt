Bullock County Courthouse Historic District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Bullock County Courthouse Historic District''' is a historic district in Union Springs, Alabama which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1976. &&&&& &&&&& It includes the '''Bullock County Courthouse''', a city hall, a Carnegie library, and a First Baptist Church among its 46 contributing buildings. &&&&& &&&&& The courthouse, built in 1871, is Second Empire in style. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
