Bowthorpe Marsh &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Bowthorpe Marsh''' is a  Local Nature Reserve in Norwich in Norfolk. It is owned and managed by Norwich City Council. &&&&& &&&&& This site adjacent to the River Yare has unimproved grassland, tall fen, a seasonal pond and drainage ditches, which have aquatic plants such as reed sweet-grass. &&&&& &&&&& There is access by a footpath from Doddermans Way. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
