Ashadeep Group of Schools &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Ashadeep Group of Schools''' is a group of private educational institutions in India that were founded in 1997. The Ashadeep Group started its first school in Surat in 1997 and currently has 7 schools located in Surat City in Gujarat state of India. Ashadeep Schools is in limelight for the last few years for their outstanding results in the Secondary School Certificate (SSC) examination for Standard 10 and the Higher Secondary (School) Certificate (HSC) examination for Standard XI-XII students taken by the Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board in Gujarat. &&&&& &&&&& On the occasion of 67th birthday of Indian Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, More than 3000 children of Ashadeep Schools united to form the face of Narendra Modi to wish him a 'Happy Birthday' through the human chain. &&&&& &&&&& List of Ashadeep Schools &&&&& Following is the list of total 7 Schools of Ashadeep Group of Schools located within the Surat, &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& School Name &&&&& &&&&& Location &&&&& &&&&& City &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Ashadeep Vidhyalay - 1 &&&&& &&&&& Simada &&&&& &&&&& Surat &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Ashadeep Vidhyalay - 2 &&&&& &&&&& Simada Gam &&&&& &&&&& Surat &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Ashadeep Vidhyalay - 3 &&&&& &&&&& Chikuwadi, Varachha &&&&& &&&&& Surat &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Ashadeep Vidhyalay - 4 &&&&& &&&&& Mota Varachha &&&&& &&&&& Surat &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Ashadeep Vidhyalay - 6 &&&&& &&&&& Kapodara  &&&&& &&&&& Surat &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Ashadeep International School &&&&& &&&&& Yogichowk &&&&& &&&&& Surat &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Ashadeep Science & Commerce Bhavan &&&&& &&&&& Simada Naka &&&&& &&&&& Surat &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& &&&&& * Official Website &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
