Abdul Akbar Chitrali &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Moulana Abdul Akbar Chitrali''' is a Pakistani politician who has been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan, since August 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Political career &&&&& He was elected to the National Assembly as a candidate of Muttahida Majlis-e-Amal (MMA) from Constituency NA-1 (Chitral) in Pakistani general election, 2018.
