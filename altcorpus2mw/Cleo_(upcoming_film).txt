Cleo (upcoming film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Cleo''''' is an upcoming Belgian drama film directed and written by Eva Cools, her first feature-length film. &&&&& &&&&& Background &&&&& Cools funded the film via crowdsourcing, with additional support from the Flemish Audiovisual Fund and Screen Brussels. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& CLEO is the story of a 17-year-old girl who survives a serious road accident. Her parents did not have that chance. After this fatal night, she gets lost in Brussels and takes refuge with Leos, a mysterious man older than her. Cleo's father was a renowned pianist, and the girl has been playing since a very young age. If Cleo likes to listen to contemporary music, it is the piano (and especially the work of Rachmaninov) that is at the heart of her reconstruction process. &&&&& &&&&& Cast &&&&& * Anna Franziska Jaeger as Cleo &&&&& * Roy Aernouts as Leos &&&&& * Natali Broods as Arianne &&&&& * Yolande Moreau as Jet &&&&& * Martha Canga Antonio as Myra &&&&& * Jan Hammenecker as De Wachter &&&&& * Lucie Debay as Jeanne &&&&& * David Murgia as Alex &&&&& * Ishaq El Akel as Bruno &&&&& &&&&& Production &&&&& Principal photography on the film began on 24 January 2018 and lasted til 9 March 2018 in Belgium. &&&&& &&&&& Accolades &&&&& Cools won the Visser-Neerlandia Prize in 2016 for the film's script. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
