Colette Sultana &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Colette Sultana''' (born 25 June 1995 in Cambridge) is an English-born professional squash player who represents Malta internationally, for example at the Commonwealth Games. As of August 2018, she was ranked number 103 in the world.
