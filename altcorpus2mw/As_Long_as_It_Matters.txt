As Long as It Matters &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''As Long as It Matters'''" is a song by the American power pop band Gin Blossoms. It was released in 1996 on A&M as the third single and as well as the fifth track from their third studio album, ''Congratulations I'm Sorry'' (1996). It is a power pop song that was written by Phillip Rhodes, Jesse Valenzuela and Robin Wilson, and produced by John Hampton and by its own band.
