Carrowcastle Wedge Tomb &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Carrowcastle Wedge Tomb''' is a wedge-shaped gallery grave and National Monument located in County Mayo, Ireland. &&&&& &&&&& Location &&&&& &&&&& Carrowcastle Wedge Tomb is located southeast of Ballina village, to the west of Slieve Gamph. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& &&&&& This wedge tomb was built c. 2500 – 2000 BC, in the Copper or Bronze Age. &&&&& &&&&& Description &&&&& &&&&& The round capstone sits above a small gallery. There is lots of cairn material and the remains of a mound visible. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
