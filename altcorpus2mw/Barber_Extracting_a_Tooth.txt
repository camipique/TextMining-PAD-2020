Barber Extracting a Tooth &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Barber Extracting a Tooth''''' is a 1630-1635 oil on panel genre painting by Adriaen van Ostade, now in the Kunsthistorisches Museum in Vienna, Austria. &&&&& &&&&& It shows a village barber pulling out a villager's tooth. Typically for the artist the scene is illuminated from a window on the left, whilst the right remains in shadow A set of scissors on the wall refers to the man's trade as a barber. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
