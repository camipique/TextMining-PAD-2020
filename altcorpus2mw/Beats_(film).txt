Beats (film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Beats''''' is an upcoming American coming-of-age-drama directed by Chris Robinson from a screenplay by Miles Orion Feldsott. The film stars Anthony Anderson, Khalil Everage, Uzo Aduba, Emayatzy Corinealdi, Paul Walter Hauser, Dave East, Ashley Jackson, Evan J. Simpson, and Dreezy. &&&&& &&&&& Premise &&&&& A musical prodigy with borderline agoraphobia collides with a former music manager who has fallen from grace.
