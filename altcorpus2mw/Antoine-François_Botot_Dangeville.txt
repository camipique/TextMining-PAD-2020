Antoine-François Botot Dangeville &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Antoine-François Botot''', known as '''Dangeville''' (26 May 1681 - c.1737) was a French dancing master, dancer and ballet teacher. &&&&& &&&&& He was born in Paris. His brother was the actor Charles Botot Dangeville. He made his début at the Académie royale de musique in 1701 and retired in 1748, although he remained a member of the Académie royale de danse until his death.
