Christine Rose (dredge) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Christine Rose''' is an self-propelled barge excavator dredge used to mine Bering Sea placer gold deposits in the region around Nome, Alaska, USA. It is owned by Pomrenke Mining and its registered home port is Nome, Alaska, USA.
