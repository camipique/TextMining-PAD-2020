Alex Thomson (rugby union) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Alex Thomson''' (born 20 February 1921) is a former Scotland international rugby union player. Thomson played as a Lock. &&&&& &&&&& Rugby Union career &&&&& &&&&& ===Amateur career=== &&&&& &&&&& Thomson played for University of St Andrews RFC. &&&&& &&&&& ===Provincial career=== &&&&& &&&&& Thomson was selected for the combined North of Scotland team to play the South on 12 November 1949. &&&&& &&&&& ===International career=== &&&&& &&&&& He was capped for Scotland just once.
