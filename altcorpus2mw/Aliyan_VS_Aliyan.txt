Aliyan VS Aliyan &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Aliyan V/S Aliyan''' is an Indian Malayalam language sitcom directed by Rajesh Thalachira which is being premiered on Amrita TV since February 17, 2017. The show airs every Monday to Friday at 9:00 PM (IST) on Amrita TV.
