Anastasiia Masiutkina &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Anastasiia Masiutkina D’Ambrosio (née '''Anastasiia Masiutkina'''; born 9 November 1985) is a Ukrainian journalist, digital influencer, and Television Presenter. She is the creator of Eatdresstravel.com, founder of the conference Bloggers Forum, and a contributing author of Elle Ukraine. &&&&& &&&&& Early life and education &&&&& Anastasiia was born in Nikopol, Ukraine, but spent most of her childhood moving between Kiev, Ukraine and Berlin, Germany for her parents work. &&&&&  &&&&& She graduated from Kiev National Linguistic University having studied journalism. &&&&&  &&&&& Career &&&&& During her youth Anastasiia was signed to a Kiev-based modeling agency, quickly getting very known and recognized in Ukraine. Upon graduation, she received a letter from the French modeling agency Metropolitan, where she was offered a contract. Anastasiia worked as a model for several years, walking shows in Paris, Tokyo and New York for designers including Givenchy and Comme des Garçons. &&&&&  &&&&& Returning to her roots, Anastasiia worked as a journalist for the Ukrainian national TV channel Novy Canal from 2011 to 2014. At the end of 2014, Anastasia created the lifestyle blog Eatdresstravel.com, allowing her to become the first Ukrainian blogger to be invited to Fashion Weeks in Milan, Paris, and New York. Through her blog, she has worked with brands such as Saint Laurent and Bulgari. Since then, Anastasiia has continued her career in journalism, writing for magazines such as Marie Claire, Vogue, L’Officiel and jetsetter.ua. She is currently a permanent contributor of Elle Ukraine. &&&&&  &&&&& As a TV presenter, Anastasiia worked in fashion TV in 2015, and hosted the Cosmopolitan Awards in 2017. &&&&&  &&&&& In 2018 Anastasia founded the conference Bloggers Forum with the scope of empowering Ukrainian bloggers to achieve more visibility at international level and creating collaboration opportunities between Ukrainian bloggers and local and international businesses. At the forum, she participated as a panelist of experts from mass-media and IT business. &&&&&  &&&&& Thanks to her annual educational project, Ukrainians have the possibility to be always updated and informed about the latest digital innovations in the fashion sphere. Additionally, Anastasiia is the promoter and supporter of many charities activity. &&&&&  &&&&& In September 2018, Anastasiia launched her new luxury handbag collection that she designed herself. &&&&& &&&&& Awards &&&&& In 2017, Anastasiia received the Elle Style Award from Elle Ukraine as “style influencer”. &&&&&  &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Anastasia married businessman Teodoro D’Ambrosio on 25 July 2015 at Lake Como. The couple currently lives in Lugano, Switzerland, where she often travels into Milan for work. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
