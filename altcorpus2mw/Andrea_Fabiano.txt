Andrea Fabiano &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Andrea Fabiano''' (born May 17, 1976 in Bari) is an Italian company executive. He is the Director of Rai 2 since October 12, 2017. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& He began his career at Rai in 1999 as a marketer. In 2015 he was appointed Deputy Director of Rai 1, and then became director in 2016, appointed by the director general of Rai Antonio Campo Dall'Orto.
