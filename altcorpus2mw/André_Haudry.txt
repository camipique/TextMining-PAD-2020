André Haudry &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Haudry by an unknown artist &&&&& '''André Haudry''', seigneur de Soucy (hamlet of Fontenay-lès-Briis), Fontenay, Janvry, de Segrez and other places (7 November 1688 - 30 December 1769) was an 18th century French fermier général. Born in Corbeil-Essonnes, he died in his Paris home in 1769 and was buried in the chapel of his château de Soucy on 1 January 1770. His body was moved to the communal cemetery in Fontenay-lès-Briis by decision of its town council in 1857. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& category:1688 births &&&&& category:1769 deaths &&&&& &&&&&
