Bengisu Erçetin &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Bengisu Erçetin''' (born 1 January 2001) is a Turkish badminton player. She was crowned as the girls' doubles champion at the 2016 European U15 Badminton Championships in Kazan, Russia partnered with Zehra Erdem, and also won the bronze medal in the singles event. Teamed-up with Nazlıcan İnci, they grabbed the silver medal in U17 Championships in 2016, and made it to the gold medal in 2017 with Erdem. She won her first senior international tournament at the 2017 Turkey International tournament.
