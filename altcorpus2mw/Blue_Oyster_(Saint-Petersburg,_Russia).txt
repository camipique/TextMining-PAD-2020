Blue Oyster (Saint-Petersburg, Russia) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Blue Oyster''' () together with '''Priscilla''', which is directly connected to it, is a gay bar and night club in Saint-Petersburg, Russia. The name alludes to both to fictional gay bar from Police Academy series of comedy films and to Russian translation of the word "blue" – "голубой", which means gay in Russian. The bar is open daily until the morning, the entrance is always free for men but may be restricted for women and there is a tight face control.
