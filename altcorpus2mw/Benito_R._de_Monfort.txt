Benito R. de Monfort &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Benito Raimundo Monfort Blanch''', known in French as '''Benito R. de Monfort''' (1800-1871) was a Spanish photographer, publisher and businessman. He is most notable as the co-founder of both the Société héliographique and the casino de Biarritz.
