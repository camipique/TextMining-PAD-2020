118	Ámr
Ámr &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& ''''' Ámr '''''(Old Norse and translates to "black", "loathsome") is the seventh studio album by Norwegian progressive metal artist Ihsahn. The album was released on May 4, 2018 through Candlelight Records.
