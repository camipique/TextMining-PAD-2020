Bagan, Bagansky District, Novosibirsk Oblast &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Bagan''' () is a selo and centre of Bagansky District, Novosibirsk Oblast, Russia. &&&&& &&&&& It has a population of 5510 (as data from the 2010 census). &&&&& &&&&& It lies 450 km South-West from Novosibirsk at the Southern part of Baraba Plain, on the bank of the river with the same name..
