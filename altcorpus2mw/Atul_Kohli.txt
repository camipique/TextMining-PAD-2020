Atul Kohli &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Atul Kohli''' is a professor of politics and international affairs at Woodrow Wilson School of Public and International Affairs, Princeton University. Kohli was promoted to full professor in 1991, and was also appointed as David K.E. Bruce Professor of International Affairs at Princeton in 2002. Kohli is also the Editor in Chief of the journal of World Politics. In addition, he had served as the Vice President of the American Political Science Association in 2009 to 2010.
