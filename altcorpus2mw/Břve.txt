61	Břve
Břve &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& Břevský rybník &&&&& '''Břve''' is a small village, which is a part of the town of Hostivice (Central Bohemian Region, Czech Republic). It lies on a cadastral area of Litovice. There is a pond called ''Břevský rybník''. &&&&& &&&&& Originally it was a separate village.
