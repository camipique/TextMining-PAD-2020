Anthony Richardson (boxer) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Earl Anthony Richardson''' (born February 7, 1947 in Anguilla) is a retired light-middleweight boxer from the Netherlands, who represented his country at the 1972 Summer Olympics. There he was eliminated in the third round of the men's light-middleweight (– 71 kg) division by Loucif Hamani from Algeria.
