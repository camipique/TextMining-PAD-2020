Beck Dorey-Stein &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Beck Dorey-Stein''' (born June 11, 1986) was a stenographer in the White House from 2012 to 2017 under U.S. President Barack Obama. She is the author of the non-fiction book ''From the Corner of the Oval'' which recounts her years providing stenography services to the President.
