Amalija Pihajlić &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Amalija Pihajlić''' (born 4 July 1944) is a Serbian chess player who hold the FIDE title of Woman International Master (1977). She is a two-time winner of the Yugoslav Women's Chess Championship (1973, 1977). &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& In the 1980s Amalija Pihajlić was one of the leading Yugoslav women's chess players. She twice won the Yugoslav Women's Championship in 1973 and 1977. In 1977, she was awarded the FIDE International Women Master (WIM) title.
