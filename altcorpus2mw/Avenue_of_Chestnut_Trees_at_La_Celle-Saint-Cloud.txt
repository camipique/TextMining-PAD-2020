Avenue of Chestnut Trees at La Celle-Saint-Cloud &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& thumb &&&&& '''''Avenue of Chestnut Trees at La Celle-Saint-Cloud''''' (French - ''Allée de châtaigniers à La Celle-Saint-Cloud'') or '''''Edge of the Fontainebleau Forest''''' (''Lisière de la forêt de Fontainebleau'') is an 1865 pre-Impressionist painting by Alfred Sisley, produced in the woods at La Celle-Saint-Cloud. It was refused by the Paris Salon of 1867 and bought by Jean-Baptiste Faure in 1877. It was acquired in 1919 by Joseph Duveen, who in 1921 gave it to the Petit Palais, where it still hangs. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& category:Landscape paintings &&&&& category:Paintings by Alfred Sisley &&&&& category:1865 paintings &&&&& &&&&&
