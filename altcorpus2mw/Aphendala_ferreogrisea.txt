Aphendala ferreogrisea &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Aphendala ferreogrisea''''' is a moth of the family Limacodidae first described by Edward Meyrick in 1913. It is found in Sri Lanka, and Australia. &&&&& &&&&& Forewings dark grayish brown. A dentate medial fasciae found in the forewing.
