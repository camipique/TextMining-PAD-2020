Alejandro de los Santos &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Alejandro Nicolás de los Santos''' (17 May 1902 — 16 February 1982) was an Argentinian footballer who played as a forward for San Lorenzo, Dock Sud, El Porvenir, Huracán and Argentina. &&&&& &&&&& Early life &&&&& De los Santos was born in Paraná on 17 May 1902 to Angolan Argentine parents. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& In 1921, de los Santos signed for San Lorenzo. On 22 May 1921, de los Santos made his debut for the club in a 2–0 win against Banfield. After spells at Dock Sud and El Porvenir, de los Santos signed for Huracán in 1931, scoring 21 goals in 73 league appearances, making a total of 25 goals in 88 appearances in all competitions. &&&&& &&&&& In December 1925, de los Santos won the South American Championship with Argentina. De los Santos was the first black footballer to appear for Argentina. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
