Barnabas Long &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Barnabas Long''' or '''Longe''' was Archdeacon of Cleveland from 1683 to 1685. &&&&& &&&&& Long was born in Pontefract was educated at University College, Oxford. He became Vicar of Selborne in 1680; Chancellor of Llandaff in 1681, Canon of York in 1682; Rector of Fyfield in 1684, and of Oddington that same year. &&&&& &&&&& He died on the 11 April, 1685. &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
