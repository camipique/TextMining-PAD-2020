Arriya Yenyueak &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Arriya Yenyueak''' (born 1 August 2000) is a Thai cricketer. In July 2018, she was named in Thailand's squad for the 2018 ICC Women's World Twenty20 Qualifier tournament.
