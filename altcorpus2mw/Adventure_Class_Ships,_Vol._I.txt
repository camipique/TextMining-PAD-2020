Adventure Class Ships, Vol. I &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Adventure Class Ships, Vol. I''''' is a 1981 role-playing game supplement for ''Traveller'' published by FASA. &&&&& &&&&& Contents &&&&& ''Adventure Class Ships, Vol. I'' is a set of 15mm scale deck plans with sheets for ten different Imperial, Zhodani and independent vessels, with an accompanying identification booklet of ship stats and descriptions, plus smaller scale plans of four auxiliary vessels. &&&&& &&&&& Reception &&&&& William A. Barton reviewed ''Adventure Class Ships, Vol. I'' in ''The Space Gamer'' No. 49. Barton commented that "Overall, ''Adventure Class Ships, Vol. I'' proves an excellent play aid for ''Traveller'' and is recommended fully." &&&&& &&&&& Reviews &&&&& * ''Different Worlds'' #19 (Feb., 1982) &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
