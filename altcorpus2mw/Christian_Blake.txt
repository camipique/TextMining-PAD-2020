Christian Blake &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Christian Blake''' (born June 8, 1996) is an American football wide receiver for the Atlanta Falcons of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Northern Illinois. &&&&& &&&&& Professional career &&&&& Blake signed with the Atlanta Falcons as an undrafted free agent on May 1, 2018. He was waived on September 1, 2018. He was re-signed to the practice squad on September 10, 2018. He was released on September 26, 2018. He was re-signed to the practice squad on December 18, 2018.
