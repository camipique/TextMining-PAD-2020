Abiromorphus &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Abiromorphus''''' is a genus of leaf beetles in the subfamily Eumolpinae that are found in eastern Asia. It was first described by the French entomologist Maurice Pic in 1924 for a single species from China.
