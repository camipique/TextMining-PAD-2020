Anton Ivanovich Is Angry &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Anton Ivanovich Is Angry''''' () is a 1941 Soviet comedy film directed by Aleksandr Ivanovsky. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& The film tells about the musician Anton Ivanovich, who performs exclusively the bach and his daughter, who studies music in the conservatory. She is in love with a young composer, composing operettas, which categorically do not like Anton Ivanovich.
