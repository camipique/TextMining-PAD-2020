Camponay Falls &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Camponay Falls''' is located in Camponay Village in barangay Tabontabon, Sibagat, Agusan del Sur in the southern Philippine island of Mindanao. It is 2.5 km away from the junction of Eureka, Tabon-tabon and National Highway of Sibagat, Agusan del Sur. A cave can be found in the middle of the waterfalls within a secondary forests which is a potential for local outdoor and recreation site. Listed as one of the attractive landmarks in the Province of Agusan del Sur. &&&&& &&&&& ''Camponay'' Falls name is derived from the name of its sitio "Camponay" a place where a military camp was established in early years. &&&&& &&&&& It entails around an hour of trekking inorder to get to the scenic destination of Camponay Waterfalls and Cave. &&&&& Geography &&&&& Camponay Waterfalls and Cave is located in Sitio Camponay, Barangay Tabontabon, Sibagat, Agusan del Sur. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * Sibagat, Agusan del Sur &&&&& * Agusan del Sur &&&&& * Caraga Region &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
