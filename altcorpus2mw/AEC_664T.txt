AEC 664T &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''AEC 664T''' was a three-axle double deck trolleybus chassis manufactured by AEC between 1933 and 1942. Based on the AEC Renown bus chassis, 796 were built for British operators including 660 for the London Passenger Transport Board for whom it was primarily developed. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
