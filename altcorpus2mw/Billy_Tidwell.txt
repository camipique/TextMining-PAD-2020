Billy Tidwell &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Billy Reyne Tidwell''' (August 3, 1930 – December 19, 1990) was American football player and coach. Tidwell was drafted in the third round of the 1952 NFL Draft by the San Francisco 49ers.
