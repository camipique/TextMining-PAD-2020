Cerro Plataforma Formation &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Cerro Plataforma Formation''' is a sedimentary formation cropping out at Cerro Plataforma south of Puelo Lake in the Andes of Argentine Patagonia. The formation contains marine fossils of bivalves, gastropods and echinoderms. &&&&&  &&&&& To the southwest in Chile lies the geologically equivalent La Cascada Formation.  &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Further reading &&&&& * Orts, D.
