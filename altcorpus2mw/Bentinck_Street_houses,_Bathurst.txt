Bentinck Street houses, Bathurst &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Bentinck Street houses''' are two separately heritage-listed semi-detached houses at 67 and 71 Bentinck Street, Bathurst, Bathurst Region, New South Wales, Australia. The houses are privately-owned.
