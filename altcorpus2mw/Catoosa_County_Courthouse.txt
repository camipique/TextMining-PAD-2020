Catoosa County Courthouse &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Catoosa County Courthouse''', at 7694 Nashville St. in Ringgold, Georgia, was built in 1939. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2006. &&&&& &&&&& It was designed by architects Crutchfield and Law in Colonial Revival style. &&&&& &&&&& It is a two-story, brick building with a pediment and cupola and a hipped roof. It has a central block with three bays and flanking symmetrical wings, each with three bays. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
