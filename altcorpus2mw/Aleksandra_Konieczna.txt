Aleksandra Konieczna &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Aleksandra Konieczna, 2016 &&&&& '''Aleksandra Konieczna''' is a Polish actress. She is a two-time Polish Film Award winner for her roles in ''The Last Family'' (2016) and ''Jak pies z kotem'' (2018).
