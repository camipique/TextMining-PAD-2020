Aurelia Reinhardt Alumnae Memorial House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Aurelia Reinhardt Alumnae Memorial House''' is a building on the campus of Mills College in Oakland, California. It was designed by architect Clarence W. Mayhew, and named after college president Aurelia Henry Reinhardt. It was completed in 1949.
