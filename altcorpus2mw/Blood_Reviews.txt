Blood Reviews &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Blood Reviews''''' is a quarterly peer-reviewed medical journal that publishes review articles in the field of hematology. It was established in 1987 and is published by Elsevier. The editors-in-chief are Hillard Lazarus (University Hospitals Case Medical Center) and Drew Provan (Royal London Hospital).
