Broome Heath &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Broome Heath''' is a  Local Nature Reserve in Ditchingham in Norfolk. It is owned by South Norfolk District Council and managed by the Broads Authority. An area in the north is designated a geological Site of Special Scientific Interest as Broome Heath Pit, and there is a Scheduled Monument in the middle. &&&&& &&&&& This site in the valley of the River Waveney has marshy grazing land and lakes. At the southern end there is a Neolithic settlement, and in the middle there are long and round barrows. &&&&& &&&&& There is access from Loddon Road. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
