Church Bay, Anglesey &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Holy Island and Holyhead Mountain. &&&&& &&&&& '''Church Bay''' is a settlement located on the north west coast of Anglesey, Wales, United Kingdom. &&&&& &&&&& The village is very small, with most of the properties there being holiday homes for rent. Amenities include a cafe and a small museum based in a preserved 17th century thatched cottage. The nearby sandy beach is named Porth Swtan ''(Port Whiting)''.
