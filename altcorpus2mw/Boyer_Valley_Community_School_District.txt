Boyer Valley Community School District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Boyer Valley Community School District''' ('''BVCSD''') is a school district headquartered in Dunlap, Iowa. &&&&& &&&&& The district occupies sections of Crawford, Harrison, Monona, and Shelby counties. The district serves Dunlap, Arion, and Dow City. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& It was formed on July 1, 1994 by a merger of the Dunlap and Dow City-Arion school districts. &&&&& &&&&& Circa 1997 the Boyer Valley district and the East Monona Community School District began a grade-sharing program. On September 9, 2003, the residents of the Boyer Valley and East Monona districts voted on a plan to consolidate the districts. Even though Boyer Valley residents voted in favor on a 385 to 30 basis, the East Monona residents voted it down on a 313 to 178 basis. &&&&& &&&&& On July 1, 2004 the East Monona district was dissolved, with portions going to several districts.
