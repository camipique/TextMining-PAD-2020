After impressing in Vindbjart he joined Start ahead of the 2017 season. He then made his Eliteserien debut in April 2018 against Vålerenga. &&&&& &&&&& However, when first-choice goalkeeper Håkon Opdal sustained an injury, Boujar proceeded to concede six goals against Vålerenga.
