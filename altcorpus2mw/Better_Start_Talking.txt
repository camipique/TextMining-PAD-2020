Better Start Talking &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''Better Start Talking'''" is a song by American singer Donell Jones. It was written by Jones along with Ryan Leslie and Sean Garrett for his fourth studio album ''Journey of a Gemini'' (2006), while production was helmed by Leslie and Garrett.
