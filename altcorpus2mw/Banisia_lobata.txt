Banisia lobata &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Banisia lobata''''' is a moth of the family Thyrididae first described by Moore in 1882. It is found in India, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, China, Hong Kong and Brunei. &&&&& &&&&& Both wings are fawn colored. Several small dark spots are found on both wings. About three white spots can be found near the cell of the forewings. &&&&& &&&&& Two subspecies are recognized. &&&&& *''Banisia lobata caesia'' Whalley, 1976 &&&&& *''Banisia lobata ceylonensis'' Whalley, 1976 &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
