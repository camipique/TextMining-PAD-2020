Annunciation (El Greco, Museo Thyssen-Bornemisza) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Annunciation''''' is a 1575–1576 oil on canvas painting of the Annunciation by El Greco, one of the earliest paintings by the artist.
