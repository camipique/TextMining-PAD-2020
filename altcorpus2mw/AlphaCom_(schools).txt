51	AlphaCom_(schools)
AlphaCom (schools) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''AlphaCom''' is an operator of for-profit virtual schools based in Carmel, Indiana. The schools, including Indiana Virtual School, have been criticized for low graduation rates and high student-teacher ratios.
