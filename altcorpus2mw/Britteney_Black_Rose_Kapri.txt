Britteney Black Rose Kapri &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Britteney Black Rose Kapri''' is a Chicago-based author, educator, activist and poet, performer, and playwright. She has been published in ''Poetry'', ''Button Poetry'', and ''Seven Scribes'' and anthologized in ''The BreakBeat Poets'' and ''The BreakBeat Poets Vol. 2: Black Girl Magic''. Kapri has written two chapbooks:''Winona and Winthrop'' (New School Poetics, 2014) and ''Black Queer Hoe'' (Haymarker Books, 2018 ). She was a winner of the 2015 Rona Jaffe Foundation Writer's Award. &&&&& &&&&& Education &&&&& Kapri is a Grand Valley State University alumna &&&&& &&&&& ''Black Queer Hoe'' &&&&& ''Black Queer Hoe'' discusses black women's sexuality and sexual liberation. Kapri included Tweets in this collection.
