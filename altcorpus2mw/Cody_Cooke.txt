Cody Cooke &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Cody Cooke''' (born 2 March 1993) is an English professional footballer who plays for Scottish club St Mirren, as a striker. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& Born in Truro, Cooke began his career in English non-league football with Truro City. After spending time with the V9 Academy, Cooke turned professional in June 2018, after signing for Scottish club St Mirren alongside Josh Heaton.
