121	Callipta
Callipta &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Callipta''''' is a genus of leaf beetles in the subfamily Eumolpinae. It is known from Asia and Africa. This genus was originally named ''Calliope'' by Julius Weise in 1882; however, as Édouard Lefèvre pointed out, the name "Calliope" had been used a number of times for genera described previously (for instance, the passerine bird genus ''Calliope'' Gould, 1836), so it was renamed to ''Callipta'' by Lefèvre in 1885. &&&&& &&&&& Species &&&&& * ''Callipta borealis'' Lopatin, 1976 &&&&& * ''Callipta fausti'' (Weise, 1882) &&&&& ** ''Callipta fausti badghyza'' Lopatin, 1997 &&&&& ** ''Callipta fausti balchana'' Lopatin, 1997 &&&&& ** ''Callipta fausti fausti'' (Weise, 1882) &&&&& ** ''Callipta fausti murgabica'' Lopatin, 1997 &&&&& ** ''Callipta fausti palvanica'' Lopatin, 1997 &&&&& ** ''Callipta fausti turkomana'' Medvedev, 1957 &&&&& * ''Callipta iranica'' Lopatin, 1997 &&&&& * ''Callipta oberthuri'' (Fairmaire, 1876) &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
