Bob Henshaw &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Robert Cullen Henshaw''' (September 15, 1918 – May 29, 2011) was an American professional basketball player. He played for the Youngstown Bears in the National Basketball League for three total games during the 1945–46 season and averaged 1.0 point per game.
