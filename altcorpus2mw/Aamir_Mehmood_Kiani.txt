Aamir Mehmood Kiani &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Aamir Mehmood Kiani''' () is a Pakistani politician who served Federal Minister for National Health Services, Regulation and Coordination, from 20 August 2018 to 18 April 2019. He has been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan since August 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Political career &&&&& He was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan as a candidate of Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) from Constituency NA-61 (Rawalpindi-V) in Pakistani general election, 2018. He received 105,000 votes and defeated Malik Ibrar Ahmed. &&&&& &&&&& On 18 August, Imran Khan formally announced his federal cabinet structure and Kiani was named as Minister for National Health Services, Regulations and Coordination. On 20 August 2018, he was sworn in as Federal Minister for National Health Services, Regulations and Coordination in the federal cabinet of Prime Minister Imran Khan. On 18 April 2019, in mega reshuffle, Prime Minister Imran Khan ousted him. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
