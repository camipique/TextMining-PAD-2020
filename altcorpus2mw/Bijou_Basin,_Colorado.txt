Bijou Basin was five miles southeast of Sidney in northeastern El Paso County &&&&& '''Bijou Basin''' was a settlement in northern El Paso County, Colorado near Elbert County, Colorado, where there was a train station. Travelers could also take post road 49. It was located five miles southeast of another early settlement, Sidney. &&&&& &&&&& A rural post office was established at Bijou Basin in 1869. Settlers established sheep and cattle ranches.
