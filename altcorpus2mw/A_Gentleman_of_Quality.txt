A Gentleman of Quality &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''A Gentleman of Quality''''' is a 1919 American silent drama film directed by James Young and starring Earle Williams, Kathryn Adams, Joyce Moore, James Carpenter, and Robert Bolder. It is based on the 1909 novel of the same name by Frederick Van Rensselaer Dey.
