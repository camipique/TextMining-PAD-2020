Arnout Schuijff &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Arnout Schuijff''' (born 1967/1968) is a Dutch billionaire Internet entrepreneur, and the co-founder and chief technology officer (CTO) of Adyen. &&&&&  &&&&& From 1997 to 2004, Schuijff was systems architect at Bibit Global Payment Services. &&&&& &&&&& In 2006, Schuijff co-founded Adyen. &&&&& &&&&& In June 2018, he became a billionaire after Adyen's IPO, based on his 6.4% stake in the company. Co-founder and CEO Pieter van der Does has a 4.8% stake worth $800 million, and his brother, Joost Schuijff, has a stake worth $500 million. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
