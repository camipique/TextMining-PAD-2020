Ankolali &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Ankolali''' is a village / panchayat located in the Gir Gadhada Taluka of Gir Somnath district in Gujarat State, India. Earlier, until August 2013, Ankolali was part of Una Taluka and Junagadh district. The latitude 20.866356 and longitude 70.933183 are the geo-coordinate of the Village Ankolali. Gandhinagar is the state capital of Ankolali village which is located around 400 kilometres away from Ankolali. &&&&& &&&&& According to Census 2011, with the 176 families, the population of this village is 1056. Out of this, 532 are males and 524 are females. Most residents are dependent on agriculture. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Demographics &&&&& &&&&& According to the 2011 census of India, Ankolali has 176 households.
