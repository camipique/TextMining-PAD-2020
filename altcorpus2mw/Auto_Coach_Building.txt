Auto Coach Building &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Auto Coach Building''', at 1730-34 Oak St. in Kansas City, Missouri, was built in two phases in 1917 and 1926. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2007. &&&&& &&&&& It is a three-story, brick and stone, two part commercial block, in plan. &&&&& &&&&& It was part of Kansas City's "Automobile Row" which, in the early 1900s, included auto dealerships, parts suppliers, rubber tire manufacturers, and other automotive related firms. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
