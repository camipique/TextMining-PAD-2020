A Night on the Danube &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''A Night on the Danube''''' (German: '''''Eine Nacht an der Donau''''') is 1935 German comedy film directed by Carl Boese and starring Olga Engl, Wolfgang Liebeneiner and Gustav Waldau. &&&&& &&&&& The film's sets were designed by the art directors Emil Hasler and Arthur Schwarz.
