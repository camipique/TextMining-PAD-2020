Anastasios Douvikas &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Anastasios "Tasos" Douvikas''' (; born 2 August 1999) is a Greek football player who plays as a forward for the Super League club Asteras Tripolis. &&&&& &&&&& Club career &&&&& ===Asteras Tripolis=== &&&&& Douvikas made his professional debut on 19 August 2017, in a 2–1 home loss against PAS Giannina, coming in as a substitute. On 26 October 2017, he scored his first professional goal in a 2–1 home win against AO Chania−Kissamikos, for the Greek Cup. On 26 November 2017, he scored in a 2–0 home win against Levadiakos, thus contributing to the team's third successive win.
