Beitou Park &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Beitou Park''' () is a park in Beitou District, Taipei, Taiwan. &&&&& &&&&& Geography &&&&& The park spans over an area of 6 hectares and located along the Beitou River. &&&&& &&&&& Architecture &&&&& The park features the Beitou Hot Spring Museum and Taipei Public Library Beitou Branch. It also has five small waterfalls with hot springs. &&&&& &&&&& Transportation &&&&& The park is accessible within walking distance east of Xinbeitou Station of Taipei Metro. &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * List of parks in Taiwan &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
