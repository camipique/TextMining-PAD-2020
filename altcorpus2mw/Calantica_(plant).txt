Calantica (plant) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Calantica''''' is a genus of flowering plants in the family Salicaceae. It contains 10 species of shrubs and trees endemic to Madagascar, seven of which are threatened. ''Calantica'' is closely related to the pantropical and diverse genus ''Homalium'', from which it differs in having a superior ovary, instead of a semi-inferior ovary.
