Bailey Rice &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Bailey Rice''' (born 10 February 1997) is an Australian rules footballer who plays for the St Kilda Football Club in the Australian Football League (AFL). He was selected by St Kilda at pick #49 in the 2015 national draft as a father-son selection, with St Kilda matching a bid from Richmond. Carlton had put in a father-son bid for Rice who had nominated St Kilda as his preferred club.
