51	Altacreodus
Altacreodus &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Altacreodus''''' is a genus of basal, placental eutherians. Fossils have been found in North America, where they first appeared during the Late Cretaceous; they died out prior to the start of the Paleocene. &&&&& &&&&& The type species is "''Cimolestes''" ''magnus'' but was renamed to '''''Altacreodus magnus''''' in 2015. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
