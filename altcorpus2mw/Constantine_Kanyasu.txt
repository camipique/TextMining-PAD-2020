Constantine Kanyasu &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Constantine John Kanyasu''' (born October 17, 1969) is a Tanzanian politician and a member of the Chama Cha Mapinduzi political party. He was elected MP representing Geita in 2015. On November 10, 2018 he was appointed by President John Magufuli and on November 13, 2018 he was sworn in as Deputy Prime Minister for Natural Resources and Tourism. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
