Conor Loftus &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Conor Loftus''' is a Gaelic footballer for the Mayo senior team and also plays club football for Crossmolina Deel Rovers. He replaced Andy Moran as a substitute in the 2017 All-Ireland Senior Football Championship Final.
