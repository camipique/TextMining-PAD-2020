Andreas Nærstad &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Andreas Nærstad''' (5 March 1920 – 25 September 1983) was a Norwegian sports administrator. &&&&& &&&&& With a background in ski jumping, he served as president of the Norwegian Ski Federation from 1970 to 1974. He also chaired the football department of Strømmen IF.
