Autoba olivacea &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Autoba olivacea''''', the '''brinjal leaf roller''', is a moth of the family Erebidae. The species was first described by Francis Walker in 1858. It is found in several African countries such as Botswana, Eritrea, Madagascar, Malawi, Mozambique, Nigeria, South Africa, Tanzania, Uganda and Zimbabwe.
