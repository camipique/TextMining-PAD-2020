Caroline & Friends &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Caroline & Friends''''' is an American video clip game show hosted by Caroline Rhea. It premiered July 16, 2018 on Game Show Network. &&&&& &&&&& Premise &&&&& Hosted by Caroline Rhea, ''Caroline & Friends'' is a family-friendly comedy series that showcases comic amateur internet videos. Guest stars include other comedians and comic actors, who choose their favorite videos. A live audience votes to determine the best video from each episode.
