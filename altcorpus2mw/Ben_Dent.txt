Ben Dent &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Ben Dent''' is a German rugby league footballer who plays for the Hewarth ARLC in the National Conference League competition. He primarily plays as a er and has previously played for the York City Knights. He plays for Germany internationally. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& Dent made his debut season for the York City Knights in 2012, playing 5 games. In his 79 appearances for the Knights, he scored 37 tries. At the end of the 2017 season, it was announced that Dent had signed with the Newcastle Thunder. &&&&& &&&&& Dent made his international debut for Germany in 2016 against the Wales Dragonhearts, scoring a try.
