Cecilia Margaret Nairn &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Cecilia Margaret Nairn''' (1791 — 4 June 1857) was an Irish wax modeller and painter. She specialised in landscape watercolor paintings. &&&&& &&&&& She was a pupil of her father John Henry Campbell (1757–1829), himself a painter. She married Irish horse painter George Nairn (1799 — 25 January 1850), and their daughter was Anna Langley Nairn (1844–1848), also a painter. &&&&& &&&&& Nairn's paintings were exhibited at the Royal Hibernian Academy from 1826 to 1851. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
