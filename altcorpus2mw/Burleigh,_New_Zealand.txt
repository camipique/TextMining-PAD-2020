Burleigh, New Zealand &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Burleigh''' is a suburb in inner Blenheim, in the Marlborough region of the South Island of New Zealand. &&&&& &&&&& Ōmaka Marae is located in Burleigh. It is a ''marae'' (meeting ground) for the Tarakaipa ''hapū'' (sub-tribe) of Ngāti Apa ki te Rā Tō and includes Te Aroha o te Waipounamu ''wharenui'' (meeting house). &&&&& &&&&& Notes &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
