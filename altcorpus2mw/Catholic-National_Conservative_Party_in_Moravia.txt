Catholic-National Conservative Party in Moravia &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Mořic Hruban &&&&& &&&&& The '''Catholic-National Conservative Party in Moravia''' (), was a Czech catholic political party in Moravia. Party was founded by Mořic Hruban, who before cooperated with the Old Czech Party. Party operated in close relationship as a counterpart of the Catholic-National Conservative Party in Bohemia. On political congress on 26 January 1919 in Brno, party merged into newly established Czechoslovak People's Party. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
