Alessandro Momesso &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Alessandro "Alex" Momesso''' is a former Italian-Canadian soccer player. He was a standout player that most notably played for the Montréal Italia / Cantalia FC franchise in the 1950s. &&&&& &&&&& From 1951 to 1958, Momesso played for Italia / Cantalia, who played in Montréal League and the National Soccer League of Ontario/Québec. In 1951, he helped Italia reach the semi-final stage of Canadian final.
