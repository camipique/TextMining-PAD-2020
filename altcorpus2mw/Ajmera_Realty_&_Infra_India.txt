Ajmera Realty & Infra India &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Ajmera Realty & Infra India''' (, ) is a listed company based in Mumbai, India. It is one of India's largest privately held real estate developers. Its market capitalization is approimately $450 million. Its core businesses are realty, construction, cement, steel rolls and construction-related businesses. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& Founded in 1985 as '''Percolated Steels''', it is a flagship company of the Ajmera group. The name was changed to Ajmera Realty & Infra India in 2009. Ajmera Realty & Infra India is listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange () and the National Stock Exchange (). &&&&& &&&&& The group entered the solar power generation market in 2012 with subsidiaries named Ajmera Biofuel and Contra Power. Ajmera Biofuel acquired a 49% stake in the Solar project at Osiyan, in the Jodhpur district of Rajasthan. &&&&& &&&&& Ajmera Realty jointly with the Mayfair group has been a major real estate developer in the Baharin Bay project at Manama. &&&&& &&&&& The Ajmera group diversified into the manufacture of seamless capsules. &&&&& &&&&& Ajmera Realty group was also in the news when they jointly with Bakeri group purchased Ahmedabad's Calico Mills property at a government auction for Rs. 350 crores in 2010. &&&&& &&&&& Rajnikant Ajmera, heading the group was elected as the President of CREDAI (Confederation of Real Estate Developers Association of India) in April 2007. He served as President of Maharashtra Chamber of Housing Industry (MCHI).
