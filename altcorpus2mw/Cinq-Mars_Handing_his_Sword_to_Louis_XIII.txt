Cinq-Mars Handing his Sword to Louis XIII &&&&& Introduction &&&&& 300px &&&&& '''''Cinq-Mars Handing his Sword to Louis XIII''''' (French - ''Cinq-Mars rendant son épée à Louis XIII'') is a c.1836-1837 painting by Claudius Jacquand. It is now held at the Royal Monastery of Brou, though it also appeared in the 2014 exhibition ''L'invention du passé.
