Chaffee County Courthouse and Jail Buildings &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Chaffee County Courthouse and Jail Buildings''', at 501 E. Main St. in Buena Vista, Colorado, were listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1979. &&&&& &&&&& The courthouse is a two-story five-bay brick building, with brick laid in stretcher bond, on an ashlar foundation. It was built after the county seat was switched from Salida, Colorado. &&&&& &&&&& The jail is a smaller building. Both were built in 1882. &&&&& &&&&& A different Chaffee County Courthouse, built in Salida in 1929 was designed by architect Walter DeMourdant, after the county voted to move the courthouse back to Salida. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
