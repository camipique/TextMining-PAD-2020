Ángel Medardo Luzuriaga &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Ángel Medardo Luzuriaga''' (14 September 1935 – 19 June 2018) was an Ecuadorian musical artist, precursor of the Andean cumbia. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Medardo was born in Loja on 14 September 1935, and in his music he extolled the beauty of the Ecuadorian territory, especially that of the province of Manabí. On 11 September 1967, he founded the orchestra "Don Medardo y sus Players", a group with which he had a record production of 105 albums.
