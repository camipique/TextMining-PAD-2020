Caldwell Building &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Caldwell Building''', at 1001 Noble St. in Anniston, Alabama, is a historic building built in Italianate style in 1889. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1982. &&&&& &&&&& It is a three-story building with red brick and russet colored stone trim.
