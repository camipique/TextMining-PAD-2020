Akhil Katyal &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Akhil Katyal''' is an Indian poet and queer activist. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& &&&&& Katyal has published two poetry books - ''How Many Countries Does the Indus Cross'' and ''Night Charge Extra''. During Fall 2016, he was the International Writing Fellow at the University of Iowa. In 2018, he translated Ravish Kumar's book of Hindi poems ''Ishq Mein Shahar Hona'' as ''A City Happens in Love''. &&&&& &&&&& Katyal is from Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh and teaches creative writing at Ambedkar University Delhi. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
