Agony in the Garden (El Greco, London) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Agony in the Garden''''' is a 1590 oil on canvas painting by El Greco or his studio, dating to his second stay in Toledo and still showing the major influence of Titian on his work. It is now in the National Gallery, London. &&&&& &&&&& An angel appears to Christ in the left foreground, holding a chalice in his hand. In the right-hand background Judas and a group of soldiers approach to arrest Christ, marching through a dry landscape without vegetation. &&&&& &&&&& Bibliography &&&&& * ÁLVAREZ LOPERA, José, ''El Greco'', Madrid, Arlanza, 2005, Biblioteca «Descubrir el Arte», (colección «Grandes maestros»). . &&&&& * SCHOLZ-HÄNSEL, Michael, ''El Greco'', Colonia, Taschen, 2003. . &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& *  &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& category:Paintings by El Greco &&&&& category:Collections of the National Gallery, London &&&&& category:1590 paintings &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
