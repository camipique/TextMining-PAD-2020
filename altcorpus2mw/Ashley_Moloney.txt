Ashley Moloney &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Ashley Moloney''' (born 13 March 2000) is an Australian decathlete who won the Gold medal at the 2018 World Junior Championships in Tampere. In achieving this result, Ashley broke the Competition Record, previously held by Niklas Kaul, and Oceania Junior Record, previously held by Cedric Dubler.
