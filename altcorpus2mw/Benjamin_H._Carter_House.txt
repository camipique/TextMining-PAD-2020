Benjamin H. Carter House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Benjamin H. Carter House''' is a historic house in Quitman, Mississippi. It was built in 1911-1912 by George Carson for Benjamin Harvey Carter, the sheriff of Clarke County. Carter served as the president of the Bank of Quitman from 1931 to 1961. &&&&& &&&&& The house was designed in the Colonial Revival architectural style.
