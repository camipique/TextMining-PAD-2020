Catherine Allgor &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Catherine Allgor''' (born 26 November 1958) is an American historian and bestselling author. She specializes in biographies of American First Ladies. &&&&& &&&&& Life &&&&& Allgor was born on 26 November 1958. She completed her PhD in history at Yale University. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& Allgor was professor of history at UC Riverside. She has also taught at Claremont McKenna College, Harvard University, and Simmons College. She currently serves as President of the Massachusetts Historical Society. Allgor has also served as the Nadine and Robert Skotheim Director of Education at the Huntington Library. &&&&& &&&&& Awards &&&&& Allgor has received numerous awards: &&&&& &&&&& * James H.
