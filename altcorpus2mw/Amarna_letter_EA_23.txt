(high-resolution expandable photo) &&&&& &&&&& '''Amarna letter EA 23''', titled: ''"A Goddess Travels to Egypt"'' is a short letter to Pharaoh from Tushratta. Due to the ill health of Pharaoh, a statue of Goddess Šauška is being sent to Egypt, to aid in the health of Pharaoh. &&&&& &&&&& The letter &&&&& &&&&& ===EA 23: ''"A Goddess Travels to Egypt"''=== &&&&& EA 23, letter seven of thirteen from Tushratta.
