Bernardo Paz &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Bernardo Paz''' is a former mining businessman and creator of Inhotim, an outdoor art complex and botanical garden in Brazil that was once home to his own personal rural estate and art collection.
