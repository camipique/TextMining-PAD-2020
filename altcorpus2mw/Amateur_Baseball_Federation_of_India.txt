Amateur Baseball Federation of India &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Amateur Baseball Federation of India (ABFI)''' is the governing body for baseball in India. The ABFI was founded on 11 December 1983 in Rohtak, Haryana, and became a member of the Baseball Federation of Asia (BFA) and the International Baseball Federation in 1985.
