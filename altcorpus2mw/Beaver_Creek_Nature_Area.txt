Beaver Creek Nature Area &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Beaver Creek Nature Area''' is a nature area in Minnehaha County, South Dakota in the United States. It is located along Beaver Creek, a tributary of the Big Sioux River just east of Sioux Falls. Birdwatching, fishing, hiking, and snowmobiling are popular activities within this area.
