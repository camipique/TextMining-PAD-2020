Bigg Boss Kannada (season 6) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Bigg Boss Kannada 6''''' ('''''BBK6''''') will be the sixth season of the Kannada television series Bigg Boss Kannada, which is scheduled to go on air from 21 October 2018 at 6p.m. Sudeep will reprise his role as the host of the show. The auditions for commoners was launched through the Voot platform in July 2018. The show will be telecasted every day on the Colors Super and Colors kannada HD channels at 8p.m. IST. &&&&& &&&&& Housemates &&&&& Along with the usual celebrity contestants, the housemates of this season includes contestants selected via an online audition process. The total of eighteen housemates includes nine celebrities and nine commoners. (The following list of contestants is according to the contestants entering the house.)
