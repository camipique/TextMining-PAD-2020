268	Cantillation_(ensemble)
Cantillation (ensemble) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Cantillation''' is an Australian vocal ensemble founded in 2001 by Antony Walker and Alison Johnston. They were founded alongside orchestras Sinfonia Australis and Orchestra of the Antipodes. &&&&& &&&&& Cantillation often performs with the Pinchgut Opera and appear on many ABC Classics albums.
