Clistopyga crassicaudata &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Clistopyga crassicaudata''''' is a species of ichneumon wasp in the family Ichneumonidae which was first described in 2018. It is found in Peru and was one of seven new species found in a narrow zone of vegetation between the Amazon rainforest and the Andes.
