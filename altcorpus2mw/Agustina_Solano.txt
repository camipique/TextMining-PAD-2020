Agustina Solano &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Agustina Solano García Tejera''' (born 5 April 1995) is a Chilean field hockey player. &&&&& &&&&& Solano mad her international debut for Chile senior team at the 2016-17 Hockey World League Semifinal in Johannesburg, South Africa. &&&&& &&&&& Solano made her junior debut at the 2016 Pan-Am Junior Championship, where the team won a bronze medal. From this tournament, the team qualified for the 2016 Junior World Cup where Solano also represented Chile. &&&&& &&&&& Solano was part of the Chile team at the 2017 Pan American Cup.
