Adoration of the Magi (Artemisia Gentileschi) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Adoration of the Magi''''' is a 1636–1637 painting by Artemisia Gentileschi. Along with ''Saint Januarius in the Amphitheatre at Pozzuoli'' and ''Saints Proculus and Nicea'', it was commissioned by Martín de León Cárdenas, bishop of Pozzuoli for Pozzuoli Cathedral. ''Adoration'' was held in the Museo di San Martino in Naples for around fifty years for conservation before being returning to its original position in the Cathedral in May 2014. &&&&& &&&&& Bibliography &&&&& *Angelo D'Ambrosio - Storia di Pozzuoli... in pillole.
