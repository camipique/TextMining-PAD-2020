Christopher Wyvill (priest) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Christopher Wyvill,''' D.D. was an eminent Anglican priest in the first half of the 18th century. &&&&& &&&&& The seventh son of Sir Christopher Wyvill, 3rd Baronet, M.P. for Richmond, he was educated at Trinity College, Cambridge. He was ordained in 1678. He was a Canon of York from 1700 until his death in January 1710. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
