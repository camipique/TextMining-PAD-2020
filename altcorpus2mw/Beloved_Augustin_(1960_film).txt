Beloved Augustin (1960 film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Beloved Augustin''''' (German: '''''Der liebe Augustin''''') is a 1960 West German historical comedy film directed by Rolf Thiele and starring Matthias Fuchs, Ina Duscha and Veronika Bayer. It is not a remake of the 1940 film of the same title. &&&&& &&&&& The film's sets were designed by the art directors Arno Richter and Felix Smetana. It was shot in agfacolor with location filming taking place in Austria and Bavaria.
