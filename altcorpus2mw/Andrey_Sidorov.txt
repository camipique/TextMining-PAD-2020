Andrey Sidorov &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Andrey Sidorov''' (born 30 March 1995) is an Uzbekistani footballer who plays as a midfielder. He currently plays for Uzbekistan Super League team Qizilqum Zarafshon. &&&&& &&&&& He made his debut for the Uzbekistan national football team in May 2018, against Iran in a friendly.
