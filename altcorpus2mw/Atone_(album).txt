Atone (album) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Atone''' is the second studio album by the progressive pop group White Moth Black Butterfly, made up of Daniel Tompkins of TesseracT, Keshav Dhar of Skyharbor, Randy Slaugh, Jordan Bethany and Mac Christensen. It was released through Kscope Records on 1 September 2017.
