Caloocan Sports Complex &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Caloocan Sports Complex''' is an indoor sporting arena located in Caloocan, Philippines. &&&&& &&&&& Built and operated by the Caloocan City Government, the coliseum has played host to the basketball games of the ASEAN Basketball League (ABL), the Philippine Super Liga, and the Maharlika Pilipinas Basketball League Home of The Caloocan Supremos.
