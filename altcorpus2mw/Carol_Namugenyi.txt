Carol Namugenyi &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Carol Namugenyi''' (born 27 September 1990) is a Ugandan cricketer. In July 2018, she was named in Uganda's squad for the 2018 ICC Women's World Twenty20 Qualifier tournament.
