Austin and Northwestern Railroad Historic District-Fairland to Llano &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Austin and Northwestern Railroad Historic District-Fairland to Llano''' is a historic district in Burnet County, Texas and Llano County, Texas. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1997. The listing included four contributing buildings, 43 contributing structures, and three contributing sites. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& It runs roughly along railroad tracks from Fairland to Llano, and is located roughly near Kingsland, Texas, and is also known as '''Southern Pacific Railroad--Fairland to Llano branch'''. &&&&& &&&&& It includes at least one hotel. &&&&& &&&&& It includes a Recorded Texas Historic Landmark. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
