Ace Stream &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Ace Stream''' is a peer-to-peer multimedia streaming protocol, built using BitTorrent technology. Ace Stream has been recognized by sources as a potential method for broadcasting and viewing bootlegged live video streams. The protocol functions as both a client and a server. When users stream a video feed using Ace Stream, they are simultaneously downloading from peers and uploading the same video to other peers. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& Ace Stream began under the name TorrentStream as a pilot project to use BitTorrent technology to stream live video.
