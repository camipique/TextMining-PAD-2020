A Nun Cares for a Soldier in a Cloister &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''A Nun Cares for a Soldier in a Cloister''''' is an 1822 painting by Claudius Jacquand, which has been in the Museum of Fine Arts of Lyon since 2015. It is one of his earliest surviving works.
