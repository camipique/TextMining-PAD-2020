Anelise Chen &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Anelise Chen''' is an American writer of fiction and nonfiction. Her first novel, ''So Many Olympic Exertions,'' appeared in 2017 and was named one of the best books of the year by Brooklyn Rail. She holds degrees from UC Berkeley (B.A. English) and New York University (MFA Fiction). Her essays and reviews have appeared in the New York Times, National Public Radio, BOMB Magazine, The New Republic, VICE, and The Village Voice.
