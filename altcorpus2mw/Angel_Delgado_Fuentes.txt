Angel Delgado Fuentes &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Angel Delgado''' is a Cuban visual artist who lives in Long Beach, California. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Delgado studied at the Elementary School of Visual Arts of Havana, between 1976 and 1980.
