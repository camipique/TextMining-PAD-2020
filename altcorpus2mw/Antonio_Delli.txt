Antonio Delli &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Antonio Delli Rocioli Ferreira''' better known as '''Antonio Delli''' is a Venezuelan actor of television, theater, films, announcer and dubbing. Born in Caracas, Venezuela. Delli ventured into television in 2006 in the Venezuelan telenovela ''Ciudad Bendita''. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Delli began doing theater at the same time of studying administration in the Universidad Católica Andrés Bello.
