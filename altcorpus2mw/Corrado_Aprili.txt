Corrado Aprili &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Corrado Aprili''' (born 13 November 1964) is a former professional tennis player from Italy. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Aprili, a right-handed player from Verona, played on the professional circuit in the 1980s. He made the quarter-finals of a Grand Prix event in Palermo in 1986, with wins over Henrik Sundstrom and Richard Matuszewski, to reach his career best ranking of 108. In 1987 he defeated the top seeded Andrés Gómez at a tournament in Florence.
