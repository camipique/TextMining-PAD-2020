Blackburn Fork River &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Blackburn Fork River''' is a river located in Jackson County, Tennessee. It originates near Cookeville, Tennessee, and is a tributary of the Roaring River. It contains the seventy-five foot high waterfall, Cummins Falls, and runs within Cummins Falls State Park.
