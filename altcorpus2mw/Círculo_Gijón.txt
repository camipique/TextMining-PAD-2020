Círculo Gijón &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Círculo Gijón Baloncesto y Conocimiento''' (), also known as '''TeslaCard Círculo Gijón''' for sponsorship reasons, is a professional basketball club based in Gijón, Asturias that currently plays in LEB Plata, the third tier of Spanish basketball. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& Match against Iraurgi SB in the 2018–19 LEB Plata season. &&&&& Círculo Gijón Baloncesto y Conocimiento was founded in 2017, by a group of entrepreneurs that acquired the rights of local club ''CD Barrio La Arena'', with the aim of giving to the city of Gijón a completely professional basketball team with possibilities to promote to the top leagues, as did the former main team Gijón Baloncesto, that played in Liga ACB during four seasons. &&&&& &&&&& The club started in the Primera División, fifth tier, and played its first official game on 19 October 2017 in Avilés, beating Colegio San Fernando by 65–72. On 12 February 2018, Círculo Gijón signed former NBA player Robert Swift for two seasons. &&&&& &&&&& However, Círculo Gijón did not achieve promotion to Liga EBA after being defeated in the league semifinals by the reserve team of Oviedo CB.
