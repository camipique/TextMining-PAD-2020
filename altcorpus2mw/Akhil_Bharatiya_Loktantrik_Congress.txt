Akhil Bharatiya Loktantrik Congress &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Akhil Bharatiya Loktantrik Congress''' was a regional political party in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. It was founded in October 1997 by Jagdambika Pal along with Naresh Agarwal, Atul Kumar Singh, Bachha Pathak, Rajeev Shukla and Shyam Sunder Sharma. The party was form when these leaders broke away from the Indian National Congress to join the All India Indira Congress (Tiwari), led by N. D. Kalyan Singh moved Allahabad High Court which termed the dismissal of government unconstitutional on 23 February 1998, thereby reinstating the Kalyan Singh government.
