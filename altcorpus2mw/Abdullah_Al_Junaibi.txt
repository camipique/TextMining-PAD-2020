Abdullah Al Junaibi &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Abdullah Al Junaibi''' (Arabic:عبد الله الجنيبي) (born 25 July 1970) is a Emirati director and actor in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. &&&&& &&&&& Films &&&&& ''Camera'' is an Emirati psychological thriller. It is the horrific story of 3 friends who found a camera buried in the sands of a desert. The movie was produced by FAM Films and premiered on 5 of April 2018. Al Junaibi won best director at the 14th Dubai International Film Festival in 2017. &&&&& &&&&& His other films are ''On Borrowed Time'', 2018, ''Bahar'', year 2013, ''The Path'' (short), 2012, ''Bent Alhabel'' (2013'' and ''Film When'' (2002). &&&&& &&&&& Al Junaibi was the TV director at the Sharjah Satellite Channel and the Dubai Media Foundation (SAMA Dubai); major anchor of Arabian Television program and series (Operetta, Vimto Ads Series, and Series Ads Old age, etc.).
