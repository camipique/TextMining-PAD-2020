Blue Frog Tavern &&&&& Introduction &&&&& right &&&&& '''''Blue Frog Tavern''''' is a 1981 role-playing game adventure for ''Tunnels & Trolls'' published by Flying Buffalo. &&&&& &&&&& Plot summary &&&&& ''Blue Frog Tavern'' is an adventure for a single ''T&T'' character involving a stop at a tavern. &&&&& &&&&& Reception &&&&& Harry White reviewed ''Blue Frog Tavern'' in ''The Space Gamer'' No. 50. White commented that "Some encounters route you to earlier solitaire dungeons; if you don't have them, buy or die! Other encounters smack of the sudden death paragraphs in former efforts from FBI. But come on in and have a beer, anyway, if you are a solitaire T&T freak." &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&&  &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
