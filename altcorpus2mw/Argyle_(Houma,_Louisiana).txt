68	Argyle_(Houma,_Louisiana)
Argyle (Houma, Louisiana) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Argyle''' is a historic house on a former sugarcane plantation in Houma, Louisiana. It was built circa 1906 for Phelin Bonvillain, a sugar planter. It belongs to the Ingram family since 1947. &&&&& &&&&& The house was designed in the Eastlake architectural style, with "a few Colonial Revival features." It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since July 1, 1994. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 	 &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
