Cynthia Johnston &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Cynthia Johnston''' (born 11 October 1968) is a Canadian basketball player. She competed in the women's tournament at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta. She attended Bishop's University in Quebec where she was named four time Bishop's University Female Athlete of the Year as well as two time conference most valuable player.
