Chy Nwakanma &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Chy Nwakanma''' (born December 19, 1990) is a Nigerian actress. &&&&& &&&&& Early life &&&&& Chy Nwakanma is from Aba in Abia State. She was born in Abia state by Nigerians, whose names are Eric Acho Nwakanma and Ezinwanyi Joy Nwakanma. She attended Living Word Academy, Aba for her elementary education. After which she attended Command Secondary school in Abakiliki for a year before moving to the United States at age 11. While in Chicago, she attended Gwendolyn Brooks Middle School, in Oak Park Illinois (2003–2005). She also attended Buffalo Grove High School, Arlington Heights, Illinois (2005–2009).
