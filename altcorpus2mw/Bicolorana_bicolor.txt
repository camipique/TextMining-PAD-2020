Bicolorana bicolor &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Bicolorana bicolor''''' &&&&& is a species of bush cricket in the subfamily Tettigoniinae and tribe Platycleidini: found in mainland Western Europe. Individuals are normally brachypterous, but long-winged forms may be encountered. &&&&& &&&&& Databases currently (June 2018) disagree on the preferred placement of this and the synonym ''Metrioptera bicolor''.
