Budge Budge railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Budge Budge railway station''' is a Kolkata Suburban Railway Station on the Sealdah–Budge Budge line with an approximate distance from Sealdah railway station. It is under the jurisdiction of Eastern Railway zone. Budge Budge railway station is one of the busiest railway stations of Sealdah railway division. More than 45 pairs of E.M.U. Local Trains ply through the railway station on a daily basis. It is situated in South 24 Parganas district in the Indian State of West Bengal.
