93	Cyclotriol
Cyclotriol &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Cyclotriol''' (developmental code name '''ZK-136295'''; also known as '''14α,17α-ethanoestriol''') is a synthetic estrogen which was studied in the 1990s and was never marketed. It is a derivative of estriol with a bridge between the C14α and C17α positions. The drug has 40% of the relative binding affinity of estradiol for the human ERα.
