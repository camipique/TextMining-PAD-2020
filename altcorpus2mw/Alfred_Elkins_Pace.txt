Alfred Elkins Pace &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Alfred Elkins Pace''' (June 29, 1811 - January 20, 1868) was a Texas House member from late 1849 to late 1850. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Life &&&&& Pace was born on June 29, 1811 to Twitty Pace and Susannah Duncan. He is the oldest of 8 children. Not much information is known about his life from his birth until his marriage. He married Elizabeth Saphronia Hardaway on May 25, 1851 and had 5 children together between 1852-1860. He later died on January 20, 1868 at the age of 56. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
