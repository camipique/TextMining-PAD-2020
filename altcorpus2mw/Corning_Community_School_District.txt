Corning Community School District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Corning Community School District''' is a school district headquartered in Corning, Iowa. Along with the Villisca Community School District, it has the branding "Southwest Valley Schools". &&&&& &&&&& The district is in parts of Adams and Taylor counties, and serves Corning and Carbon. &&&&& &&&&& It operates Corning Elementary School and Southwest Valley High School in Corning. The district sends its students to Southwest Valley Middle School, operated by Villisca SD. The two districts also share superintendents. the shared superintendent is Willie Stone. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The district previously operated Corning High School. &&&&& &&&&& On July 1, 2008 the district absorbed portions of the former New Market Community School District. &&&&& &&&&& By 2011 the process of establishing a grade-sharing agreement with Villisca was under way.
