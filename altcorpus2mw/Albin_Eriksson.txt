Albin Eriksson &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Albin Eriksson''' (born 20 July 2000) is a Swedish ice hockey winger who is currently playing with the Skellefteå AIK organization. Eriksson was selected in the second round, 44th overall, of the 2018 NHL Entry Draft by the Dallas Stars. &&&&& &&&&& Eriksson made his Swedish Hockey League debut playing with Skellefteå AIK during
