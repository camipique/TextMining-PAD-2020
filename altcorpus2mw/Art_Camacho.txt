Art Camacho &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Art Camacho''' is an award winning film director, producer, actor and stuntman. His directorial work includes ''Recoil'' in 1998, ''13 Dead Men'' in 2003, ''Confessions of a Pit Fighter'' in 2005 and ''Half Past Dead 2'' in 2007. His acting work includes ''Chinatown Connection'' in 1990, ''The Power Within'' in 1995'', Tiger Heart'' in 1996 and ''Little Bigfoot 2: The Journey Home''. &&&&& &&&&& Background &&&&& Camacho started out as an actor and did the ruin of assorted television programs and action films He later started producing and directing commercials before moving into directing, choreographing and producing action films. By 2015, he had worked in at least 35 action films as either stuntman or choreographer. One of the films he has directed is ''Recoil'' which is from PM Entertainment, the company responsible for many of the direct to video action films in the 1990s to early 2000s.
