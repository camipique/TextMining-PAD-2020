Cambodia Airways &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Cambodia Airways Co., Ltd.''', operating as '''Cambodia Airways''' is a full-service airline based in Phnom Penh of Cambodia. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& Cambodia Airways Co. Ltd with a registered capital of 200 million USD, was founded in Phnom Penh. &&&&& &&&&& Cambodia Airways obtained the approval documents from the Cambodian government in August 2017 and completed the business registration in September of the same year. On the 6th July 2018, Cambodia Airways officially obtained the AOC certificate with IATA code KR and ICAO code KME.
