Baiju Bhatt &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Baiju Prafulkumar Bhatt''' (born 1984/1985) is an American billionaire entrepreneur, and the co-founder (with Vladimir Tenev) of Robinhood, a US-based financial services company. &&&&& &&&&& Early life &&&&& Baiju Prafulkumar Bhatt, the son of Indian immigrants, earned a bachelor's degree in physics in 2007 and a master's in mathematics in 2008, both from Stanford University, where he met Vladimir Tenev. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& In 2013, Bhatt co-founded the trading platform Robinhood with Tenev. The idea stemmed from witnessing the issues in the financial industry during the time of Occupy Wall Street. &&&&& &&&&& Following a funding run in May 2018 which increased Robinhood's valuation to $6 billion, Bhatt and Tenev became billionaires. &&&&& &&&&& Awards and recognition &&&&& Bhatt spoke at Disrupt SF 2018 on investing; it was at this event where he announced plans for reaching IPO in 2019. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
