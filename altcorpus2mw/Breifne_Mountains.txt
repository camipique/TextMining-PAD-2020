Breifne Mountains &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Breifne Mountains''' (), is a range of hills and mountains in counties Cavan, Fermanagh and Leitrim, Ireland. They are within a region historically known as Bréifne. The range has two sub-ranges: the southern '''Iron Mountains''' that span across Leitrim and Cavan, and the northern '''Cuilcagh Mountains''' that straddle Cavan and Fermanagh.
