Arjun Patiala &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Arjun Patiala''''' is an upcoming Indian romantic cop spoof comedy film directed by Rohit Jugraj. Co-produced by Bhushan Kumar and Dinesh Vijan under T-Series and Maddock Films, it stars Diljit Dosanjh, Kriti Sanon and Varun Sharma. &&&&& &&&&& The film is a character-driven comedy with  Sanon playing a crime journalist and Dosanjh playing a quirky, small town cop. The film is scheduled to be released on 19 July 2019. &&&&& &&&&& Cast &&&&& *Diljit Dosanjh as Arjun Patiala &&&&& *Kriti Sanon as Ritu Randhawa &&&&& *Varun Sharma as Onida Singh &&&&& *Ronit Roy as Amarjeet Singh Gill &&&&& *Sunny Leone (Cameo) &&&&& &&&&& Production &&&&& ===Development and filming=== &&&&& Shooting for the film started in February 2018 and ended in July 2018. It was shot in Chandigarh and some areas of Punjab. The story is written by Ritesh Shah. &&&&& &&&&& Marketing and release &&&&& The film was supposed to release on 13 September 2018 but was postponed to 3 May 2019 and then to 19 July 2019. &&&&& &&&&& Dinesh Vijan of Maddock Films has stated that the film won't be released in Pakistan due to the Pulwama Attack that happened in Kashmir, India. &&&&& &&&&& Soundtrack &&&&& Sachin-Jigar have composed music for the film. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * &&&&& * ''Arjun Patiala'' on Bollywood Hungama &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
