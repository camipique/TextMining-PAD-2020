Chen Xiaojia (badminton) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Chen Xiaojia''' (; born 20 October 1991) is a retired Chinese badminton player from Xiangtan, Hunan. She was selected to join the national second team in 2008. Chen was the gold medalist at the 2009 Asian Junior Championships and the bronze medalist at the World Junior Championships, She represented China at the 2009 East Asian Games, and claimed the gold medal in the women's team event, and a bronze medal in the women's singles.
