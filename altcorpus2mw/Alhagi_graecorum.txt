Alhagi graecorum &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Alhagi graecorum''''' is a species of legume commonly known as '''mannatree''' or '''manna tree'''. Previously it was considered a subspecies of ''Alhagi maurorum''. Drought-tolerant, it is found in the eastern Mediterranean and the Middle East. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
