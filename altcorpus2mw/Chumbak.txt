181	Chumbak
Chumbak &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Chumbak''''' (also designated as '''''Chumbak - THE LOTTERY''''') is a 2017 Indian Marathi-language slice of life drama film directed by Sandeep Modi, produced by Aruna Bhatia, Naren Kumar and Cape of Good Films and stars a renowned lyricist Swanand Kirkire in the lead role. It was released on 13 October 2017 in MAMI 19th Mumbai Film Festival. &&&&& &&&&& Later, the film attracted attention of Akshay Kumar who found it "honest and pure", announcing on social media, and also went on to ''present'' it, making his Marathi cinema debut as a presenter. It is slated to be re-released on 27 July 2018. Trailer for the film was released on 5 July 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& The plot revolves around friendship of a restaurant worker boy – Baalu – and a simple mentally-ill middle-aged village man – Prasanna (Swanand Kirkire) – who are thrown together due to circumstances.
