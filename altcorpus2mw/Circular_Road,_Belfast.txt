Circular Road, Belfast &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Circular Road''' is a cricket ground in Belfast, Northern Ireland. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The home ground of the C.I.Y.M.S. Cricket Club, Circular Road has held one major cricket match, when Northern Knights played North West Warriors in a Twenty20 match as part of the 2017 Inter-Provincial Trophy.
