Her brother was also a writer. She worked for the insurance company Hansa from 1935 to 1951. Falk published her first novel ''Fruntimmer'' in 1944. She later translated children's literature from Danish and Norwegian into Swedish. &&&&& &&&&& Falk won a competition sponsored by publisher Wahlström & Widstrand in 1950. She won the in 1953 and the Astrid Lindgren Prize in 1968. &&&&& &&&&& She was married twice: first to Jan Falk, a journalist, in 1935 and then to the writer in 1948.
