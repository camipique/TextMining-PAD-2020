Catholic People's Party (Austria-Hungary) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& The '''Catholic People's Party''' () was a conservative party in Austria-Hungary formed in 1895. Party leaders were Alfred Ebenhoch and Joseph von Dipauli. &&&&& &&&&& The party won 23 seats in the 1901 election. It joined the Christian Social Party in 1907. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
