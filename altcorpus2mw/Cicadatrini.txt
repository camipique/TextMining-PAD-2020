145	Cicadatrini
Cicadatrini &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Cicadatrini''' is a tribe of cicadas in the family Cicadidae. There are about 12 genera and at least 120 described species in Cicadatrini. &&&&& &&&&& Members of Cicadatrini are found in the Palearctic and Indomalaya.
