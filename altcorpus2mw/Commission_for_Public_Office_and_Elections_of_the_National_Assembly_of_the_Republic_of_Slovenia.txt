Responsibilities &&&&& Commission: &&&&& &&&&& * confirms mandates of the MPs &&&&& * discusses issues relating to the immunity of deputies, judges of the Constitutional Court, judges and the rights of Members and the Secretary-General, &&&&& * address the issues regarding election, appointment and dismissal, when required by law, &&&&& * propose to the National Assembly in the appointment
