Constitution of the Hunan Province &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Constitution of the Hunan Province''' was promulgated on January 1, 1922. Thirteen authors of drafted the document, including Wang Zhengting. Zhao Hengti became the first Governor of Hunan under the constitution. It was the only document of its kind drafted by a Provincial government in China during the time. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
