74	Aflatoxin_B1_exo-8,9-epoxide
Aflatoxin B1 exo-8,9-epoxide &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Aflatoxin B1 exo-8,9-epoxide''' is a toxic metabolite of aflatoxin B1. It's formed by the action of cytochrome P450 enzymes in the liver. &&&&& &&&&& In the liver, aflatoxin B1 is metabolized to aflatoxin B1 exo-8,9-epoxide by the cytochrome P450 enzymes. The resulting epoxide can react with guanine in the DNA to cause DNA damage. &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& *Aflatoxin B1 &&&&& *Cytochrome P450 &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
