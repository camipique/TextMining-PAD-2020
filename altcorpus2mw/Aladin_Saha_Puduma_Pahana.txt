Aladin Saha Puduma Pahana &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Aladin Saha Puduma Pahana''''' () is an upcoming Sri Lankan Sinhala fantasy thriller film directed by Sumith Kumara and produced by Prasad Wijesuriya. It stars Dhananjaya Siriwardane and Udari Warnakulasuriya in lead roles along with Nethu Priyangika and D.B. Gangodathenna. Music composed by Ruvin Diaz.
