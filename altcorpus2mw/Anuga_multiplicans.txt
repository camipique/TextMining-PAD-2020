Anuga multiplicans &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Anuga multiplicans''''' is a moth of the family Noctuidae first described by Francis Walker in 1858. It is found in India, Sri Lanka, Hong Kong, Korea, Taiwan, Philippines and Borneo. &&&&& &&&&& Forewings darker and with subtornal orange spot. Discal spot on hindwing. Hindwing tornus is orange. &&&&& &&&&& Subspecies &&&&& Two subspecies are recognized.
