Ann O'Dea &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Ann O'Dea''' is an Irish Businesswoman who is the co-founder and CEO of Silicon Republic and the CEO of Inspirefest. &&&&& &&&&& O'Dea co-founded the Silicon Republic online newspaper for science, business and technology with Darren McAuliffe in 2001. She is a champion of diversity in science, technology, engineering and mathematics and set up the Inspirefest conference to address inclusion at technology conferences. &&&&& &&&&& She was awarded a fellowship of the Irish Computer Society for her work promoting women in the technology sector.
