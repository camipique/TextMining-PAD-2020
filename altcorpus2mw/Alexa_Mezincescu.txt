Alexa Mezincescu &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Alexa Mezincescu''' (b. 1936) is a Romanian ballet dancer and choreographer. &&&&& &&&&& Mezincescu trained at the Bucharest Choreography School, and was subsequently sent to train in Leningrad. She was a principal soloist with the Romanian National Opera, where she was noted for her interpretations of Bruckner. &&&&& &&&&& She was granted the National Order of Faithful Service by Ion Iliescu. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
