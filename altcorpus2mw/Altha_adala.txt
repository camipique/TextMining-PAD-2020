Altha adala &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Altha adala''''' is a moth of the family Limacodidae first described by Moore in 1859. It is found in Sri Lanka, India, Thailand, Myanmar, Vietnam, Java, Bali, Borneo, and Sumatra. &&&&& &&&&& Forewings with dull orange shade with a central dark brown dot. The caterpillar has a greenish-white body which is ovate and without tubercles. &&&&& &&&&& Larval food plants include ''Annona'', ''Bauhinia'', ''Coffea'', ''Mangifera'', ''Eugenia'', ''Rosa'', ''Psidium guajava'' and ''Mangifera indica''. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
