All of Us (EP) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''All of Us''''' is the debut extended play by American girl group June's Diary. It is their first original release after appearing on the BET docs-series Chasing Destiny. The EP was released on June 30, 2018 through the independent label Music of Sound. Lyrically, the extended play discusses themes of female empowerment, heartbreak and love.
