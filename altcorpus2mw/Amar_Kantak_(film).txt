Amar Kantak (film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Amar Kantak''''' is a Bengali drama film directed by Sukhen Das and produced by Mala Gupta. It was released in 1986 in the banner of Parna Chitram. This movie received BFJA award in 1987. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& Writer Debesh goes from place to place searching for his younger brother Binu. Binu is missing for a long time. Debesh goes to an Ashram in Amarkantak and meets a lady monk named ''Mataji''. He realises that Mataji is hiding something.
