Bedford Community School District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Bedford Community School District''' is a school district headquartered in Bedford, Iowa. &&&&& &&&&& It is mostly in Taylor County with portions in Ringgold and Page counties. Communities served include Bedford, Blockton, Conway, Gravity, and a portion of New Market. &&&&& &&&&& On July 1, 2008 the district absorbed portions of the former New Market Community School District.
