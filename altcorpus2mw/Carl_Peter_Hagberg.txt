Carl Peter Hagberg &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Carl Peter Hagberg''' (22 November 1778 – 15 September 1841) was a Swedish minister and orator who served in the Swedish Academy. &&&&& &&&&& Hagberg became master of philosophy in 1803. The same year he was ordained as a minister and subsequently served as a preacher at the court, from 1808 with Queen dowager Sophia Magdalena. In 1809 he became the head of the theological seminary at Lund University. The same year he was awarded with a degree of doctor of theology. In 1811 he became professor of pastoral theology.
