Christopher Columbus Before the Council of Salamanca &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Christopher Columbus Before the Council of Salamanca''''' is an 1841 painting by the German-American artist Emanuel Leutze, now in the Louvre in Paris. It was lent to the Musée des beaux-arts de Lyon in 2014 for its exhibition ''L'invention du Passé.
