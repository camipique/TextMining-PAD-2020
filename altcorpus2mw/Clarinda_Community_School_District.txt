Clarinda Community School District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Clarinda Community School District''' is a school district headquartered in Clarinda, Iowa. The district, in sections of Page and Taylor counties, serves Clarinda, Hepburn, Yorktown, and sections of New Market. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& On July 1, 2008, the New Market Community School District was dissolved; portions went to the Clarinda district. &&&&& &&&&& Schools &&&&& * Clarinda High School &&&&& * Clarinda Middle School &&&&& * Garfield Elementary School &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * Clarinda Community School District &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
