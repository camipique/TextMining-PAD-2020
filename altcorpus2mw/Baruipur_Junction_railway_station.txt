Baruipur Junction railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Baruipur Junction railway station''' is a Kolkata Suburban Railway Junction Station on the Sealdah–Namkhana line and Sealdah–Diamond Harbour line with an approximate distance from Sealdah railway station. It is under the jurisdiction of Eastern Railway zone. Baruipur Junction railway station is one of the busiest railway stations of Sealdah railway division. More than 80 pairs of E.M.U. Local Trains ply through the railway station on a daily basis. It is situated in South 24 Parganas district in the Indian State of West Bengal.
