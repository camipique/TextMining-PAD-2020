Clitennestra &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Clitennestra''''' is an opera in a prelude and two acts, was the last work by Ildebrando Pizzetti. The libretto is in Italian language. The opera play is based from ancient Greek mythology written by Aeschylus and Sophocles.
