Bathinda–Rajpura line &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Rajpura-Bathinda line''' is a railway line connecting Rajpura in the Indian state of the Punjab and Bathinda in Punjab. The line is under the administrative jurisdiction of Northern Railway. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&  &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&  &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Main Stations &&&&&  Rajpura Junction railway station  &&&&&  Patiala &&&&&  Nabha &&&&&  Dhuri Junction railway station  &&&&&  Barnala &&&&&  Tapa &&&&&  Rampura phul &&&&&  Lehra mahabat &&&&&  Bhuchchu &&&&&  Bathinda Junction railway station &&&&& &&&&& ==History and key developments on the route== &&&&& It is said that Maharaja of patiala built the railway station in the perpendicular orientation as he had hoped that one day the Ambala-Ludhiana would be diverted via Patiala, but that never happened. One reason was the trains would have to pay the taxes levied by the Pepsu state. &&&&& &&&&& In Feb 2015, Doubling of track between Rajpura-Patiala-Bathinda was sanctioned. &&&&& &&&&& In 2016, electrification of Single Broad Gauge railway line from Rajpura to Dhuri junction was under progress which is being executed by Rail Vikas Nigam Ltd. &&&&& &&&&& Patiala Railway Station on the line is also one of the 400 stations to be redeveloped under Govt of India's AMRUT scheme. In 2016, the work for the platform-1 extension is in progress which shall be extended to another 200 meter to accommodate longest possible train. &&&&& &&&&& In March 2017, work for doubling of Rajpura-Patiala-Bathinda line was partly given to Northern railway construction but later all was allotted and interested to rail vikas nigam limited. As it is also a feeder route for Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor (EDFC) it has capacity utilisation to the peak. And the doubling is scheduled to start for December 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Electrification &&&&& The rail link has been approved by the rail broad,for electrification and doubling till Lehra mahabat railway station as the rest till bhathinda junction is doubled and electrified. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
