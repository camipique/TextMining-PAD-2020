Cadia, New South Wales &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Cadia''' is a locality in the Cabonne Shire of New South Wales, Australia. It was formerly a private township established 1860s by the Cadiangullong Consolidated Copper company. It is now part of the Cadia-Ridgeway Mine lease, operated by Newcrest Mining.
