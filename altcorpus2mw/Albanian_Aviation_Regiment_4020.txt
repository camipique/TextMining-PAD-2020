Albanian Aviation Regiment 4020 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& The '''Aviation Regiment 4020''' was the bomber regiment of the Albanian People's Air Force. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& Aviation Regiment 4020 operated one Il-28 acquired in 1957 attached to ''2 Skuadrilja'' (2nd Squadron). This aircraft was traded for an H-5, the Chinese version of the Soviet Il-28, in 1971 and retired from service in 1992. &&&&& &&&&& The Aviation Regiment 4020 was Europe's last operator of the Harbin H-5.
