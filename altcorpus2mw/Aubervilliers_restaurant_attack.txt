Aubervilliers restaurant attack &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& On 11 June 2017, a Molotov cocktail was thrown in a restaurant in Aubervilliers, a suburb of northern Paris in France. The attack injured 12 people, including six policemen, and caused a large blaze of the five-storey block the restaurant was part of. It happened on the day of French parliamentary elections. &&&&& &&&&& Three people were airlifted to hospital suffering severe burns. The arson attack has been linked to a "robbery gone wrong".
