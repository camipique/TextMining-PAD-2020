37	Alkanibacter
Alkanibacter &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Alkanibacter''''' is a Gram-negative genus of bacteria from the family of Sinobacteraceae with one known species (''Fontimonas thermophila''). ''Alkanibacter difficilis'' has been isolated from a hexane degrading biofilter in Hamm in Germany. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
