Chalvey Sports F.C. &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Chalvey Sports Football Club''' is a football club based in the Chalvey area of Slough, England. They are currently members of the and play at Arbour Park, where they share with Slough Town. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The club was founded in 1885. They played in the Great Western Combination, where they won Division One in 1954-55. Shortly afterwards, they joined the Slough & District League, which merged into the East Berkshire Football League in 1992. They won Division One in 1992-93 and were promoted to the Premier Division, where they remained until 2016, joining the Hellenic Football League. They won Division Two East in two consecutive seasons, winning promotion to Division One East in 2018. &&&&& &&&&& They were previously known as Chalvey WMC Sports and were based at Chalvey Working Men's Club.
