Carina Driscoll &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Carina Driscoll at the 2017 Burlington Progressive Party Caucus &&&&& '''Carina Driscoll''' is the stepdaughter of U.S. Senator Bernie Sanders and daughter of Jane O'Meara Sanders. She served as a State Representative in the Vermont House of Representatives for the Chittenden District from 2001-2002, replacing Terry Bouricius. She is a co-founder of Vermont Woodworking school with her husband Blake Ewoldson.
