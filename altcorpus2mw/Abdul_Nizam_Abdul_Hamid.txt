Abdul Nizam Abdul Hamid &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Abdul Nizam Abdul Hamid''' (1966-2016), also known as '''Abdul Nizam''', was an independent filmmaker and musician from Singapore. His final year school project was an art-house short called ''Datura'', which won Best Short Film at the 1999 Singapore International Film Festival and third place at the International Film and TV Schools' Festival.
