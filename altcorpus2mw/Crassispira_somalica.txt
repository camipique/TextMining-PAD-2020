70	Crassispira_somalica
Crassispira somalica &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Crassispira somalica''''' is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Pseudomelatomidae. &&&&& &&&&& Description &&&&& The length of the shell attains 20 mm. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Distribution &&&&& This marine species occurs off Somalia and is the first typical ''Crassispira'' species found off East Africa. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& * Morassi M. & Bonfitto A.
