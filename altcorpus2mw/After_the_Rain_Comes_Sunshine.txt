After the Rain Comes Sunshine &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''After the Rain Comes Sunshine''''' (German: '''''Nach Regen scheint Sonne''''') is a 1949 West German comedy film directed by Erich Kobler and starring Sonja Ziemann, Gert Fröbe and Rudolf Platte.
