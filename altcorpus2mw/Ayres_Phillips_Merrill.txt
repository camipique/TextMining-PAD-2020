Ayres Phillips Merrill &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Ayres Phillips Merrill''' (1825 - 1883) was an American planter and diplomat. He was the owner of a plantation in Adams County, Mississippi, and he served as the United States Minister Resident to Belgium from 1876 to 1877.
