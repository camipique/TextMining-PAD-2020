Aguri Ōnishi &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& is a Japanese actress and voice actress from Aichi Prefecture. She is managed by Link Plan Inc, a subsidiary of Pro-Fit. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& In 2011, Ōnishi had a starring role in NHK's "Junior High School Diaries" (中学生日記 転校生シリーズ「僕と君のメロディ」''Chūgakusei Nikki Tenkōsei Shirizu "Boku to Kimi no Melody"''). In 2013 "Junior High School Diaries" won an International Emmy Kids Award in the series category. &&&&& &&&&& Ōnishi was a finalist in the 13th Japan Bishojo Contest in 2012, which led to her becoming part of an idol group called Jisedai Unit X21. The group was made up of all of the other finalists from the contest. They began performing in January 2013. She graduated from both X21 and the production agency Oscar Promotion on April 16, 2017. &&&&& &&&&& She began working with Link Plan productions in July 2017, after completing Pro-Fit's training course. Ōnishi made her debut as a voice actress later that year as Miu Ichinose in the YomeKura drama cd.
