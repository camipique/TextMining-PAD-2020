Brachybacterium nesterenkovii &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Brachybacterium nesterenkovii''''' is a species of Gram positive, facultatively anaerobic, yellow-pigmented bacterium. The cells are coccoid during the stationary phase, and irregular rods during the exponential phase. It was first isolated from milk products in 1984. The species was first described in 1992, and is named for O. A.
