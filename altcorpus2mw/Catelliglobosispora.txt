37	Catelliglobosispora
Catelliglobosispora &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Catelliglobosispora''''' is a genus of bacteria from the family of ''Micromonosporaceae'' with one known species (''Catelliglobosispora koreensis''). ''Catelliglobosispora koreensis'' has been isolated from soil from a gold mine cave from Kongju in Korea. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
