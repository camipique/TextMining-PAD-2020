Behind Closed Doors (video) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Behind Closed Doors''''' is a video album by British-Australian recording artist Reece Mastin. It was released in September 2012 by Sony Music Australia. &&&&& &&&&& The DVD features a documentary on Mastin's music career to date, with interviews from close friends and family and The X Factor mentor Guy Sebastian. The DVD also features video clips, behind the scenes footage of the making of the videos and stripped-back acoustic performances. &&&&& &&&&& Background &&&&& In 2011, Reece Mastin auditioned for the third season of The X Factor (Australia, where he was mentored by Guy Sebastian. In November 2011, he was declared the winner and released his debut single "Good Night" which debuted at number one on the ARIA Charts and was certified 4x Platinum. In December 2011, Mastin's debut self titled album was released and debuted at number one and was certified 2x Platinum. In 2012, further platinum singles "Shut Up & Kiss Me" and "Shout It Out" were released, the latter peaking at number one, making Mastin the first Australian this decade to score two chart-topping singles.
