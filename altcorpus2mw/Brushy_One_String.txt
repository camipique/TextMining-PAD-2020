Brushy One String &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Andrew Chin''', better known by his stage name '''Brushy One String''', is a Jamaican singer and guitarist. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Son of Jamaican reggae singer Freddie McKay, Brushy created a unique sound using an acoustic guitar with only one string (hence his stage name, ''One String''), and the body of the guitar as percussion. &&&&& &&&&& Following an encounter with filmmaker Luciano Blotta, who was in Jamaica shooting his 2007 documentary Rise Up, his career took off. His song "Chicken in the Corn" was featured in the soundtrack of the movie.
