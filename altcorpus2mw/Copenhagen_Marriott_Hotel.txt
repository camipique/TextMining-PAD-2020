Copenhagen Marriott Hotel &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Copenhagen Marriott Hotel''' is located at Kalvebod Brygge 5 in central Copenhagen, Denmark. It is owned by BC Hospitality and operated by Marriott International under the Mariott brand. It was the chain's first hotel in Scandinavia and is Denmark's largest five-star hotel. &&&&& &&&&& The construction of the hotel started in 1999 and the hotel was opened to the public in 2001.
