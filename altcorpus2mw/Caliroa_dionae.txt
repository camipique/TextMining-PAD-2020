Caliroa dionae &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Caliroa dionae''''' is a sawfly whose larvae eat the leaves of the blueberry plant. It was first discovered near Trois-Rivieres, Quebec. &&&&& &&&&& It is the first Caliroa in North America found to eat blueberry plants. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
