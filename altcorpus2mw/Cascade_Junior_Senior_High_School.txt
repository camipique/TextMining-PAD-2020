Cascade Junior/Senior High School &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Cascade Junior/Senior High School''', also known as '''Cascade High School''' ('''CHS''') is a public secondary school in Cascade, Iowa. A part of the Western Dubuque Community School District , it serves grades 7-12. it has 360 students.
