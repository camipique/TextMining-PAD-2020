Bodianus vulpinus &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Bodianus vulpinus''', is a species of wrasse native to tropical and warm temperate waters of the Pacific Ocean, namely Western Australia. &&&&& &&&&& Taxonomy &&&&& ''Bodianus vulpinus'' is placed within in the subgenus ''Verreo'' in ''Bodianus''. The record of ''B. vulpinus'' from the Hawaiian Islands by some authors was eventually recognized as a distinct species, ''Bodianus bathycapros''. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
