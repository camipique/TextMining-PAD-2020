Colleen (1927 film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Colleen''''' is a 1927 American comedy film directed by Frank O'Connor and written by Randall Faye. The film stars Madge Bellamy, Charles Morton, J. Farrell MacDonald, Tom Maguire, Sammy Cohen and Marjorie Beebe.
