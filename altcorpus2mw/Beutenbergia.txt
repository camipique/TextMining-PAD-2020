45	Beutenbergia
Beutenbergia &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Beutenbergia''''' is a Gram-positive, non-spore-forming and non-motile genus of bacteria from the family of Beutenbergiaceae with one known species ('''''Beutenbergia cavernae'''''). ''Beutenbergia cavernae'' has been isolated from soil from the Reed Flute Cave in Guilin in China. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
