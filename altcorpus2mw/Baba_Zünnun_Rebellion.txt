Baba Zünnun Rebellion &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Baba Zünnun rebellion''', Baba Zünnun was an Alevi who struck the palace of Lord Mustafa of the sandjak of Bozok in 1526 August and revolted with his troops. After this he encountered troops who were sent to crush the revolt, but Baba Zünnun won. Hürrem Paşa, Berham Bey of the sandjak of Kayseri, Ali bey of the sandjak of İçel died during this battle. &&&&& &&&&& Baba Zünnun has been killed after the attack of Pîrî Bey. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Sources &&&&&  &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
