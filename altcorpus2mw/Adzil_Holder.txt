Adzil Holder &&&&& Introduction &&&&&  &&&&& '''Adzil Harcourt Holder''' (born 22 October 1931) is a former Barbados cricketer who played first-class cricket for Barbados from 1951 to 1959. &&&&& &&&&& Holder's outstanding match was against Trinidad in 1956-57, when he took 7 for 38 with his left-arm spin in the first innings and then made 52 batting at number 10, he and Frank King added 78 for the last wicket in 64 minutes; Barbados won by an innings. Later that season he played in the 12-a-side trial matches for the forthcoming tour of England. He took 6 for 60 in the second innings of the second trial match, but was not selected for the tour. &&&&& &&&&& He later went to Scotland, where he played as a professional for the Ferguslie, Clydesdale, and Clackmannan County clubs.  &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
