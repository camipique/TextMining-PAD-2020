Coiba Airport &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Coiba Airport''' is an airport serving Isla Coiba, a Pacific island in the Veraguas Province of Panama. The island and the surrounding waters are part of the Coiba National Park, a World Heritage site. &&&&& &&&&& The airport is on the eastern shore of the island.
