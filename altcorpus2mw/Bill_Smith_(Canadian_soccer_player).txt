Bill Smith (Canadian soccer player) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Bill Smith''' is a former Canadian soccer player. He was a Canadian champion with North Shore FC in 1949. &&&&& &&&&& In the Pacific Coast League, Smith won four championships with three teams: once with Vancouver Boeing FC (1943–44), twice with North Shore FC (1948–49 and 1950–51), and once with Vancouver Canadians (1962–63).
