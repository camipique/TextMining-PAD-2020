Alexander''' (born November 7, 1996) is an American football cornerback for the Washington Redskins of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Virginia Tech, and was drafted by the Redskins in the sixth round of the 2018 NFL supplemental draft. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& Alexander attended Independence High School in the city. He then attended Virginia Tech, and was ruled academically ineligible for his final year, preventing him from entering the 2018 NFL Draft.
