Amar Oli &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Amar Oli''' (born 20 August 1980 at Dang district, Nepal) is an award-winning film director working primarily in the Nepali film industry for more than a decade. He began his career as an assistant director and actor. Later, he focused his attention on other aspects of filmmaking such as production, cinematography, and direction. He has produced and directed several Nepali movies, documentaries and music videos. His work as a director includes movies such as ''Aarop, Chot'', and ''Dhartiputra''. &&&&& &&&&& Early life &&&&& Oli was passionate about movies since his childhood.
