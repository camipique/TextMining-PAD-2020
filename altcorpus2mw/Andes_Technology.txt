Andes Technology &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Andes Technology Corporation''', founded in Hsinchu Science Park, Taiwan in 2005, is a Taiwanese supplier of 32/64-bit embedded CPU cores. It focuses on the embedded market and delivers CPU cores with integrated development environment and associated software and hardware solutions for SoC development. Andes CPU cores are applied to touch-panel controllers, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, FM, GPS controllers, and sensor hubs. Andes currently is ranked the fifth IP company that accounted for about 2.2% of the global market share. &&&&& &&&&& Andes went public on the Taiwan Stock Exchange and began trading on 14 March 2017. &&&&& &&&&& Besides Taiwan and Mainland China, the company has also expanded its market into South Korea, Japan and the United States. More than 2 billion units of SoC embedded with AndesCore have shipped (November, 2017).
