Centro de Eventos do Ceará &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Centro de Eventos do Ceará''' (English: Ceará Event Center) is a multi-purpose indoor arena in Fortaleza, Brazil. It is the second largest convention center in Latin America in a total area of 76,000 m2. &&&&& &&&&& The arena had its first event on June 30, 2012, with a show by Jennifer Lopez and Ivete Sangalo as a part of Arte Music Festival. However, the arena was officially inaugurated on August 15, 2012, with a show by Spanish tenor Plácido Domingo and Orquestra de Câmara Eleazar de Carvalho. &&&&& &&&&& Structure &&&&& The arena is divided into two large blocks, composed by two exhibition halls and two mezzanines, with eighteen rooms each.
