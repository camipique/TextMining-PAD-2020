Character Role Playing &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Character Role Playing''''' is a 1981 fantasy role-playing game supplement published by Ragnarok Enterprises. &&&&& &&&&& Contents &&&&& ''Character Role Playing'' is a course in creatively role playing the most common fantasy character types. &&&&& &&&&& Reception &&&&& Ronald Pehr reviewed ''Character Role Playing'' in ''The Space Gamer'' No. 49. Pehr commented that "Experienced role players don't need ''Character Role Playing'', others won't buy it. Get it for new FRPG players (who don't yet worship the dice), if you can convince them to read and heed." &&&&& &&&&& Reviews &&&&& *''Pegasus'' #8 (July/Aug., 1982) &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
