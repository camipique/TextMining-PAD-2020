Better than Humans &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Better than Humans''' () is a Russian science fiction television series created by Andrey Junkovsky. Produced by Yellow, Black and White, in cooperation with Sputnik Vostok Production, for the Russian State channel, C1R. However, the series was purchased by Netflix, and will be the first Russian series under the Netflix Originals Banner. &&&&& &&&&& Tagline: A dysfunctional family accidentally gets a killer robot. &&&&& Plot Summary &&&&& The story takes place in a future city where androids serve the population in various positions, even replacing humans in some jobs. They raise children and work as chauffeurs and security guards. Although most people believe they are just soulless machines, they are able to think and feel empathy. One android, Arisa, suddenly begins to behave like a human and, at times, appears more intelligent than most human beings.
