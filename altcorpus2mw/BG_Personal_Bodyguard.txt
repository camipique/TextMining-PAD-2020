BG Personal Bodyguard &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''BG Personal Bodyguard''''' (Japanese: 身辺警護人) is a Japanese drama series first broadcast in January 2018 on TV Asahi channel. &&&&& &&&&& Shimazaki Akira is divorced and lives with his teenage son. Akira used to work as a personal bodyguard but has since left the industry after an accident occurred in 2012 that his soccer superstar client was injured. He now works as a security guard at a construction site. The private security company where he works is going to set up a new bodyguard division. Without telling his manager about the past, Akira Shimazaki begins work as a novice bodyguard. &&&&& &&&&& Broadcast &&&&& The first episode was broadcast on January 18, 2018, in Japan. It was also aired at the same month by Wakuwaku Japan to several Asian countries including Indonesia, Mongolia, Myanmar, Singapore, Sri Lanka and Taiwan.
