Chris Muckert &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Chris Muckert''' (born 24 January 1981) is an Australian former professional rugby league footballer who played as a and forward for the Parramatta Eels and the North Queensland Cowboys in the 2000s.  &&&&& &&&&& Muckert made his first grade debut for North Queensland in round 7 of the 2001 season against St George.
