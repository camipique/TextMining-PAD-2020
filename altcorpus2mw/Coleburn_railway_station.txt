Coleburn railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Coleburn railway station''' served the area of Coleburn, Moray, Scotland from 1863 to 1926 on the Morayshire Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened on 5 June 1863 as Coleburn's Platform by the Great North of Scotland Railway. . The station closed and reopened when the name was shortened to Coleburn in April 1867.
