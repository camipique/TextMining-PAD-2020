Church of Alibeglo &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''St. Nino Church of Alibeglo''' () is a Georgian Orthodox Church located in Qakh District, northwestern Azerbaijan, on the border with Georgia. The last time it was renovated by the local community was in the period of the reintroduction of Christianity in Hereti (Saingilo), between 1850-1855. The church is of ''kuppelhalle (domed rectangle)'' type with a projecting apse and an elongated west arm.
