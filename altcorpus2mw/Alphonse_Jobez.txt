Alphonse Jobez &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Alphonse Jobez''' (1 August 1813 - 13 May 1893) was a French businessman and politician. He was the manager of the Forges de Syam. He represented Jura in the National Constituent Assembly in 1848-1849 during the Second French Republic.
