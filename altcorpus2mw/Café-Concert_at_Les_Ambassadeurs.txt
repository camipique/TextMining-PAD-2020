Café-Concert at Les Ambassadeurs &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The Lyon version. &&&&& '''''Café-Concert at Les Ambassadeurs''''' (French - ''Le Café-concert aux ambassadeurs'') is a monotype pastel by Edgar Degas of the cafe-concert at the Les Ambassadeurs restaurant. It was first exhibited in 1877 at an Impressionist exhibition and is now in the Musée des Beaux-Arts de Lyon.
