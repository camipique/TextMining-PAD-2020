Adam Brand (album) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Adam Brand''''' is the debut studio album by Australian recording artist Adam Brand. The album was released in July 1998 and peaked at number 44 on the ARIA charts. A special bonus tour edition was released in 1999.
