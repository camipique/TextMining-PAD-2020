Abrahão Saliture &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Abrahão Saliture''' (born January 1885, date of death unknown) was a Brazilian rower. He competed in the men's coxed four event at the 1920 Summer Olympics.
