Coheelee Creek Covered Bridge &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Coheelee Creek Covered Bridge''' near Hilton, Georgia is a covered bridge which was built in 1883. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1976. &&&&& &&&&& It was built by J.W. Baughman. It has two spans and rests upon three abutments. &&&&& &&&&& It is located north of Hilton on Old River Rd. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
