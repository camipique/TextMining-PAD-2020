Bai Yu (actor) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Bai Yu''' (, born 8 April 1990), also known as '''Johnny Bai''', is a Chinese actor. &&&&& He is best known for his roles in the dramas ''Love O2O'', ''Memory Lost'' and ''Guardian''. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& In 2014, he made his acting debut in the web drama ''0.5 Diors''. The same year, he co-starred in the war drama ''Young Marshal'', portraying the male lead's student. &&&&& &&&&& In 2015, Bai starred in the youth medical drama ''Grow Up'', portraying a rebellious yet realistic intern. The same year, he was cast in the modern romance dramas ''Above the Clouds'' and ''Hello, Joann''. &&&&& &&&&& Bai gained recognition with his supporting role in the hit romantic comedy drama ''Love O2O'' (2016). The same year, he starred in the crime suspense web drama ''Memory Lost''; portraying the male lead Han Chen. The series was a hit in China and led to increased popularity for Bai. &&&&& &&&&& In 2018, Bai starred in the acclaimed youth melodrama ''Suddenly This Summer''. &&&&& In June, he starred in the fantasy action web drama ''Guardian'' with Zhu Yilong. The series developed a cult following online and led to increased popularity for the actors. &&&&& &&&&& In 2019, Bai starred in the republic detective drama ''Detective L''. &&&&& &&&&& Filmography
