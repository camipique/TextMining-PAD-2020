Bellamy Historic District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Bellamy Historic District''', in Franklin County, Georgia near Carnesville, Georgia, is located on GA 51, approximately 2.75 mi. NW of junction with I-85. It is a historic district which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1996.
