Andrzej Komorowski &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Andrzej Komorowski''', F.S.S.P. (born 19 October 1975) is a Polish Catholic priest and the current Superior General of the Priestly Fraternity of Saint Peter. He was elected to a six-year term on 9 July 2018 by the General Chapter of the Fraternity at Our Lady of Guadalupe Seminary located in Denton, Nebraska. &&&&& Fr Komorowski resides at the Fraternity's General House in Fribourg, Switzerland.
