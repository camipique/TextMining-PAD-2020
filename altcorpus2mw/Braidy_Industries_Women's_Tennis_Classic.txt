Braidy Industries Women's Tennis Classic &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Braidy Industries Women's Tennis Classic''' (formerly known as the '''''Our Lady of Bellefonte Hospital Tennis Classic''''') is a tournament for professional female tennis players played on outdoor hard courts. The event is classified as a $60,000 ITF Women's Circuit tournament and has been held in Ashland, United States, since 2004.
