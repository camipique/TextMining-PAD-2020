AG-DMS metro station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& }} &&&&& &&&&& '''AG-DMS metro station''' is a Metro railway station on the Blue Line of the Chennai Metro. The station is among the underground stations along corridor I of the Chennai Metro, Washermanpet–Chennai International Airport stretch. The station serves the neighbourhoods of Teynampet and T. Nagar. &&&&& &&&&& Etymology &&&&& The station is named so because of the presence of the Accountant General's (AG) Office and the office of the Directorate of Medical and Rural Health Services (DMS) in the vicinity. &&&&& &&&&& ===Construction=== &&&&& &&&&& The station &&&&& The station has a length of 380 meters. It is one of the two stations in Phase I of Chennai Metro, the other being the Chennai Central metro station, that measure a length of 380 meters or more. The station was opened for public on 25 May 2018. &&&&& &&&&& ===Station layout=== &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''G''' &&&&& &&&&& Street Level &&&&& &&&&& Exit/ Entrance &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''M'''
