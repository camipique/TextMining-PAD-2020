Clarence C. Caldwell &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Clarence C. Caldwell''' (1877-1957) was an American attorney and the 9th Attorney General of South Dakota. &&&&& Early life and education &&&&& Caldwell born in 1877. He was raised in Minnehaha County, South Dakota, and graduated from South Dakota State University, (then State University), in 1902 with his BA. He received his L.L.B. from the University of South Dakota School of Law in 1906 after initially attending the University of Chicago Law School. &&&&& &&&&& Legal career &&&&& He was elected the 9th Attorney General of South Dakota in 1914, just 8 years after graduating law school. &&&&& &&&&& Death &&&&& He died in 1957. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& - &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
