She represented Mexico at Miss Universe 2018 pageant but was unplaced. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Toscano lives in Manzanillo, Mexico. She succeeded outgoing Nuestra Belleza México 2017 Denisse Franco. &&&&& &&&&& === Miss Universe 2018 === &&&&& As Mexicana Universal, Toscano represented Mexico at the Miss Universe 2018 pageant in Bangkok, Thailand.
