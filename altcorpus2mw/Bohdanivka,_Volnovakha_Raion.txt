Bohdanivka, Volnovakha Raion &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Bohdanivka''' (; ) is a village in Volnovakha Raion (district) in Donetsk Oblast of eastern Ukraine, at 57.7 km SSW from the centre of Donetsk city. &&&&& &&&&& The War in Donbass, that started in mid-April 2014, has brought along both civilian and military casualties. Three Ukrainian servicemen were killed at the village on 27 June 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Demographics &&&&& In 2001 the settlement had 90 inhabitants.
