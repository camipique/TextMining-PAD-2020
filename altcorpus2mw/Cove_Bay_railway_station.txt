Cove Bay railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Cove Bay railway station''' served the suburb of Cove Bay, Aberdeen, Scotland from 1850 to 1956 on the Aberdeen Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened as Cove on 1 April 1850 by the Aberdeen Railway. It was situated north of and south of station. The name was changed to Cove Bay on 1 October 1912.
