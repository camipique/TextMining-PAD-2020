Alexandros Gounas &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Alexandros Evgenios Gounas''' (born 3 October 1989) is a Greek water polo player. He is member of the Greece men's national water polo team since 2008 and won a bronze medal at the '''16th FINA World Championships''' and competed at the 2016 Summer Olympics. He plays for Greek powerhouse Olympiacos, with whom he won the 2017–18 LEN Champions League in Genoa.Moreover in his collection are 5 Greek Championships and 5 Greek Cups in which three times voted Most Valuable Player.
