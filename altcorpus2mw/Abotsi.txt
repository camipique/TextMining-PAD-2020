84	Abotsi
Abotsi &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Abotsi''' (), ancient historical-geographical province of Georgia, located south to Javakheti, in modern day Armenia. In the late medieval period, as a result of influx of Turkoman tribes Abotsi was called '''Kaikuli,''' which was later one of the Sadrosho of Kartli Kingdom. ruled by Mouravi. In 1801, when Russia annexed eastern Georgia (Kingdom of Kartli-Kakheti), Kaikuli (Abotsi) was included into Lori district of Georgian Governorate.
