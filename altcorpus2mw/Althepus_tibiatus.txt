Althepus tibiatus &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Althepus tibiatus''''' is a species of spider of the genus ''Althepus''. &&&&& &&&&& Distribution &&&&& The species is endemic to Chiang Mai Province in Thailand.
