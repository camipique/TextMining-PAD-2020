Bough Beech Reservoir &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Bough Beech Reservoir''' is a  nature reserve in Bough Beech, south-west of Sevenoaks in Kent. It is managed by the Kent Wildlife Trust. It is in the Kent Downs Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty. &&&&& &&&&& This nature reserve covers the northern end of Bough Beech reservoir, and there is a hide for viewing the many species of birds. The shores have great crested newts, toads and dragonflies. &&&&& &&&&& There is public access to the site. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
