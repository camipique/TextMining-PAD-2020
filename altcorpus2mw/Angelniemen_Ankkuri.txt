Angelniemen Ankkuri &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Angelniemen Ankkuri''' is an orienteering club in Salo, Finland. It was founded in 1946 as club for sports and gymnastics, but nowadays it has only orienteering. Its members have won 17 medals in Finnish championships. It has won Tiomila, the Venla relay and the Jukola relay. The orienteers Johanna Asklöf, Kirsi Boström, Ari Kattainen, Mika Kuisma, Marika Mikkola, Matti Mäkinen and Seppo Väli-Klemelä have run for the club. &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
