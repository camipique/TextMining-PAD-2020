Amith Chakalakkal &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& Amith Chakalakkal Saju, known as '''Amith Chakalakkal''', is an Indian film actor in Malayalam Cinema. He lives in Kochi, Kerala. He is known for his role from the 2013 movie Honey Bee. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Amith Chakalakkal was born on 13 August 1985 to a Christian family. He studied in Rajagiri High School, Kalamassery, Kochi (class of 2012). He is a BE Mechanical Engineer. He paved his way into modeling through the TV reality show "Mammootty The Best Actor Award", in Asianet. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& Amith Chakalakkal was first featured in an album, 'Atma', in the song "Mounam Melle" along with Pushpa Mathew. &&&&& &&&&& He appeared first in Jean Paul Lal's directorial debut, Honey Bee. He played the role of Nizam Rawther in the 2014 Indian Malayalam-language period thriller film, Iyobinte Pusthakam.The following year, he acted in Lal Bahadur Shastri and Sapthamashree Thaskaraha. He was well received and appreciated for his potryal of Prince Chakkalakkal in the movie C/O Saira Banu.
