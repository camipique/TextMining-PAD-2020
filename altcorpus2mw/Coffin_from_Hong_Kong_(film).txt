Coffin from Hong Kong (film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Coffin from Hong Kong''''' (German: '''''Ein Sarg aus Hongkong''''') is a 1964 West German thriller film directed by Manfred R. Köhler and starring Heinz Drache, Elga Andersen and Ralf Wolter. It is based on a novel of the same title by James Hadley Chase. &&&&& &&&&& The film's sets were designed by the art director Nino Borghi.
