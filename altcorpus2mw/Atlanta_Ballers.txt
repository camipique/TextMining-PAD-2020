Atlanta Ballers &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Atlanta Ballers''' is a basketball team based in Atlanta, Georgia. The team competes in the Junior Basketball Association (JBA), a league created for high school and junior college players as an alternative to the National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA). &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The JBA was first announced on December 20, 2017, when media personality LaVar Ball said to ''Slam'' magazine that he would create a professional league targeted at high school graduates and fully funded by his sports apparel company Big Baller Brand. In April 2018, the league held tryouts near Atlanta, at Pebblebrook High School in Mableton, Georgia to choose players for the Atlanta Ballers roster.
