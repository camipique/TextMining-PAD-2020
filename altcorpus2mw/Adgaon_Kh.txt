Adgaon Kh &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Adgaon Kh''', is a small village in the Indian state of Maharashtra. Adgaon Kh village is 548898. It is located in Aurangabad Tehsil of Aurangabad district, Maharashtra, India. &&&&& &&&&& Geography &&&&& It is located 35km away from sub-district headquarter Aurangabad. The total geographical area of Adgaon Kh is 1063.17 hectares.
