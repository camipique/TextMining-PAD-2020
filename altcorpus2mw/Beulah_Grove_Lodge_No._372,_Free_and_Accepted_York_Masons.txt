Beulah Grove Lodge No. 372, Free and Accepted York Masons &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Beulah Grove Lodge No. 372, Free and Accepted York Masons''', also known as '''Pleasant Grove School''' and '''Pleasant Grove Colored School''', in Douglas County, Georgia near Douglasville, Georgia, was built in 1910. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2010. &&&&& &&&&& It is a two-story wood building. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
