Bianca Pitzorno &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Bianca Pitzorno''' (; born August 12, 1942) is an Italian writer best known for her books for children and young adults. She is considered one of Italy's most important authors in that field. &&&&& &&&&& She was born in Sassari. She studied archaeology and classical literature and then began a career producing programs for television. She began writing books for girls when she was 28. She also writes plays, screenplays and lyrics for songs. Pitzorno lives and works in Milan. &&&&& &&&&& During the 1970s, she was head of children's programming for RAI television.
