Cubeatz &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Cubeatz''' (sometimes stylized as '''CuBeatz''') are a German hip hop production duo from Sindelfingen, Germany consisting of twin brothers Kevin Gomringer and Tim Gomringer. Embarking on a production career in the 2010s, they are best known for their successful collaborations with rap artists such as Future, Gucci Mane, 21 Savage, Lil Uzi Vert, and Travis Scott, among others. &&&&& &&&&& In 2016, Cubeatz co-produced charting hits such as "Summer Sixteen" by Drake, "That Part" by Schoolboy Q featuring Kanye West, "No Shopping" by French Montana featuring Drake, "No Heart" by 21 Savage and Metro Boomin. In 2017, they have produced high charting singles such as "Tunnel Vision" by Kodak Black, "No Frauds" by Nicki Minaj, Drake and Lil Wayne and "MotorSport" by Migos, Nicki Minaj and Cardi B. &&&&& &&&&& Early life &&&&& Kevin and Tim Gomringer were born on March 15, 1991, in Sindelfingen, Germany.
