A Night in the Town &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''A Night in the Town''''' is a 1913 silent short film directed by Phillips Smalley and starring Pearl White and Chester Barnett. This film was released as a split-reel with ''An Innocent Bridegroom''.
