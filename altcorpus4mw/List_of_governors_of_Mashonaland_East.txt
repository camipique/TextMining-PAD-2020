List of governors of Mashonaland East &&&&& Introduction &&&&& The '''Minister of State for Mashonaland East''' is the governor of Mashonaland East Province in Zimbabwe. The governor oversees provincial affairs and sits in the House of Assembly of the Parliament of Zimbabwe. The governor is appointed by the President of Zimbabwe and is not appointed to a set term.
