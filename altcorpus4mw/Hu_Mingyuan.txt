Hu Mingyuan &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Hu Mingyuan''' (; born May 17, 1996 in Qiqihar, Heilongjiang) is a female Chinese volleyball player. She is a middle blocker of the China women's national volleyball team.
