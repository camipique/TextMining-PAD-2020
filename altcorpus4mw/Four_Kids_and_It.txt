Four Kids and It &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Four Kids and It''''' is an upcoming film based on the eponymous 2012 novel by Jacqueline Wilson, and will be directed by Andy De Emmony, starring Paula Patton, Russell Brand, Michael Caine and Matthew Goode. &&&&& &&&&& Principal photography started in July 2018 in Ireland. &&&&& The teenage kids in the picture are played by Ashley Aufderheide (''Going in Style'') and Teddie Malleson-Allen (''Swallows and Amazons'').
