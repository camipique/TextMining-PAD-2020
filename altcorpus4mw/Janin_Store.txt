Janin Store &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Janin Store''', also known as the '''Old Janin Store''', is a historic commercial building located at 123 North Morgan Avenue in Broussard, Louisiana. &&&&& &&&&& Built c.1890 by Francois Janin, the structure is an Italianate style one-and-a-half story frame commercial building. The store was run by Janin family until late 1960s. &&&&& &&&&& The building was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on March 14, 1983. &&&&& &&&&& It is one of 10 individually NRHP-listed properties in the "Broussard Multiple Resource Area", which also includes: &&&&& *Alesia House &&&&& *Billeaud House &&&&& *Martial Billeaud Jr. House  &&&&& *Valsin Broussard House &&&&& *Comeaux House &&&&& *Ducrest Building &&&&& &&&&& *Roy-LeBlanc House &&&&& *St. Cecilia School &&&&& *St. Julien House &&&&& *Main Street Historic District &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * National Register of Historic Places listings in Lafayette Parish, Louisiana &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
