Lynn S. Whiting &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Lynn S. Whiting''' (June 28, 1939 - April 19, 2017) was a trainer of Thoroughbred racehorses best known for his major upset win of the 1992 Kentucky Derby with the colt Lil E. Tee.
