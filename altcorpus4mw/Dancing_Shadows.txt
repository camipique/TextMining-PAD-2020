Dancing Shadows &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Dancing Shadows''''' is the fifth studio album from American singer and songwriter Mario, released on October 5, 2018. The release of the album marks the first under the singer's own imprint New Citizen LLC, in partnership with EMPIRE Distribution. &&&&& &&&&& The album serves as a follow-up to his fourth album ''D.N.A.'' (2009) and his first studio effort in almost a decade. It features production from Mario himself, Jake Gosling, xSDTRK, Daecolm, Pham, J Warner, P2J and Ryan McDermott. &&&&& &&&&& The album's lead single, "Drowning" was released on July 20, 2018, along with the accompanying music video, while the second single, "Dancing Shadows" was released on September 14, 2018 and also includes an accompanying music video. &&&&& &&&&& The album debuted atop the iTunes R&B charts. To further promote the album, Mario will appear as supporting act for B2K on The Millennium Tour. &&&&& &&&&& Background and development &&&&& In August 2011, RCA Music Group announced it was disbanding J Records along with Arista Records and Jive Records. With the shutdown, Mario, and all other artists previously signed to these three labels, will release his future albums on the RCA Records label. &&&&& &&&&& During his appearance on BET's 106 & Park on July 10, 2013 to premiere, the video for "Somebody Else", Mario stated that he felt the album recorded with Rico Love did not feel part of his career plan and that he scrapped that album and went on to record his then-titled album ''Evolve'', which was set to be released on September 17, 2013. The album was not released, and Mario left RCA Records in 2015. &&&&& &&&&& In the summer of 2015, Mario announced that he has reunited with Scott Storch, who produced his 2004 hit "Let Me Love You", on a new album, titled ''Never 2 Late''. The album was scheduled to be released on November 27, 2015, but was pushed to December 4, 2015, but the album was not released. &&&&& &&&&& On May 6, 2016, Mario premiered and released the single "I Need More". On December 13, 2016, he released the single "Let Me Help You". On June 16, 2017, he released the single "Pain is the New Pleasure". A remixed version of "Let Me Help You" featuring Jamaican recording artist Konshens was released on August 18, 2017. &&&&& &&&&& A year later, Mario spoke with the FunX radio station during the Oh My! Music Festival in June 2018, announcing that the album's final title was ''Dancing Shadows'' and would be released later in 2018 with a new single called "Drowning": "We changed the name of it. Making an album is like giving birth to something. You go through different names and different vibes." &&&&& &&&&& Following the release of the second single, "Dancing Shadows", and its accompanying music video, the album's cover art, release date and tracklist were revealed. &&&&& &&&&& Singles &&&&& "Drowning", was released on July 20, 2018, as the album's first single and was accompanied by it's music video on the same day. The song has peaked at number 30 on the US Adult R&B Songs chart. &&&&& &&&&& The title track "Dancing Shadows" was issued as the album's second single on September 14, 2018, alongside its music video. &&&&& &&&&& "Care for You" was released alongside its music video on March 15, 2019, as the third single from the album. The song was written by Mario, Felisha King, Jackson Dimiglio-Wood and Jake Gosling, while production was handled by Gosling. The music video was directed by Khufu Najee. &&&&& &&&&& Despite not being released as a single, the music video for the track "Goes Like That" premiered on October 4, 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Track listing &&&&& Credits are adapted from the album's liner notes and Tidal. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Notes''' &&&&& * signifies a co-producer &&&&& * signifies an additional producer &&&&& * "Good Times" originally didn't credit featured vocals by Buddy Guy &&&&& &&&&& Charts &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Chart (2018) &&&&& &&&&& Peakposition &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& US R&B Album Sales (''Billboard'') &&&&& &&&&& 9 &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& US R&B/Hip-Hop Album Sales (''Billboard'') &&&&& &&&&& 41 &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Release history &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Region &&&&& &&&&& Date &&&&& &&&&& Format &&&&& &&&&& Label &&&&& &&&&&  &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Various &&&&& &&&&& October 5, 2018 &&&&& &&&&&  &&&&& &&&&&  &&&&& &&&&&  &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& November 16, 2018 &&&&& &&&&& CD &&&&& &&&&&  &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& January 25, 2019 &&&&& &&&&& Vinyl &&&&& &&&&&  &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * 2018 in American music &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
