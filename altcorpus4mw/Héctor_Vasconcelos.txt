Héctor Vasconcelos &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Héctor Vasconcelos''' is a Mexican diplomat. He is the former Mexican Ambassador to Denmark, Norway and Iceland. He was expected to be appointed as the Mexican Minister of Foreign Affairs by President Andrés Manuel López Obrador, but was subsequently replaced by Marcelo Ebrard on 5 July 2018 as he was elected to be senator instead.
