Devin Dreeshen &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Devin Dreeshen''' is a politician in Alberta, Canada, currently serving as Member of the Legislative Assembly (MLA) for Innisfail-Sylvan Lake. &&&&& &&&&& Political career &&&&& Dreeshen was a policy advisor to Agriculture Minister Gerry Ritz from 2008 to 2015. Between February and November 2016 he worked on Donald Trump's presidential campaign. He won the United Conservative nomination for Innisfail-Sylvan Lake in 2018, and won the ensuing by-election. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Dreeshen is a farmer near Pine Lake.
