Dichromia cognata &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Dichromia cognata''''' is a moth of the family Erebidae first described by Moore in 1885. It is found in Sri Lanka, India, Hainan, Borneo, New Guinea, Bismarck Islands, Solomon Islands and Vanuatu. &&&&& &&&&& Male antennae have long cilia. In male genitalia, valves and saccus are long.
