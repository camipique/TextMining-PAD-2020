Hugo Historic District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Hugo Historic District''' in Hugo, Oklahoma is a historic district which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1980. It is located at U.S. 70 and U.S. 271. &&&&& &&&&& The district is a 12 block area including 64 buildings, most built between 1900 and 1920.
