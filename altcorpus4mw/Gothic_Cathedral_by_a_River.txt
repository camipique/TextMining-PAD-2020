Gothic Cathedral by a River &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Gothic Cathedral by a River''''' (German - ''Gotischer Dom am Wasser'') is an 1813 painting by Karl Friedrich Schinkel. It shows an imaginary Gothic cathedral on an island in a river - Schinkel later became a noted proponent of neo-Gothic architecture.
