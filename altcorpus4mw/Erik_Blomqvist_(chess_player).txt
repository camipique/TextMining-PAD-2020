Erik Blomqvist (chess player) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Erik Blomqvist''' (born 19 October 1990) is a Swedish chess grandmaster. He was Swedish Chess Champion in 2016. &&&&& &&&&& Chess career &&&&& Born in 1990, Blomqvist earned his international master title in 2008 and his grandmaster title in 2013. He won the Swedish Chess Championship in 2016 with a score of 7½/9 (+6–0=3), two points ahead of runner-up Nils Grandelius.
