Grand Democratic Alliance &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Grand Democratic Alliance''' ('''GDA''') is an electoral alliance of various political parties based in Pakistan. It was formed in the wake of Pakistani general election, 2018. &&&&& &&&&& One of the aims of this alliance is to defeat the Pakistan Peoples Party (PPP) in the Sindh province, which is considered a stronghold of the PPP. The alliance includes the Pakistan Muslim League (Functional), a party which emerged as the runner up to the PPP in the 2013 provincial elections in interior Sindh and garnered 12% of the vote in the province by itself. The alliance has been seen as a major challenger to the PPP in the 2018 elections. &&&&& &&&&& Composition &&&&& The following individuals and political parties are part of the GDA: &&&&& &&&&& * Fehmida Mirza / Zulfiqar Mirza &&&&& *Pakistan Muslim League (F) (PML (F)) &&&&& *Pakistan Muslim League (Q) only in Sindh &&&&& *Qaumi Awami Tehreek (QAT) &&&&& *National Peoples Party (NPP) &&&&& *Sindh National Front &&&&& *Pakistan Peoples Party Workers (PPPW) &&&&& *Pakistan Peoples Party-Shaheed Bhutto (PPP-SB) &&&&& &&&&& General election 2018 &&&&& For the Pakistani general election 2018, the GDA joined hands with the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf by forming seat adjustments in more than ten seats with the aim of defeating the PPP. As a result of the election, the GDA won 13 seats in the Provincial Assembly of Sindh, 2 seats in the National Assembly and garnered almost 15% of the vote in Sindh. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
