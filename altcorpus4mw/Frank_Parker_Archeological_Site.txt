Frank Parker Archeological Site &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Frank Parker Archeological Site''', near Florence, Nebraska, is an archeological site which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2009. It is associated with Frank Parker and has designations 25WN1 and 25DO169. &&&&& &&&&& It is a prehistoric village site and a processing site and was listed for its potential to yield information in the future. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
