Lad from Our Town &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Lad from Our Town''', () is a 1942 Soviet drama film directed by Boris Ivanov and Aleksandr Stolper. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& The film tells about Saratovite Sergey Lukonin, who goes to a tank school in Omsk, leaving his bride in the city, who becomes an actress. In 1936 Sergei was sent to the front in Spain.
