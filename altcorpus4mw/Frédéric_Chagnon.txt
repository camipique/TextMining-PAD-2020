Frédéric Chagnon &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Frédéric Chagnon''' (born November 20, 1992) is a Canadian football linebacker for the BC Lions of the Canadian Football League (CFL). He was selected in the third round and 24th overall by the Lions in the 2017 CFL Draft and signed with the team on May 23, 2017.
