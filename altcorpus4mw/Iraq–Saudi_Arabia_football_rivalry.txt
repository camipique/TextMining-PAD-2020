Iraq–Saudi Arabia football rivalry &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Iraq''' and '''Saudi Arabia''' are two fierce rivals of the Arab World. Their matches are often considered to be one of the most heated up rivalries in the world. &&&&& &&&&& Although received lesser attention comparing to rivalry with Iran of both sides, it is one of the oldest rivalries in Asia. Emotions often run high when two teams meet each others. &&&&& &&&&& Reason behind the rivalry &&&&& The main reason behind this rivalry to exist is mainly due to political differences, and historical grievances. During the Gulf War at 1990s, Iraq invaded Kuwait, an ally of Saudi Arabia, and both fought a short but bloody war that would remain heavily affect on their relations.
