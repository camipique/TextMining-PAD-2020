El Puente (TV series) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''El Puente''''' (''The Bridge'') is a Spanish reality television series in which a group of contestants is challenged to work together as a team in order to build a bridge. &&&&& &&&&& Created and produced by Zeppelin TV (part of Endemol Shine Iberia), the first season aired on #0 from 29 May 2017 to 17 July 2017. It was filmed in the Argentinian Patagonia. The series was renewed for a second season, which was filmed in Vietnam, and all of its episodes were made available on VOD on 23 September 2018. The series is hosted by Paula Vázquez. &&&&& &&&&& Format &&&&& In the first season, a group of fifteen strangers was brought together and challenged to build a bridge to an island 300 meters away in 30 days. If they were successful, each contestant had to vote on who deserved to receive the €100,000 prize that was kept there. The person with the most votes had then to decide whether to keep it for themselves or share it among the group. &&&&& &&&&& In the second season, two competing groups were introduced, among other twists.
