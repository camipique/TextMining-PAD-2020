Fabio Maistro &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Fabio Maistro''' (born 5 April 1998) is an Italian football player. He plays for Rieti as a midfielder. &&&&& &&&&& Club career &&&&& === Fiorentina === &&&&& He is a product of Fiorentina youth teams, and featured for their Under-19 squad in 2015–16 and 2016–17 seasons. In 2016–17 Serie A, he also appeared on the bench for the senior squad numerous times, but never saw on-field time. He was released by Fiorentina at the end of the season. &&&&& &&&&& === Gavorrano === &&&&& On 25 January 2018, Maistro joined Serie C club Gavorrano as a free-agent, 3 days later he made his professional debut as a substitute replacing Giulio Favale in the 63rd minute of a 1–1 away draw against Robur Siena.
