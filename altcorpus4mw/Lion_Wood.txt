Lion Wood &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Lion Wood''' is a  Local Nature Reserve in Norwich in Norfolk. It is owned and managed by Norwich City Council. &&&&& &&&&& Around a third of this wood is believed to be ancient. The dominant trees are oak and sycamore, and there is a variety of woodland birds such as blackcaps and green and greater spotted woodpeckers. &&&&& &&&&& The wood is open to the public. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
