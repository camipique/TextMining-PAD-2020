Gerald Field &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Gerald Gordon Field (born 1954) is an Anglican priest. &&&&& &&&&& Field was educated at King's College London and the College of the Resurrection Mirfield; and ordained in 1978. After curacies at Broughton and Blackburn he held incumbencies in Skerton, Shap, Nethertown and Tullamore.
