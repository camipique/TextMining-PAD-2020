Je parle à n'importe qui &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Je parle à n'importe qui''''' (English: ''I talk to anyone'') is an album by Léo Ferré posthumously released in 2018 by La Mémoire et la Mer. This is the eighth Ferré's posthumous album and the first to be published since ten years. &&&&& &&&&& This is a demo tape of one long poem recorded by hand and at home by the artist, during 1977.
