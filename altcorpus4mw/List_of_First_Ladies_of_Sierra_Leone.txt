List of First Ladies of Sierra Leone &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The First Lady of Sierra Leone is the spouse of the President of Sierra Leone. Though it is not an elected position, the First Lady is a representative of Sierra Leone at home and abroad. The Office of the First Lady is an extension of State House and is responsible for social events and ceremonies at State Lodge. &&&&& &&&&& Six women have held the office of First Lady since Sierra Leone the office was formed in 1971.
