Grand Avenue (film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Grand Avenue''''' is a 1996 American drama film directed by Daniel Sackheim and written by Greg Sarris. It is based on the 1994 novel ''Grand Avenue'' by Greg Sarris. The film stars Irene Bedard, Tantoo Cardinal, Eloy Casados, Deena-Marie Consiglio, Alexis Cruz, Diane Debassige, Jenny Gago, Cody Lightning, A Martinez, Simi Mehta, August Schellenberg, Sheila Tousey and Sam Vlahos.
