Evolutionary Ecology (journal) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Evolutionary Ecology''''' is a bimonthly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering the study of ecology from an evolutionary perspective. It was established in 1987 and is published by Springer Science+Business Media. The editor-in-chief is Matthew Symonds (Deakin University).
