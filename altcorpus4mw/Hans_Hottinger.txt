Hans Hottinger &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Hans Hottinger''' was a Swiss rower. He rowed for Basler RC. He was European champion in the double scull event with his partner Helmut von Bidder in 1930, 1931, and 1934.
