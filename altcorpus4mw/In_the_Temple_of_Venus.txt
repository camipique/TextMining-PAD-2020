In the Temple of Venus &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''In the Temple of Venus''''' (German: '''''Im Tempel der Venus''''') is a 1948 German drama film directed by Hans H. Zerlett and starring Olga Tschechowa, Willy Birgel and Hermann Speelmans. The film was originally produced during the final stages of the Second World War in 1945, but was not given a release until three years later. &&&&& &&&&& The film's sets were designed by the art directors Franz Bi and Bruno Lutz.
