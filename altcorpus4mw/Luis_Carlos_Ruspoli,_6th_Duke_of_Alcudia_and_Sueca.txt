Luis Carlos Ruspoli, 6th Duke of Alcudia and Sueca &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Don Luis Carlos Ruspoli y Sanchiz, Morenés y Núñez-Robres, dei Principi Ruspoli''' (born April 4, 1963) is a Spanish aristocrat, son of Luis Ruspoli, 7th Marquis of Boadilla del Monte, and first wife Doña María del Carmen Sanchíz y Núñez-Robres, Arróspide y Rodríguez de Valcárcel (Madrid, February 28, 1942 –), 13th Marchioness of La Casta. &&&&& &&&&& He is the 6th Duke of Alcudia, Grandee of Spain First Class, 6th Duke of Sueca, Grandee of Spain First Class, both with a Coat of Arms of de Godoy and 20th Count of Chinchón Grandee of Spain First Class with a Coat of Arms of de Borbón-Anjou, 8th Marquis of Boadilla del Monte with a Coat of Arms of Ruspoli and Godoy and 3rd Baron of Mascalbo with a Coat of Arms of de Godoy.
