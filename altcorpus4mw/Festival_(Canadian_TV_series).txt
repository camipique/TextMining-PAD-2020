Festival (Canadian TV series) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Festival''''' (initially titled '''''Festival '61''''') is a Canadian entertainment anthology television series which aired on CBC Television from 1960 to 1969. &&&&& &&&&& Premise &&&&& CBC Television aired dramatic and musical anthology series such as ''Scope'' and ''Folio'' during the 1950s. Robert Allen, a producer on ''Folio'', became supervising producer of the new ''Festival'' series. &&&&& &&&&& Production &&&&& The production cost of a typical ''Festival'' drama show was approximately $45,000 in 1961, among the highest production costs of CBC programming at the time.
