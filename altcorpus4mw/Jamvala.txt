Jamvala &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jamvala''' (), also known as Gir Jamvala, is a village / panchayat located in the Gir Gadhada Taluka of Gir Somnath district in Gujarat State, India. Earlier, until August 2013, Jamvala was part of Una Taluka and Junagadh district. The latitude 20.98354 and longitude 70.767629 are the geo-coordinate of the Village Jamvala. Gandhinagar is the state capital of Jamvala village which is located around 375 kilometres away from Jamvala. &&&&& &&&&& According to Census 2011, with the 748 families, the population of this village is 3797. Out of this, 1961 are males and 1836 are females. Most residents are dependent on agriculture. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Demographics &&&&& &&&&& According to the 2011 census of India, Jamvala has 748 households.
