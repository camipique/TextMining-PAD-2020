Dmytro Lypovtsev &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Dmytro Lypovtsev''' (; born October 10, 1986) is a Ukrainian basketball player for Cherkaski Mavpy and the Ukrainian national 3x3 team.
