Dutch Junior Open Squash 2018 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''DPD Dutch Junior Open Squash 2018''' is the men's edition of the 2018 Dutch Junior Open Squash, which is a World Junior Squash Circuit Tier 2 event. The event took place at the Frans Otten Stadion from July 12 to 15.
