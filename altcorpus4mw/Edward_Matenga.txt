Edward Matenga &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Edward J. Matenga''' is a Zimbabwean archaeologist and the former director of the Great Zimbabwe World Heritage Site. He is now an independent consultant and a member of the International Council on Monuments and Sites (ICOMOS). &&&&& &&&&& Matenga obtained his PhD in archaeology and heritage from Uppsala University, in Sweden and his BA from the University of Zimbabwe (1986).
