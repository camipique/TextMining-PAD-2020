Energy (Stay Far Away) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& "'''Energy (Stay Far Away)'''" is a song by English grime artist Skepta and Nigerian musician Wizkid. It initially debuted at number 73 on the UK Singles Chart, but reached a peak of number 59 following the release of its music video in September. The song spent a total of 15 weeks on the UK Singles chart. &&&&& &&&&& Critical reception &&&&& Pulse Nigeria called it a "groovy song which has a message for anyone with a negative vibe" and a "perfect anthem for clubs". ''HotNewHipHop'' called it an "underrated banger" and "just the record to seal Skepta's ambitious switch to the big stage". &&&&& &&&&& Music video &&&&& The music video for the song was directed by Meji Alabi and features Skepta and Wizkid partying on a beach in Lagos, Nigeria. It pays homage to Fela Kuti.
