List of kings of Ebla &&&&& Introduction &&&&& The '''list of kings of Ebla''' includes the known monarchs of Ebla who ruled three consecutive kingdoms. For the first kingdom's monarchs, tablets listing offerings to kings mention ten names, and another list mentions 33 kings.
