Deepak Raj Joshee &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Deepak Raj Joshee''' () is a Justice of the Supreme Court of Nepal. He was appointed as Acting Chief Justice of Supreme Court of Nepal on 19 March 2018. He had been recommended as the Chief Justice of Supreme Court of Nepal on 12 June 2018.
