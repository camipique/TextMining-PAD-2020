Desideria Ocampo &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Desideria Ocampo Arriola''' (1861 – April 9, 1910) was the wife of President Manuel Estrada Cabrera, and served as First Lady of Guatemala during his presidency. She was daughter of Gabriel Ocampo and María Encarnación Arriola She died in France in April 1910.
