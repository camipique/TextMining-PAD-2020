Fareda &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Fareda''' is a village / panchayat located in the Gir Gadhada Taluka of Gir Somnath district in Gujarat State, India. Earlier, until August 2013, Fareda was part of Una Taluka and Junagadh district. The latitude 20.938265 and longitude 70.900065 are the geo-coordinate of the Village Fareda. Gandhinagar is the state capital of Fareda village which is located around 375 kilometres away from Fareda. &&&&& &&&&& According to Census 2011, with the 231 families, the population of this village is 1312. Out of this, 695 are males and 617 are females. Most residents are dependent on agriculture. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Demographics &&&&& &&&&& According to the 2011 census of India, Fareda has 231 households.
