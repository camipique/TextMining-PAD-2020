John Wallwork (surgeon) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''John Wallwork''' (CBE), is a retired cardiothoracic surgeon and emeritus professor who performed Europe's first successful combined heart-lung transplant in 1984, and in 1986 performed the world's first heart-lung and liver transplant with Sir Roy Calne.
