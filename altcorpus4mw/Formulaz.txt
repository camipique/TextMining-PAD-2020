Formulaz &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Handmade Wooden Car Race - Red Bull Formulaz, Turkey 2017 &&&&& '''Formulaz''' (; Laz: ჶორმულაზი ''Formulazi'') is an annual Laz festival and traditional wooden car race held each year on the 21st August, in the northern Turkish province of Rize's Ardeşen district.
