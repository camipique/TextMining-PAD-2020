Emotionless (Drake song) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''Emotionless'''" is a song by Canadian musician Drake from his fifth studio album ''Scorpion'' (2018). Drake co-wrote the song with its producers No I.D., Noah "40" Shebib, and The 25th Hour. Mariah Carey, Robert Clivillés, and David Cole received songwriting credits for the sampling of a club remix of Carey's 1991 hit single "Emotions".
