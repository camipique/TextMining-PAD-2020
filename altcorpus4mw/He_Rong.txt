He Rong &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''He Rong''' (; born October 1962) is a Chinese politician currently serving as Deputy Party Secretary of Shaanxi province. She is one of less than a handful of women serving in this office at the provincial level. She has a professional background in law. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Born in Linyi County, Shandong, she studied litigation at the China University of Political Science and Law. She joined the Communist Party of China in June 1984. A legal professional by trade, she rose through the Beijing court system as a researcher, law clerk, then judge. She was named the chief of the Second Intermediate People's Court of Beijing, and then deputy chief of the Beijing High Court (a court of appeal). &&&&& &&&&& In December 2011, she began serving on the Supreme People's Court as part of the jury. In October 2013 she was elevated to the vice-president of the Supreme People's Court. &&&&& &&&&& In March 2017, He was named a member of the Shaanxi provincial party standing committee.
