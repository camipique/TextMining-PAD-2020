Federation Space &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''''Federation Space''''' is a 1981 Star Trek-based board game published by Task Force Games. It is a strategic component to the ''Star Fleet Battles'' system, a game which consists of a strategic map of the area of space surrounding the United Federation of Planets. &&&&& &&&&& Reception &&&&& William A. Barton reviewed ''Federation Space'' in ''The Space Gamer'' No. 49. Barton commented that "''Federation Space'' succeeds in its purpose to present a relatively simple, playable ''Star Trek'' game which can serve as a strategic module for ''Star Fleet Battles''. Recommended to Trek gamers everywhere." &&&&& &&&&& Review &&&&& *''Dragon'' #76 &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
