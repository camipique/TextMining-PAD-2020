Dictyota &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Dictyota''''' is a genus of seaweed in the Dictyotaceae family. Species are predominantly found in tropical and sub-tropical seas, and are known to contain numerous chemicals (diterpenes) which have potential medicinal value.
