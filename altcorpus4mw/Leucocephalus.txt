50	Leucocephalus
Leucocephalus &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Leucocephalus''''' is a genus of biarmosuchian belonging to the family Burnetiidae dating to the Wuchiapingian (Late Permian). It was found in the Tropidostoma Assemblage Zone (''Tropidostoma'') of the Main Karoo Basin of South Africa. It is a monotypic taxon which contains one only species, ''Leucocephalus wewersi''. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
