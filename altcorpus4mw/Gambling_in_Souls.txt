Gambling in Souls &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Gambling in Souls''''' is a 1919 American silent crime drama film directed by Harry F. Millarde and starring Madlaine Traverse, Herbert Heyes, Murdock MacQuarrie, Lew Zehring, Mary McIvor, and Henry A. Barrows.
