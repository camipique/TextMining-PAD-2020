Jan Peder Jalland &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Jan Peder Jalland''' (born 1980) is a Norwegian football manager, currently the assistant manager of Eliteserien side Stabæk Fotball. &&&&& &&&&& Hailing from Nykirke, he is a brother of Jørgen Jalland. He played youth football for Ørn-Horten and 3. Divisjon football for Borre IF and Fossum IF. He coached Borre from 2004 through 2006, and then Nesodden IF in 2007 and 2008. &&&&& &&&&& He was hired as junior coach in Stabæk and also employed at Nadderud Upper Secondary School which at the time had a programme for elite football. Following a successful tenure, he was promoted to assistant manager for the senior team ahead of the 2013 season. Serving under Petter Belsvik, he continued serving under Bob Bradley, Billy McKinlay and Toni Ordinas. &&&&& &&&&& In 201 he obtained the UEFA Pro Licence. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
