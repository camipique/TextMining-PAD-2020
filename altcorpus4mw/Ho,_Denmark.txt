Ho, Denmark &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Ho''' is a village in southwestern Jutland in the Varde Municipality, in Region of Southern Denmark. Many tourists, especially Germans, visit the village primarily because of the nature of the area. It is 7 kilometers from Blåvand, 11 kilometers from Oksbøl and 32 from Esbjerg. &&&&& &&&&& Etymology &&&&& Ho is linked to Ho Bugt. Ho is derived from Hõi, which means trough or cart.
