Du bist wie ich &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''Du bist wie ich'''" (English: ''You Are Like Me'') is a song by German recording artist Jessica Wahls. It was written by Diane Weigmann and Till Brönner and produced by the latter along with duo Schumann & Bach.
