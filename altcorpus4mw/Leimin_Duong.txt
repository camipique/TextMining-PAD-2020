Leimin Duong &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Leimin Duong''' is a Vietnamese-Australian entrepreneur. &&&&& &&&&& Duong was born and raised in Sydney, Australia. She studied brewing in her spare time while working as an office administrator in a bank, and interned briefly at St. Peter's Brewery in Sydney to gain practical experience. She established a brewing company, Zeven Lemon Beerworks, and introduced its first product, a strawberry-flavoured beer, in 2013. &&&&& &&&&& In 2015, Duong was included in the BBC series ''100 Women''. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
