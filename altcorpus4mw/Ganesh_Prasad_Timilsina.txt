Ganesh Prasad Timilsina &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Ganesh Prasad Timilsina''' () is a Nepalese politician, belonging to the Nepal Communist Party (NCP). In the 2018 National Assembly election he was elected from the Gandaki Pradesh.
