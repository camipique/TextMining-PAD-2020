Frank Howard House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Frank Howard House''' is a historic house in Atchison, Kansas. It was built in 1884-1885 for Frank Howard, a dry goods merchant and clothing manufacturer. &&&&& &&&&& The house was designed by H. B. Prudden in the Stick-Eastlake architectural style.
