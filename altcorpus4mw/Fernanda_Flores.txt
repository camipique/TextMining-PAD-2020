Fernanda Flores &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Fernanda Flores Cariola''' (born 14 September 1993) is a Chilean field hockey player. &&&&& &&&&& Flores has been part of both the Chile junior and senior national teams. She made her junior debut at the 2012 Pan-Am Junior Championship, and her senior debut a year earlier in 2011. &&&&& &&&&& Flores has medalled with the national team at two South American Games, in Santiago in 2014 and Cochabamba in 2018.
