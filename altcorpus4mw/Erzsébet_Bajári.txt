Erzsébet Bajári &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Erzsébet Némethné Bajári''' (née as '''Erzsébet Bajári''', in publications it is used as '''N. Bajári Erzsébet''' ; (1912–1963) was a Hungarian entomologist. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& She graduated at the Biography Chemistry Faculty of Eötvös Loránd University as a teacher. In 1948 she get a position at the Hymenoptera collection of Hungarian Natural History Museum. Starting from 1954 she was the leader of the collection. At the beginning she started to process the collection of Sphecidaes and later on she went on to Ichneumonidaes. She was the first specialist of this function since the death of Győző Szépligeti, so it became a hard to handle part of the collection.
