GTFO (video game) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''GTFO''''' is an upcoming first-person shooter action survival horror cooperative game developed by Swedish indie studio 10 Chambers Collective. ''GTFO'' is scheduled for release in 2019 for Microsoft Windows/PS4. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& The Chicxulub Crater was formed 66 million years ago by an asteroid strike which resulted in a mass extinction that wiped out the dinosaurs. A team of scientists has a plan to drill for rock samples in the area. Only to be discovered, they have unleashed an unimaginable nightmare awaiting to roam the world. &&&&& &&&&& Gameplay &&&&& ''GTFO'' players form a team of four scavengers who are forced to explore the vast underground complex while searching for valuable artifacts. Complicating the process is the presence of hideous monsters, who have overrun the whole area. The players must gather weapons, tools, and resources to survive, and work to unearth the answers about the past and how to escape. &&&&& &&&&& Reception &&&&& ''GTFO'' has received a warm reception at The Game Awards 2017. &&&&& &&&&& ''GTFO'' was voted Best Cooperative Game at E3 2018 by DualShockers. &&&&& &&&&& ''GTFO'' is one of the best games of E3 2018 by Rock Paper Shotgun - "The atmosphere is pure Aliens". &&&&& &&&&& ''GTFO'' is Gamereactor's Game of the Show at E3 2018 - "Long after our week in Los Angeles" GTFO had us "waking up in cold sweats thinking about the horror". &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * &&&&& * 10 Chambers &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
