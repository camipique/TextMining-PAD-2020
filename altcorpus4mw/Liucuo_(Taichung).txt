62	Liucuo_(Taichung)
Liucuo (Taichung) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Liucuo''' (劉厝) is a historical name for an area located in Nantun District, Taichung, Taiwan. Situated on the bank of the Fazi River, it encompasses roughly the south end of Xinsheng Village. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The name translates to "House of Liu". Liucuo is one of the first areas to be settled in Taichung, being recorded in early Japanese era maps. &&&&& &&&&& 參考文獻 &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
