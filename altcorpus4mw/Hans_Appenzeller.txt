Hans Appenzeller &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Hans J. Appenzeller''' (14 March 1899 – ?) was a Swiss rower. &&&&& &&&&& Appenzeller was born in 1899. He was a member of the See-Club Zürich, a rowing club in Zürich.
