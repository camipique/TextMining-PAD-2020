DTaP-IPV vaccine &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''DTaP-IPV vaccine''' is a combination vaccine whose full generic name is diphtheria and tetanus toxoids and acellular pertussis adsorbed and inactivated poliovirus vaccine. It is also known as DTaP/IPV or dTaP/IPV or DTPa-IPV. It protects against the infectious diseases diphtheria, tetanus, pertussis, and poliomyelitis. &&&&& &&&&& Branded formulations marketed in the USA are Kinrix from GlaxoSmithKline and Quadracel from Sanofi Pasteur. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
