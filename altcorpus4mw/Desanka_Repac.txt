Desanka Repac &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Desanka Repac''' (; born 1949) is a politician in Serbia. She has served in the National Assembly of Serbia since 2016 as a member of the Serbian Progressive Party. &&&&& &&&&& Private career &&&&& Repac is a dentist based in Subotica. &&&&& &&&&& Political career &&&&& Repac was elected to the Subotica municipal assembly in the 2012 local elections and served in the term that followed. She also received the 222nd position on the Progressive Party's Let's Get Serbia Moving electoral list in the 2012 Serbian parliamentary election. This was too low a position for election to be a realistic prospect, and indeed she was not elected. &&&&& &&&&& ===Member of the National Assembly=== &&&&& Repac received the 126th position on the Progressive Party's ''Aleksandar Vučić – Serbia Is Winning'' list in the 2016 Serbian parliamentary election and was on this occasion elected when the list won a majority victory with 131 out of 250 mandates. She is a member of the assembly's health and family committee; a deputy member of the environmental protection committee and the committee on labour, social issues, social inclusion, and poverty reduction; and a member of the parliamentary friendship groups with Belarus, Georgia, Japan, Kazakhstan, Russia, and the United States of America. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
