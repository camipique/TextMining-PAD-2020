Jean-Paul Nomel &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jean-Paul Nomel''' (born 23 June 1988 in Ivory Coast) is an Ivorian footballer who is last known to have played for St. George's of the Maltese Second Division. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& &&&&& ===Hungary=== &&&&& &&&&& Added to Szeged 2011 of the Hungarian Nemzeti Bajnokság III, Nomel was indoctrinated into believing he could achieve by Szeged coach Major László who described him as physical and good with the head, but needed to improve on positioning.
