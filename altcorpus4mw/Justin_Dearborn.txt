Justin Dearborn &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Justin Dearborn''' (born c. 1971) is an American businessman. He formerly served as the chairman and chief executive officer of the Tribune Publishing Company, a newspaper publishing company listed on the NYSE and NASDAQ. &&&&& &&&&& Early life &&&&& Dearborn was born circa 1971. He graduated from Illinois State University with a bachelor's degree and he received a JD from the DePaul University College of Law. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& Dearborn began his career at Motorola, and later worked for Click Commerce. He was the Managing Director and General Counsel of Merrick Ventures, a private equity firm. He served as the chief executive officer of Merge Healthcare from August 2013 to October 2015, when it merged with IBM for US$1 billion. &&&&& &&&&& Dearborn was appointed as the chief executive officer of Tronc in February 2016 and as its chairman in March 2018.
