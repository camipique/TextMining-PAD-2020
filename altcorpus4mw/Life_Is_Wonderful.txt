Life Is Wonderful &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Life Is Wonderful''''' () is a 2018 Dutch comedy film directed by Frans Weisz. It was based on the book of the same name by Remco Campert, written in 1961.
