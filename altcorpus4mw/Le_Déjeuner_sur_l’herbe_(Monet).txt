Le Déjeuner sur l’herbe (Monet) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Le Déjeuner sur l’herbe''''' (English - ''Dinner on the Grass'') is an 1865-1866 oil on canvas painting by Claude Monet, produced in response to the 1863 work of the same title by Édouard Manet. It remained unfinished, but two large fragments are now in the Musée d’Orsay in Paris. &&&&& &&&&& Monet included the artist Gustave Courbet in the painting. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& category:Paintings by Claude Monet &&&&& category:1866 paintings &&&&& &&&&&
