Daniel Gafford &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Daniel Gafford''' (born October 1, 1998) is an American college basketball player who last played for the Arkansas Razorbacks of the Southeastern Conference (SEC). &&&&& &&&&& High school career &&&&& Gafford was a four star recruit in high school, and received offers in 2015 from several schools, including Kansas, Vanderbilt, and Florida. On August 1, 2015, Gafford committed to Arkansas. Gafford also played football growing up and was a wide receiver until ninth grade at El Dorado High School. He also was in the marching band and credits that to the development of his skill set. &&&&& &&&&& College career &&&&& In his first start for the Razorbacks, Gafford finished a perfect 8 for 8 shooting, along with 7 rebounds and 6 blocks against Minnesota. Gafford posted 21 points, 10 rebounds and seven blocks against No. 14 Auburn. After a freshman season where he averaged 11.8 points 6.2 rebounds per game, Gafford announced that he would return to Arkansas for his sophomore year rather than declare for the 2018 NBA Draft. After his freshman season, Gafford was named to the SEC All-Freshman team.
