Jabbertalky &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Jabbertalky''''' is a 1981 video game for the Apple II and TRS-80 published by Automated Simulations. &&&&& &&&&& Contents &&&&& ''Jabbertalky'' is programmable word game which includes three games and a method of creating a vocabulary for use in these games. &&&&& &&&&& Reception &&&&& Ron Boerger reviewed ''Jabbertalky'' in ''The Space Gamer'' No. 48. Boerger commented that "Unless you are (a) crazy about word games, (b) want to buy every game for the Apple, or (c) both of the above, don't waste your money on this one." &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
