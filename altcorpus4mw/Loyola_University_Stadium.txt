Loyola University Stadium &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Loyola University Stadium''' was a multi-purpose stadium in New Orleans. It was home to the Loyola University Wolf Pack football team and track and field team. The stadium opened in 1928. &&&&& &&&&& The stadium was a double-decker stadium with a track surrounding the grass playing field. It was located on Freret Street at Calhoun Street between Bobet Hall and the gymnasium.
