Georgian Honciu &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Georgian Honciu''' (born 24 April 1989) is a Romanian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Dunărea Călărași. &&&&& &&&&& Honciu made his Liga I debut on 22 July 2018, at 29 years old, when he played for Dunărea Călărași, club that also made its debut in the top flight, against Viitorul Constanța, scoring the only goal of the match.
