Empire of Sin &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Empire of Sin: A Story of Sex, Jazz, Murder, and the Battle for Modern New Orleans''' is a 2014 non-fiction book by American author Gary Krist. The book is focused on the early 20th century in New Orleans, around the time that jazz became in vogue in the city. Much of the book is set in and around Storyville, New Orleans.
