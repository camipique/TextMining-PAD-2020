She participated in the Miss Chinese International Pageant in 2013 and was the runner-up for it. &&&&& &&&&& ===2016: Acting debut=== &&&&& Tan made her acting debut in 2016, in the Malaysian-Hong Kong production, "The Hiddens" playing as Rachel in the series. &&&&& &&&&& ===2017: Entry to MediaCorp=== &&&&& In 2017, Tan was introduced to Singaporean viewers with ''Legal Eagles'' and she signed as a full-time artist with Mediacorp and continued filmed ''Have A Little Faith'' and her first villian role in, ''While We Are Young''. She was also the co-host on the outdoor cooking segment in the long-time game show ''The Sheng Siong Show'', replacing Seraph Sun. &&&&& &&&&& In 2018, she had filmed ''Mind Matters'', ''Fifty &
