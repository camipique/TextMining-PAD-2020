List of Beat Bugs episodes &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Beat Bugs''''' is a British-Australian-Canadian animated children's television series, created by Josh Wakely, and produced for Netflix by Grace: A Storytelling Company and Thunderbird Entertainment. The series is centered around five young anthropomorphised insects who live in an overgrown suburban backyard and learn life lessons while having adventures. Wakely acquired worldwide rights from Sony/ATV Music Publishing to a catalogue of music by the Beatles to feature in the series.
