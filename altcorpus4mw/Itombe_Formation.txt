Itombe Formation &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Itombe Formation''' is a geological formation of the Kwanza Basin in Angola dating back to the Turonian stage of the Late Cretaceous. The environment of deposition is shallow marine. Reptile fossils have been recovered from the Tadi beds locality within the formation, including the dinosaur ''Angolatitan'', the mosasaurs ''Angolasaurus'' and ''Mosasaurus iembeensis'' and the turtle ''Angolachelys''..
