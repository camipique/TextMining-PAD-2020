Labidostomis cyanicornis &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Labidostomis cyanicornis''''' is a species of ''short-horned leaf beetles'' belonging to the family Chrysomelidae, subfamily Clytrinae. &&&&& &&&&& Distribution &&&&& This species can be found in Southern part of Europe, in Romania, Ukraine, in basin of Volga and in the Eastern Palaearctic ecozone. &&&&& &&&&& Description &&&&& ''Labidostomis cyanicornis'' can reach a body length of . Pronotum is strongly punctate, with rather short hairs or it is almost hairless, while the abdomen is rather hairy. Antennae are serrated from the 5th article. Labrum is yellowish. The head and pronotum are black with distinct greenish metallic reflex , while the elytra are pale orange-brown. Also legs and underside are greenish-black. &&&&& &&&&& Biology &&&&& Adults can be found in May-June feeding on ''Salix alba'', ''Salix cinerea'' (Salicaceae) and Populus species.Females lay 10-20 eggs in each spawn and seal them in an eggs case consistimg of pure secretion. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& External link &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& * Alsphotopage &&&&& * Ukrainian Biodiversity Information Network &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
