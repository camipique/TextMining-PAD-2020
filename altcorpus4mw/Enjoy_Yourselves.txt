Enjoy Yourselves &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Enjoy Yourselves''''' (German: '''''Freut Euch des Lebens''''') is a 1934 German musical comedy film directed by Hans Steinhoff and starring Dorit Kreysler, Ida Wüst and Wolfgang Liebeneiner. &&&&& &&&&& The film's sets were designed by the art directors Artur Günther and Benno von Arent. &&&&& &&&&& Cast &&&&& *  Dorit Kreysler as Gusti Melzer, Kellnerin im "Bratwurstglöckl" &&&&& * Ida Wüst as Camilla Raveck &&&&& * Wolfgang Liebeneiner as Carl Maria, ihr Sohn &&&&& * Leo Slezak as Gottfried Bumm, Gesangspädagoge &&&&& * Eugen Rex as Emil Weissberg &&&&& * Anton Pointner as Igo von Lindstedt &&&&& * Gertrud Wolle as Frau Senkpiel &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Bibliography &&&&& * Bock, Hans-Michael & Bergfelder, Tim.
