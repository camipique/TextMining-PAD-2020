Hammad Azhar &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Muhammad Hammad Azhar''' () is a Pakistani politician who is the current Minister of State for Revenue, in office since 11 September 2018. He has been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan since August 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& He was born to the former Governor of Punjab Mian Muhammad Azhar. &&&&& &&&&& He is a barrister by profession. &&&&& &&&&& Political career &&&&& He joined Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) in 2011. &&&&& &&&&& He ran for the seat of the National Assembly of Pakistan as a candidate of PTI from Constituency NA-121 (Lahore-IV) in Pakistani general election, 2013 but was unsuccessful. He received 68,307 votes and lost the seat to Mehr Ishtiaq Ahmed. &&&&& &&&&& He was elected to the National Assembly as a candidate of PTI from Constituency NA-126 (Lahore-IV) in Pakistani general election, 2018. &&&&& &&&&& On 11 September 2018, he was inducted into the federal cabinet of Prime Minister Imran Khan and was appointed as Minister of State for Revenue. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
