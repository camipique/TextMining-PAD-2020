Le Soleil des eaux &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''''Le Soleil des eaux''''' (The Sun of waters) is a cantata for soprano, choir and orchestra by Pierre Boulez. Composed in 1965 after two poems by René Char, it was preceded by three successive versions, the first in 1948 for the radio, the second in 1950 for soprano, tenor, bass, choir and chamber orchestra, the third in 1958 for voice and orchestra.
