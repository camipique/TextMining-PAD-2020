David Emmanuel Ombugadu &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''David Emmanuel Ombugadu''' is a Nigerian politician who is currently a member of House of Representatives for Nassarawa state, Nigeria. &&&&& &&&&& Early life and education &&&&& Emmanuel Ombugadu was born January 10, 1978. He attended LEA Primary School Kakuri in Kaduna State. In 1995, he attended Government College Keffi where he obtained his SSCE certificate. He then proceeded to the University of Jos to get a bachelor's degree (Bsc.) in Economics in 2001. &&&&& &&&&& He later proceeded to do his NYSC in 2003. He proceeded to doing his masters (Msc.) at Rivers State University of Science and Technology. &&&&& &&&&& Political career &&&&& Emmanuel Ombugadu was a committee member in the privatization and commercialization committee, rules and business committee and public accounts committee until May 2015. &&&&& &&&&& He then proceeded to take his political career further by running for Nasarrawa House of Representatives from 2011 – 2015 and again from 2015 – 2019. &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * List of members of the House of Representatives of Nigeria, 2015–2019 &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
