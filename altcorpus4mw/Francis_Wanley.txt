Francis Wanley &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Francis Wanley,''' Doctor of Divinity (b Marske 25 April 1709; d Ripon 9 July 1791) was an eminent Anglican priest in the second half of the 18th century. &&&&& &&&&& Wanley was educated at Christ's College, Cambridge. He held livings at Thirkleby, Aldborough and Stokesley.
