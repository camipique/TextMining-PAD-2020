Dual Air Density Explorer &&&&& Introduction &&&&& DADE-A. &&&&& '''Dual Air Density Explorer''' was a set of 2 satellites, '''DADE-A''' and '''DADE-B''', released as part of NASA's Explorers program. DADE-A and DADE-B was launched on 6 December 1975 at 15:35 UTC, by a Scout F-1 launch vehicle from Launch Complex 5, Vandenberg AFB, California.
