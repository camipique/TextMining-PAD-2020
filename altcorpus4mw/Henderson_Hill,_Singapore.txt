Henderson Hill, Singapore &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Henderson Hill''' () is a subzone within the planning area of Bukit Merah, Singapore, as defined by the Urban Redevelopment Authority (URA).
