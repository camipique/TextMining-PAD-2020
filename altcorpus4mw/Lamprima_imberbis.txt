Lamprima imberbis &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Lamprima imberbis''''' is a species of beetles in the family Lucanidae; for much of its history it was treated as a synonym of ''Lamprima aurata'', but was restored to independent status in 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Of the five species in the genus ''Lamprima'', only two occur on the Australian mainland: ''L. aurata'' and ''L. imberbis''. The former is widely-distributed, while ''imberbis'' is restricted to northeastern New South Wales. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
