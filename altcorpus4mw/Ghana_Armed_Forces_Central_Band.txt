Ghana Armed Forces Central Band &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Ghana Armed Forces Central Band''' (GAF Central Band or AFCB) is the official military music service of the Ghana Armed Forces. Its command structure runs through the Support Services Brigade of the armed forces.  &&&&& &&&&& Musicians are recruited from all branches of the armed forces, with soldiers being required to have 2 credits and 4 passes at West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE), as well as have to be at least 27 years old. The current director of music of the AFCB is Colonel Francis Ennin. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
