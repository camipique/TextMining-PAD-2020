Dahiya Khap &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Dahiya Khap''' is a major leading Khap of Jat community in Haryana. There are more than 52 villages of Dahiya Jats in Sonipat district of Haryana. Dahiya is the largest Jat gotra in Haryana. Dahiya Jats are popularly known as Dahiya Badshah in all over India. Barona village of Dahiya Jats is the oldest one from where Dahiyas spread to other places.
