59	Estradiol_17β-tetrahydropyranyl_ether
Estradiol 17β-tetrahydropyranyl ether &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Estradiol 17β-tetrahydropyranyl ether''' is a synthetic estrogen and estrogen ether which was never marketed. It has been reported to possess improved oral activity relative to estradiol.
