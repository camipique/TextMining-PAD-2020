John Bayne (footballer) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''John Bayne''' (23 November 1877 – 21 July 1915) was a Scottish football centre forward who played in the Southern League for Reading and Brentford. He also played in the Scottish League for Raith Rovers. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Bayne served as a private in the Black Watch during the Second Boer War. He was recalled to the Black Watch after the outbreak of the First World War in 1914 and was killed on the Western Front on 21 July 1915.
