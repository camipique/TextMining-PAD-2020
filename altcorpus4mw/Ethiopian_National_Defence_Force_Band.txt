Ethiopian National Defence Force Band &&&&& Introduction &&&&& An American propaganda poster during the Second World War depicting a bugler from the band. &&&&& The '''Ethiopian National Defence Force Band (ENDFB)''' (Amharic: የኢትዮጵያ ብሔራዊ መከላከያ ባንድ) is a musical division in the Ethiopian National Defense Force. The band is the successor to the Imperial Bodyguard Band for the Ethiopian Empire. It commonly receives assistance from the British Corps of Army Music. It is composed of a Marching band, a Big band and a Corps of Drums.
