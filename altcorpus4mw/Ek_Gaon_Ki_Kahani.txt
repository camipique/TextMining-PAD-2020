Ek Gaon Ki Kahani &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Ek Gaon Ki Kahani''''' is a Bollywood movie directed by Dulal Guha which was released in the year 1957. The star cast was Talat Mehmood, Nirupa Roy, Mala Sinha etc. The movie was producer by Basu Chitra Mandir.
