David Hempstead &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''David Hempstead''' (October 2, 1909 - January 9, 1983) was an American film producer known for ''None but the Lonely Heart'' (1944), ''The Sky's the Limit'' (1943), directed by Edward H. Griffith, and ''Joan of Paris'' (1942), directed by Robert Stevenson. He cowritted the script of ''Hell and High Water'' (1954) alongside Jesse Lasky. &&&&& &&&&& He produced with RKO and worked alongside Milton Holmes.
