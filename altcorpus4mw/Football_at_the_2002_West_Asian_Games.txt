Football at the 2002 West Asian Games &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''football tournament''' at the '''2002 West Asian Games''' took place from 4 to 12 April 2002. It was originally scheduled for October 2001, but postponed due to the September 11 attacks. Oman were originally scheduled to take part in group A along with Kuwait and Qatar but withdrew and the format was changed to one group.
