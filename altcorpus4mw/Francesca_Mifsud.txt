Francesca Mifsud &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Francesca Mifsud''' (born July 19, 1996) is a Maltese beauty pageant titleholder who won Miss Universe Malta 2018 on July 13, 2018. she represented Malta at Miss Universe 2018 pageant. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Mifsud lives in Zejtun, Malta. She is a law student at University of Malta and would like to pursue a career in criminal and civil law. In August 2016, Mifsud entered for the first time the Miss Universe Malta pageant where she finished in the Top 5 and awarded Miss Photo Model. In October of the same year, she won the contest Best Model of the World and represented Malta in Istanbul Turkey where she won Best Face of the World. On July 13, 2018 Francesca represented her local council, Zejtun and competed against 24 other contestants in a direct transmission on TVM.
