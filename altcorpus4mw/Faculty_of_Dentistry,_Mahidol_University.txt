Faculty of Dentistry, Mahidol University &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Faculty of Dentistry, Mahidol University''' () is the university faculty specialising in dentistry of Mahidol University in Thailand. It operates the Dental Hospital, Faculty of Dentistry, Mahidol University at Phaya Thai and the Maha Chakri Sirindhorn Dental Hospital at Salaya. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The Faculty of Dentistry was founded by royal decree on 7 June 1968 as the "'''Faculty of Dentistry Phaya Thai, University of Medical Sciences'''", as there was already a dental school at Pathum Wan District. It was aimed to increase the number of dentists in Thailand. On 2 March 1969, the university was renamed Mahidol University and thus the faculty's official name became the "'''Faculty of Dentistry Phaya Thai, Mahidol University'''". On 1 May 1972, the dental faculty at Pathum Wan was transferred to Chulalongkorn University and became the Faculty of Dentistry, Chulalongkorn University. On 28 February 1974, the words "Phaya Thai" were removed from the name and the faculty then became known as "'''Faculty of Dentistry, Mahidol University'''". &&&&& &&&&& Departments &&&&& &&&&& * Department of Anatomy &&&&& * Department of Advanced General Dentistry &&&&& * Department of Community Dentistry &&&&& * Department of Masticatory Science &&&&& * Department of Operative Dentistry and Endodontics &&&&& * Department of Oral and Maxillofacial Pathology &&&&& * Department of Oral and Maxillofacial Radiology &&&&& * Department of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery &&&&& * Department of Oral Biology &&&&& * Department of Oral Medicine and Periodontology &&&&& * Department of Oral Microbiology &&&&& * Department of Orthodontics &&&&& * Department of Pediatric Dentistry &&&&& * Department of Pharmacology &&&&& * Department of Prosthodontics &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& &&&&& * Mahidol University &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& * Article incorporates material from the corresponding article in the Thai wikipedia. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
