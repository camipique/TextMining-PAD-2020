Joseph and Potiphar's Wife (Murillo) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& 300px &&&&& '''''Joseph and Potiphar's Wife''''' is a 1640–1645 oil on canvas painting by the Spanish artist Murillo, now in the Gemäldegalerie Alte Meister (Kassel). His later 1660s version of the subject is now in a private collection. &&&&& &&&&& The theme relates to the story told in Book of Genesis chapter 39, of Joseph in Potiphar's house. &&&&& &&&&& Sources &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
