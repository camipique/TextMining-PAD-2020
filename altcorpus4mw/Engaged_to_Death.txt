Engaged to Death &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Engaged to Death''''' (Italian: '''''I fidanzati della morte''''') is a 1957 Italian sports drama film directed by Romolo Marcellini and starring Rik Battaglia, Sylva Koscina and Margit Nünke . The veteran German star Hans Albers appears in a supporting role. The film portrays the lives of top motorcycle racing competitors. &&&&& &&&&& The film's sets were designed by the art director Carlo Egidi.
