Elizabeth Pipko &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Elizabeth Pipko''' (born June 26, 1995) is an American author and model and spokesperson of the Jexodus movement. &&&&& &&&&& Poetry &&&&& At age 17, Pipko's first book ''Sweet Sixteen'' was published by Archway Publishing, a self-publishing service from Simon and Schuster. In June 2018, her second book, a collection of poems, was released with the same publisher under the title ''About You'' and was named one of the "best reads before you sleep" by Thrive Global. In 2018, Pipko appeared on the "Social Media Stars As Authors" panel at the BookExpo America at the Javits Center. &&&&& &&&&& Modeling &&&&& Pipko was signed to Wihemina Models at 17, and has appeared in DT magazine, Maxim, and Esquire. She appeared in the "Perfectly Imperfect” campaign for Vizcaya Swimwear, an anti-photoshop campaign promoting positive body image which was covered by major publications such as ''People'' and ''Vanity Fair''. Elizabeth Pipko has also appeared on the cover of Supermodels SA and L’officiel Baltics in June 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Jexodus and support for Donald Trump &&&&& In 2016, Pipko worked for the Donald J Trump For President campaign headquarters in the Trump Tower in New York City. &&&&& &&&&& In December 2018, Pipko married a staffer for Trump's 2020 reelection campaign. &&&&& &&&&& At the start of 2019, Pipko declared herself the primary spokesperson of the newly formed group Jexodus (an apparent portmanteau of "Jewish" and "Exodus"). She describes the organization as representing millennial American Jews who purportedly have left the Democratic Party because of recent antisemitism. The domain name for Jexodus.org was created and registered on November 5, 2018. Although Pipko is the only person identified on the website, the conservative blog site, Daily Wire, credits lobbyist Jeff Ballabon and others of having developed the idea.  &&&&& &&&&& On March 12, 2019, Trump tweeted a quote from Pipko from her earlier appearance that Fox News' morning show ''Fox & Friends'': "Jewish people are leaving the Democratic Party. We saw a lot of anti Israel policies start under the Obama Administration, and it got worse & worse. There is anti-Semitism in the Democratic Party. They don’t care about Israel or the Jewish people." &&&&& &&&&& On March 21, 2019, Jexodus rebranded as "TheExodusMovement" by Pipko and incorporated as a tax exempt organization. &&&&& &&&&& Personal &&&&& Pipko is the granddaughter of Soviet born American Artist Marc Klionsky. &&&&& &&&&& Pipko attended Harvard Extension School. &&&&& &&&&& On December 26th, 2018, Pipko married Darren Centinello in West Palm Beach, Florida at the Mar-A-Lago Club. At the time, Centinello was working on re-election campaign for U.S. President Donald Trump.
