Grace Church (Clarkesville, Georgia) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Grace Church''' in Clarkesville, Georgia, also known as '''Grace-Calvary Episcopal Church''', was built in 1839. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1980. &&&&& &&&&& It was designed and built in Greek Revival style by local master builder Jarvis Van Buren. It has mortise-and-tenon framing. &&&&& &&&&& While it was separately listed on the National Register in 1980, it is also included as a contributing building in the National Register-listed Washington-Jefferson Street Historic District. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
