Li Xian (actor) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Li Xian''' (; born 19 October 1991) is a Chinese actor best known for his roles in the dramas ''Who Sleeps My Bro'', ''Medical Examiner Dr. Qin'' and ''Tientsin Mystic''. &&&&& &&&&& Early life &&&&& Li Xian was born at Jingzhou, Hubei, China. He studied at Hubei Jingzhou High School. In 2010, he was enrolled into Beijing Film Academy, where he majored in performance. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& In 2011, Li Xian debuted as an actor with a role in the film ''Feng Shui''. &&&&& In 2013, he starred in the romance teen flick ''Singing When We Are Young'', produced by Andy Lau. &&&&& In 2014, Li made his small-screen debut in the omnibus web drama ''City Of Fantasy''. &&&&& &&&&& In 2016, Li Xian starred in the coming-of-age brotherhood web series ''Who Sleeps My Bro''. The popularity of the series brought him wider recognition. The series was later adapted into a film starring the original cast. &&&&& &&&&& In 2016, Li starred alongside Zhang Ruoyun and Jiao Junyan in the crime medical drama ''Medical Examiner Dr. Qin'', which was acclaimed for both its storyline and performance. &&&&& &&&&& In 2018, Li starred as the title role in the fantasy suspense web drama ''Tientsin Mystic'', which was broadcast internationally on Netflix. The series was another critical and commercial hit; and established Li as a leading actor. &&&&& &&&&& In 2018, Li starred in the drama film ''The Enigma of Arrival'', which premiered at the Busan International Film Festival. The same year, he was cast in the romance film ''Love Song 1980'' directed by Mei Feng. &&&&& &&&&& In 2019, Li is set to star in the wuxia drama ''Sword Dynasty'' produced by Feng Xiaogang; and romance comedy drama ''Go Go Squid!''.
