Esha Rohit &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Esha Rohit''' (born 1 August 1998) is an Emirati cricketer. In July 2018, she was named in the United Arab Emirates' squad for the 2018 ICC Women's World Twenty20 Qualifier tournament. She made her Women's Twenty20 International (WT20I) for the United Arab Emirates against the Netherlands in the World Twenty20 Qualifier on 7 July 2018.
