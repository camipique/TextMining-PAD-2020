Lacy J. Davis &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Lacy J. Davis''' is a writer and a body image activist. Her memoir, ''Ink in Water: An Illustrated Memoir (Or, How I Kicked Anorexia's Ass and Embraced Body Positivity)'' details Davis' struggles with body dysmorphia and dieting.
