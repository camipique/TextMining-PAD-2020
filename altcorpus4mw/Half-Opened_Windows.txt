Half-Opened Windows &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Half-Opened Windows''''' is an Armenian talk show hosted by Armenian presenter-producer Hrach Muradyan. Starting in 2010, it is the longest run Armenian talk show ever. In 2011, it was named the best talk show of the year. It aims to address difficult social issues. &&&&& &&&&& Controversies &&&&& In 2016, the talk show had a gay guest, who received some hate speech during the show. He was specifically discriminated for being gay by lawyer Garik Galikyan and producer Sargis Mikayelyan.
