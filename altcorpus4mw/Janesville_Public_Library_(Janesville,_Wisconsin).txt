Janesville Public Library (Janesville, Wisconsin) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Janesville Public Library''' building is located in Janesville, Wisconsin. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& A Carnegie library, the building housed it until 1968, when library operations were moved elsewhere. It has since been used as an auditorium and a senior center. Located in the South Main Street Historic District, it was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1981. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
