Ingham Mills, New York &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Ingham Mills''' is a hamlet located in the Town of Oppenheim in Fulton County and partially in the Town of Manheim in Herkimer County. It was founded in 1802 with the construction of grist and saw mills. Kyser Lake is located north of Ingham Mills. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
