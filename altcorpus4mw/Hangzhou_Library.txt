Hangzhou Library &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Hangzhou Library''' () is a public library located at East Jiefang Road, Jianggan District of Hangzhou, Zhejiang, China. Hangzhou Library has a collection of over 2.8 million items, with 31 thousand newspapers, 40 thousand Republic of China books, 5058 ancient books, and 1 thousand and 4 hundred rubbings. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& Hangzhou Library was established in July 1958 at Youth Road, Hangzhou, Zhejiang, China. &&&&& &&&&& In the mid-1980s, the library was relocated to Huansha Road. &&&&& &&&&& In 1994, it was designated as a national first-grade library. &&&&& &&&&& In 2003, it became a member of the International Federation of Library Associations and Institutions. &&&&& &&&&& In October 2008, the Hangzhou Municipal Government appropriated 400 million yuan for constructing the new library. &&&&& &&&&& In 2010, the library was listed among the second batch of the "Key Protection Units of Ancient Books in China" by the State Council of China. &&&&& &&&&& Gallery &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
