Intelliwave Technologies &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Intelliwave Technologies Inc.''' is a software company headquartered in Alberta, Canada. providing tracking and control solutions for construction, fabrication and maintenance projects. SiteSense web and mobile software, developed by Intelliwave Technologies, provides a full suite of site material control and location tracking products, including equipment and workforce tracking components for one all-inclusive solution catering to the supply chain and construction projects. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& History/Timeline &&&&& Intelliwave Technologies was formed to provide new solutions in the construction industry to help increase "Time on Tools" for craft labor and improve site safety. Initially focused on the oil sands in Alberta, Intelliwave now serves clients globally in oil and gas, power, chemical, mining, civil, and in buildings construction projects. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Year &&&&& &&&&& Details &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2007 &&&&& &&&&& Intelliwave Technologies was founded in Leduc, Alberta. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2007 &&&&& &&&&& SiteSense software launches with RFID and GPS tracking for construction materials. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2008 &&&&& &&&&& SiteSense offered as a cloud software as a service product. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2009 &&&&& &&&&& SiteSense mobile app with full background sync launched. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2010 &&&&& &&&&& Workforce tracking tools such as automated mustering added to SiteSense. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2011 &&&&& &&&&& Remote Operating Visibility Enabled Reader (ROVER) added. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2012 &&&&& &&&&& Construction equipment tracking and maintenance added to SiteSense. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2014 &&&&& &&&&& Preventive maintenance for plant tagged equipment added to SiteSense. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2015 &&&&& &&&&& Full site material control procedures added to SiteSense. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2016 &&&&& &&&&& A suite of dashboards and analytics added to SiteSense. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2017 &&&&& &&&&& Automated workforce timecard tracking by zone added to SiteSense. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 2017 &&&&& &&&&& Blockchain technology is integrated into SiteSense transaction records. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Awards &&&&& *2012 – CME-MEC Regional Awards for New Technology &&&&& *2013 – Winner of the Bentley "Be Inspired" Award &&&&& *2014 – Gold Winner of the Connected World Awards &&&&& *2016 – Top 20 Construction Tech Solution Providers by CIOReview &&&&& *2017 – Top Product Award Winner from Constructech Magazine &&&&& *2017 – EY Entrepreneur Of The Year 2017 Prairies finalists &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
