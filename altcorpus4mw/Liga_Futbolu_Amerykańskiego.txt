Liga Futbolu Amerykańskiego &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Liga Futbolu Amerykańskiego''' ('''LFA''') is an amateur American football league in Poland. Founded in 2017 after a split in the Polish American Football Association. It consists of 22 teams competing in two conferences, the LFA1 and LFA2.
