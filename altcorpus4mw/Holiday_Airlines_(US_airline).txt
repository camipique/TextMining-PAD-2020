Holiday Airlines (US airline) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Holiday Airlines''' was an intrastate airline in the United States. Holiday operated scheduled passenger service with Lockheed L-188 Electra turboprops in California primarily serving Lake Tahoe Airport from both the Los Angeles-Burbank-San Diego area and the San Francisco Bay Area during the 1960s and 1970s. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
