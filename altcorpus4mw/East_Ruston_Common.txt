East Ruston Common &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''East Ruston Common''' is a biological Site of Special Scientific Interest south-east of North Walsham om Norfolk. &&&&& &&&&& This is an area of unimproved fen, heath and carr woodland in the valley of a tributary of the River Ant. it is the only known English locality for the rare spider, ''acanthophyma gowerensis'', and it also has another nationally rare spider, ''hygrolycosa rubrofasciata''. &&&&& &&&&& There is public access to the common. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
