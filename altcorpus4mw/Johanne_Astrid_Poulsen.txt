Johanne Astrid Poulsen &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Johanne Astrid Poulsen''' (born in 2006) is a Danish drummer, best known as a winner of ''Denmark's Got Talent'' TV show. Since her final performance, she became viral on social media sites and has received recognition from both Danish and foreign stars for her abilities. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Johanne Astrid was born in Viborg, Denmark. At early age, parents got her a toy drum set which was replaced with ''Drum Limousine'' drum kit when she became 7 years old. In 2016 she played with a real band for the first time, followed by first live performance for the audience.
