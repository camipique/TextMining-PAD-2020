Daniel Isn't Real &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Daniel Isn't Real''' is a 2019 American thriller film, directed by Adam Egypt Mortimer, from a screenplay by Mortimer and Brian DeLeeuw, based upon the novel ''In This Way I Was Saved'' by DeLeeuw.
