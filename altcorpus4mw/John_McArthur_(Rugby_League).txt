John McArthur (Rugby League) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''John McArthur''' (born 20 October 1964) is an Australian former professional rugby league footballer of the 1980s and 1990s. McArthur started his first grade career at Western Suburbs in 1984 which was a tough year for the club on the field where they only claimed one victory in the entire season and finished last on the table.
