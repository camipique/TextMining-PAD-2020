The company is based in Santa Monica, California and was founded by Matthew Gunnin. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& On February 27, 2017 it was announced that Esports One, formerly known as ''Esportsology'', would be part of Quake Capitalâ€™s Spring Startup Accelerator for 2017.
