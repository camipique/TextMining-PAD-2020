Haima M6 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Haima M6''' is a compact sedan produced in China since 2013 under the Haima brand replacing the Haima 3 or Haima Family compact cars. The Haima M6 debuted on the 2013 Shanghai Auto Show in April 2013 as a pre-production model with the official market launch in April 2015.
