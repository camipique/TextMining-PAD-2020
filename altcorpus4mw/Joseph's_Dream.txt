Joseph's Dream &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Joseph's Dream''''' is a 1620s painting by Daniele Crespi, now in the Kunsthistorisches Museum in Vienna. It shows an angel appearing to Joseph of Nazareth in his sleep to warn him of Herod the Great's intent to kill Jesus and to instruct him to flee into Egypt (Matthew 2:13). &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
