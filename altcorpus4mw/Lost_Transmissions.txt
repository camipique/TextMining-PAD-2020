Lost Transmissions &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Lost Transmissions''''' is an upcoming independent drama film, written and directed by Katharine O'Brien. It stars Simon Pegg, Juno Temple, Alexandra Daddario, Tao Okamoto, Rosanna Arquette and Bria Vinaite. &&&&& &&&&& The film will have its world premiere at the Tribeca Film Festival on April 28, 2019. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& Hannah (Juno Temple), a shy songwriter, discovers that her friend, respected record producer Theo Ross (Simon Pegg), has lapsed on his medication for schizophrenia. Hannah rallies a group of friends to help commit Theo to a psychiatric hospital, chasing him as he outruns his colorful delusions through the glamour and grit of Los Angeles.
