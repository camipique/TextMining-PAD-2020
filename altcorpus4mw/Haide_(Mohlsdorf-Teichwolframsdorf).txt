46	Haide_(Mohlsdorf-Teichwolframsdorf)
Haide (Mohlsdorf-Teichwolframsdorf) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Haide''' is a hamlet in the municipality of Mohlsdorf-Teichwolframsdorf in the Greiz district of Thuringia, southwest of the hamlet Gottesgrün, located on the Neumark–Greiz railway. The residential area consists of two farmsteads and three other residential buildings. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
