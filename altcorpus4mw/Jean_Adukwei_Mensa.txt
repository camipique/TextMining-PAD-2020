Jean Adukwei Mensa &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jean Adukwei Mensa''' is a Ghanaian lawyer and Chairperson of the Electoral Commission of Ghana. &&&&& Before her assumption of office at the Electoral Commission, she was the Executive Director of the Institute of Economic Affairs (IEA), and Co-ordinator of the Ghana Political Parties Programme. &&&&& &&&&& Early life and education &&&&& Jean Adukwei Mensa had her secondary education at the St Mary's Senior High School in Korle Gonno. She had her university education at the School of Law, University of Ghana, Legon. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& Jean Adukwei Mensa was appointed as the Chairperson of the Electoral Commission of Ghana on 23 July 2018. She has been involved in the development of policies such as the Presidential Transition Act of 2012, the Revised 1992 Constitution of Ghana (draft), the Political Parties Funding Bill, and the Revised Political Parties Bill. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
