Erodium astragaloides &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Erodium astragaloides''''' is a species of flowering plant in the geranium family endemic to Sierra Nevada, southern Spain. &&&&& &&&&& Habitat &&&&& The distinctive species can grow in sparse grasslands on less developed rocky-sandy dolomitic soils. It is associated with ''Juniperus phoenicea'', as a part of communities which are rich at endemic species, such as ''Rothmaleria granatensis'', ''Helianthemum pannosum'', ''Silene boryi'', ''Convolvulus boissieri'', ''Anthyllis tejedensis'', ''Thymus granatensis'', and ''Saxifraga erioblasta''. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
