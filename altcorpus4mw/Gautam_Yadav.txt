Gautam Yadav &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Gautam Yadav''' is an Indian gay rights activist. Gautam has been on board of several national and international organisations including UNAIDS Youth advisory forum (Asia Pacific), Youth Voices Count, Youth LEAD and more. Gautam was awarded with LGBT heroes award in Bangkok by APCOM in 2017.
