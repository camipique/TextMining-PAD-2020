Dron &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Dron''' is a village / panchayat, located in the Gir Gadhada Taluka of Gir Somnath district in Gujarat State, India. Earlier, until August 2013, Dron was part of Una Taluka and Junagadh district. The latitude 20.928161 and longitude 70.949985 are the geo-coordinate of the Village Dron. Gandhinagar is the state capital of Dron village which is located around 362 kilometres away from Dron. &&&&& &&&&& According to Census 2011, with the 510 families, the population of this village is 2892. Out of this, 1485 are males and 1407 are females. Most residents are dependent on agriculture. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Demographics &&&&& &&&&& According to the 2011 census of India, Dron has 510 households.
