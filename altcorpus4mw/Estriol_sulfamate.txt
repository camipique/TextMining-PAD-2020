Estriol sulfamate &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Estriol sulfamate''' (developmental code name '''J1034'''), or '''estriol 3-''O''-sulfamate''', is a synthetic estrogen and estrogen ester which was never marketed. It is the C3 sulfamate ester of estriol. The drug shows substantially improved oral estrogenic potency (vagina, uterus) relative to estriol in rats but without an increase in hepatic estrogenic potency.
