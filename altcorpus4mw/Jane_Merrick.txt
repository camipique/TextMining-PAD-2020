Jane Merrick &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Jane Merrick''' is a journalist who is the former political editor of ''The Independent on Sunday'' and a founding member of ''The Spoon''. She currently writes for ''The Daily Telegraph, The Observer'' and ''The Times''. &&&&& &&&&& Michael Fallon &&&&& &&&&& In 2017, Former Defence Secretary, Michael Fallon had been forced to resign in part due to an allegation of inappropriate behavior towards Merrick. Fallon was accused of having "lunged" at Merrick a decade and a half earlier. &&&&& &&&&& Labour &&&&& Merrick joined Labour in 2016 to support Angela Eagle in the Labour leadership election. &&&&& &&&&& On 20 July 2018 Merrick announced her resignation from the Labour Party in an op-ed for ''The Times''; Merrick explained she would not vote Labour again until there was a new leader who "undoes the damage Corbyn has inflicted" and that she was leaving the party due to antisemitism. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Merrick is married to ''The Observer''s political editor, Toby Helm. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
