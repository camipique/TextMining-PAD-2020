Georgia Steel &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Georgia Steel''' (born 28 March 1998) is an English television personality and actress. She is known for appearing in the fourth series of ITV2 reality series ''Love Island'' in 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& In 2013, Steel made her television debut in two episodes of CBBC sitcom ''All at Sea'', portraying the role of Amanda Bryce. &&&&& &&&&& Steel rose to fame after appearing in the fourth series of ITV2 reality series ''Love Island'' in 2018. She finished in 8th place, alongside Sam Bird, after being eliminated on Day 47. In 2019, Steel appeared in the sixth series of British dating reality series ''Celebs Go Dating''. She also appeared in 5Star reality series ''Celebs on the Ranch'', which aired in April 2019. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Before fame, Steel trained as an actress at ALRA and worked part-time as a barmaid. She is also a trained dancer.  &&&&& &&&&& When Steel was 14, she began dating Will Sykes, but their relationship ended when Steel was 18. During ''Love Island'', Steel began dating Sam Bird. However, the couple broke up in October 2018.
