85	Lin'an_Prefecture
Lin'an Prefecture &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Lin'an Prefecture''' (1129–1277) was after 1138 the capital of the Southern Song dynasty (1127–1279). With over one million people by 1276, it was the most populous city in the world and according to Jacques Gernet, also the "most advanced". Lin'an Prefecture was located in modern northern Zhejiang around Hangzhou.
