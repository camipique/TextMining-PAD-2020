Live at Sweet Basil (Steve Lacy album) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Live at Sweet Basil''''' is a live album by saxophonist Steve Lacy's Sextet, which was recorded in New York in 1991 and first released on the RCA Novus label in 1992. &&&&& &&&&& Reception &&&&& &&&&& The Allmusic review by Scott Yanow stated "This live set is an excellent example of the group's unique music performed live. The versions of Lacy's five scalar originals are each at least ten minutes long and find the musicians playing with enthusiasm and consistent creativity.
