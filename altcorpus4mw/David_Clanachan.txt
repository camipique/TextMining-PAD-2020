David Clanachan &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''David F. Clanachan''' (born 1962) is Commissioner of the Canada Premier League (CPL), a post he has held since 2018. As Commissioner, Clanachan oversees all aspect of the league along with the Canadian Soccer Business (CSB). Clanachan is also the Chairman of Restaurant Brands International, Canada. He was named President and Chief Operating Officer of Tim Hortons in 2014, and has more than 35 years with the brand.
