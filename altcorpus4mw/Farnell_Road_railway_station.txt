Farnell Road railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Farnell Road railway station''' served the village of Farnell, Angus, Scotland from 1848 to 1956 on the Aberdeen Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened in 1848 by the Aberdeen Railway. It was situated north of and south of station.
