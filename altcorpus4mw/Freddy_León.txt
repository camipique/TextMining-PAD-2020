Freddy León &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Freddy Alberto León Aristizábal''' (born 24 September 1970) is a retired Colombian football striker. &&&&& &&&&& He was born in Bogotá. He spent the main part of his career in Millonarios from 1990 through 2000, except a spell in Deportes Tolima from 1996 through 1998.
