Hora Uncle &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Hora Uncle''''' () is an upcoming Sri Lankan Sinhala children's film of debut direction by Saman Dharmawansha and co-produced by director himself with Nishantha Senarathna. It stars Mahendra Perera and Dulani Anuradha in lead roles along with Janaka Kumbukge and Kumara Thirimadura.
