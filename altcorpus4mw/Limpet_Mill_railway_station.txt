Limpet Mill railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Limpet Mill railway station''' served the village of Muchalls, Kincardineshire, Scotland from 1849 to 1850 on the Aberdeen Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened in 1849 by the Aberdeen Railway. It was situated north of and south of station.
