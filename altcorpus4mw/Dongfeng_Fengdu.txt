Dongfeng Fengdu &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Launched during the 2013 Shanghai Auto Show, Fengdu (风度) is a sub-brand of Zhengzhou Nissan, a subsidiary of Dongfeng Automobile. As the aim of he Fengdu brand is to focus on producing affordable CUVs, the product line of the brand started by producing out of production Nissan CUVs bearing the Dongfeng logo . &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& Dongfeng Automobile Company has decided to diversify the Dongfeng CUV product line. For this purpose the sub-brand of Fengdu was created. Older Nissan tooling was set up in China by Zhengzhou-Nissan to produce the second generation Nissan X-Trail rebadged as the Dongfeng Fengdu MX6. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Products &&&&& The current Fengdu range comprises the following models: &&&&& *Fengdu MX3 &&&&& *Fengdu MX5, a compact five-door CUV based on the Fengshen AX7 &&&&& *Fengdu MX6, a compact five-door CUV based on the Nissan X-Trail &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Further reading &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
