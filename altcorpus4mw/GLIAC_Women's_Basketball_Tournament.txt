GLIAC Women's Basketball Tournament &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Great Lakes Intercollegiate Athletic Conference (GLIAC) Women's Basketball Tournament''' is the annual conference basketball championship tournament for the Great Lakes Intercollegiate Athletic Conference. The tournament has been held annually since 1991. It is a single-elimination tournament and seeding is based on regular season records. &&&&& &&&&& The winner, declared conference champion, receives the GLIAC's automatic bid to the NCAA Women's
