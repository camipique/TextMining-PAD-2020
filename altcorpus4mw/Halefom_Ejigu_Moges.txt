Halefom Ejigu Moges &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& Major General '''Halefom Ejigu Moges''' is an Ethiopian major general who was acting Head of Mission of the United Nations Interim Security Force for Abyei (UNISFA) from 2014-2015.
