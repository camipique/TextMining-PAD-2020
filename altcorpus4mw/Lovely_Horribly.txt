Lovely Horribly &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Lovely Horribly''''' () is a South Korean television series starring Park Si-hoo and Song Ji-hyo with Lee Gi-kwang, Hahm Eun-jung and Choi Yeo-jin. It aired on KBS2 from August 13 to October 2, 2018, every Monday and Tuesday at 22:00 (KST). &&&&& &&&&& Synopsis &&&&& Philip (Park Si-hoo) and Eul-soon (Song Ji-hyo) are bound by an unusual fate: one’s happiness always results in the other’s misfortune.
