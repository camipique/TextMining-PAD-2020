John Arbanas &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''John Arbanas''' (born 7 February 1970) is a former professional tennis player from Australia. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Arbanas, a Victorian, played mostly in satellite tournaments, but is notable for featuring in the main draw as a qualifier at the 1991 Australian Open.
