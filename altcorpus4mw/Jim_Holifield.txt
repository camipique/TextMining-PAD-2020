Jim Holifield &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''James Lee Holifield''' (born January 18, 1946) is a former American football player and coach. He was selected by the New York Giants in the 1968 NFL/AFL Draft and played with the Giants in the National Football League (NFL) from 1968 to 1969.
