International Military Staff &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''International Military Staff''' ('''IMS''') is an advisory body of the North Atlantic Treaty Organisation's (NATO) Military Committee (MC), which in turn supports the North Atlantic Council (NAC).
