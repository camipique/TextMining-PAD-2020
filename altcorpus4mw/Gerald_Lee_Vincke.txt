Gerald Lee Vincke &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& __NOTOC__ &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Gerald Lee Vincke''' (born July 9, 1964) is an American prelate of the Catholic Church. He was appointed bishop for the Diocese of Salina in 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& On July 12, 1999, Vincke was ordained to the priesthood. Pope Francis appointed Vincke bishop for the Diocese of Salina on June 13, 2018. His was consecrated as bishop on August 22, 2018. Vincke was employed at Father Gabriel Richard Catholic High School in Ann Arbor Michigan. &&&&& &&&&& In September 2018, the Archdiocese of Washington announced that disgraced former cardinal Theodore McCarrick would reside at St. Fidelis Church in Victoria, Kansas.
