Francisco Griéguez &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Francisco Griéguez Pina''' (25 October 1918 – 20 June 2018) was a Spanish soldier during Second World War. He was transferred to Mauthausen concentration camp on 3 April 1940, and was released on 5 May 1945 thanks to United States troops. &&&&& &&&&& In 1946 he was transferred to Gardanne, where he got married with Juana, and in 1975, after the death of Francisco Franco, he could return to Spain. &&&&& &&&&& He died on 20 June 2018 at the age of 99. He was one of the last Spanish Mauthausen concentration camp survivors. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
