Geng Shuang &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Geng Shuang''' (; born April 1973) is a Chinese politician currently serving as deputy director of the Foreign Ministry Information Department of the People's Republic of China. He is the 30th spokesperson since the spokesperson system was established in the ministry back in 1983. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Geng was born in Beijing in April 1973. He obtained a master's degree in literature from Tufts University in 2006. &&&&& &&&&& Beginning in 1995, he served in several posts in the Foreign Ministry of the People's Republic of China, including staff member, secretary, counsellor, and division director. &&&&& &&&&& He was counsellor of the Chinese Embassy in the United States in 2011, and held that office until 2015. &&&&& &&&&& In 2015, he returned to Beijing and appointed the counsellor of Foreign Ministry's International Economic Division. &&&&& &&&&& He was elevated to deputy director of the Foreign Ministry Information Department of the People's Republic of China in 2016. On September 26, he became a spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Geng is married and has a daughter. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
