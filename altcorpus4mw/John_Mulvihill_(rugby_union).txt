John Mulvihill (rugby union) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''John Mulvihill''' (born 1966 ) is an Australian professional rugby union football coach. He is currently the head coach of the Cardiff Blues team that plays in the Pro14 competition.
