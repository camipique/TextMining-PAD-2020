58	Eociconia
Eociconia &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Eociconia''' is a extinct genus of primitive stork, and one of the earliest members of Ciconiiformes. The holotype, which is a metatarsus, was found in Yixibaila Formation of Xinjiang, China, dated back to the Eocene period. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& * ig. 5. Distal end of the tarsometatarsus. A.
