HMS Glasgow (Type 26 frigate) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''HMS ''Glasgow''''' is the first Type 26 frigate to be built for the United Kingdom's Royal Navy. The Type 26 class will partially replace the navy's thirteen Type 23 frigates, and will be a multi-mission warship designed to support anti-submarine warfare, air defence and general purpose operations. &&&&& &&&&& The ship is being assembled on the River Clyde in Glasgow. The first steel was cut for ''Glasgow'' in July 2017 with the ship expected to be delivered in 2023. In January 2018, the first hull section was completed and work started on the second hull section. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
