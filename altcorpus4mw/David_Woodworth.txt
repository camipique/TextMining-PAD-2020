David Woodworth &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Gerald Mark David Woodworth''' (1939 - 1994) was an Anglican priest in Ireland. &&&&& &&&&& Woodworth was educated at Trinity College, Dublin and the Church of Ireland Theological College; and ordained in 1965. After curacies in Dublin he held incumbencies at Kilkenny then Bandon.
