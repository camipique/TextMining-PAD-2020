HMS Montford &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''HMS ''Montford''''' was one of 23 boats of the of patrol boats built for the Royal Navy in the 1950s. &&&&& &&&&& Their names were all chosen from villages ending in ''-ford''. This boat was named after Montford, Shropshire. She was launched on and sold to Nigeria on . Renamed '''NNS ''Ibadan''''', she was captured at the declaration of independence of Biafra by independantists in Calabar.
