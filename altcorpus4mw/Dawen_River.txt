Dawen River &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Dawen River''' () or River Dawenhe is a river in Shandong Province, China. &&&&& The main branch of the river originates from Yiyuan, flows through Xintai, Laiwu, and merges with Chaiwen River at Dawenkou (, it literally translates into river mouth of Dawen) . &&&&& The merged river is also known as Mouwen River () which flows through Tai'an, Feicheng, empties into Dongping Lake which enters Yellow River. &&&&& The section of the river from Dongping onwards is also known as Daqing River () . &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& *List of rivers in China &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
