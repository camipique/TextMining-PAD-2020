Le Musée de la mer &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''The Aquarium of Biarritz''', or '''le Musée de la Mer''' (Museum of the Sea), is an aquarium in Biarritz. The current building, in art deco style, was opened in 1933. It went through the first renovation in 1992 to add an important collection of marine animals. An extension was added between 2009 and 2011 which doubled the visiting area and added more tanks, at the expense of space for birds. &&&&& &&&&& The aquarium &&&&& The museum is laid out across 4 floors: &&&&& * various tanks of marine life from the Bay of Biscay &&&&& * information on fishing, cetaceans, and the history of whaling, as well as temporary exhibits &&&&& * the turtle tank the underwater part of the seal tank &&&&& * the terrace and the outdoor part of the seal tank, then with the extensions, includes the north Atlantic tanks, the Caribbean exhibit, the Indo-Pacific exhibit (including the shark tank), and the gift shop &&&&& &&&&& Gallery &&&&& &&&&& Image:Biarritz.
