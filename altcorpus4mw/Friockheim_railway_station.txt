Friockheim railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Friockheim railway station''' served the village of Friockheim, Angus, Scotland from 1848 to 1955 on the Arbroath and Forfar Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened in 1848 by the Aberdeen Railway. It was situated north of and south of station. The goods yard was to the south.
