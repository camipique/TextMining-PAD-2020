Immaculate Nakisuuyi &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Immaculate Nakisuuyi''' (born 26 January 1996) is a Ugandan cricketer. In July 2018, she was named in Uganda's squad for the 2018 ICC Women's World Twenty20 Qualifier tournament. She made her Women's Twenty20 International (WT20I) for Uganda against Scotland in the World Twenty20 Qualifier on 7 July 2018.
