Geumganggul Cave &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& View from Geumganggul Cave &&&&& '''Geumganggul Cave''' is a cave located in Seoraksan National Park in Sokcho, South Korea. The cave is in the rocks of the mountain and is around 600 meters above sea level. It was once a place of worship and contains a Buddha stone. At the end of the route, hikers need to use stairs and bridges.
