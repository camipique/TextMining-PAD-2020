John Adam Squire House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''John Adam Squire House''' is a historic house in Palo Alto, California. It was built in 1904-1905 for John Adam Squire, a Greek professor. Squire lived here with his wife Georgina and their three daughters until his death in 1930. His wife died in 1959. &&&&& &&&&& The house was designed by architect T. Paterson Ross in the Neoclassical architectural style. It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since March 6, 1972. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 	 &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
