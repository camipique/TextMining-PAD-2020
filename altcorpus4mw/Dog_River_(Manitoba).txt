Dog River (Manitoba) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Dog River''' is a river in Census division 23 in Northern Manitoba, Canada. It is in the Hudson Bay drainage basin and is a right tributary of the Churchill River. &&&&& &&&&& The Dog River begins at an unnamed lake and flows east, then turns north, heading roughly parallel to the Deer River, which it takes in as a right tributary, and reaches its mouth at the Churchill River, upstream of that river's own mouth at Hudson Bay. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
