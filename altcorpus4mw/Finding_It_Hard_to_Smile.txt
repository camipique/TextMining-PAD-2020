Finding It Hard to Smile &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Finding It Hard to Smile''''' (stylized as "'''finding it hard to smile'''") is the debut studio album by indie pop band Lovelytheband, released on August 3, 2018. The forthcoming album was announced on June 29, 2018, along with the release of the track "Alone Time". &&&&& &&&&& Prior to its release, the band had gained a reputation through their opening performances for several bands, including Awolnation and 5 Seconds of Summer. After said tours, they ventured on a festival tour throughout the summer of 2018. The album was promoted with two singles: "Broken" and "These Are My Friends", as well as two promotional singles: "Alone Time" and "Pity Party". &&&&& &&&&& The tracklist includes the songs released on the band's debut EP, ''Everything I Could Never Say'', excluding "Strangers" and "Don't Worry, You Will". &&&&& &&&&& Reception &&&&& Upon release, the album reached 168 on the US ''Billboard'' 200. &&&&& &&&&& Track listing &&&&& Credits adapted from Tidal. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& *All tracks are stylized in lowercase letters. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
