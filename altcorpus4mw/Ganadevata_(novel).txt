74	Ganadevata_(novel)
Ganadevata (novel) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Ganadevata''''' (People as God, first published 1942), is a Bengali novel written by Tarasankar Bandyopadhyay. The author received Jnanpith Award in 1966 for this novel. In this novel, Bandyopadhyay narrated the lives of Indian/Bengal villages and lives of the villagers affected by poverty, ignorance and primitive instinct. The novel has been translated into several languages.
