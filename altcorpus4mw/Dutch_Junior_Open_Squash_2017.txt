Dutch Junior Open Squash 2017 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Dutch Junior Open Squash 2017''' is the men's edition of the 2017 Dutch Junior Open Squash, which is a World Junior Squash Circuit Tier 2 event. The event took place at the Frans Otten Stadion from July 13 to 16.
