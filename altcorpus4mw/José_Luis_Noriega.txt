José Luis Noriega &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''José Luis Noriega''' (born 14 September 1969) is a former professional tennis player from Peru. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Noriega, who grew up in the Peruvian capital Lima, was a four-time All-American collegiate tennis player at the University of San Diego (USD). Known by his nickname "Tato", he attended USD from 1989 to 1992 and amassed a 138-30 win-loss record across singles and doubles. &&&&& &&&&& From 1992 to 1994 he competed on the professional tennis tour, where he had a best ranking of 161 in the world. He won a Challenger title in Brazil in 1993 and made the final round of US Open qualifying the following year.
