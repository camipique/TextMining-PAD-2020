Epoch (video game) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Epoch''''' is an Apple II game written by Larry Miller and published by Sirius Software in 1981. &&&&& &&&&& Gameplay &&&&& ''Epoch'' is game in which the galaxy is being overrun by enemy ships, and the player needs to destroy as many of them as possible. &&&&& &&&&& Reception &&&&& Ron Boerger reviewed ''Epoch'' in ''The Space Gamer'' No. 48. Boerger commented that "this is an excellent game. If you like arcade-type games, ''Epoch'' is a must; it's among the best out for the Apple." &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
