Holy Family with the Infant Saint John the Baptist (Rosso Fiorentino) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& 340px &&&&& '''''Holy Family with the Infant Saint John the Baptist''''' is an unfinished c.1521 oil on panel painting by Rosso Fiorentino, produced early in his stay in Volterra. The Christ Child's pose is influenced by that in Andrea del Sarto's ''Madonna of the Harpies'' (1517). The work is now in the Walters Art Museum in Baltimore. &&&&& &&&&& Rosso &&&&& category:Paintings of the Walters Art Museum, Baltimore &&&&& category:1521 paintings &&&&& &&&&&
