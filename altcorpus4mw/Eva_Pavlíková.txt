Eva Pavlíková &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Eva Pavlíková''' (born 17 September 1960) is a Slovak actress. At the 2004 Crystal Wing Awards she won in the theatre category, for her work at the Andrej Bagar Theatre in Nitra.
