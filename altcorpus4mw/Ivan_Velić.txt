Ivan Velić &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Ivan Velić''' (born October 19, 1972) is a Bosnian basketball coach and former professional player. He currently serves as a head coach for Cibona of the ABA League and the Croatian League. &&&&& &&&&& ==Coaching career== &&&&& Velić spent most of his coaching career with Široki. He had two stints with Jolly JBŠ of the Croatian League, and in 2016–17 season he won second place in Croatian Cup and he advanced to the playoffs in the Croatian League. On June 21, 2015, Ivan Velić was hired as a new head coach of Slovenian team Krka. In 2018, he coached Sloboda Tuzla. &&&&& &&&&& On June 8, 2018, Velić was named a head coach for Croatian team Cibona. &&&&& &&&&& === National teams === &&&&& Velić was the head coach in the past of both the Bosnia and Herzegovina national U18 team as well as the Croatian U20 national team. Also, he was an assistant coach for Bosnia and Herzegovina national basketball team under head coach Duško Ivanović.
