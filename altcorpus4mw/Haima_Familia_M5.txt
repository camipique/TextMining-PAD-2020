Haima Familia M5 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& Haima Familia M5 rear &&&&& The '''Haima Familia M5''' is a compact sedan produced in China since 2014 for the model year 2015 under the Familia (福美来) product series within the Haima brand and sold alongside the similarly sized Haima M6. Originally launched as the third generation Haima Family sedan, the model was later renamed to Haima Familia M5, replacing the Haima 3 or Haima Family compact cars.
