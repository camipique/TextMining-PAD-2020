DPT-Hib vaccine &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''DPT-Hib vaccine''' is a combination vaccine whose generic name is diphtheria and tetanus toxoids and whole-cell pertussis vaccine adsorbed with Hib conjugate vaccine, sometimes abbreviated to DPT-Hib. It protects against the infectious diseases diphtheria, tetanus, pertussis, and Haemophilus influenzae type B. &&&&& &&&&& A branded formulation was marketed in the USA as Tetramune by Lederle Praxis Biologicals (subsequently acquired by Wyeth). Tetramune has since been discontinued. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
