Jerzy Młynarczyk &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jerzy Młynarczyk''' (2 August 1931 – 9 September 2017) was a Polish politician and basketball player. He competed in the men's tournament at the 1960 Summer Olympics. He was professor of law, including principal of University of Business and Administration in Gdynia and served as a Mayor of Gdansk in 1977–1981.
