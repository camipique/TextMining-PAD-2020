Franklin Najjumba &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Franklin Najjumba''' (born 3 December 1987) is a Uganda women's cricketer. In July 2018, she was named in Uganda's squad for the 2018 ICC Women's World Twenty20 Qualifier tournament.
