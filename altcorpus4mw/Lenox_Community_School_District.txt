Lenox Community School District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Lenox Community School District''' is a school district headquartered in Lenox, Iowa. &&&&& &&&&& It includes portions of Adams, Ringgold, Taylor, and Union counties. Communities in its area include Lenox, Clearfield, and Sharpsburg. &&&&& &&&&& The district began operations in 1878. The district has a single school building at 600 S. Locust St., built in 1968. The elementary school previously had a separate building at 301 W. Michigan Street but in 2005 moved into 600 S. Locust. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& &&&&& When the Clearfield Community School District closed in 2014, the Lenox district absorbed a portion of it. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * Lenox Community School District &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
