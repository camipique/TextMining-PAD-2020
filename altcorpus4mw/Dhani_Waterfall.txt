Dhani Waterfall &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Dhani Waterfall''' also known as '''Dhani Noseri Waterfall''' is located in Neelum Valley, Muzaffarabad District of Azad Kashmir in Pakistan. It is from Muzaffarabad District.
