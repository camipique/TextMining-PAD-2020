59	Iramsiphai
Iramsiphai &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Iramsiphai''' is a village situated in Manipur. The village is separated into two parts by Imphal River. The north part of the village belongs to Thoubal district and another southern part belongs to Imphal West districts of Manipur. The Thoubal part belongs to Lilong Constituency and the Imphal West part comes under Wangoi Assembly constituency. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
