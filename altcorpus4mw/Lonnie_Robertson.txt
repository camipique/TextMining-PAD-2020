Lonnie Robertson &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Lonnie Robertson''' was a Missouri fiddler. Born at Longrun, Missouri on January 8, 1908; he died in 1981. He recorded many of his tunes from the 1960s to his death in 1981, often on Caney Mountain Records. He played guitar and mandolin as well as fiddle, sang, and composed fiddle tunes. They also printed and sold song books.
