Her Highness Dances the Waltz (1935 film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Her Highness Dances the Waltz''''' (German: '''''Hoheit tanzt Walzer''''') is a 1935 musical comedy film directed by Max Neufeld and starring Irén Ágay, André Mattoni and . The operetta film is based on the 1912 operetta ''Hoheit tanzt Walzer'' by Leo Ascher (music) and Alfred Grünwald (libretto). It was made as an Austrian-Czech co-production at the Barrandov Studios in Prague. The film's sets were designed by the art director Artur Berger.
