Great Renewed National Alliance &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Great Renewed National Alliance''' (), also known as '''GANAR Alliance''' () or by its acronym '''GANAR''' (literally "WIN"), is a political coalition of opposition parties, formed in 2017 in Paraguay. It postulated the duo consisting of Efraín Alegre and Leonardo Rubín as president and vice president of the Republic, respectively, ahead of the 2018 general elections of Paraguay.
