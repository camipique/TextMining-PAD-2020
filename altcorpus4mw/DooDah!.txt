DooDah! &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''DooDah!'''" is a song by the Danish bubblegum dance group Cartoons. It was released in 1998 on FLEX Records and EMI as the debut single and as well as the second track from their debut studio album, ''Toonage'' (1998).
