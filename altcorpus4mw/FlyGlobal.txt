FlyGlobal &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''flyGlobal Charter Sdn Bhd''' is a Malaysian charter and wet lease operator based in Kuala Lumpur International Airport. Headquartered in Petaling Jaya, Malaysia the airline operates Boeing 777 aircraft to provide passenger Aircraft, Crew, Maintenance and Insurance (ACMI) wet leases. It focuses on the Asian and Middle Eastern charter and wet lease markets. The Malaysian Aviation Commission (MAVCOM) has granted flyGlobal with Air Service Permit (ASP) on 17 July 2017. &&&&& &&&&& Established in 2016, flyGlobal wet leased its first B777-200 aircraft in July 2017, to its launch client, Biman Bangladesh Airlines, the national flag carrier for Bangladesh, over the Hajj period.
