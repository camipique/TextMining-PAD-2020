Holocryptis erubescens &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Holocryptis erubescens''''' is a moth of the family Noctuidae first described by George Hampson in 1893. It is found in Sri Lanka, Thailand and Japan. &&&&& &&&&& Forewings brownish. There is a light yellow-brown oblique streak which runs from the center of the leading edge to the outer edge.
