67	Lahellefjord
Lahellefjord &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Marøyskjæra Island in the Lahellefjord. &&&&& '''Lahellefjord''' is a minor fjord-arm in the Tønsbergfjord in Sandefjord, Norway. It stretches from Natholmen Island to its base at Lahelle. The Lahellefjord is 5 kilometers long, and is one of four fjords located in Sandefjord. &&&&& &&&&& Islands and islets include Natholmen (by its mouth), Faraholmen, Trondskjær, Rauern, Marøyskjæra, and Møyern. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
