236	Footprints_(school)
Footprints (school) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& Footprint is a childcare and pre-school chain founded by Purvesh Sharma, Raj Singhal, and Ashish Aggarwal in 2013. They function on a hybrid franchise model and is based in Gurgaon, India. &&&&& &&&&& Overview &&&&& Footprints was founded by Purvesh Sharma, Raj Singhal, Ashish Aggarwal who were batchmates in IIT Delhi. It turned into a business idea when Purvesh was checking for daycare centers for his son and was not satisfied with the options available. So Purvesh along with his friends started Footprint which currently has centers across Gurgaon, Noida, Delhi, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad. It offers full-day daycare and preschool facility for children from three months to four years. &&&&& &&&&& Footprints raised over $2 million through angel investments and venture funding. &&&&& &&&&& Services &&&&& They also developed a technology solution using Reado stick that provides pre-programmed voice output and acts as an automated teaching assistant. Footprints have an all female staff who are given a twenty-one day training, are screened for criminal and medical backgrounds and follows an adult to infant ratio of 1:8. &&&&& &&&&& Curriculum &&&&& Footprints follow HighScope Early Childhood Education Approach which is developed by Michigan based non-profit organization HighScope Educational Research Foundation. The curriculum is based on age and is divided into; Toddlers program for 9-18 months, Playgroup program for 19-30 months, Pre-Nursery program for 31-42 months and Nursery program for 42 months and above. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
