La Covatilla &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''La Covatilla''' is a ski resort in the Central System of the Province of Salamanca, in Castile and León, Spain. The resort is in the municipality of La Hoya. &&&&& &&&&& Cycling &&&&& The resort is known for having held a stage finish, on several occasions, of the Vuelta a España.
