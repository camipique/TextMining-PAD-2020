Graeme Newman &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Graeme R. Newman''' (born December 13, 1939) is an American scholar of criminal justice and Distinguished Teaching Professor at University at Albany. He is a recipient of J. Francis Finnegan Memorial Prize in Criminology. &&&&& &&&&& Newman is the vice president of Center for Problem Oriented Policing and pioneered the establishment of the United Nations Crime and Justice Information Network.
