Duel with Samurai &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Duel with Samurai''''' is a 1971 Hong Kong ''wuxia'' film directed by Chin Sheng-en and stars Chan Hung-lit as a Rōnin who arrives in China during the Ming dynasty, causing havoc among the martial world. The film was released on 3 September 1971 in Taiwan, and five days later on 8 September 1971 in Hong Kong &&&&& &&&&& ==Plot== &&&&& During the Ming dynasty in China,the coastal areas were rampant. A Wokou who refers himself as the Nippon Ronin (Chan Hung-lit), colludes with the coastal local tyrants of Fujian and Zhejiang Provinces, along with cult religious groups. They occupy Baiyunzhuang and plundered everywhere.
