66	Llanomolpus
Llanomolpus &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Llanomolpus''''' is a genus of leaf beetles in the subfamily Eumolpinae. It was described by the Czech entomologist Jan Bechyné in his "Evaluación de los datos sobre los Phytophaga dañinos en Venezuela (Coleoptera)" before his death in 1973, but the work (including the description of the genus) was unpublished until October 1997.
