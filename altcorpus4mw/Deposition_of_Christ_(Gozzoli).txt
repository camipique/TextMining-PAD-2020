Deposition of Christ (Gozzoli) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& thumb &&&&& '''''Deposition of Christ''''' is a 1497 painting by Benozzo Gozzoli, bought by Herbert Horne in 1907 and now in the Museo Horne in Florence. It was restored in 1990. &&&&& &&&&& Among Gozzoli's last works, it was still unfinished in his studio by the time of his death. Years later his heirs sold it to the Diocese of Pistoia. It then passed to the Sozzifanti family, and from them, inherited by Charles Borbone, Duke of Lucca. &&&&& &&&&& Sources &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
