Losaline Ma'asi &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Losaline Ma'asi''' is a Tongan politician, Cabinet Minister, and a member of the Legislative Assembly of Tonga for Tongatapu 5. &&&&& &&&&& Ma'asi is a former chief executive of the Ministry of Agriculture. She was first elected to parliament in the 2017 election.
