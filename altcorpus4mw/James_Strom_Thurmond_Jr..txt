James Strom Thurmond Jr. &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& Thurmond and Gerald Ford in 1974 &&&&& '''James Strom Thurmond Jr.''' is a former United States Attorney for the District of South Carolina and 2nd Circuit Solicitor. He is the son of former United States Senator Strom Thurmond and Nancy Moore Thurmond. His younger brother, Paul is a former member of the South Carolina Senate. He is the half brother of the late author Essie Mae Washington-Williams, through his father.
