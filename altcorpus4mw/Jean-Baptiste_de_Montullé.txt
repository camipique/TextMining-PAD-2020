Jean-Baptiste de Montullé &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Jean-Baptiste'''-François '''de Montullé''' (3 February 1721 - 26 August 1787) was a French magistrate, who was born and died in Paris. His daughter Émilie-Sophie became an artist, whilst Jean-Baptiste himself was an art collector, though he had to sell off his collection thanks to financial difficulties at the end of 1783 - it included five paintings by Antoine Watteau.
