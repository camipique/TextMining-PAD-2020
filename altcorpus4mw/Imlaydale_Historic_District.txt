Imlaydale Historic District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Imlaydale Historic District''' in Hunterdon County, New Jersey and Warren County, New Jersey is a historic district which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1991. The listing included 12 contributing buildings, four contributing structures, and two contributing sites. &&&&& &&&&& It includes essentially all of the hamlet of '''Imlaydale''' in the Musconetcong River valley of southern Warren County.
