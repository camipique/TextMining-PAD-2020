Laps Around the Sun &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& ''''' Laps Around The Sun '''''is the second studio album by American country music artist Chris Lane. It was released on July 13, 2018, the album is published via Big Loud Records. &&&&& &&&&& Content &&&&& The lead single to the album is "Take Back Home Girl", a duet with Tori Kelly which has charted within Top 15 of Hot Country Songs and Country Airplay. This song, along with "All the Right Problems" and "Old Flame", previously appeared on an EP titled ''Take Back Home'' which was released digitally in late 2017. Of the album's content, Lane said, "My goal was to make sure anyone who hears this album finds a song they can relate to. I hope I accomplished that."
