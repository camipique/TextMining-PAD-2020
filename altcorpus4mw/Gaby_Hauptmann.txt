Gaby Hauptmann &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Gaby Hauptmann''' (born 14 May 1957) is a German journalist and writer. &&&&& &&&&& Life &&&&& Hauptmann was born in Trossingen in 1957. Her grandfather was the noted Black Forest based painter . She started her writing career at the Südkurier newspaper. She left after two years and formed her own business running a press office. She then became chief editor at the radio station from 1987. &&&&& &&&&& She began working for the broadcaster initially as a writer but in time a director and producer. &&&&& &&&&& Her first book was for children, but her first bestseller was "Suche impotenten Mann fürs Leben" which was made into the film . Her books have sold six million copies in total. &&&&&  &&&&& She has written novels in German, and filmscripts and has worked as a TV producer.
