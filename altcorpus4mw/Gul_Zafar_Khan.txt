Gul Zafar Khan &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Gul Zafar Khan''' is a Pakistani politician who has been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan, since August 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Early life &&&&& He used to work as a waiter before entering politics. ''The News International'' reported he was a millionaire. &&&&& &&&&& Political career &&&&& He was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan as a candidate of Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) from Constituency NA-41 (Tribal Area-II) in Pakistani general election, 2018. He received 22,730 votes and defeated an independent candidate, Qari Abdul Majeed. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
