Halil Dervişoğlu &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Halil İbrahim Dervişoğlu''' (born 8 December 1999) is a Turkish professional footballer who plays as a forward for the Dutch club Sparta Rotterdam. &&&&& &&&&& Professional career &&&&& Dervişoğlu joined the Sparta Rotterdam youth academy at the age of 10, and worked his way up their youth academy. Dervişoğlu made his senior debut for Sparta Rotterdam in a 2017–18 Eredivisie playoff win over FC Dordrecht on 10 May 2018, and assisted his side's game-winning goal in the 90th minute.
