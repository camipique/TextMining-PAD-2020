Her Highness Dances the Waltz (1926 film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Her Highness Dances the Waltz''''' (German: '''''Hoheit tanzt Walzer''''') is a 1926 Austrian silent romance film directed by Fritz Freisler and starring Claire Rommer, Magda Karmen and Walter Rilla.
