Damen 1800-class offshore patrol vessel &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Damen 1800 OPV''' is an offshore patrol vessel built by the Malaysian company THHE-Destini with the assistance of Damen company from the Netherlands. This class is based on the design of the Damen 1800 OPV vessel. A total of three ships of this class are planned to be built. &&&&& &&&&& Development &&&&& &&&&& Rear view of Damen 1800 OPV. &&&&& &&&&& The Damen 1800 OPV contract was awarded to THHE-Destini local company in early 2017. The ship contract is worth RM738.9 million and the construction started in December 2017. The first ship will be completed in mid-2020. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
