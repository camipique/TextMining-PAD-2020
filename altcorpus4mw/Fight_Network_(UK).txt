Fight Network (UK) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Fight Network''' is a British programming block that broadcasts nightly over the channel space of Showcase TV. The channel is owned by Anthem Sports & Entertainment available through Showcase TV at 9:00 PM every night. Programming features combat sports from around the world. The channel launched on 17 July 2018 and is based on the Canadian channel of the same name.
