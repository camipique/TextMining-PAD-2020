Livienne Russellia &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Livienne Russellia''' (born in Pontianak, March 4, 1976) is a businesswoman, socialite, and beauty expert in Indonesia. She holds a graduate degree in cosmetic pharmacy from the Universitas Pancasila. &&&&& &&&&& Russellia is not only active in business and the beauty industry, but also in other organizations such as PPA Kosmetika Indonesia and Lions Club. In 2017 she was awarded the Top 10 Influenced Women Award by Asosiasi Duta Indonesia (ADI). In 2015 she also received the MNC Lifestyle award. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& Livienne Russellia is listed as the commissioner of PT.
