Gary Turner (sideshow performer) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Gary Turner''' is a sideshow performer. He holds the current Guinness World Record for the stretchiest skin, caused by a serious form of Ehlers Danlos syndrome. On October 29, 1999, in Los Angeles, he stretched the skin on his abdomen to a total length of 6.25 inches, earning him the record. He performed in the short film ''He Took His Skin Off For Me''. &&&&& &&&&& When he was born, his midwife only mentioned that he had loose skin.
