Luksimi Sivaneswaralingam &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Luksimi Sivaneswaralingam''' (born 26 September 1993, age 25 years), is a playback singer and classical dancer originating from Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Luksimi was just three years old when she began her lessons in music. She is popular on YouTube. &&&&& &&&&& Her journey began as a Carnatic vocal student, and soon included lessons in Western classical and popular vocals, Bharathanatyam, Veena and Piano. Performing and participating in competitions, in both Carnatic and film genres, helped build her confidence as a vocalist. She has shared the stage internationally and in Canada with several renowned musicians and artists. &&&&& &&&&& Luksimi is a University of Toronto alumni, as she graduated from the University of Toronto Faculty of Music with a Bachelor of Music – Voice Performance Specialist (western classical), in addition to a Bachelor of Music – Carnatic Vocal from Annamalai University Canada Campus. Continuing with her education, in 2017 she completed her post graduate degree at Wilfrid Laurier University for curriculum based music programs.
