Detroit Regional LGBT Chamber of Commerce &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Detroit Regional LGBT Chamber of Commerce''' (@LGBTchamber) is a U.S. not-for-profit advocacy group that aims to expand the economic opportunities and advancement of the LGBT business community in Southeast Michigan. Its headquarters are in Detroit. The chamber is an affiliate of the exclusive certifying body for LGBT-owned businesses known as LGBT Business Enterprises (LGBTBEs). Since its inception, it was an advocate for the inclusion of LGBT identities at the national level to replace just lesbian and gay.
