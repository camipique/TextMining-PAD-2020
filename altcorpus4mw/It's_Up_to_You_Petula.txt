It's Up to You Petula &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& "'''It's Up to You Petula'''" is a popular song by "one-hit wonder" Edison Lighthouse. It was released as a non-album single, but was included on their 1971 compilation LP, ''Already''. &&&&& &&&&& The single became a modest international hit as a follow-up to the group's major hit during the previous year, "Love Grows (Where My Rosemary Goes)." It reached number 49 in the UK and number 18 in New Zealand.
