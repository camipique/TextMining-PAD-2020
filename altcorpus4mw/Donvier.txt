Donvier &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Donvier''' was a brand of popular Japanese-made ice cream makers, produced by Nippon Light Metal, and distributed in the US by Virginia Beach, Virginia-based Nikkal Industries from 1984 to 1990.
