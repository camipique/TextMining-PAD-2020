Hypena vestita &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Hypena vestita''''', is a moth of the family Erebidae first described by Moore in 1885. It is found in India, Sri Lanka and Borneo. &&&&& &&&&& Forewings uniform greyish brown. Submarginal dark marking distal to dentate.
