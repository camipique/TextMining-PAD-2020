Dan Lorge &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Daniel Peter Lorge''' (born February 8, 1962) is an American voice actor. He currently teaches acting at Take 2 Performers Studio in Reno, Nevada. &&&&& &&&&& Due to his pseudonym of "Dan Martin", for the longest time Lorge's roles were all mis-credited to the actor of the same name.
