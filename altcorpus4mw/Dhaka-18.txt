Dhaka-18 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Dhaka-18''' is a constituency represented in the Jatiya Sangsad (National Parliament) of Bangladesh since 2008 by Sahara Khatun of the Awami League. &&&&& &&&&& Boundaries &&&&& The constituency encompasses Dhaka North City Corporation wards 1 and 17, and Dakshinkhan, Khilkhet, Turag, Uttara, and Uttar Khan thanas. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The constituency was created when, ahead of the 2008 general election, the Election Commission redrew constituency boundaries to reflect population changes revealed by the 2001 Bangladesh census.
