DN73A (Romania) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''DN73A''' is a national road that starts from Predeal, reaching Șercaia, Brașov. &&&&& &&&&& Road &&&&& The national road ''DN73A ''starts from the entrance to Predeal, coming from DN1 from Bucharest, goes by Cold River, goes down to Burzenland in Râșnov, where it intersects with DN73. Then it goes through ''Old Tohan ''neighborhood of Zărnești city, reaches the Poiana Mărului and then it goes down to Țara Făgărașului, goes to Șinca Nouă, and Sinca Veche. Between Sinca Veche and Ohaba, DN73A has a common road with ''DJ104''. From Ohaba, it goes through Vad and it ends at Șercaia, at the intersection with DN1.    &&&&& &&&&& Historic and architecture monuments &&&&& &&&&& * Cetatea Râșnov &&&&& * Comuna Șinca &&&&& ** ''Grota'' (''Monastery'') under ''Plesul hill, also known as Temple of Fate, from Sinca Veche''  &&&&& ** ''Monastic Hermitage Birth of Mary the Virgin  from Sinca Veche'' &&&&& ** ''The Great Cave from Meresti, in Perșani Mountains, discovered in the 18th century where it was found ceramics marks from the first Epoch of Steel (Hallstatt culture).'' &&&&& ** ''Bucium Monastery, near Bucium village'' &&&&& * ''Fortified Saxon Church'' ''from Sercaia, attested in 1429, owned by Saint Katherine.'' &&&&& * ''Fortified Evangelical-Lutheran church from Halmeag, built in the second half of the 11th century, which illustrates the passing phase from Romanesque architecture to Gothic art.'' &&&&& &&&&& Other places with touristic interests and nature monuments &&&&& &&&&& * Predeal &&&&& ** Ski area, with 5 areas &&&&& ** ''Climatic Station Cold River'' &&&&& * Zărnești &&&&& ** The City Day is celebrated each summer, in August: "Zarnesti city days"-"Edelweiss Festival" &&&&& ** National Park Stone of Crawl &&&&& * Comuna Șinca &&&&& ** ''Persani Baths  from Persani, with mineral springs '' &&&&& * Vad &&&&& ** Plugarul, folkloric habit that takes place in the second day of Easter &&&&& ** Poiana Narciselor from Vad, Nature reserve &&&&& &&&&& Bibliography &&&&& &&&&& * ADAC ''Straßenatlas Ost-Europa'', ADAC e.V.
