George Stephen Goodspeed &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''George Stephen Goodspeed''' (1860 - 1905) was an American historian and author. &&&&& &&&&& He specialized in Assyriology and Sumerian history &&&&& &&&&& Life &&&&& &&&&& He was born on 14 January 1860 in Janusville, Wisconsin. &&&&& &&&&& His son Thomas Harper Goodspeed went on to become a botanist. &&&&& &&&&& He died in 1905. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& &&&&& He graduated from Brown University in 1880. &&&&& &&&&& He briefly taught at the University of Chicago. He was a contemporary of James Henry Breasted.
