Jingfengmen station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jingfengmen station''' () is an interchange station under construction on Lines 14 and 19 of the Beijing Subway in Fengtai District. &&&&& &&&&& Planned opening time &&&&& Line 19: 2021 &&&&& &&&&& Line 14 (middle section): As of January 2019, the construction for Line 14 (middle section) near Lize Business District station has not fully started. The opening time of the Line 14 (middle section) is still unknown. &&&&& &&&&& Description &&&&& During early planning stages, it was known as You'anmenwai station (). In October 2015, it was renamed to Jingfengmen station. The name "Jingfengmen" derives from the old gate in the Jin dynasty capital, Zhongdu. &&&&& &&&&& Trains travelling past on the current eastern section of the Line 14 use a crossover at Jingfengmen to turn around. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
