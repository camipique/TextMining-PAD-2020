Estádio Municipal do Dande &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Estádio Municipal do Dande''' is a football stadium in Caxito, the capital city of Angola's Bengo Province. It is the current home stadium of first division side Domant FC.
