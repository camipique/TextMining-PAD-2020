Love County Courthouse &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Love County Courthouse''', located at 100 S. 4th St. in Marietta, Oklahoma, was built in 1907-10 to serve Love County. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1984. &&&&& &&&&& It is three stories tall, or two stories plus a raised basement.
