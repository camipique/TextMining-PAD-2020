Herpestoidea &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Herpestoidea''' is a superfamily of mammalia carnivores which includes mongooses, carnivores of Madagascar and the hyenas. &&&&& &&&&& Herpestoidea, with the exception of the hyenas, have a cylindrical and elongated body, which allows them to catch their prey and get into holes.
