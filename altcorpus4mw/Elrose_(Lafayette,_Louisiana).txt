Elrose (Lafayette, Louisiana) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Elrose''' is a historic house located at 217 West University Avenue in Lafayette, Louisiana. &&&&& &&&&& Built in 1900 by Crow Girard, the house is a large one-and-a-half story frame residence in Queen Anne style with Colonial Revival elements. The distinctive roofline comprises an octagonal corner turret, a central dormer, and a very large side dormer. &&&&& &&&&& The house was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on June 14, 1984. &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * National Register of Historic Places listings in Lafayette Parish, Louisiana &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
