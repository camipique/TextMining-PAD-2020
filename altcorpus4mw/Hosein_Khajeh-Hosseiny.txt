Hosein Khajeh-Hosseiny &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Hosein Khajeh-Hosseiny''' (born 1964) is a British private equity investor, venture capitalist and philanthropist. He is a senior advisor to McKinsey & Company, a Board Trustee of the Brookings Institution in Washington DC,and Be The Business, a U.K. organization created to close the U.K. productivity gap. He is the founder and Chairman of Open X Innovations LLC, an asset management firm. &&&&& &&&&& Khajeh-Hosseiny is the chairman of Trinity Sky LLP, a family investment office, and the founder and chairman of Open X Innovations LLC, a London and San Francisco-based investment, advisory and asset management firm with a focus on artificial intelligence-enabled enterprise technologies. He is the former CEO and Managing General Partner of Northgate Capital, a venture capital and private equity firm with offices in San Francisco, London and Mexico City. Prior to Northgate he was Head of Global Private Equity Investments at McKinsey's Investment Office (MIO Partners) in New York and London. &&&&& &&&&& Public service &&&&& In June 2018, Khajeh-Hosseiny was elected to the board of trustees of the Brookings Institution headquartered in Washington, D.C.In 2000, he helped found the U.K. non-profit, UnLtd (The U.K. Foundation for Social Entrepreneurs) and continues as a member of the organization's investment committee. In September 2018, he was elected to the Board of Trustee of Be the Business, an organization created to close the UK's productivity gap. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Khajeh-Hosseiny is married to Afsaneh Yasmin Khajeh-Hosseiny.
