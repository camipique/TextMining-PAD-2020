John M. T. Ford &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''John M. T. Ford''' is an English general practitioner physician and president of the History of Medicine Society of the Royal Society of Medicine from 1987 to 1988. His writings on medical history include "Medical Indications for Taking the Waters of Tunbridge Wells" (1984) and "R. E. Ford CBE, MD, MRCP" (1995).
