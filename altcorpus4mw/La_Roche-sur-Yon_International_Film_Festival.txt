La Roche-sur-Yon International Film Festival &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''The La Roche-sur-Yon International Film Festival''' is a film festival created in 2001. Renewed in 2010, it takes place every year in mid-October in the town of La Roche-sur-Yon in Vendée, France.
