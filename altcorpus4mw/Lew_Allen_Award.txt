Lew Allen Award &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Lew Allen Award''' is a medal of the Jet Propulsion Laboratory. Until 1990 it was called the ''Director’s Research Achievement Award;'' it was then renamed in honor of Lew Allen.
