Dance with Me Into the Morning &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Dance with Me Into the Morning''''' (German: '''''Tanze mit mir in den Morgen''''') is a 1962 Austrian musical film directed by Peter Dörre and starring Gerhard Wendland, Guggi Löwinger and Rex Gildo. &&&&& &&&&& The film's sets were designed by the art director Sepp Rothaur.
