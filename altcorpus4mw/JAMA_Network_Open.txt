JAMA Network Open &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''JAMA Network Open''''' is a monthly open access medical journal published by the American Medical Association covering all aspects of the biomedical sciences. It was established in 2018 and the founding editor-in-chief is Fred Rivara (University of Washington).
