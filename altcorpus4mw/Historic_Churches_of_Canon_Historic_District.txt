Historic Churches of Canon Historic District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Historic Churches of Canon Historic District''' in Canon, Georgia is a historic district which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1985. The listing included three contributing buildings. &&&&& &&&&& It covers three wood-framed churches: Canon Baptist Church, Canon Universalist Church, and Canon Methodist Church. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
