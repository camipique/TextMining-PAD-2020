Jupiter Capital Private Limited &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Jupiter Capital Private Limited''' is a Bengaluru-based private equity and venture capital firm investing in technology, media, and other start-ups. It was founded in 2005 by Rajeev Chandrasekhar.
