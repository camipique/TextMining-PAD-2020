Each manager's entry includes his dates of tenure and the club's overall competitive record (in terms of matches won, drawn and lost), where known as well as honours won and significant achievements while under his care. &&&&& &&&&& Managers &&&&& All first-team matches in national competitions are counted. Manager dates and statistics are sourced from Boroguide.co.uk. Statistics are complete up to and including 5 May 2018.
