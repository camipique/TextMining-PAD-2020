E.R. (Emergency Room) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& "'''E.R. (Emergency Room)'''" is a song by American R&B singer Joe. It was written by Dernst "D'Mile" Emile II, Norquon "Fats" Greg, and LaShawn Daniels for his seventh studio album ''Joe Thomas, New Man'' (2008), while production was helmed by D'Mile and Greg.
