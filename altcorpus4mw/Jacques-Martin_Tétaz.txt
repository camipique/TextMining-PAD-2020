Jacques-Martin Tétaz &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jacques-Martin Tétaz''' (6 March 1818 – 16 October 1865) was a French architect. He was admitted at the École nationale supérieure des Beaux-Arts where he was the student of Jean-Nicolas Huyot and Louis-Hippolyte Lebas. He was the recipient of the second architecture Prix de Rome in 1841. He was housed at the Villa Medici from 1844 to 1848. &&&&& &&&&& He is only known by the Palais de l'Alma, which was built between 1861 and 1864. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
