Jackpot (2018 film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Jackpot''''' is a 2018 Pakistani romantic comedy film, directed by Shoaib Khan and produced by Khurram Riaz. The script has been written by Babar Kashmiri, and the cinematography has been done by Anubhay Bansal. The film's leading cast includes Sanam Chaudhry, Noor Hassan, Sana Fakhar, Adnan Shah Tipu, and Javed Sheikh.
