John Fearnley &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''John Fearnley''' (born 13 September 1968) is an Australian former professional rugby league footballer of the 1990s. He played for the Parramatta Eels, and the South Sydney Rabbitohs. Fearnley made his first grade debut for Parramatta in Round 3 of the 1991 season against Norths. Fearnley went on to make 76 appearances for Parramatta before switching to Souths.
