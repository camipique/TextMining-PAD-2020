Dongfeng Fengshen H30 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Dongfeng Fengshen H30''' is a compact hatchback produced by Dongfeng Motor Corporation under the Dongfeng Fengshen sub-brand. It is the hatchback version of the Dongfeng Fengshen S30 subcompact sedan, with prices starting from 69,800 yuan and ending at 93,800 yuan. The Dongfeng Fengshen H30 compact hatchback shares the same platform as the Peugeot 307 as both cars based on the PSA PF2 platform. &&&&& Dongfeng Fengshen H30 Cross &&&&& A version of the H30 featuring crossover accents such as plastic cladding around the wheel arches and bumpers was introduced also at the 2010 Beijing Auto Show as the '''Dongfeng Fengshen H30 Cross''', with prices starting from 86,800 yuan and ending at 97,800 yuan.
