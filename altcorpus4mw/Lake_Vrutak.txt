Lake Vrutak &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Lake Vrutak''' is artificial reservoir on the Trebišnjica river in Popovo Polje, near Hutovo village, Bosnia and Herzegovina. The lake serves as compensation and storage basin for Pump-Storage Hydroelectric Power Plant "Čapljina". &&&&& &&&&& Hydropower and irrigation system &&&&& Lake Vrutak is tha last and smallest of three artificial reservoirs in hydroelectric power plant system Trebišnjica - HES "Donji Horizonti", and serves as compensation basin for Pump-Storage Hydroelectric Power Plant "Čapljina".
