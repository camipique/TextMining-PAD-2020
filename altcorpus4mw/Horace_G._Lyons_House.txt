Horace G. Lyons House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Horace G. Lyons House''' is a historic house. It was built in 1860 for Horace G. Lyons, a settler and farmer. It was expanded in 1893. In 1900, Lyons authored a religious booklet called ''The Devil Problem'', in which he argued that the devil could be found in every man. The house remained in the Lyons family until 1977. &&&&& &&&&& The house was designed in the Second Empire and Italianate architectural styles.
