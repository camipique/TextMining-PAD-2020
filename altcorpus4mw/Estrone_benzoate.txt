Estrone benzoate &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Estrone benzoate''', or '''estrone 3-benzoate''', is a synthetic estrogen and estrogen ester – specifically, the C3 benzoate ester of estrone – which was discovered in 1931 and was never marketed.
