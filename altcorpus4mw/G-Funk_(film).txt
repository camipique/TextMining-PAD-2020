G-Funk (film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''G-Funk''''' is a 2017 documentary film distributed by YouTube Premium. The film uses previously unreleased archival footage to describe the rise of G-Funk in the early 1990s. It features interviews with Warren G, Snoop Dogg, Chuck D, Ice Cube, Ice T, Too Short, The D.O.C., Wiz Khalifa and others.
