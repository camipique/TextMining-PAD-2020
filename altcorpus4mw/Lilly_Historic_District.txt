Lilly Historic District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Lilly Historic District''' in Lilly, Georgia is a historic district which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1998. Roughly bounded by the CSX RR tracks, and Church, Montezuma, Third, and School Streets, it included 29 contributing buildings, two contributing structures, and a contributing site.
