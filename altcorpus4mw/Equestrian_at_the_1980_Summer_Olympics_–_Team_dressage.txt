Equestrian at the 1980 Summer Olympics – Team dressage &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The team dressage in equestrian at the 1980 Olympic Games in Moscow was held at Trade Unions' Equestrian Complex on 31 July. &&&&& &&&&& Competition format &&&&& &&&&& The team medals were awarded after the Grand-Prix portion of the individual competition. After the Grand-Prix portion of the individual event the three rides of each team were added up and the highest score was the winner, all three scores counted towards the final.
