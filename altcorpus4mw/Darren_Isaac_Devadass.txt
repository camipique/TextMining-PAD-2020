Darren Isaac Devadass &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Darren Isaac Devadass''' (born 3 February 1995) is a Malaysian badminton player. He won his first international title at the 2012 Smiling Fish International in the men's doubles event partnered with Tai An Khang. Devadass competed at the 2012 World and Asian Junior Championships, helped the team reaching the semi-finals, and settle for the bronze medal in Asia. In 2014, he and Tai finished as the runner-up at the Bangladesh International Challenge.
