Ei Ei &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Ei Ei''' (; born 14 November 1965) is a Burmese singer. She is best known for her music around 1990s and was a popular singer in karaoke musical industry. &&&&& She sings and works together with singer Zarli Naing and Thiri Ko Ko. They released a solo album "''3 Arrows'' ". &&&&& &&&&& She completely disappeared from a music career for 23 years from the time of her marriage and had a child. Her husband is Maung Maung Cho, a salior, married on 24 November 1995. They have two children; one daughter and one son. In 2018, she returned to her music industry career and released new version of ''3 Arrows'' album with Zarli Naing and Thiri Ko Ko in 31 May 2018.
