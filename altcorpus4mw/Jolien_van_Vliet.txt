Jolien van Vliet &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jolien van Vliet''' is a Dutch cricketer. In July 2018, she was named in the Netherlands' squad for the 2018 ICC Women's World Twenty20 Qualifier tournament.
