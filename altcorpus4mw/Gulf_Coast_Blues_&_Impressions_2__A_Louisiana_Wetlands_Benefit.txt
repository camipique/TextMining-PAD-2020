Gulf Coast Blues & Impressions 2: A Louisiana Wetlands Benefit &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Gulf Coast Blues & Impressions 2 - A Louisiana Wetlands Benefit''''' is the thirteenth album of pianist George Winston, also his thirteenth solo piano album, released in 2012. It is his third benefit release, made as a fundraiser for the Louisiana Wetlands.
