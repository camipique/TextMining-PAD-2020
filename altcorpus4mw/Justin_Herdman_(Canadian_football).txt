Justin Herdman (Canadian football) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Justin Herdman''' (born July 21, 1994) is a professional Canadian football linebacker for the Toronto Argonauts of the Canadian Football League (CFL). He was drafted 54th overall in the 2017 CFL Draft by the Argoanuts and signed with the team on May 15, 2017. He played college football with the Simon Fraser Clan. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Herdman's twin brother, Jordan Herdman, who is two minutes older, also plays professionally as a linebacker for the BC Lions.
