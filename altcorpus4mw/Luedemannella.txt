33	Luedemannella
Luedemannella &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Luedemannella''''' is a Gram-positive, aerobic and non-motile genus of bacteria from the family of ''Micromonosporaceae''. ''Luedemannella'' is named after the Russian actinomycetologist George M.
