Italian Brigands Surprised by Papal Troops &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& 300px &&&&& '''''Italian Brigands Surprised by Papal Troops''''' is an 1831 painting produced in Rome by Horace Vernet. It was recorded in the "John T. Johnston Collection", which was sold by its owner to William T Walters of Baltimore on 20 December 1876 for $10,110, the most expensive work in Johnston's collection. In 1894 it passed to Henry Walters and in 1931 one of his heirs left it to its current owner, the Walters Art Museum, Baltimore. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
