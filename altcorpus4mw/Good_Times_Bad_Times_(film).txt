Good Times Bad Times (film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Good Times Bad Times''''' is a Canadian documentary film, directed by Donald Shebib and released in 1969. A depiction of military veterans, the film juxtaposed original BBC documentary footage from World War I and World War II with contemporary footage of veterans recalling their experiences at Royal Canadian Legion halls, Remembrance Day commemorations and veterans hospitals. &&&&& &&&&& Narrated by John Granik, the film was distributed primarily as an episode of the CBC Television documentary series ''The Way It Is''. &&&&& &&&&& The film won the Canadian Film Award for Best Feature Length Documentary in 1969. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
