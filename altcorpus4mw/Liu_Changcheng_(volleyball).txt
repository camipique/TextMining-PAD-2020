Liu Changcheng (volleyball) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Liu Changcheng''' (, born 3 May 1964) is a former Chinese volleyball player. He participated in the 1984 Summer Olympics. He later immigrated to Germany, and played for the German clubs Moerser SC (1989–1993) and Post Telekom Berlin (1993–1995).
