John Barron (footballer) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''John Salmond Barron''' (17 February 1879 – 24 July 1908) was a Scottish professional football inside right who played in the Scottish League for Dundee. He also played in the Southern League for Brentford. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Barron emigrated to the United States in September 1905 and died of electrocution while working on overhead wires in Westfield, New Jersey in 1908.
