Goran Pauk &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Goran Pauk''' (born 23 February 1962) is a Croatian politician and former footballer who has been the prefect of Šibenik-Knin County. &&&&& &&&&& ==Biography== &&&&& He was born in Šibenik. He finished High Economic School in Šibenik. From 1976 to 1993 he played football, but since 2006 he has been the prefect of Šibenik-Knin County. He is also a president of Croatian County Assembly. &&&&& &&&&& ===Football career=== &&&&& He started football career in youth team of NK Šibenik and later Hajduk Split. From 1980 to 1993 he played and captained senior team of NK Šibenik.
