Dale E. Klein &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Dale E. Klein''' was the Chairman of the United States Nuclear Regulatory Commission under President George W. Bush. He served from May 13, 2009 until his resignation on March 29, 2010.
