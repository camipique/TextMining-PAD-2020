Jamie Smith (cricketer) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jamie Luke Smith''' (born 12 July 2000) is an English cricketer. He made his Twenty20 debut for Surrey in the 2018 t20 Blast on 5 July 2018. He made his first-class debut for Surrey against Marylebone Cricket Club in Dubai on 24 March 2019. He scored a century, becoming the ninth batsman for Surrey to score a century on first-class debut.
