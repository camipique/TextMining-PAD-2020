HMS Aberfoyle &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''HMS ''Aberfoyle''''' was a barge of the British Royal Navy . The vessel was purchased on 4 November 1920Lough Swilly Steamboat Company in Derry. The vessel was named in March 1938 as HMS ''Dolphin'' and was placed as a mother ship in Portsmouth.
