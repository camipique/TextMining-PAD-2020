I Am Who &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''I Am Who''''' (stylized as '''I am WHO''') is the second extended play by South Korean boy group Stray Kids. The EP was released digitally and physically on August 6, 2018, by JYP Entertainment. A showcase titled ''Stray Kids Unveil: Op. 02: I Am Who'' was held the day before the release.
