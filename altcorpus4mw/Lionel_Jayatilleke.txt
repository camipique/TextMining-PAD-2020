Lionel Jayatilleke &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Lionel Jayatilleke''' (July 24, 1924 - September 1988) was a Sri Lankan politician. He was elected for Kuliyapitiya in the 8th parliamentary election in 1977.
