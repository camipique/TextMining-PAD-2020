Hemidactylus fragilis &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Hemidactylus fragilis''''' is a species of gecko. It is found in Ethiopia and Somalia. It is sometimes considered conspecific with the common house gecko. In fact, it was synonymized with the species by Loveridge, but resurrected by Mazuch et al. in 2016. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
