She made her junior debut at the 2012 Pan-Am Junior Championship, and her senior debut two years later at a four-nation tournament in Santiago. &&&&& &&&&& Tala has played for Chile at two South American Games, in Santiago 2014 and Cochabamba 2018. The team medalled at both evens, winning silver in 2014 and bronze in 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Tala was a member of the Chilean team at the 2017 Pan American Cup.
