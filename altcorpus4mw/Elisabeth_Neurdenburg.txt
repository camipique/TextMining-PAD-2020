Elisabeth Neurdenburg &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Elisabeth Neurdenburg''' (1882 – 1957) was a Dutch art historian. &&&&& &&&&& She contributed to the large inventory of 17th-century Dutch paintings by Cornelis Hofstede de Groot, and became a specialist on Dutch Kraak ware.
