Federal Building (Ruston, Louisiana) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Federal Building''', also known as the '''Old Post Office''', is a historic institutional building located at 201 North Vienna Street in Ruston, Louisiana. &&&&& &&&&& Built in 1909 to host Ruston Post Office, the structure is a small one-story rectangular limestone building with a hipped roof featuring circular dormers. The building was vacated about 1961 when a new post office was built, and was then used since 1963 as a federal office building. &&&&& &&&&& The building was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on October 9, 1974.
