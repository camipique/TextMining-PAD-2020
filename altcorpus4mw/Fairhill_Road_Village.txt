Fairhill Road Village &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Fairhill Road Village''' is a small housing development on the border of Cleveland and Cleveland Heights, Ohio. &&&&& &&&&& As '''Fairhill Road Village Historic District''' it was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1990. &&&&& &&&&& The village is a small planned community, a collection of five pairs of semi-detached buildings and three separate dwellings, designed in English Cotwold style. &&&&& &&&&& It was designed by landscape architect A. Donald Gray with architects Antonio DiNardo and H.O. Fullerton. &&&&& &&&&& It includes 12 contributing buildings and Tudor Revival architecture. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Cleveland Heights, Ohio &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
