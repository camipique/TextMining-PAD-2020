Hale Boggs Federal Complex &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Hale Boggs Federal Complex''', also known as the '''Hale Boggs Federal Building-Courthouse''', is a historic building in New Orleans, Louisiana. It was built in 1962. It was designed in the Modernist architectural style.
