FC LNZ-Lebedyn &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''FC LNZ-Lebedyn''' () is an amateur Ukrainian football from Lebedyn, Shpola Raion. The club is owned by an agriculture company LNZ Group. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The club was created in 2006 by director of the Lebedyn Seed Factory (LNZ) Dmytro Kravchenko. The club was based in the city of Shpola and played at its Central Stadium. &&&&& &&&&& Until 2013 the club was known as FC Shpola-LNZ-Lebedyn as it was sponsored from the raion budget. Among other sponsors were Shpola-Agro Industry owned by a French national Frederic Chavigny. &&&&& &&&&& The club was coached by former FC Dnipro Cherkasy players Slavinskyi and Hrechanyi. &&&&& &&&&& Honours &&&&& * '''Ukrainian Amateur Cup''' &&&&& ** Winners (1): 2017–18 &&&&& &&&&& * '''Cherkasy Oblast Championship''' &&&&& ** Winners (4): 2009, 2011, 2016, 2017 &&&&& ** Runners-up (2): 2007, 2012 &&&&& &&&&& * '''Cherkasy Oblast Cup''' &&&&& ** Runners-up (2): 2009, 2012 &&&&& &&&&& Head coaches &&&&& * 2006–2011 Petro Slavinskyi &&&&& * 2012 – present Vasyl Hrechanyi &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * FC Viktoriya Mykolaivka &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * LNZ-Lebedyn: Quarterfinal perspectives, regional triumph and new stadium for your homeland ("ЛНЗ-Лебедин": чвертьфінальні перспективи, обласний тріумф та новий стадіон для краю).
