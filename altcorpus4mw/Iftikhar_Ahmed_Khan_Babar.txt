Iftikhar Ahmed Khan Babar &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Nawabzada Iftikhar Ahmed Khan Babar''' is a Pakistani politician who has been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan since August 2018. He is the son of a renowned politician of Pakistan, Nawabzada Nasrullah Khan (late). &&&&& &&&&& Early life and education &&&&& He was born on 11 March 1955 in Khangarh, Pakistan. &&&&& &&&&& Political career &&&&& He started his political career in 1976 and participated in local government elections. &&&&& &&&&& He served as divisional president of Pakistan Peoples Party (PPP) for Dera Ghazi Khan. &&&&& &&&&& He was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan from Constituency NA-184 (Muzaffargarh-IV) as a candidate of PPP in Pakistani general election, 2018. He received 54,879 votes and defeated Malik Ahmad Karim Qaswar Langrial. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
