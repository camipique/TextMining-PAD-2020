Joséphine Berthault &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Josephine Berthault''' (20 November 1853 in Angers – 6 August 1923) was a French painter. &&&&& &&&&& Life &&&&& Berthault was a student of her uncle, Jules Lenepveu. She studied academic painting and drawing from a young age. Berthault created mostly landscapes, portraits, and still lifes.
