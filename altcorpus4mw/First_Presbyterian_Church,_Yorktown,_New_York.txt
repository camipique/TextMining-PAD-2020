First Presbyterian Church, Yorktown, New York &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''First Presbyterian Church of Yorktown''' is a Presbyterian church in Yorktown Heights, New York. The current church building is the third in the history of the congregation, completed in 1840. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The congregation dates back to 1730. A meeting house was built in 1738, and a first resident pastor led the congregation from 1761. During the Revolutionary War, the church was used as barracks. It was destroyed by British troups in June 1779. A second church was built in 1785. A third church in the sam style replaced it in 1840. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
