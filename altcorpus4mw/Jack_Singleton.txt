Jack Singleton &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Jack Singleton''' (born 14 May 1996 in Dacorum, Herts, England) is a rugby union player for Worcester Warriors in the Aviva Premiership. His preferred position is hooker. &&&&& &&&&& Club Career &&&&& Singleton made his professional rugby debut for Worcester Warriors in Russia against Enisei-STM, in October 2016. In what proved to be a markedly good first professional season at Worcester, Singleton made 20 appearances. His Premiership debut came against Northampton Saints in November 2016. Jack's good form as a young player, saw him collect a 'Club's Young Player of the Season' award at the close of the English Premiership 2016-2017 season. This gained him interest from the England selectors. &&&&& &&&&& International &&&&& Appeared in England training camps and making his England debut against Barbarian F.C. in May 2017.
