33	Dakarella
Dakarella &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Dakarella''''' is a Gram-negative genus of bacteria from the family of Sutterellaceae with one known species (''Dakarella massiliensis''). ''Dakarella massiliensis'' has been isolated from the female genital tract. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
