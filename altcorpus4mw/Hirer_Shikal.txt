Hirer Shikal &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Hirer Shikal''''' is a Bengali drama film directed by Murari Chakraborty. This film was released in 1988 in the banner of Tumpa films. The music of the film was scored by Rabin Banerjee &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& It is a story of rivalry between two brothers for their family diamonds. One brother hired an imposter lady and send her to another brother's house. Because she is look alike of their daughter Krishna. The parents can not realise that she is not their daughter and they not only gift her those diamonds but also fix her marriage.
