Lavonia Commercial Historic District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Lavonia Commercial Historic District''' in Lavonia, Georgia is a historic district which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1983. The listing included 18 contributing buildings. &&&&& &&&&& It includes the historic commercial center of Lavonia, mostly one- and two-story brick commercial buildings. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
