Giovanna Arbunic Castro &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Brunilda Giovanna Arbunic Castro''' (born 1 July 1964) is a Chilean chess player who hold the FIDE title of Woman International Master (1982). &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& In the 1980s Giovanna Arbunic Castro was one of the leading Chilean women's chess players. She comes from the Magallanes Region. She was trained in 1981 and 1982 by Chilean FIDE Master David Arturo Godoy Bugueño (1944-2007). For a long time, she lives near Valencia (Spain) and is married to the Chilean International Master Daniel Barría Zuñiga (born 1974). Couple have a son and they founded a chess school, the Academia Online Manuelito. She eight time won the Chilean Women's Chess Championship. In 1982, she was awarded the FIDE International Women Master (WIM) title. She won women's zone tournaments in 1982 in Morón (Argentina), in 1985 in São Paulo and in 1999 in Villa Martelli (Argentina).
