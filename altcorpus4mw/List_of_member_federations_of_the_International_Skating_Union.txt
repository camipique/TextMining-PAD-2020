List of member federations of the International Skating Union &&&&& Introduction &&&&& This is a list of federations that are members of International Skating Union (ISU). As of 16 July 2018, there are 93 member federations. &&&&& &&&&& In many countries, figure skating and speed skating are governed by different federations, but both can be members of the ISU; the national federations are ISU members for figure skating, for speed skating or for both. Some of these national federations also govern other sports in their countries, but are not ISU members in that capacity. Apart from the national governing bodies, there are also some old skating clubs which are members in their own rights. &&&&& &&&&& European members (56 national + 2 club members) &&&&& The remainder
