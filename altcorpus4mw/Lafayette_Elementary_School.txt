Lafayette Elementary School &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Lafayette Elementary School''', now '''Lafayette Middle School''', is a historic school building located at 1301 West University Avenue in Lafayette, Louisiana. &&&&& &&&&& Built in 1926 and designed by William T. Nolan, the two-story Gothic Revival institutional building is a brick and concrete E-shaped structure with a long frontal main block and three rear wings. &&&&& &&&&& The building was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on June 14, 1984. &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * National Register of Historic Places listings in Lafayette Parish, Louisiana &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
