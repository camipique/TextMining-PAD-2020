Love and Other Bruises &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''Love and Other Bruises'''" is the debut single by English/Australian soft rock duo Air Supply, from their 1976 self-titled debut album. The song was a top ten hit in Australia, where it peaked at No. 6. The song also appeared on the duo's third album ''Love & Other Bruises'', which was their first release in the United States. &&&&& &&&&& Track listing &&&&& ;Australia & UK 7" single &&&&& :A.
