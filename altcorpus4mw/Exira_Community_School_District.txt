Exira Community School District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Exira Community School District''' was a school district headquartered in Exira, Iowa. Its schools were Exira Elementary School and Exira Community High School. &&&&& &&&&& In addition to Exira, the district served Brayton. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& In the late 1950s or early 1960s the Brayton school consolidated into the Exira district. &&&&& &&&&& In a period of several years before its end in 2014, the Exira district operated in a financial deficit. At one point it entered into with the Elk Horn-Kimballton Community School District, in which students from one district attended school in another district. On July 1, 2014 it consolidated with the Elk Horn-Kimballton district to form the Exira-EHK Community School District. 628 people voted in favor and 82 voted against. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
