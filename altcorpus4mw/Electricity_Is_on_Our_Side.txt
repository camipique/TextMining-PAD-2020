Electricity Is on Our Side &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Electricity is on Our Side''''' (stylized as "''electricity is on our side''") is the tenth studio album by American rapper Busdriver. It was released on 8 June 2018 by Temporary Whatever. The album features guest appearances from Hemlock Ernst, Daedelus and Denmark Vessey, among others.
