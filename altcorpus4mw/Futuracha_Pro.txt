Futuracha Pro &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Futuracha Pro''' is a typeface designed by Odysseas Galinos Paparounis. &&&&& The appearance of individual letters adjusts automatically depending on the words typed. The shape and design of the font, which has Art Deco stylings, were inspired by the movements of a cockroach. &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
