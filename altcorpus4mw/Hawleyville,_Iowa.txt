53	Hawleyville,_Iowa
Hawleyville, Iowa &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Hawleyville''' is an unincorporated community in Page and Taylor Counties in the U.S. state of Iowa. The Page County portion of the community is in Nebraska Township, while the Taylor County portion is in Dallas Township. Hawleyville is located along County Route J31 east-northeast of Clarinda. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
