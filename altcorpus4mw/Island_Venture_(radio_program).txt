Island Venture (radio program) &&&&& Introduction &&&&&  &&&&& &&&&& '''''Island Venture''''' is an American old-time radio adventure drama. It was broadcast on CBS from November 8, 1945, until June 20, 1946. &&&&& &&&&& The program originated at WBBM in Chicago, Illinois, replacing ''The First Line'' (1942-1945), which focused on the work of the U.S. Navy during World War II. &&&&& &&&&& ''Island Venture'' dramatized the adventures of ex-Navy pilot Gil Perry as he operated the Inter-Island Air Service, based on Ilopan island in the Central Pacific. Episodes also featured Trigger Brett, Berry's partner; Chula, a big shot; Mendoza, who owned a cargo line, and Mendoza's daughter. In February 1946, the character of Nancy Goodwin was added as both the operator of a competing air service and as a romantic interest for Perry. &&&&& &&&&& The cast featured Jerry Walter as Perry, Hugh Rowlands as Brett, Clare Boreum as Mendoza, Willard Waterman as Chula, and Jane Webb as Mendoza's daughter.
