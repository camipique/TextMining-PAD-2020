Los Angeles Hibernian Bank &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Los Angeles Hibernian Bank''' was a Los Angeles based-banking company. In 1988 was put up for sale by its Hong Kong-based parent First Pacific Holdings. Security Pacific agreed to buy Hibernian Bank for an agreed figure of $160 million; included in the sale was their approximate 35 branches. &&&&& &&&&& At the time of the sale, Hibernian Bank was the 13th-largest bank in the state of California, with assets of $1.57 billion. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
