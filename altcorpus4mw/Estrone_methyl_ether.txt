53	Estrone_methyl_ether
Estrone methyl ether &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Estrone methyl ether''', or '''estrone 3-methyl ether''', is a synthetic estrogen and estrogen ether – specifically, the C3 methyl ether of estrone – which was never marketed.
