Higgins Pond &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Higgins Pond''' is a kettle pond in Brewster, Massachusetts. It is located in Nickerson State Park. &&&&& &&&&& The Massachusetts Division of Fisheries and Wildlife stocks the pond in spring with various trout species.
