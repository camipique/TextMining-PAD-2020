James Crowther &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''James Gerald Crowther''' (26 September 1899, Halifax–1983) was one of the founders of science journalism. He was appointed the scientific correspondent of ''The Manchester Guardian'' in 1928. &&&&& &&&&& James was the second child of James Crowther, the principal of a technical school, and his wife, Alice, (née Ainscow).
