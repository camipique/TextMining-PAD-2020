Gartly railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Gartly railway station''' served the hamlet of Gartly, Aberdeenshire, Scotland from 1854 to 1968 on the Great North of Scotland Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened on 20 September 1854 by the Great North of Scotland Railway.
