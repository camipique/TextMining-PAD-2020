Journal of Osteopathy &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''''Journal of Osteopathy''''' was a monthly medical journal that was first published in May 1894 in Kirksville, Missouri by the American School of Osteopathy. It was the first regular publication about the field of osteopathy. Dr. Andrew Taylor Still was one of the key contributors to the journal. Its first editor-in-chief was Jenette H. Bolles.
