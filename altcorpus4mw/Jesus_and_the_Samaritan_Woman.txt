Jesus and the Samaritan Woman &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 300px &&&&& '''''Jesus and the Samaritan Woman''''' is a 1742 painting by Jean-François de Troy of the Samaritan woman at the well. It is one of a series of six paintings by the artist for Pierre Guérin de Tencin and his archepiscopal palace at Lyon - the others were ''The Death of Lucretia'', ''The Death of Cleopatra'', ''The Judgement of Solomon'', ''The Idolatry of Solomon'' and ''The Woman Caught in Adultery''. It is now at the Museum of Fine Arts of Lyon. &&&&& &&&&& Bibliography &&&&& * M.F. Amigues-de Uffrédi, S. Charret-Berthon et M.F.
