Hydrillodes gravatalis &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Hydrillodes gravatalis''''' is a moth of the family Erebidae first described by Francis Walker in 1859. It is found in the Indian subregion, Sri Lanka and Sundaland. &&&&& &&&&& Forewings blackish brown. Punctate submarginals are straw white.
