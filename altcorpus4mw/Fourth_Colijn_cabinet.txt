Fourth Colijn cabinet &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Fourth Colijn''' was the cabinet of the Netherlands from 24 June 1937 until 25 July 1939. The cabinet was formed by the political parties Roman Catholic State Party (RKSP), Anti-Revolutionary Party (ARP) and the Christian Historical Union (CHU) after the election of 1937. The right-wing cabinet was a majority government in the House of Representatives.
