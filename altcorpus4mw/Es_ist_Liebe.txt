Es ist Liebe &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''Es ist Liebe'''" (English: ''It Is Love'') is a song by German recording artist Nadja Benaissa. It was written by Benaissa and Tino Oac for her debut solo album ''Schritt für Schritt'' (2006), while production was helmed by Oac.
