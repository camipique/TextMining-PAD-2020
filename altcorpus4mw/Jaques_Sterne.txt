Jaques Sterne &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Jaques Sterne''' (b Woodhouse 29 June 1695; d Rise 9 June 1759) was a cleric and politician in the mid 18th century. &&&&& &&&&& He was educated at Jesus College, Cambridge. He was ordained in 1720. He held livings at Rise and Hornsea. He was Archdeacon of Cleveland from 1735 until 1750; and then of East Riding from 1750 until 1755.
