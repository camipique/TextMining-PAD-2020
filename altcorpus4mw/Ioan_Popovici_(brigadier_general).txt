Ioan Popovici (brigadier general) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Ioan Popovici''' (23 August 1865 – 1953, in Sighet) was a Romanian general assigned to the general staff headquarters, during World War I. He attended the (Military Infantry and Cavalry School) in Bucharest from 1885 to 1887. Afterward he rose through the ranks until he became a Brigadier General in 1916 as World War I was raging, engulfing Romania.
