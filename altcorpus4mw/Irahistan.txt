146	Irahistan
Irahistan &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Irahistan''' () or '''Irahestan''' is a historical region consisting of southern Fars Province and western Hormozgan Province in Iran. &&&&& &&&&& The region of Irahistan currently consists of several counties in Fars province (Larestan, Khonj County, Gerash County, Lamerd County and Mohr County) and Bastak County, Bandar Lengeh County and Parsian County in Hormozgan. The region importance grew during Sassanid period with vast expansion of settlements in Irahistan. &&&&& &&&&& In the medieval times, the region was ruled by the local lords until they were removed by a Safavid invasion in 1610. In the thirteenth century, the city of Lar became a center of trade and commerce in Irahistan and the population of this city grew rapidly outpacing the more historical towns and cities.
