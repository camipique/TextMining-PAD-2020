Hypericum foliosum &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Hypericum foliosum''''' is a shrub in the genus ''Hypericum'', in the section ''Androsaemum''. &&&&& &&&&& Distribution and habitat &&&&& ''Hypericum foliosum'' is located in the Azores, Portugal, and can be found in damp shaded places in the mountains from above sea level. &&&&& &&&&& Description &&&&& ''Hypericum foliosum'' is a shrub that grows to be tall. Its inflorescence is golden yellow and has 12-18 petals. It has 20-30 stamens grouped into several stamen fascicles. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
