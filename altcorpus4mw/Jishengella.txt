38	Jishengella
Jishengella &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Jishengella''''' is a genus of bacteria from the family of ''Micromonosporaceae'' with one known species (''Jishengella endophytica''). ''Jishengella endophytica'' has been isolated from the roots of the plant ''Acanthus ilicifolius'' from Wenchang in China. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
