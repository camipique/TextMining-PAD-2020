Just as I Am (Air Supply song) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''Just as I Am'''" is a song by English/Australian soft rock duo Air Supply from their eighth studio and second eponymous album, ''Air Supply''. It was released in 1985 as the first single from the album. &&&&& &&&&& The song was first recorded by co-writer Rob Hegel in 1982 and released via Mercury Records. &&&&& &&&&& Chart performance &&&&& "Just as I Am" was a Top 20 hit on the ''Billboard'' Hot 100, peaking at #19. On the Adult Contemporary chart, it peaked at #3. It was to be Air Supply's last major hit on the ''Billboard'' Hot 100. The song also reached #12 on the Canadian ''RPM'' singles chart, and #9 on its Adult Contemporary chart.
