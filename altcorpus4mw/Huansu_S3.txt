Huansu S3 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Huansu S3''' is a 7-seat Compact SUV produced by Huansu, a sub-brand of BAIC Motor. It is the 7-seat variant of the Huansu S2 5-seat compact SUV, both manufactured by Beiqi Yinxiang Automobile.
