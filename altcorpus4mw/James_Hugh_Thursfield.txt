James Hugh Thursfield &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''James Hugh Thursfield''' FRCP (July 1869 - 20 June 1944) was a British pediatrician. He was one of the editors of ''Diseases of Children'' (1913) and of the ''Archives of Diseases in Childhood'' (founded 1926).
