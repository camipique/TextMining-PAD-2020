Electricity (Silk City and Dua Lipa song) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''Electricity'''" is a song by British-American music duo Silk City and English singer Dua Lipa. It was released as a single on 6 September 2018 by Columbia Records. "Electricity" was included on the complete edition of Lipa's eponymous debut studio album. The song won Best Dance Recording at the 61st Grammy Awards. &&&&& &&&&& Music video &&&&& The music video for "Electricity" was uploaded to Ronson's YouTube account on 5 September 2018. Directed by Bradley & Pablo, the visual contains references to the 2003 Northeast blackout in America whilst Lipa dances in an abandoned warehouse. Ronson and Diplo also appear in the video, giving cameo appearances in a broken-down elevator. &&&&& &&&&& Live performances &&&&& Lipa performed the song at the iHeartRadio Music Festival on 28 September 2018. She also performed the song along with "One Kiss" at the 2018 American Music Awards.
