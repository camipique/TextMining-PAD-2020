Lola and Mila &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Lola and Mila''''' () is a Serbian animated television series created by Sergej Ćetković and produced by ''Studio Carousel'' and ''Event Media Production''. The show focuses on the adventures of two little girls Lola and Mila, named after Sergej's daughters. As they spend their time exploring new things, they fall into situations that are entertaining but educational as well. &&&&& &&&&& Production &&&&& Sergej Ćetković together with spouse Kristina Ćetković and other team members started the work on this project in 2017.
