Forts (video game) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Forts''''' is a 2D real-time strategy video game developed and published by Australian studio EarthWork Games. It was released on April 19, 2017. ''Forts'' started development in March, 2003, as a game originally based on early bridge building games. ''Forts'' had sold 120,000 copies as of October 6, 2017. In ''Forts'', the player builds a base, defends it, and acquires weapons through tech trees in an attempt to destroy the opponent's reactor. &&&&& &&&&& The game has been reviewed by ''CG Magazine'' and Kotaku, the former of which described ''Forts''s gameplay as being similar to the ''Worms'' series. &&&&& &&&&& Notes &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
