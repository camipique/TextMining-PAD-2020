George Washington McCoppin &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''George Washington McCoppin''' (November 3, 1904 - July 1, 1993) was a former Texas House member who served in the House from 1957-1963. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Life &&&&& McCoppin was born on November 3, 1904 to Edward Richard McCoppin and Zema Phillips. He married Alice Ann Johnson and had 2 children together. Their children are named George Edward McCoppin (1936-?) and Sally Beth McCoppin (1939-?). McCoppin died on July 1, 1993 at the age of 88. He is buried at Hillcrest Cemetery in Texarkana. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
