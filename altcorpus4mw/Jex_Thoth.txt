Jex Thoth &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jex Thoth''' is a doom metal band from Madison, Wisconsin, USA signed to the Swedish record label I Hate. Since 2007 they have released two full-length studio albums, three EPs and a split with Pagan Altar.
