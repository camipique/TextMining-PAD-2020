Low lying gecko &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Low lying gecko''' (''Microgecko depressus'') is a species of lizard in the family Gekkonidae. It is endemic to western Pakistan. &&&&& &&&&& Locomotion &&&&& Tropiocolotes depressus possess a "serpentine" position for a short period of time, when alarmed, or, threatened.
