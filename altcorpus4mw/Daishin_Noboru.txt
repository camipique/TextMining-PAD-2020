Daishin Noboru &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Daishin Noboru''' (born August 2, 1937 as '''Tomeo Nagata'''; died March 20, 2012) was a sumo wrestler from Oshamambe, Hokkaidō, Japan. His highest rank was ''maegashira'' 8. He was an original member of the Miyagino stable founded by ''yokozuna'' Yoshibayama. He made his professional debut in May 1956, reaching the top ''makuuchi'' division in July 1965. He won the ''jūryō'' division championship or ''yūshō'' in May 1966.
