Ivan Beroš &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Ivan Beroš''' (born 30 December 1979) is a former professional tennis player from Croatia. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& A right-handed player from Makarska, Beroš had a best ranking on tour of 260 in the world. His only ATP Tour main draw appearance came at the 1999 Croatia Open Umag, where featured as a qualifier and was beaten in three sets by Finland's Ville Liukko in the first round.
