Efrosinia Yanenko-Khmelnytska &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Efrosinia Yanenko-Khmelnytsky''' (died 1684), was a Ukrainian Hetmana by marriage to Petro Doroshenko, Hetman of Zaporizhian Host (r. 1665-1676). She was an influential figure among the Ukrainian Cossacks. She is known for her alleged life style, as she was said to have several extramarital affairs, and also for a myth regarding her importance for the independence of Ukraine. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& * Степанков В.
