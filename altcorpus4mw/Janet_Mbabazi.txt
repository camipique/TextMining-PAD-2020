Janet Mbabazi &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Janet Mbabazi''' (born 26 January 1996) is a Ugandan cricketer. In July 2018, she was named in Uganda's squad for the 2018 ICC Women's World Twenty20 Qualifier tournament.
