Dudurkote &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Dudurkote''' is a small town in subdivision of Hindol, Dhenkanal District of Orissa state. It is located from NH 42 to Gudiakateni and old NH 42 is passed in the center of the Dudurkote. It is surrounded by reserve forest from the East, South and West. It was a part of the Hindol princely State before independence. It is from Dhenkanal. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& Dudurkote is popularly known as Dudurkote Garha and old capital of Hindol princely State and is located the central part of Hindol subdivision.
