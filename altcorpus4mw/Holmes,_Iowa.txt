Holmes, Iowa &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Holmes''' is an unincorporated community in Lake Township, Wright County, Iowa, United States. Holmes is located along County Highway R33 west of Clarion. &&&&& &&&&& Holmes is a part of the Clarion-Goldfield-Dows Community School District. It was in the Clarion-Goldfield Community School District, until July 1, 2014, when it merged into the current district. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
