Jagjivanrao Pant Pratinidhi &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Jagjivan Rao Pant Pratinidhi''' (also known as '''Dadoba''') served as Pratinidhi (Chief Delegate) during Chhatrapati Shahu I reign. He is the younger brother of Shripatrao Pant Pratinidhi.He succeeded as Pratinidhi after the death of his brother in 1691 at the age of fifty-five. &&&&& &&&&& Early life &&&&& Jagjivan Rao, was born in 1691 to Parshuram Pant Pratinidhi.
