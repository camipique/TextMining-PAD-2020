Janata College, Satmile &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Janata College, Satmile, Dudurkote Hindol Dhenkanal''' is a college in Satmile, Hindol, Dhenkanal, Odisha, India. &&&&& &&&&& The college started with intermediate Arts & Science classes on 9 August 1978. The College received government recognition and affiliation from Utkal University, Vani Vihar, Bhubaneswar in the year 1979-1980. It opened degree classes in arts in 1986 and received first Grant-in-Aid in 1985.
