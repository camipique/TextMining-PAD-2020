Dante in Hell &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& thumb &&&&& '''''Dante in Hell''''' or '''''Dante, led by Virgil, Consoles the Souls of the Envious''''' is an 1835 oil on canvas painting by Hippolyte Flandrin. Contrary to its primary title, it shows a scene from the Circle of the Envious, the second circle of Purgatory in Canto III of ''Purgatorio''. It is now in the Museum of Fine Arts of Lyon. &&&&& &&&&& Sources &&&&& *Léon LAGRANGE, Hippolyte Flandrin, sa vie et ses œuvres, in Le Correspondant, 11 Avril 1864, p745 &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
