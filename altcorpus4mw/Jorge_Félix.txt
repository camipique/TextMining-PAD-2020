Jorge Félix &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jorge Félix Muñoz García''' (born 22 August 1991) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays for Polish club Piast Gliwice as a forward. &&&&& &&&&& Club career &&&&& Born in Madrid, Félix graduated from the youth academy of Atlético Madrid and went on to represent it's C-team. After featuring for CDC Moscardó, Getafe CF B, he moved to the reserve team of AD Alcorcón on 25 June 2014. After playing 16 times for the club, he switched to CF Trival Valderas on 23 January 2015 for the remainder of the season. &&&&& &&&&& On 24 June 2015, Félix moved to fellow league club CF Rayo Majadahonda. On 23 August, he made his debut for the club, coming on as a 66th minute substitute for Álex García in a 0–0 draw against CD Mensajero. After having spent two seasons with the club, he was released in June 2017. &&&&& &&&&& On 27 June 2017, Félix signed for Lleida Esportiu in the same tier. On 20 August, he scored his first goal for the club in a 1–1 draw against CF Badalona. He played regularly for the club, collecting 43 caps and scoring 10 times during his stint. &&&&& &&&&& On 2 July 2018, Félix moved abroad after agreeing to a two-year deal with Polish club Piast
