Dutch Junior Open Squash &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''The Dutch Junior Open''' squash championship is one of the largest individual junior squash tournaments in the world and is considered one the most prestigious junior squash championships. It has been running since 1987 every year, and is highly established as it is just one of just five Tier 2 events used in the WSF World Junior Squash Circuit. The tournament hosts over 400 players from more than 30 countries every year and is organized by the Dutch Squash Federation and European Squash Federation (ESF), usually in July. It is a Super Series tournament on the ESF junior circuit, as it attracts top players from all over the world. &&&&& &&&&& The tournament is divided into ten categories — Boys Under-19, Boys Under-17,
