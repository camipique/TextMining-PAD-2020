Jaeden Graham &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jaeden Graham''' (born October 10, 1995) is an American football tight end for the Atlanta Falcons of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Yale. As a senior at Yale, Graham was a first team All-Ivy League honoree after starting 10 games and finishing the season with four touchdowns and 26 receptions. &&&&& &&&&& Professional career &&&&& Graham was signed by the Atlanta Falcons as an undrafted free agent on June 1, 2018. He was waived on September 1, 2018 and was signed to the practice squad the next day.
