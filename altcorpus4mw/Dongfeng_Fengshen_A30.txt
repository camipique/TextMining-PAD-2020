Dongfeng Fengshen A30 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& Dongfeng Fengshen A30 rear &&&&& &&&&& The '''Dongfeng Fengshen A30''' is a subcompact sedan produced by Dongfeng Motor Corporation under the Dongfeng Fengshen sub-brand. Originally known by its codename Fengshen D23, the Dongfeng Fengshen A30 was planned to be launched during the 2014 Chengdu Auto Show in September 2014. However, the Dongfeng Fengshen A30 sedan debuted in August 2014 instead as Fengshen's second sedan entry following the Fengshen S30 and positioned slightly upmarket with prices ranging from 59,700 yuan to 85,700 yuan.
