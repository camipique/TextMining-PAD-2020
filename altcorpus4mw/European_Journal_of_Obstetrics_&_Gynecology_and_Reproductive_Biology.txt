European Journal of Obstetrics & Gynecology and Reproductive Biology &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''''European Journal of Obstetrics & Gynecology and Reproductive Biology''''' is a monthly peer-reviewed medical journal covering obstetrics and gynecology and reproductive biology. It was established in 1971 and is published by Elsevier. The editor-in-chief is Janesh K. Gupta (University of Birmingham).
