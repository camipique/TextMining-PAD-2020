Izteraab &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Izteraab''''' was a 2014 Pakistani television series that aired on Hum TV. Series was written by Amna Muftil and directed by Misbah Khalid. It is Produced by M&M Productions. It stars Saba Qamar and Mekaal Zulfiqar on their second on-screen collaboration after ''Pani Jaisa Piyar'' in 2011. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& Izteraab is the tale of a man having two wives. It explores the story of Jazib (Mikaal Zulfiqar). He falls in love with Zara (Saba Qamar) and they get married. Jazib is conservative by nature whereas Zara is liberal minded, which causes disagreements between them resulting in separation. Jazib then marries Dua (Sania Shamshad). The story takes a new turn when he comes to know that Zara has cancer. Later Jazib and Zara reunite.
