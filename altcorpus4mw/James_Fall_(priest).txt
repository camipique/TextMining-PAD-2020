James Fall (priest) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''James Fall''', DD was Archdeacon of Cleveland from his installation on 7 December 1700 until his death on 12 June 1711. &&&&& &&&&& Fall was educated at the University of St Andrews and migrated to University of Cambridge in 1698. He was ordained in 1720.
