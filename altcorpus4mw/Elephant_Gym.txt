Elephant Gym &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Elephant Gym''' (Chinese: 大象體操) is a math rock band from Kaohsiung, Taiwan, founded in February 2012. The group consists of KT Chang (bass, keyboard, vocals), Tell Chang (guitar, keyboard, vocals) and Chia-Chin Tu (drums). In the band name, the ''Elephant'' symbolizes their bass-driven melodies, and the ''Gym'' refers to their agile and irregular rhythms. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& &&&&& ===Formation and ''Balance'' EP=== &&&&& Bassist KT Chang and Tell Chang were brought up on classical music by their mother. After meeting drummer Chia-Chin Tu in a music club while attending high school, the trio created ''Elephant Gym'' to share their mutual love for math and post rock. &&&&& &&&&& In May 2012, they were included in the final of the creation team at the beginning of the Golden Star Awards. &&&&& &&&&& In 2012, a limited edition manual EP was produced, and featured the "Daily Waterway" tour. &&&&& &&&&& On May 8th, 2013, the band released their first EP, entitled ''Balance''. It was produced in cooperation with Hongshen Hong, the lead singer of transparent magazine, "Night Scenery", and the filming tour created an exclusive ring-shaped stage. &&&&& &&&&& In 2013, they were nominated for the 4th Golden Sound Award for Best Newcomer (Team). November 2013 saw their "Into the Winter" tour. &&&&& &&&&& In 2014, they cooperated with Lin Biaojia on "The shape of the mouth". Here they performed as a guest performer at the Hong Kong Hung Hom and Taipei Arena, and went to Japan to issue limited edition EP. &&&&& &&&&& In May 2014, an Elephant Gym game was launched. &&&&& &&&&& ===''Angle'' and hiatus (2014-2016)=== &&&&& In June 2014, their debut album "Angle" was released. In cooperation with Barney, Zheng Yinong and dancers, the album tour featured stage lighting and a sound team to create a special tent stage. &&&&& &&&&& From August 2014 to January 2015, China, Taiwan, Japan, Malaysia, Singapore and other places began to conduct "angle" Asian tours, including Tokyo, Taipei, Hong Kong, Singapore and other venues. &&&&& &&&&& In 2014, they were nominated for the 5th Golden Sound Award for Best Orchestra, Best Album, Best Music, Best Rock Single, Best Style Album, Best Style Single, Best Jazz-type Single awards, winning the Best Style Album award. &&&&& &&&&& The band and their debut LP ''Angle'' were recognized at the 5th Golden Indie Music Awards, annually presented to showcase Taiwanese artists. Held in Taiwan in the December of 2014, ''Elephant Gym'' received six nominations for the categories of best album, best band, best rock single, best jazz single, album with the best style and single with the best style, the most nominations of any other artist. They won the award for album with the best style for ''Angle''. &&&&& &&&&& In December 2014, the group members announced a year long hiatus due to Taiwanese compulsory military service, and held a performance entitled ''SEE YOU THEN'' in addition to a short documentary of the same name. &&&&& &&&&& In August 2016, the debut album "Angle ANGLE" was released in Japan. The group was invited to participate in the SUMMER SONIC festival. &&&&& &&&&& ===''Work'' EP and ''Underwater'' (2016-present)=== &&&&& After just over two years, the group released their second EP ''Work'' in October 2016. The ''Work'' Taiwan tour was held to visit small venues in Hengchun, Taitung City, Chiayi, Tainan, Taoyuan, Taiwan and Keelung in November of the same year. &&&&& &&&&& In April 2017, the "Imaginary Gymnastics" experiment, with choreographer Chen Yujun and the Xiaoge Guoxiao children, was exhibited at the Taiwan Cultural Fair. &&&&& &&&&& In June 2017, the group was invited to Tokyo to play with the American orchestra The Fall of Troy and Hikes. &&&&& &&&&& In August 2017, combined with drama, dance and other materials, the challenge broke through the existing orchestra performance form, and held the "Bridge" concert hall tour in Taipei Songyan Eslite, Taichung Opera House, and Kaohsiung Cultural Center, among other. The "Bridge" tour also spawned a single of the same name. &&&&& &&&&& In September 2017, they were nominated for the 8th Golden Sound Award for Best Style Type Singles. &&&&& &&&&& In September 2017, the group was invited to Singapore to attend the Music Matters Festival. &&&&& &&&&& In October 2017, they visited Sapporo, Hokkaido to participate in the No Maps Creative Convention. &&&&& &&&&& In November 2018, the band released their second full-length album ''Underwater'' accompanied by a world tour. &&&&& &&&&& Discography &&&&& * ''Balance'' (2013) &&&&& * ''Angle'' (2014) &&&&& * ''Work'' (2016) &&&&& * ''Bridge'' (2017) &&&&& * ''Underwater'' (2018) &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
