Laughing Bill Hyde &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Laughing Bill Hyde''''' is a lost 1918 American adventure film directed by Hobart Henley and written by Willard Mack. The film stars Will Rogers, Anna Lehr, Clarence Oliver, Joseph Herbert, Robert Conville, and Dan Mason. The film was released on September 22, 1918, by Goldwyn Pictures.
