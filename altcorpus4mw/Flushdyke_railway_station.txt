Flushdyke railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Flushdyke''' was a Great Northern Railway station on Wakefield Road in Flushdyke, West Yorkshire. The station opened on 7 April 1867 and closed on 3 May 1941.
