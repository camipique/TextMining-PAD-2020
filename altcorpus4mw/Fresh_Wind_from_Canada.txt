Fresh Wind from Canada &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Fresh Wind from Canada''''' (German: '''''Frischer Wind aus Kanada''''') is a 1935 German comedy film directed by Erich Holder and starring Max Gülstorff, Dorit Kreysler and Paul Hörbiger. &&&&& &&&&& The film's sets were designed by the art director Erich Kettelhut and Max Mellin. It was shot at the Babelsberg Studios outside Potsdam. &&&&& &&&&& Cast &&&&& * Max Gülstorff as J. N.
