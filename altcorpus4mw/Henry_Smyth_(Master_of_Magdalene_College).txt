Henry Smyth (Master of Magdalene College) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Henry Smyth, D.D. was a 17th century priest and academic. &&&&& &&&&& Smyth was educated at Trinity College, Cambridge; He was Master of Magdalene College, Cambridge from 1626 until 1642; and Vice-Chancellor of the University of Cambridge from 1626 until 1627. He was a Prebendary of Lincoln from 1611 until 1629; and then of Peterborough from then until his death in 1642. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
