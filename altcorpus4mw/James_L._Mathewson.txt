James L. Mathewson &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''James L. "Jim" Mathewson''' (born March 16, 1938) is a former American Democrat politician who served in the Missouri Senate and the Missouri House of Representatives. &&&&& &&&&& Born in Warsaw, Missouri, Mathewson graduated from schools from Warsaw, Missouri. He attended Redding Junior College and California State University in Chico, California. He has been the owner of a real estate appraisal firm and a broadcasting company. Matthewson has served in the U.S. Army. &&&&& &&&&& His wife Doris H. Angel Mathewson, a native of Sedalia, Missouri, died June 14, 2017.
