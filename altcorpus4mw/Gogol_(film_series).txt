Gogol (film series) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Gogol''''' is a series of fantasy-horror films directed by Egor Baranov and produced by the Sreda production company. The films are loosely based on works by Nikolai Gogol from the collection ''Evenings on a Farm near Dikanka''. The title role is played by Alexander Petrov. &&&&& &&&&& ''Gogol. The Beginning'' is the first of three films of the project ''Gogol'' and the first Russian television series to have a theatrical release. It was released on August 31, 2017. It earned $7,757,988 at the box-office. &&&&& &&&&& The film ''Gogol. Viy'' was released on April 5, 2018, The box-office gross was $8 011 641. &&&&& &&&&& ''Gogol.
