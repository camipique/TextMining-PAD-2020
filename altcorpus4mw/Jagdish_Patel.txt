Jagdish Patel &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Dr. Jagdish Balar''', better known as '''Dr. Jagdish Patel''' is a BJP politician from Surat, Gujarat. He is the 36th and current Mayor of Surat / Surat Municipal Corporation.
