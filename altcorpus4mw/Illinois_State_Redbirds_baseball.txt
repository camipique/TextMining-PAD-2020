Illinois State Redbirds baseball &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Illinois State Redbirds''' baseball team is the varsity intercollegiate athletic team of Illinois State University in Normal, IL. The team competes at the Division I level of the NCAA and is a member of the Missouri Valley Conference. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The program's first year of competition was 1890. Thirteen players that played for Illinois State have made it to the MLB level, with the most recent being Paul DeJong of the St. Louis Cardinals. &&&&& &&&&& Stadium &&&&& Illinois State plays at Duffy Bass Field, a 1,000-seat (1,200, including non-seating areas) facility located in the northwest corner of the university's campus in Normal, IL.
