Greater Washington Partnership &&&&& Introduction &&&&& The '''Greater Washington Partnership''' is an organization which seeks to promote economic growth throughout the Washington metropolitan area, the Baltimore-Columbia-Towson, MD Metro Area, and the Richmond Metro area. &&&&& &&&&& In May 2018 the organization advocated for higher tolls. &&&&& &&&&& Transportation and improving workplaces were two early goals of the organization. &&&&& &&&&& Developing the region is part of a strategy to attract more companies to establish in the region. &&&&& &&&&& The organization seeks establish some standardization in regional educational credentials. &&&&& &&&&& The Greater Washington Partnership motto is "From Baltimore to Richmond, fostering unity, advancing growth." &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
