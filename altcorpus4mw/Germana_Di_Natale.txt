Germana Di Natale &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Germana Di Natale''' (born 2 April 1974) is a former professional tennis player from Italy. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& A right-handed player born in Rome, Di Natale started playing tennis at the age of 10 and began touring in the early 1990s. &&&&& &&&&& Di Natale was a singles bronze medalist at the 1997 Summer Universiade. &&&&& &&&&& As a professional player she is most noted for her quarter-final appearance as a qualifier at the 2000 WTA Madrid Open, where she had a win over the top seed Mary Pierce en route. Starting the tournament with a ranking of 258, she upset the world's sixth ranked player Pierce with a straight sets second round win. In the quarter-finals she had to retire hurt while trailing Iva Majoli by a set due to an injury to her right arm. &&&&& &&&&& During her career she competed in the qualifying draws of all four grand
