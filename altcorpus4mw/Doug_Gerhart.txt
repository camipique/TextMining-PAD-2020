Doug Gerhart &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Douglas Arthur Gerhart''' (born November 23, 1936) is a former American football coach and player. He served as head football coach at Occidental College from 1967 to 1969, compiling a record of 8–17–1. Gerhart was the offensive coordinator at Colorado State University from 1973 to 1974.
