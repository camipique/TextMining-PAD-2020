Don't Quote Me (TV series) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''''Don't Quote Me''''' was a television panel game produced by Open Media and transmitted by Channel 4 in 1990. It was hosted by Geoffrey Perkins, who said "The show exploits foot in mouth quotes.
