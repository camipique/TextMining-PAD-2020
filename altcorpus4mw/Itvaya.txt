Itvaya &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Itvaya''' () is a village / panchayat located in the Gir Gadhada Taluka of Gir Somnath district in Gujarat State, India. Earlier, until August 2013, Itvaya was part of Una Taluka and Junagadh district. The latitude 20.95438 and longitude 70.976896 are the geo-coordinate of the Village Itvaya. Gandhinagar is the state capital of Itvaya village which is located around 350 kilometres away from Itvaya. &&&&& &&&&& According to Census 2011, with the 384 families, the population of this village is 1902. Out of this, 956 are males and 946 are females. Most residents are dependent on agriculture. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Demographics &&&&& &&&&& According to the 2011 census of India, Itvaya has 384 households.
