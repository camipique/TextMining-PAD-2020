Identity Card (Azerbaijan) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& An '''Identity card''' of a citizen of the Republic of Azerbaijan is an identity document in the territory of the Republic. It is forbidden to obtain it by using illegal way and to refuse to issue or change the identity card. When the identity card is lost, the holder or the legitimate representative of the holder must immediately apply to the state body issuing the document. &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& *Azerbaijani nationality law &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
