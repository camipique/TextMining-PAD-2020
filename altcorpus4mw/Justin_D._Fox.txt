Justin D. Fox &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Justin D. Fox''' (born May 4, 1967) is a South African author, photojournalist, lecturer and editor living in Cape Town, South Africa. He is editor of Getaway travel magazine and has freelanced internationally for many newspapers and magazines. Educated at SACS junior and high schools, he graduated from the University of Cape Town with a BA in English in 1990. He was elected as a Rhodes Scholar in 1991 and graduated with a masters and a doctorate in English from Oxford University.
