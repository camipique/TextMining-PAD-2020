89	Dagblaðið
Dagblaðið &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Dagblaðið''''' () was an Icelandic newspaper founded in 1975 by former employees of Vísir with Jónas Kristjánsson as its first editor. It marked a breakthrough in Icelandic newspaper publication as it was the first major newspaper that was independent of political parties.
