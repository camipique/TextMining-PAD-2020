Hostess House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Hostess House''' is a historic house in Palo Alto, California. It was built in 1918 by the YMCA for members of the United States Army to meet their relatives while they were still serving in World War I. The location was then an army camp. The house was designed by architect Julia Morgan. It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since July 30, 1976. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
