Dance with Me (Air Supply song) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''Dance with Me'''" is a song by English/Australian soft rock duo Air Supply from their seventeenth album, ''Mumbo Jumbo''. It was released as the first single from the album.
