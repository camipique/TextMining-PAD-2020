Ísak Ernir Kristinsson &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Ísak Ernir Kristinsson''' (born 22 July 1993) is an Icelandic basketball referee and a politician. &&&&& &&&&& Basketball &&&&& Ísak Ernir started refereeing in the Icelandic national leagues at the age of sixteen and soon established himself as one of the top referees in the country. In 2017 he was selected to referee in the NBA Summer League. On 24 January 2018, he ejected a fan from a game between ÍR and Njarðvík's after the fan had leaned over him from the sidelines and called him a racist. &&&&& &&&&& Politics &&&&& Ísak Ernir ran for Alþingi in 2016 as a member of the Independence Party. In July 2018 he was appointed by Bjarni Benediktsson as chairman of Kadeco, a state owned redevelopment company that was created in 2006 to oversee the sales of buildings that belonged to the former US Naval Air Station in Keflavík. as a chairman Ísak Ernir has taken active part in daily operations. He has also stated that he understand that people find him to be too young and inexperienced for the job but will do his best in this important role. &&&&& &&&&& . &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Ísak Ernir is the son of long time basketball referee Kristinn Óskarsson. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * 2016 Alþingi election profile at ruv.is &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
