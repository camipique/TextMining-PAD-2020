E.G. Ellis House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''E.G. Ellis House''' is a historic house in Hamilton, Montana. It was built in 1900 for Sidney A. Wheeler, an immigrant from Canada. Wheeler was a farmer who owned a slaughterhouse. He was a member of the Hamilton Masonic Lodge, and he died in 1947. &&&&& &&&&& The house was designed in the Colonial Revival and Queen Anne architectural styles. It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since August 26, 1988. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 		 &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
