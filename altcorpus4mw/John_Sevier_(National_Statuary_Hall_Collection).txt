John Sevier (National Statuary Hall Collection) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''John Sevier''''' is a bronze sculpture depicting the American politician of the same name by Belle Kinney and Leopold Scholz, installed in the United States Capitol's National Statuary Hall, in Washington, D.C., as part of the National Statuary Hall Collection.
