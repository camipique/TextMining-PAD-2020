Gifted (Singaporean TV series) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Gifted''''' (Chinese: 天之骄子) is a Singaporean drama produced by Mediacorp Studios Malaysia and telecast on Mediacorp Channel 8. The show is currently airing at 9pm on weekdays and has a repeat telecast at 8am the following day.
