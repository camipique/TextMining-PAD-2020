Herzeg-Bosnia national football team &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Herzeg-Bosnia national football team''' (Nogometna reprezentacija Herceg-Bosne) represented Croatian Republic of Herzeg-Bosnia in international association football competitions. The team was managed by the Football Federation of Herzeg-Bosnia. &&&&& &&&&& The only match played by Herzeg-Bosnia was against Paraguay on 21 April 1996. The match was played at the Estadio Defensores del Chaco in Asunción, Paraguay before 10,988 viewers. Paraguay won 3-0. The coach for Herzeg-Bosnia was Branko Međugorac. Herzeg-Bosnia played in the Paraguayan away kit, because their kits were very similar to the Paraguayan home kit. However, FIFA officially holds this match to be between Paraguay and Bosnia and Herzegovina. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
