Dohrmann Building &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Dohrmann Building''', also known as the '''Dohrmann Hotel Supply Company Building''', is a historic building in San Jose, California. It was built in 1926 for A.B.C. Dohrmann, a businessman from San Francisco. It was first used as an office building by the Dohrmann Hotel Supply Company in 1927. &&&&& &&&&& The building was designed in the Classical Revival and Spanish Colonial Revival architectural styles.
