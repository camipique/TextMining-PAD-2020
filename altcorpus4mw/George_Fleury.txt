George Fleury &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''George Fleury''' (1740-1825) was an Irish Anglican priest in the last decades of the 18th century and the first three of the 19th. &&&&& &&&&& Fleury was educated at Trinity College, Dublin. A prebendary of Lismore, he was Archdeacon of Waterford from 1773 until his death.
