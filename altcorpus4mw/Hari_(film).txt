Hari (film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Hari''''' (Nepali: हरि) is a 2018 Nepalese film directed and written by Pratik Gurung and Safal K.C.. The film stars Bipin Karki in the lead role alongside Kamal Mani Nepal, Thinley Lhamo and Sunita Thakur. The film was released on 1 June 2018 in Nepal under the banner of Shubhalabh Film Production. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& Bishnu Hari (Bipin Karki), commonly known as Hari, is a strict religious man who is following rules set by his father, grandfathers and his strict mother known as Parvati.
