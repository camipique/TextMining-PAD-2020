Emily Mannix &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Emily Mannix''' (born 16 April 1994) is an Australian netball player in the Suncorp Super Netball league, playing for the Melbourne Vixens. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& Originally from Geelong, Mannix made her debut for the Melbourne Vixens in 2015, working as a defensive understudy to Bianca Chatfield and Geva Mentor. She won the club Coaches Award in 2016 and was runner up in the club MVP in 2017, a run of form which culminated that year in her debut for the national team.
