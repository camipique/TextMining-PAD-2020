FlyErbil &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''FlyErbil''' is a Kurdish airline based at Erbil International Airport, Iraqi Kurdistan.. The only aircraft they have, a Boeing 737-300, is operated by the Ukrainian Airline, Bravo Airways. All flights on the airline in and out of Erbil must connect at Kiev.
