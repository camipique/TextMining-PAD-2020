LeadNow &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''LeadNow''' (French: ) is a Canadian non-profit, multi-partisan citizens' advocacy organization formed in 2010. It campaigns for changes to environmental policy, workers rights, education, and proportional representation. &&&&& &&&&& The organization has been a vocal opponent to the Trans Mountain Pipeline. &&&&& &&&&& During elections, LeadNow has advocated for its members to vote strategically as a tool to advance its advocacy. ''Vote Together'' campaigns have targeted swing ridings.
