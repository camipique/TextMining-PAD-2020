Grant's leaf-toed gecko &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Grant's leaf-toed gecko''' (''Hemidactylus granti'' ) is a species of gecko, a lizard in the family Gekkonidae. The species is endemic to Socotra. &&&&& &&&&& Etymology &&&&& The specific name, ''granti'', is in honor of Scottish ornithologist William Robert Ogilvie-Grant. &&&&& &&&&& Reproduction &&&&& ''H. granti'' is oviparous. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Further reading &&&&& *Boulenger GA (1899).
