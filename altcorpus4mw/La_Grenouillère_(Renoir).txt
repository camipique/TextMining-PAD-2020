La Grenouillère (Renoir) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''La Grenouillère''''' is an 1869 oil on canvas painting by Pierre-Auguste Renoir, now in the Nationalmuseum in Stockholm. It shows the 'camembert', a small island planted with a single tree, linking the island to a pontoon-bridge. &&&&& &&&&& It was painted from the 'La Grenouillère' restaurant on the Île de la Grenouillère at Croissy-sur-Seine, as was Monet's ''Bain à la Grenouillère'', also painted in 1869 in the early days of Impressionism. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& category:Paintings of the Nationalmuseum Stockholm &&&&& category:1869 paintings &&&&& category:Paintings by Pierre-Auguste Renoir &&&&& &&&&&
