Felmingham Cutting &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Felmingham Cutting''' is a  Local Nature Reserve west of North Walsham in Norfolk. It is owned by North Norfolk District Council and managed by Butterfly Conservation. &&&&& &&&&& Sixteen species of butterfly breed on this railway cutting, which is on a former line of the Midland and Great Northern Railway, and is now part of the Weavers Way long distance footpath. &&&&& &&&&& There is access along the Weavers Way from North Walsham. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
