Jhudvadli &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jhudvadli''' (), Also spelled as Zudvadli or Judvadli, is a village / panchayat located in the Gir Gadhada Taluka of Gir Somnath district in Gujarat State, India. Earlier, until August 2013, Jhudvadli was part of Una Taluka and Junagadh district. The latitude 20.889838 and longitude 70.992009 are the geo-coordinate of the Village Jhudvadli. Gandhinagar is the state capital of Jhudvadli village which is located around 400 kilometres away from Jhudvadli. &&&&& &&&&& According to Census 2011, with the 357 families, the population of this village is 1801. Out of this, 896 are males and 905 are females. Most residents are dependent on agriculture. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Demographics &&&&& &&&&& According to the 2011 census of India, Jhudvadli has 357 households.
