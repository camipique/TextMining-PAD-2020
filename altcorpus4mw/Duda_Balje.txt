Duda Balje &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Duda Balje''' (born 16 October 1977) is a Kosovan politician of Bosniak origin.. A member of the Vakat Coalition, she served as a member of the Kosovan Assembly from 2001 to 2017.
