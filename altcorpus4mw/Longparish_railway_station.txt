Longparish railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Longparish railway station''' served the village of Longparish, Hampshire, England from 1885 to 1959 on the Fullerton to Hurstbourne Line. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened in 1885 by the London and South Western Railway. It was situated north of and south of station. The station closed to passengers in 1931 and to goods traffic in 1956.
