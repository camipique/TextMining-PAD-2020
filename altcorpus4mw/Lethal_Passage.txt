Lethal Passage &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Lethal Passage: The Story of a Gun''' is a 1995 non-fiction book by American author Erik Larson. Through the lens of a 1988 school shooting in Virginia Beach, the author explores America's gun culture and the ease in acquiring such weapons as the one used by the school shooter, the MAC-11. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
