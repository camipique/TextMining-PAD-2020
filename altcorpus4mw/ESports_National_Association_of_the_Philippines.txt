ESports National Association of the Philippines &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''eSports National Association of the Philippines''' (eSNAP) is a local governing body for eSports or electronic sports and professional video gaming in the Philippines. It was founded in 2018, following the launch of the first franchise-based eSports league in the country, The Nationals. &&&&& &&&&& eSNAP currently sanctions all of The Nationals' Dota 2, Mobile Legends: Bang Bang and console game tournaments including "Road to the Nationals" which will set from August to October, 2018. &&&&& &&&&& It has yet to be recognized as a regular member by the Philippine Olympic Committee (POC). &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
