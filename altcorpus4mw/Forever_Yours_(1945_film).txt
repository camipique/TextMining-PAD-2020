Forever Yours (1945 film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Forever Yours''''' is a 1945 American drama film directed by William Nigh and starring Gale Storm, C. Aubrey Smith and Johnny Mack Brown. It was made by Monogram Pictures. Although the studio concentrated on low-budget films, this was one of the company's more prestigious releases of the year.
