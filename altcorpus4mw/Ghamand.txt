Ghamand &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Ghamand''''' ( Pride) is a Pakistani Urdu-language drama serial that began airing on A-Plus TV from 29 June 2018. The series is produced by Sadia Jabbar under their banner Sadia Jabbar Productions. &&&&& &&&&& Noman Ijaz and Sunita Marshall playing the lead with Minal Khan in a supporting role.
