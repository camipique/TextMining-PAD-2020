57	Harrytoombsia
Harrytoombsia &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The placoderm genus '''''Harrytoombsia''''' was a fast-swimming predator. &&&&& &&&&& It had knife-like blades, covered with tissue similar to enamel, on the outer edge of the jaw. &&&&& &&&&& ''Harrytoombsia'' was the ‘little brother’ of the five-metre-long placoderm ''Dunkleosteus'', the giant shark-like fish of its time, which preyed on its smaller relatives. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
