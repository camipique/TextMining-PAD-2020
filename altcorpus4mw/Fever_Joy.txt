Fever Joy &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Fever Joy''' is a three piece Alternative Pop band formed in Los Angeles, California in 2017. The band consists of vocalist Avery Robitaille, guitarist Kevin Holm and drummer Sean Baker. &&&&& &&&&& Their debut single 'Shots' was released in June 2018 and charted on Hype Machine within the first week of its release. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& &&&&& ===Early history=== &&&&& Singer, Avery Robitaille was raised in Indonesia by missionary parents until she was 18 years old. After finishing home schooling, she relocated to Los Angeles, California to pursue a career in music. While regularly playing open mic nights at The Night Owl in Fullerton, California, she was approached by Sean and Kevin who were both interested in starting a band with her. &&&&& &&&&& ===Recording=== &&&&& Fever Joy was eventually introduced to Adam Castilla of The Colourist. After agreeing to producing their first single, Adam connected Fever Joy to Grammy Award-winning mastering engineer Joe LaPorta to finalize their sound. &&&&& &&&&& Discography &&&&& '''Singles''' &&&&& * ''Shots'' (2018) &&&&& * ''R.Y.W.B.''
