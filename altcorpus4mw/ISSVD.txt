ISSVD &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& thumb &&&&& &&&&& The '''ISSVD''', which stands for '''The International Society for the Study of Vulvovaginal Diseases''' is a non-profit organization that was founded in 1970 at the Sixth World Congress of the International Federation of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (FIGO) in New York City. It is composed of health care professionals from different areas, including, gynecologists, dermatologists, general practitioners, physiotherapist, nurse practitioners, etc. devoted to the study, investigation, and treatment of vulvovaginal diseases. &&&&& &&&&& The ISSVD regularly promotes and develops terminology, classification, and guidelines concerning these conditions.
