List of Monster High films &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& Monster High is an American fashion doll franchise created by Mattel and launched in July 2010. The characters are inspired by monster movies, sci-fi horror, thriller fiction, and various other creatures. Mattel began releasing a number of animated direct-to-videos, some of which have been broadcast as television specials and films on Nickelodeon.
