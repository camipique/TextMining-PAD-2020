Lize Business District station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Lize Business District station''' () is a planned interchange station on Lines 14, 16 and New Airport line of the Beijing Subway in Fengtai District. &&&&& &&&&& Planned opening time &&&&& Line 14 (Middle section): As of January 2019, the construction of Line 14 near Lize Business District station has not fully started. The opening time is still unknown. &&&&& &&&&& Line 16 (Southern section): 2021 (Planned) &&&&& &&&&& New Airport line (Northern extension): 2021 (Planned) &&&&& &&&&& Location &&&&& The station, located outside the Southwest 2nd Ring Road, is being built at the intersection of the east-west Lize Road (), which Line 14 will run parallel to, and the north-south East Luotuowan Road (), which Line 16 will run parallel to.
