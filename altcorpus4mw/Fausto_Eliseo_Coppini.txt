Fausto Eliseo Coppini &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Fausto Eliseo Coppini''' (born in 1870 in Milan, Italy – 1945) was an Italian-Argentine painter. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& He studied at the Brera Academy. In 1888, he moved to South America traveling through Chile and Peru. After a brief sojourn in Italy, he settled in Buenos Aires. In 1888–1889, he won a bronze prize at a National Exposition in Argentina. Among his pupils were Ángel Vena, Rodolfo Franco, Gastón Jarry, and Atilio Malinverno. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
