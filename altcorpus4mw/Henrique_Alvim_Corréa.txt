Wells's 1898 "The War of the Worlds" &&&&& &&&&& '''Henrique Alvim Corréa''' (30 January 1876–7 June 1910) was a Brazilian illustrator of military and science fiction books. He was born in Rio de Janeiro, and died in Brussels. He is best known for his illustrations of a French translation of H. G. Wells's novel "The War of the Worlds". &&&&& &&&&& Exhibitions &&&&& &&&&& * 1972 – São Paulo. Museu de Arte “Assis Chateaubriand” (MASP). &&&&& * 1973 – Rio de Janeiro.
