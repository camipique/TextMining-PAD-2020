Japan Act of Specified Commercial Transactions &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Japan Act of Specified Commercial Transactions''' (特定商取引法) aims to protect consumers from harm and to ensure fairness in commercial transactions. It is described and explained in a publication by the Japan Consumer Affairs Agency(JCAA). Door-to-door sales have been prone to conflicts between consumers and companies. It includes regulations on the act of soliciting. &&&&& &&&&& Notes &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& * Consumer Affairs Agency, Understanding the Act of Specified Commercial Transactions (Japanese) 消費者庁 (2016). 平成28年版　特定商取引に関する法律の解説.
