Fourth of July Creek (Kenai Peninsula, Alaska) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Fourth of July Creek''' is a creek on the Kenai Peninsula in Alaska. It rises on the upper Resurrection Peninsula from various small glaciers in the Chugach Mountains and flows to empty into Resurrection Bay near Seward. &&&&& &&&&& Fishing &&&&& Fishing for salmon is not permitted in the Fourth of July Creek. However, its delta remains a popular fishing spot, as Resurrection Bay has a very productive silver salmon fishery. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
