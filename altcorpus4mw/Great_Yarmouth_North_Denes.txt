Great Yarmouth North Denes &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Great Yarmouth North Denes''' is a biological Site of Special Scientific Interest in Great Yarmouth in Norfolk. It is a Nature Conservation Review site, and a Special Protection Area &&&&& &&&&& These beaches have a complete succession of dune vegetation types, from foredunes to dry acid dune grassland, the latter of which is very extensive. The site has the largest breeding colony of little terns in Britain. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
