Fisnik Zuka &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Fisnik Zuka''' (born 3 September 1995) is a Macedonian footballer who plays for FK Renova in the Macedonian First Football League. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& ===Shkupi=== &&&&& Zuka started his senior career with the club. He made his First League debut for the club on 9 August 2015 in a 2-2 away draw with Vardar, coming on as a 90th minute substitute for Ardian Nuhiu. He scored his first goal in the Macedonian top flight on 16 November 2016 in a 1-1 home draw with FK Pobeda. His goal, scored in the 24th minute, made the score 1-0 to Shkupi. &&&&& &&&&& ===Flamurtari=== &&&&& In January 2018, Zuka moved to Albanian Superliga club Flamurtari Vlorë. He made his competitive debut for the club on 26 January 2018 in a 1-0 victory in the league over Partizani Tirana, coming on as a 65th minute substitute for Ardit Hoxhaj. &&&&& &&&&& ===Renova=== &&&&& In July 2018, Zuka moved back to Macedonia, signing with First League club FK Renova.
