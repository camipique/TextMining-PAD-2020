67	Lilaceophlebia
Lilaceophlebia &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Lilaceophlebia''''' is a genus of three species of crust fungi in the family Meruliaceae. The genus was first proposed by Erast Parmasto in 1968 as a section of the genus ''Phlebia''. Viacheslav Spirin and Ivan Zmitrovich elevated the taxon to generic status in 2004. &&&&& &&&&& Species &&&&& *''Lilaceophlebia georgica'' (Parmasto) Spirin & Zmitr.
