Fancy Nancy (TV series) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Fancy Nancy''''' (titled '''''Fancy Nancy Clancy''''' in the UK and other countries) is an American computer-animated children's television series. Produced by Disney Television Animation for Disney Junior, and based on the books of the same name by Jane O'Connor, the show follows a day in the life of Nancy, a little girl who adores everything fancy. The series was developed by Jamie Mitchell and Krista Tucker. Alyson Hannigan and Rob Riggle lend their voices as Claire and Doug Clancy, Nancy's parents. &&&&& &&&&& The series premiered on July 13, 2018 in the United States, and the following day in Canada. Disney renewed the series for a second season prior to its premiere. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& Six-year-old Nancy Clancy enjoys fancy and French things that ranges from her outfit to her and her creative elaborate attire as she plans to teach some fanciness to her ordinary family and her friends. &&&&& &&&&& Characters &&&&& * Nancy Margaret Clancy (voiced by Mia Sinclair Jenness) – A 6-year-old girl who enjoys fancy things, and is a bit of Francophile. Her favorite color is fuchsia, she adores butterflies, has an adorable dog named Frenchy, her own secret delivery mailbox to share all her party invitations with her best friend Bree, and her own playhouse. She also has a doll named Marabelle and sometimes carries her everywhere. &&&&& * JoJo Clancy (voiced by Spencer Moss) – Nancy's younger sister. She has an imaginary friend named Dudley, is a PIT (Pirate In Training), loves to help, giggles a lot, and loves her stuffed animal "Mr. Monkey". She is 4 years old. &&&&& * Claire Clancy (voiced by Alyson Hannigan) – Nancy's mother. &&&&& * Doug Clancy (voiced by Rob Riggle) – Nancy's father. &&&&& * Mrs. Devine (voiced by Christine Baranski) – Nancy's neighbor.
