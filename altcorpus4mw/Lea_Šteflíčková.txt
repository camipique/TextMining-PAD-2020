Lea Šteflíčková &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Lea Šteflíčková''' (born April 21, 1998) is a Czech model and beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned Česká Miss 2018 on 3 June 2018. She represented Czech Republic at Miss Universe 2018 pageant in Bangkok, Thailand. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Šteflíčková lives in Ústí nad Labem, Czech Republic. She is currently working as a fashion model at PH Model Management. &&&&& &&&&& Pageantry &&&&& === Česká Miss 2018 === &&&&& Šteflíčková was crowned Česká Miss 2018 held its grand finale on 3 June 2018. She succeeded outgoing by Česká Miss 2017 Michaela Habáňová. &&&&& &&&&& === Miss Universe 2018 === &&&&& Šteflíčková represented Czech Republic at Miss Universe 2018 in Bangkok, Thailand.
