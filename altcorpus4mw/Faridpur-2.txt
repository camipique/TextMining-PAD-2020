Faridpur-2 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Faridpur-2''' is a constituency represented in the Jatiya Sangsad (National Parliament) of Bangladesh since 2008 by Syeda Sajeda Chowdhury of the Awami League. &&&&& &&&&& Boundaries &&&&& The constituency encompasses Nagarkanda and Saltha upazilas, and one union parishad of Sadarpur Upazila: Krishnapur. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The constituency was created for the first general elections in newly independent Bangladesh, held in 1973. &&&&& &&&&& Ahead of the 2008 general election, the Election Commission redrew constituency boundaries to reflect population changes revealed by the 2001 Bangladesh census.
