Julien (song) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''Julien'''" is a song by Canadian singer Carly Rae Jepsen from her upcoming fourth album ''Dedicated.'' It was released on April 19, 2019, by 604, School Boy and Interscope Records. &&&&& &&&&& Composition &&&&& "Julien" was written at Maderas Village, Nicaragua in August 2016. &&&&& &&&&& Promotion &&&&& Jepsen posted a line from the song and announced its release date on social media on April 16, 2019. It was released alongside the pre-order for ''Dedicated'' on April 19, 2019. "Julien" made its world premiere on Mix FM Rio de Janeiro's PopLineMix radio as First Play. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
