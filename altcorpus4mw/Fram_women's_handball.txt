Fram women's handball &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Fram women's handball''' team is the women's handball section of Icelandic multi-sport club Fram from Reykjavík. It currently plays in the Úrvalsdeild kvenna.
