Greenloaning railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Greenloaning railway station''' served the village of Greenloaning, Perth and Kinross, Scotland from 1848 to 1956 on the Scottish Central Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened on 22 May 1848 by the Scottish Central Railway.
