Elsa Cirigliano &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Elsa Asunción Cirigliano''' is a Venezuelan woman, wife of former President Fernando Romeo Lucas García. &&&&& &&&&& She was secretly married on October 12, 1978 with Fernando Romeo Lucas García, in the Presidential House, months after he assumed the Presidency of Guatemala.
