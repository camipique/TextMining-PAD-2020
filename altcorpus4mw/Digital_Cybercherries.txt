Digital Cybercherries &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Digital Cybercherries Ltd.''' is a British video game developer based in the United Kingdom. It was founded in 2015. They developed ''New Retro Arcade: Neon'' and ''Hypercharge: Unboxed''. &&&&& &&&&& The studio's first game was ''New Retro Arcade: Neon'', released independently on 1 August 2016. The game was met with favourable reception and received VRDB's Players Choice VR of the Year 2016 award.
