John Cole (choreographer) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''John Cole''' is a Zimbabwean dance choreographer, dancer, actor, TV presenter and artists director. He is also brand ambassador of Ster-Kinekor Zimbabwe. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& &&&&& John Cole was born in 1988 in a family of six. He grew up in Sunningdale suburb in Zimbabwe capital Harare. After the passing of his parents, Cole stayed with his grandmother but could not continue with school after finishing his ordinary levels. He then decided to pursue sport as a cricketer and he went as far as undergoing trials with the Zimbabwe National Cricket team but could not go on due to an injury. After the injury, John Cole decided to follow his passion for dancing. &&&&& &&&&& Having launched is career professionally in 2011, John Cole has three dance awards under his belt as well as five other collaborated awards with several artists and organisations. &&&&& &&&&& Notable works &&&&& &&&&& Cole has worked with large corporates which include Barclays Bank Zimbabwe, Steward Bank, DSTV and Coca-Cola. He has choreographed several significant shows, concerts and events in Zimbabwe including; &&&&& &&&&& *Former Zimbabwe first lady Grace Mugabe birthday party &&&&& *Harare International Carnival &&&&& *Harare International Festival of the Arts (HIFA) &&&&& *Zimbabwe Fashion Week &&&&& *Miss Tourism Zimbabwe &&&&& *Miss Deaf Zimbabwe &&&&& *Miss University of Zimbabwe &&&&& *Miss Women’s University in Africa &&&&& *Shoko Festival &&&&& *Jah Prayzah album launch &&&&& *Cynthia Mare album launch &&&&& *Africa Joy Choir &&&&& *STEM &&&&& *ZAOGA Live DVD launch &&&&& *PERMICAN Awards &&&&& *Foodworld Events &&&&& *Zimbabwe Uniformed Forces events (ZRP, ZPS, Airforce and ZDF) &&&&& &&&&& Other activities &&&&& &&&&& John Cole is the founder and president of The Zimbabwe Choreographers and Dancers Association as well as president of Zimbabwe Dance Industry Awards. &&&&& &&&&& John Cole is also a dance teacher at Harare International School, Maranatha Group of Schools as well as Allure Pre-School. &&&&& &&&&& Controversy &&&&& In 2016, when Cole won two dance awards at Zim Hip-Hop Awards and Zim Models Awards, this caused some storm on social media as many felt he was only doing choreography not dancing.
