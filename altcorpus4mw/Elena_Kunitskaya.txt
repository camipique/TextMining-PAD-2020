Elena Kunitskaya &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Elena Kunitskaya''' (died 1684), was a Ukrainian Hetmana by marriage to Stefan Kunicki, Hetman of Ukraine (r. 1683-1684). She was an influential figure among the Ukrainian Cossacks.
