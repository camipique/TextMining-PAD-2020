Le Feuvre & Roze &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Le Feuvre & Roze''' is a contemporary art gallery in Paris. &&&&& &&&&& Nowadays, Le Feuvre & Roze represents a significant number of artists of different nationalities in France. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The gallery was founded by Franck Le Feuvre by the name of Le Feuvre Gallery and in 2016, Jonathan Roze became its director. Later, in 2018, Roze became a partner in the enterprise, with the accompanying name change to Le Feuvre & Roze.
