Frederik Schwarz &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Frederik Schwarz. &&&&& '''Frederik Schwarz''' (1753-1838), was a Danish actor. He was an elite actor of the Royal Danish Theatre in 1773-1810. He played roles in both comedy and tragedy and had a great impact on the contemporary acting tradition in Denmark when forming the new organisation of the theater, as the founder and manager of the ''Det Dramatiske Selskab'' 1775-78 and as the theater's acting instructor in 1779-1816. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& * Schwarz, Frederik i Nordisk familjebok (andra upplagan, 1916) &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
