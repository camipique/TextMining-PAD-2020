Jorge Alcocer Varela &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Jorge Alcocer Varela''' (born 8 February 1946) is a Mexican healthcare professional. He is a former researcher at the Salvador Zubirán National Institute of Health Sciences and Nutrition.
