Fiorella Chiappe &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Fiorella Chiappe y Madsen''' (born 1 January 1996, Barcelona, Spain) is an Argentine athlete specialising in the 400 metres hurdles. She won the gold medal in the 400 metres hurdles at the 2018 South American Games and holds the national record of 56.08. Previously she competed in the heptathlon winning, among others, a silver medal at the 2014 South American Games. &&&&& &&&&& International competitions &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
