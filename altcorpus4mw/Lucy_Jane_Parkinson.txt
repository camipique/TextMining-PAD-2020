Lucy Jane Parkinson &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Lucy Jane Parkinson''' or '''LoUis CYfer''' is a British actor and drag king. &&&&& &&&&& Education &&&&& She studied performing arts at Rotherham College of Arts and Technology in South Yorkshire and contemporary theatre and performance at Manchester Metropolitan University before gaining an MA in contemporary performance making at Brunel University London. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& She played the central character Joan of Arc and all the other, male, characters in ''Joan'', written and directed by Lucy J. Skillbeck for the Milk Presents company. Performances included Camden People's Theatre in 2015, the Edinburgh Fringe Festival in 2016 (winning a Scotsman Fringe First award and ''The Stage'' award) and 2017, the Oval House Theatre, Brixton, in 2017 and Spazju Kreattiv in Malta in 2018. &&&&& &&&&& In 2017 she was one of the cast of four in Milk Presents' production ''Bullish'', a retelling of the myth of the minotaur, at Camden People's Theatre. &&&&& &&&&& In Summer 2018 she played D'Artagnan in The Dukes' promenade production of ''The Three Musketeers'', adapted by Hattie Naylor from Alexandre Dumas' novel, in Williamson Park, Lancaster.
