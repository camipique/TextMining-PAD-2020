Jonathan Tornato &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Jonathan Tornato''' (born June 1, 1989) is a French professional basketball player for Olympique Antibes of LNB Pro A. &&&&& &&&&& Tornato played with AS Monaco until he was cut in 2015. He posted 8.5 points and 5.2 rebounds per game in his last year. On October 1, 2016, he signed with the Antibes Sharks.
