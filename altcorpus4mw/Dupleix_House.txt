Dupleix House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Dupleix House''' is a historic house located at 106 Lafayette Street in Youngsville, Louisiana. &&&&& &&&&& Built c.1895 as a single story cottage and enlarged c.1910 by Pierre Alcide Dupleix, the house is a large two-story frame residence with Queen Anne and Colonial Revival elements. To the rear of the house there are two sheds which are considered contributing elements. &&&&& &&&&& The house was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on October 4, 1984. &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * National Register of Historic Places listings in Lafayette Parish, Louisiana &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
