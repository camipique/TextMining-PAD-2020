Gomirje &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Gomirje'''() is a settlement in north-western Croatia, situated at the far east of the mountainous region of Gorski kotar in the Primorje-Gorski Kotar County. It is part of the Vrbovsko municipality.
