Glasterlaw railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Glasterlaw railway station''' served the area of Glasterlaw, Angus, Scotland from 1848 to 1956 on the Aberdeen Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened on 1 February 1848 by the Aberdeen Railway. It was situated north of and south of station. The goods yard was to the north.
