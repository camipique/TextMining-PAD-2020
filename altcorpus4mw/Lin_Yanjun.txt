Lin Yanjun &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Lin Yanjun''' (; born August 24, 1995), also known as '''Evan Lin''', is a Taiwanese singer based in mainland China. He debuted as a member of the C-pop group Nine Percent on April 6, 2018 through the Chinese idol reality survival show named Idol Producer. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Lin Yanjun was born on August 24, 1995 in Taiwan and moved to mainland China. He studied at Guangdong University of Foreign Studies. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& &&&&& === 2018: Idol Producer and Nine Percent === &&&&& Lin was a member of the trainee group Trainee18 under the label Shanghai Project Banana Co. He then participated in the idol reality survival show Idol Producer in January 2018. He ranked 5th in the final episode with 12,131,367 votes and debuted as a member of the C-pop group Nine Percent on April 6, 2018. &&&&& &&&&& === 2019: Solo career === &&&&& On 7 January 2019, Lin released his first solo mini-album ''Just a Wound'' (剛好的傷口). It earned 5 million yuan within 28 seconds of release, breaking six sales records on QQ Music. Its title single, "Imperfect Love", debuted at No. 4 on ''Billboard'' China Social Music Chart on 3 March 2019. &&&&& &&&&& Endorsements and Ambassadorship &&&&& In 2018, Lin was chosen to endorse the cosmetics brands Elizabeth Arden and Panasonic Beauty as well as being the ambassador for multiple brands such as Innisfree and Korean skincare company MISSHA. Lin was given the chance to be the spokesperson for both Philosophy and skincare line Martiderm, both which he accepted with pride. In 2019 Lin was chosen to present the full line of YSL Beauty as well as the Korean make-up brand 3CE. &&&&& &&&&& Filmography &&&&& &&&&& === Television shows === &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
