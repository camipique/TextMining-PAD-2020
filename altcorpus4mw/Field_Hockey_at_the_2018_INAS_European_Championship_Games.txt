Field Hockey at the 2018 INAS European Championship Games &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Field Hockey at the 2018 INAS European Championship Games''' will be the 1st edition of the 2018 INAS European Championship Games. It will be held from the 17th until the 18th of July 2018 in Paris, France. &&&&& &&&&& Qualified teams &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& Format &&&&& The five teams will be put in one group and each team will play the other four teams. The top two teams advance to the final to determine the winner. The bottom three teams play in a new group for the bronze medal. The teams are mixed with boys and girls.
