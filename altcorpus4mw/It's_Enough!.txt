It's Enough! &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''It's Enough!'''" is a song by American singer Lenny Kravitz, from his 11th studio album ''Raise Vibration''. It was released as the album's lead single on May 11, 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Background &&&&& The official press release describes the song as "a tense, decisive rumination on the global state of affairs today." Says Kravitz "People are standing up. I've had enough of racism. I've had enough of war. I've had enough of the destruction of the environment and the greed and dishonesty of world leaders. We've got to get back on track towards moving forward through higher understanding."
