Ice Cream and the Sound of Raindrops &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& is a 2017 Japanese drama film directed by Daigo Matsui. The film featured as part of the Tokyo International Film Festival, and the Jeonju International Film Festival. &&&&& &&&&& == Plot == &&&&& The story centres on a director, who is pushing the cast of actors in rehearsal of a small town production of the British play "Morning" by Simon Stephens. The director pushes the actors to put as much as they can into the role.
