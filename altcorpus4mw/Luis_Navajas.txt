Luis Navajas &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Luis Manuel Navajas Ramos''' (born 17 December 1948) is a Spanish prosecutor. He has been the Lieutenant Attorney of the Supreme Court since 2014 and acting Attorney General of the State following the sudden death of José Manuel Maza in November 2017.He is the second prosecutor who holds this position in office, when Eduardo Torres-Dulce resigned from the post on 19 December 2014 and until Consuelo Madrigal did not take possession on 13 January 2015. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& He graduated in Law, became professor between 1987 and 2003 of Crime Law at Basque Institute of Criminology and entered the judicial career in 1976. He was prosecutor of the Audiencia de Province of Guipúzcoa between 1987 and 2003 He became famous for his report that bears his name and that investigated some corrupt plots in the Intxarrurrondo barracks of the Guardia Civil and in which General Enrique Rodríguez was involved.
