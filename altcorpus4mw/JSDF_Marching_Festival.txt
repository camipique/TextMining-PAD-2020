JSDF Marching Festival &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The is the main cultural military tattoo in Tokyo, which features guest bands from the Asia-Pacific regions as well as bands of the Japan Self-Defense Forces. It is regularly held at the Nippon Budokan in Tokyo every November.
