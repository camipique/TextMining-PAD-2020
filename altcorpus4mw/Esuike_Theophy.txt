Esuike Theophy &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Theophy Esuike''' (born April 25, 1986) is a Nigerian Publicist, certified Google Digital marketing manager And creative industry entrepreneur. Founder of Theory-X Entertainment. His company publicises entertainers such as comedians Gandoki, Maleke Elvis Poko and musicians such as Dr. Pat, Oritse Femi, AY.Com, Yung Brown, Sheyman And many More. &&&&& &&&&& Early life &&&&& Theophy was born in Delta State. His family lived in Ughelli North, Delta State. He was educated at Christ Mission College Benin city and earned a diploma in Statistics from Delta State Polytechnic, Otefe-Oghara in 2008. He also earned a Diploma in Computer and Information Technology at Netman Computer Institutes Lagos in 2010 and a Bachelor's degree in Business Management from the National Open University of Nigeria. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& In 2009 he began working as a talent manager tutoring and managing entertainers in Lagos. &&&&& In 2010 Theophy opened Theory-X Entertainment, a Lagos-based management and promotional company. The brand established a business network within the entertainment and media industry, representing a diverse roster of event properties.
