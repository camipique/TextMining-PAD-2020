IndieAuth &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''IndieAuth''' is a standard decentralized authentication protocol that uses OAuth 2.0 and enables services to verify the identity of a user represented by a URL as well as to obtain an access token that can be used to access resources under the control of the user.. &&&&& &&&&& IndieAuth is developed in the IndieWeb community and was published as a W3C Note.
