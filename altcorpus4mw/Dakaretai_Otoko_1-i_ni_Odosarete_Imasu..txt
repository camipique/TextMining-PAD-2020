Dakaretai Otoko 1-i ni Odosarete Imasu. &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& is a Japanese yaoi manga series by Hashigo Sakurabi. It has been serialized in Libre Publishing's ''Monthly Magazine Be x Boy'' magazine since July 2013 and has been collected in six ''tankōbon'' volumes. An anime television series adaptation by CloverWorks premiered from October 5 to December 28, 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& Takato Saijō has reigned as the idol industry's "Most Huggable" for five years, that is until a freshman actor Junta Azumaya takes his place. Takato was always considered brusque while Azumaya's angelic smile is winning over admirers by comparison. The whole fiasco drives Takato to over indulge in alcohol and his actions catch the attention of the last person he wants to see. To his surprise, Azumaya uses this new info to blackmail Takato for his affection. They begin sleeping together regularly and the beginnings of a relationship form. &&&&& &&&&& Characters &&&&& ; &&&&& : &&&&& ; &&&&& : &&&&& ; &&&&& : &&&&& ; &&&&& : &&&&& ; &&&&& : &&&&& ; &&&&& : &&&&& &&&&& Media &&&&& ===Anime=== &&&&& An anime television series adaptation, ''DAKAICHI -I'm being harassed by the sexiest man of the year-'', premiered from October 5 to December 28, 2018 on Tokyo MX. The series is animated by CloverWorks and directed by Naoyuki Tatsuwa, with Yoshimi Narita handling series composition, Minako Shiba designing the characters, Masaharu Yokoyama composing the series' music, and Satoshi Motoyama as sound director. Minako Shiba will also be the chief animation director alongside Senri Kawaguchi and Yu Kurihara. The opening theme is "Fukazen Monologue" by Tomohisa Sako. The ending theme is "Chuntaka!" by Yūki Ono and Hiroki Takahashi under their character names. Aniplex of America has licensed the series and it is streamed on Crunchyroll.
