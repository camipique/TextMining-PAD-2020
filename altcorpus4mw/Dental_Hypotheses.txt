Dental Hypotheses &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Dental Hypotheses''''' is a quarterly peer-reviewed open access medical journal covering all aspects of dentistry. It was established in 2010 by Jafar Kolahi and Edward F. Rossomando. The journal is published by Medknow Publications and the editor-in-chief is Edward F. Rossomando (University of Connecticut).
