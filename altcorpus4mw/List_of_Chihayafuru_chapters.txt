List of Chihayafuru chapters &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The cover of the first volume of the ''Chihayafuru'' manga series released by Kodansha on 13 May 2008. &&&&& The manga series ''Chihayafuru'' is written and illustrated by Yuki Suetsugu. The series has been serialized in Kodansha's ''Be Love'' magazine since 28 December 2007. It is about a school girl, Chihaya Ayase, who is inspired by a new classmate to take up ''Hyakunin Isshu karuta'' competitively. The chapters have been collected into 40 ''tankōbon'' volumes as of November 2018. Kodansha has also published the first three volumes in a two-volume bilingual edition, with English translations by Stuart Varnam Atkin and Yōko Toyozaki. On 14 February 2017, Kodansha Comics began publishing a digital edition of the series in English; 11 volumes have been released as of 15 May 2018.
