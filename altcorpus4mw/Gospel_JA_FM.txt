Gospel JA FM &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Gospel JA FM''' (91.7 & 91.9 MHz) is a Jamaican commercial FM gospel radio station. It is owned and operated by KC Broadcasting Company Limited. Gospel JA fm operates in Jamaica on 91.7 and 91.9 on the fm band. Gospel JA fm has its offices and studios at Unit 5, Tri7 Business Centre, 7 Ivy Green Crescent, Kingston 5, Jamaica. The CEO/Managing Director of Gospel JA fm is Wyatt K. C. Davis. Gospel JA fm started out by playing 80% Jamaican gospel music, but now plays 95% Jamaican gospel music. &&&&& &&&&& Radio Announcers &&&&& * Wyatt K. C.
