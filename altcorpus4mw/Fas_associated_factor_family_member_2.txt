Fas associated factor family member 2 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Fas associated factor family member 2''' is a protein that in humans is encoded by the FAF2 gene. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Function &&&&& &&&&& The protein encoded by this gene is highly expressed in peripheral blood of patients with atopic dermatitis (AD), compared to normal individuals.
