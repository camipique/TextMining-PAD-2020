HACE1 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''HECT domain and ankyrin repeat containing E3 ubiquitin protein ligase 1''' is a protein that in humans is encoded by the HACE1 gene. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Function &&&&& &&&&& This gene encodes a HECT domain and ankyrin repeat-containing ubiquitin ligase. The encoded protein is involved in specific tagging of target proteins, leading to their subcellular localization or proteasomal degradation.
