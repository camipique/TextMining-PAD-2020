Leopard Creek Country Club &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Leopard Creek Country Club''' is a Gary Player-designed country club in Malalane, South Africa. The course sits at the border of Kruger National Park, with the Crocodile River (Mpumalanga) snaking through some fairways. The course is owned by South Africa's richest man, Johann Rupert; players often stay at the nearby Sabi Sabi game lodge.
