Gabrielle Trainor Medal &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& In the AFL Women's (AFLW), the '''Gabrielle Trainor Medal''' is the name of the award given to the best and fairest player at the Greater Western Sydney Giants during the home-and-away season. The naming of the award is in honour of Gabrielle Trainor, an inaugural Giants board member and member of the AFL Commission known for pioneering the development of women's Australian rules football in New South Wales during her ten years on the AFL NSW/ACT Commission prior to joining the Giants.
