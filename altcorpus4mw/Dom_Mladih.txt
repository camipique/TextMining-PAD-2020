Dom Mladih &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Dom Mladih''' (translation: ''Home of the Youth'') is a multifunctional venue of modern concept and interior, located in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina, as part of Skenderija, which is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, consisting of a dance hall with a capacity of 2000 visitors and the amphitheater with a capacity of up to 600 visitors. &&&&& &&&&& Many non-affirmated pop and rock groups as well as entertainers have started their career in "Dom Mladih" to later become renowned musicians throughout the region and the world: Kemal Monteno, Bijelo Dugme, Dino Merlin, Indexi, Crvena jabuka, Plavi orkestar, Ambasadori, Regina, SCH and many others. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& &&&&& After it was damaged during Bosnian War, it was officially reopened in 2007. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
