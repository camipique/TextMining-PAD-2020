Inverkeithing Hillfield Swifts F.C. &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Inverkeithing Hillfield Swifts Football Club''' are a Scottish football club based at Ballast Bank in the town of Inverkeithing, Fife. They compete in the East of Scotland Football League (Conference C), the sixth tier of Scottish football, in their first season as a senior team. &&&&& &&&&& The club was founded in 1996 as Inverkeithing Boys Club, before changing their name to Inverkeithing Blue Brazil a year later.
