Hodi da ti čiko nešto da &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Hodi da ti čiko nešto da''''' () is the eighth studio album by Bosnian rock band Zabranjeno Pušenje, released through Civitas in Bosnia and Herzegovina on November 16, 2006, and Croatia on March 15, 2007 and through Mascom Records in Serbia in 2007. Moreover, ''Hodi da ti čiko nešto da'' is the second double-full-length studio album released by the band.
