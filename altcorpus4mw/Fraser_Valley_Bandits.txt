Fraser Valley Bandits &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Fraser Valley Bandits''' are a Canadian professional basketball team based in Abbotsford, British Columbia, that is announced to compete in the Canadian Elite Basketball League. The Bandits will play their home games at the Abbotsford Centre, located in the Fraser Valley. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& Fraser Valley was first announced as part of the Canadian Elite Basketball League (CEBL) in November 2017.. On July 16, 2018, the CEBL revealed their sixth inaugural team as the Fraser Valley Bandits, the logo representing a red fox wearing a bandit mask.
