David Clarke (priest) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''David George Alexander Clarke''' was an Anglican priest. &&&&& &&&&& Clarke was educated at Trinity College, Dublin and ordained in 1949. He served at: Dromore (curacy); Clonbroney, Clogher, Abbeystrewry, Usworth and Kilrossanty (incumbencies); St Patrick's Cathedral, Dublin (Prebendary); and Cashel (Dean).
