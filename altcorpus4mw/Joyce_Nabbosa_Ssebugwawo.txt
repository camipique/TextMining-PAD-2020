Joyce Nabbosa Ssebugwawo &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Joyce Ssebugwawo''' is a Ugandan politician, entrepreneur and a former minister in the Buganda government. She joined the Buganda government in early 1980s where she have worked as a Minister for women, community work and mobilisation.
