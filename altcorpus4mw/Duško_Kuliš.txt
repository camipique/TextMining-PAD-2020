Duško Kuliš &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Duško Kuliš''' (born 7 February 1960) is a Bosnian folk singer and songwriter. He released his music mostly on Jugoton and its successor, Croatia Records.
