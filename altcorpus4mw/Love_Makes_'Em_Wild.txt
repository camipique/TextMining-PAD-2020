Love Makes 'Em Wild &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Love Makes 'Em Wild''''' is a 1927 American comedy film directed by Albert Ray and written by Harold Shumate. The film stars John Harron, Sally Phipps, Ben Bard, Arthur Housman, J. Farrell MacDonald and Natalie Kingston.
