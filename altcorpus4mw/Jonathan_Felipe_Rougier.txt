Jonathan Felipe Rougier &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jonathan Felipe Rougier''' (born 29 October 1987) is an Argentinian football player who currently plays as a goalkeeper for F.C. Motagua in the Liga Nacional in Honduras. &&&&& &&&&& Club career &&&&& Rougier was promoted from Club Atlético Colón to Primera B Nacional side Boca Unidos in 2009 by coach Frank Kudelka. By 2011, he was transferred to Textil Mandiyú and to Club Defensores de Pronunciamiento in 2012 where he gained promotion to Torneo Federal A. His first international experience was with Honduran club F.C. Motagua in 2017, winning a league title immediately. He also won the best goalkeeper award in Honduras in 2018.
