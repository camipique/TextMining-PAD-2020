Huansu S2 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Huansu S2''' is a 5-seat Compact SUV produced by Huansu, a sub-brand of BAIC Motor. It is a 5-seat variant of the Huansu S3 7-seat compact SUV. Both are manufactured by Beiqi Yinxiang Automobile, a joint venture between Beijing Auto (Beiqi) and the Yinxiang Motorcycle Group.
