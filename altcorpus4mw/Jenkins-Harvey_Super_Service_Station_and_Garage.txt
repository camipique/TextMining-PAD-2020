Jenkins-Harvey Super Service Station and Garage &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Jenkins-Harvey Super Service Station and Garage''', at 124 S. College in Tyler, Texas, was built in 1929. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2002. &&&&& &&&&& It was designed by James P.
