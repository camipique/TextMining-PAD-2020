Esther Apuahe &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Esther Roibete Apuahe''' is a surgeon from Papua New Guinea, and the first woman surgeon in the country. In 2014 she won the Steamships Public Sector Award at the Westpac Outstanding Woman of the Year Awards. &&&&& &&&&& Apuahe began her surgical training in 2008 at Port Moresby General Hospital, and was the only woman in the class. She completed her training in 2011, becoming the first woman in Papua New Guinea to qualify as a surgeon. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
