How I Spent My Summer Vacation (1967 film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''''How I Spent My Summer Vacation''''' is a 1967 American film. &&&&& &&&&& It was one of the first movies made for TV. It starred famous names who would later star in other TV movies and shows, including Robert Wagner and Jill St. John. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& Man in hiding Jack Washington is contacted by another man named Lewis Gannet, who promises money and independence in exchange for a job.
