Idiot Proverbs &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Idiot Proverbs''''' is the debut studio album by Australian indie rock band Tiny Little Houses. It was released through Ivy League Records in January 2018.
