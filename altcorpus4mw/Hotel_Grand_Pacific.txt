Hotel Grand Pacific &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The hotel behind Confederation Garden Court, 2012 &&&&& '''Hotel Grand Pacific''' is a 4.5-star, 115-room hotel in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada. It was built by Murray Gammon, acquired by Standard Life in the mid 1990s, and sold to Pacific Sun in 1996.
