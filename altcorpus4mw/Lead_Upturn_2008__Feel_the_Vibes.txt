It was released on December 3, 2008, one full year after their previous tour DVD, ''Lead Upturn 2007 ~B.W.R~''. It peaked in the top forty of the Oricon DVD Charts at #37, where it remained for two consecutive weeks. &&&&& &&&&& The tour corresponded with their fifth studio album ''Feel The Vibes,'' harboring most of the songs from the album. Other songs included were "Manatsu no Magic", "Virgin Blue" and "Baby Runnin' Wild" among others.
