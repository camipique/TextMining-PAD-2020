Ek Kasak Reh Gayi &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Ek Kasak Reh Gayi''''' is a Pakistani drama serial written by K Rehman and directed by Dilawar Malik. It stars Mikaal Zulfiqar and Sanam Saeed in lead roles.
