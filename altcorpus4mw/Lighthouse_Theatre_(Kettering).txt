Lighthouse Theatre (Kettering) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Lighthouse Theatre''' is a performing arts theatre in Kettering, England which opened in 2008. It is the main performing arts venue in the town following closure and demolition of the Kettering Savoy in 1997.
