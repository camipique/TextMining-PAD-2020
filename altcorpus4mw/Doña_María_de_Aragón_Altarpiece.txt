Doña María de Aragón Altarpiece &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Doña María de Aragón Altarpiece''' was an altarpiece painted between 1596 and 1599 by El Greco for the chapel of the Colegio de la Encarnación de Madrid (also known as the Colegio de doña María de Aragón). There has been much speculation over which paintings belonged to the work - there is now consensus that it consisted of six large canvases and a seventh, now lost.
