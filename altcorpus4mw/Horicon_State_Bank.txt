Horicon State Bank &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Horicon State Bank''' is located in Horicon, Wisconsin. &&&&& &&&&& Description &&&&& The interior features terrazzo floors, a vault reinforced with Diebold doors and American Banking Company alarm bells. It was added to the State Register of Historic Places in 2017 and to the National Register of Historic Places in 2018. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
