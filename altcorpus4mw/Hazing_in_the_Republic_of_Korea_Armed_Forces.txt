Hazing in the Republic of Korea Armed Forces &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Hazing in the Republic of Korea Armed Forces''' refers to hazing and bullying carried out within the military, often to conscripts and junior members. A similar example is that of Russia's Dedovshchina. Hazing was enforced mainly under the pretext of establishing military discipline, despite evidence that it did not achieve that goal.
