Let Them Live &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Let Them Live''''' is a 1937 American drama film directed by Harold Young and written by Lionel Houser and Bruce Manning. The film stars John Howard, Nan Grey, Edward Ellis, Judith Barrett, Robert Wilcox and Benny Bartlett.
