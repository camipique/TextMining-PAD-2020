Humphrey Brown &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Humphrey Brown''' (1803 – 6 June 1860) was a British Whig politician. &&&&& &&&&& Brown was first elected Whig MP for Tewkesbury at the 1847 general election and held the seat until 1857, when he was defeated.
