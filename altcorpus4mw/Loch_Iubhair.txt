Loch Iubhair &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Loch Iubhair''' pronounced ''yoo-ar'' meaning yew loch, is a freshwater loch, located in Glen Dochart, and 4 miles east of the village of Crianlarich. Loch Dochart is located immediately to the south east, and the outflow of Loch Dochart, as the River Dochart flows into Loch Iubhair. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
