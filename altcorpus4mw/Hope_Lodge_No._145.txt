117	Hope_Lodge_No._145
Hope Lodge No. 145 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Hope Lodge No. 145''' is a historic Freemasons lodge located at 116 East Vermilion Street in Lafayette, Louisiana. &&&&& &&&&& Built in 1916, it is a two-story brick structure with a five-bay facade and a pair of entrances. The lodge was chartered on February 10, 1857. In the same year former Governor Alexandre Mouton donated the site in which the original building was located. In 1916, due to growing membership, the previous building was demolished to make space for the actual one. &&&&& &&&&& The building was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on January 21, 1983. &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * National Register of Historic Places listings in Lafayette Parish, Louisiana &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
