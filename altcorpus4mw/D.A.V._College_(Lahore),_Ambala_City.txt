D.A.V. College (Lahore), Ambala City &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''D.A.V. College (Lahore), Ambala City''' is a college in Ambala, Haryana, India. It was started in 1886 in Lahore, Punjab Province (British India). After partition it moved to present campus of Ambala in 1948.
