La Camperona &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''La Camperona''' (altitude ) is a mountain in the Cantabrian Mountains, near Sabero in the Province of León, Spain. &&&&& &&&&& The mountain pass, which gives access to the summit, has the same name. &&&&& &&&&& Cycling &&&&& The climb of La Camperona &&&&& The mountain has been used several times for stage finishes in the Vuelta a España. The average gradient of the climb from the at to at is 3.5%, with a maximum gradient of 9%.
