Frederick A. Salomonson &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Frederick A. Salomonson''' (July 20, 1860-December 19, 1911) was a three-time mayor of Tampa, Florida in the 1890s and 1900s. He was first elected mayor in March 1893 and served until March 1894. He was elected again to a second term on March 1895 and served until June 1896.
