Dhuleli language &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Dhuleli''' is a West Himalayish language of Nepal. It is closely related to Byangsi. Dhuleli has been documented by Regmi & Prasain (2017), who reported 347 speakers as of 2017. &&&&& &&&&& Dhuleli is spoken in the four villages of Dhuli, Jagera, Balaundi, and Niuna in Kanda Gaunpalika, Bajhang District, Nepal. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& *Regmi, Dan Raj; Prasain, Balaram. 2017.
