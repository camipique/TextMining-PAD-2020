Darren Chen &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Guan Hong''' (, born 15 January 1995), also known as '''Darren Chen''', is a Taiwanese actor. He is best known for his leading role as Hua Ze Lei in the 2018 television series ''Meteor Garden'', which propelled him to fame in Asia. &&&&& &&&&& Early life and education &&&&& Darren Chen was born on January 15, 1995 in Taiwan. He graduated from the Taipei Municipal Heping High School. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& ===2016–2017: Beginnings=== &&&&& Chen made his acting debut as Lin Yutang in the Chinese television series ''Proud of Love'' (2016) and ''Proud of Love 2'' (2017). &&&&& &&&&& In 2017, he was cast in the role of Huaze Lei by Angie Chai in the 2018 adaptation of ''Meteor Garden''. &&&&& He appeared in Harper's Bazaar China with his ''Meteor Garden'' costars Dylan Wang, Connor Leong, and Caesar Wu in the November issue. &&&&& &&&&& ===2018–present: Rising popularity=== &&&&& Chen rose to fame with his role as Huaze Lei in the 2018 television series ''Meteor Garden'', the remake of popular Taiwanese drama series ''Meteor Garden'' and based on the Japanese shōjo manga series written by Yoko Kamio.
