Hakatoa Tupou &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Hakatoa Tupou''' (born circa 1959) is a Tongan former rugby union player. He played as prop. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& Tupou first played for the 'Ikale Tahi against Samoa on 25 August 1982, in Nuku'alofa. He was also called up for the Tonga squad for the 1987 Rugby World Cup, where he played against Wales and against Ireland, the latter being his last international cap.
