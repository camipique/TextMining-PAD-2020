Great and Little Preston &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Field in the parish &&&&& &&&&& '''Great and Little Preston''' is a civil parish in the City of Leeds metropolitan borough, West Yorkshire, England. In 2001 census it had a population of 1,449, and 1463 in the 2011 census.
