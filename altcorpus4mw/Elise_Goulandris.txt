Elise Goulandris &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Elise Goulandris''', née Karadontis, (1917 – 25 July 2000) was a Greek art collector, and the co-founder of Greece's first Museum of Modern Art, the Museum of Contemporary Art Andros.
