Liu Shuhua &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Liu Shuhua''' (born 23 October 1962) is a former professional tennis player from China. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& Debuting for the China Davis Cup team in 1983, Shuhua went on to feature in a total of 15 ties, for a 19/12 win-loss record. &&&&& &&&&& Shuhua won multiple Asian Games medals for China, including bronze medals at the singles events in 1982 and 1986, as well as three medals in doubles. &&&&& &&&&& At the 1988 Summer Olympics in Barcelona he was a member of the Chinese team, partnering Ma Keqin in the men's doubles.
