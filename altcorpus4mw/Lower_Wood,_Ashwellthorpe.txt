Lower Wood, Ashwellthorpe &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Lower Wood, Ashwellthorpe''' is a biological Site of Special Scientific Interest south-east of Wymondham in Norfolk. It is managed by the Norfolk Wildlife Trust. &&&&& &&&&& This ancient wood on chalky boulder clay has a diverse ground flora with uncommon species such as wood spurge, early-purple orchid, common twayblade, ramsons, water avens and woodruff. &&&&& &&&&& The wood is open to the public. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
