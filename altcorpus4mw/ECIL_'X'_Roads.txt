ECIL 'X' Roads &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''ECIL 'X' Roads''' or '''ECIL Cross Roads''' is a suburb in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It is one of the most densely populated in the city and is part of HMDA. The suburb got its name because of a public sector enterprise, ECIL factory location. &&&&& &&&&& Transport &&&&& It has good connectivity of buses by TSRTC.
