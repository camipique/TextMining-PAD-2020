I Fall All Over Again &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''I Fall All Over Again'''" is a song by Canadian singer-songwriter Dan Hill, released as a single in 1991. It was the first single released from his tenth studio album, ''Dance of Love''. The song reached No. 7 on the U.S. ''Billboard'' Adult Contemporary chart in early 1992.
