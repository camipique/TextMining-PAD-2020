Dallas Ballers &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Dallas Ballers''' is a basketball team based in Dallas, Texas. The team competes in the Junior Basketball Association (JBA), a league created for high school and junior college players as an alternative to the National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA). &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The JBA was first announced on December 20, 2017, when media personality LaVar Ball said to ''Slam'' magazine that he would create a professional league targeted at high school graduates and fully funded by his sports apparel company Big Baller Brand.
