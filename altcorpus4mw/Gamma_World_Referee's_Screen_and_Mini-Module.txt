Barton reviewed ''Gamma World Referee's Screen and Mini-Module'' in ''The Space Gamer'' No. 51. Barton commented that "the ''Gamma World Referee's Screen and Mini-Module'' is a good idea that was too long in coming." &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
