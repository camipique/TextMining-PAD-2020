Jon Ewo &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jon Ewo ''' ( born 29 June 1957) is a Norwegian novelist, short story writer, crime fiction writer and children's writer. He was born in Oslo and educated as librarian. He made his literary debut in 1986 with the short story collection ''Det sies at oktober er en fin måned''. His crime novels include ''Torpedo'' (1996), ''Hevn. Torpedo II'' (1997) and ''Gissel.
