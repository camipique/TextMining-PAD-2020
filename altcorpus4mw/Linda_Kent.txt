Linda Kent &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Linda Kent''' is an American-born harpsichord & chamber organ player. Together with Genevieve Lacey Kent was nominated for the 2002 ARIA Award for Best Classical Album for the album ''Piracy''.
