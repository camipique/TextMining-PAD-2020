Damdin Tsogtbaatar &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Damdin Tsogtbaatar''' or '''Damdingiin Tsogtbaatar''' (born January 14, 1970) is a Mongolian politician. On 4 October 2017, The State Great Khural (Parliament) of Mongolia appointed him as the Foreign Minister of Mongolia. &&&&& Previously he has served as State Secretary of Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Minister for Nature, Environment and Tourism of Mongolia and Minister of Construction and Urban Development of Mongolia. Since 2016 he was member of the State Great Khural (Parliament) of Mongolia. &&&&& Tsogtbaatar is married and has two children. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
