Girl Meets Farm &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Girl Meets Farm''''' is an American cooking television series that airs on Food Network, and is presented by cookbook author and chef Molly Yeh. The series features Yeh cooking Midwestern farm meals sometimes influenced by her Jewish and Chinese heritage, primarily at her farm on the Minnesota-North Dakota border. &&&&& &&&&& ''Girl Meets Farm'' officially premiered on June 24, 2018.
