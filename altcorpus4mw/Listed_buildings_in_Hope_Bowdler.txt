Listed buildings in Hope Bowdler &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Hope Bowdler is a civil parish in Shropshire, England. It contains eleven listed buildings that are recorded in the National Heritage List for England. All the listed buildings are designated at Grade II, the lowest of the three grades, which is applied to "buildings of national importance and special interest". The parish contains the village of Hope Bowdler and the surrounding countryside. Most of the listed buildings are memorials in the churchyard of St Andrew's Church, which is also listed.
