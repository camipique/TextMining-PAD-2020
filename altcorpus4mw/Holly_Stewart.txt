Holly Stewart &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Holly Stewart''' (born 18 May 1993) is a Canadian women's field hockey player. &&&&& &&&&& Playing career &&&&& ===Senior National Team=== &&&&& Stewart first represented Canada in 2013, in a test match against Ireland. Stewart's first major tournament in the Canada team was the 2014 Commonwealth Games in Glasgow, Scotland. The team finished eighth in the tournament. &&&&& &&&&& In 2015, Stewart represented Canada at the 2015 Pan American Games, where the team finished third, winning the bronze medal. &&&&& &&&&& ===Junior National Team=== &&&&& Stewart first represented the junior national team in 2012, at the Junior Pan American Cup.
