Front for the Liberation of Champa &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Front for the Liberation of Champa''' (; abbreviated '''FLC''') was a Cham nationalist organisation active in the Central Highlands of Vietnam. It was founded in 1962 and merged with the Central Highlands Liberation Front and the Liberation Front of Kampuchea Krom to form the United Front for the Liberation of Oppressed Races (FULRO) in 1964. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
