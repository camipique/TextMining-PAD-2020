62	Goodwinslow
Goodwinslow &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Goodwinslow''', also known as '''Chapman House''', is a historic house in Raleigh, Tennessee. It was built from 1875 to 1900 for William Washington Goodwin. It remained in the Goodwin-Chapman family in the 1970s. &&&&& &&&&& The house was designed to resemble an Italian villa. It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since December 6, 1979. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
