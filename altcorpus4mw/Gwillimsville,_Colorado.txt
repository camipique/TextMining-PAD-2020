61	Gwillimsville,_Colorado
Gwillimsville, Colorado &&&&& Introduction &&&&& Monument in northern El Paso County &&&&& '''Gwillimsville''' was a settlement at the head of Cherry Creek and six miles east of Monument in northern El Paso County, Colorado. Its settlers were engaged in dairy, ranching, and timber businesses.
