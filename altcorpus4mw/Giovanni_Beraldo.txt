Giovanni Beraldo &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Giovanni Beraldo''' or '''Giovanni Beroaldo''' (died 1565) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Sant'Agata de' Goti (1557–1565) and Bishop of Telese o Cerreto Sannita (1548–1557). &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& On 14 March 1548, Giovanni Beraldo was appointed during the papacy of Pope Paul III as Bishop of Telese o Cerreto Sannita. &&&&& On 1 October 1557, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Paul IV as Bishop of Sant'Agata de' Goti. &&&&& He served as Bishop of Sant'Agata de' Goti until his death in 1565. &&&&& &&&&& While bishop, he was the principal co-consecrator of: &&&&& *Antonio Ghislieri, Bishop of Nepi e Sutri (1556); and &&&&& *Sigismondo Saraceno, Archbishop of Acerenza e Matera (1556). &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links and additional sources &&&&& * (Chronology of Bishops) &&&&& * (Chronology of Bishops) &&&&& * (for Chronology of Bishops) &&&&& * (for Chronology of Bishops) &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
