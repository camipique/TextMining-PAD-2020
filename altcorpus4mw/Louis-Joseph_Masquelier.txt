Louis-Joseph Masquelier &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Louis-Joseph Masquelier''' or '''Masquelier the Elder''' (21 February 1741 – 26 May 1811) was a French draughtsman and engraver. Born in Cysoing near Lille in northern France, he died in Paris. He was very close to François-Denis Née, with whom he studied under Jacques-Philippe Le Bas. His son Claude-Louis Masquelier was also an engraver and lithographer. &&&&& &&&&& Sources &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
