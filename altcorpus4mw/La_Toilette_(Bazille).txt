La Toilette (Bazille) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''La Toilette''''' is an 1869-1870 painting by Frédéric Bazille, which has been in the Musée Fabre in Montpellier since 1968. He produced it a few months before his death in the Franco-Prussian War in 1870. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
