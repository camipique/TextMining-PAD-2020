John Yancey (tennis) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''John Yancey''' (born January 18, 1970) is a former professional tennis player from the United States. &&&&& &&&&& Biography &&&&& As a junior Yancey partnered with Jonathan Stark to win the boys' doubles title at the 1988 US Open. &&&&& &&&&& Recruited from Grosse Pointe, Yancey played collegiate tennis at the Kentucky Wildcats. He also had an opportunity to play basketball for the Wildcats but decided to dedicate all of his efforts to tennis. &&&&& &&&&& In the early 1990s he played on the professional tour as a doubles specialist. Across 1993 and 1994 he appeared in the main draw of eight ATP Tour tournaments.
