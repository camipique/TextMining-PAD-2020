Gabriel Adrien Robinet de Cléry &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Gabriel Adrien Robinet de Cléry''' (18 August 1836 - 1914) was a French magistrate, historian and legal writer active during the Second French Empire and the Third French Republic. He was a convinced Legitimist. He was born in Metz and died in Paris. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
