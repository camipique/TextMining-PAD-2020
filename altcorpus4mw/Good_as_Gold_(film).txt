Good as Gold (film) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Good as Gold''''' is a 1927 American Western film directed by Scott R. Dunlap and written by Jack Jungmeyer. The film stars Buck Jones, Frances Lee, Carl Miller, Charles K. French, Adele Watson and Arthur Ludwig.
