Fan Club (Jellyfish album) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Fan Club''''' is a box set of demos, rarities and live performances by American power pop band Jellyfish. The box set was released in limited quality by Not Lame Recordings in November 2001. ''Fan Club'' contains demos from the band’s 1990 debut ''Bellybutton'' and their 1993 sophomore follow-up ''Spilt Milk''.
