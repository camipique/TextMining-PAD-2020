Dragon's Eye (video game) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Dragon's Eye''''' is a 1981 video game published by Automated Simulations for the Apple II, Atari 8-bit family, and Commodore PET. &&&&& &&&&& Gameplay and plot &&&&& ''Dragon's Eye'' is a computerized fantasy role-playing game in which the player takes the role of a spell-using adventurer hired by a wizard to find the legendary Dragon's Eye. &&&&& &&&&& Reception &&&&& Rudy Kraft reviewed ''Dragon's Eye'' in ''The Space Gamer'' No. 48.
