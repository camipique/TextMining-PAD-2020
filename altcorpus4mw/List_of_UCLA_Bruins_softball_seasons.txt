List of UCLA Bruins softball seasons &&&&& Introduction &&&&& The following is a '''list of UCLA Bruins softball seasons'''. The University of California, Los Angeles is a member of the Pac-12 Conference of the NCAA Division I. The Bruins are thirteen time Women's College World Series champions,, with the first of those titles coming during the AIAW years, and the remaining 12 under NCAA organization. UCLA has also appeared in the final event 31 times - 3 under the AIAW and 28 under the NCAA.
