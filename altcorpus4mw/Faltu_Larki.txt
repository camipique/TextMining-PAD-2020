Faltu Larki &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Faltu Larki''''' is a Pakistani family drama series, produced by Tariq Mujeeb. The drama airs weekly on A-Plus Entertainment every Thursday. It stars Hina Dilpazeer, Anum Fayyaz and Samiya Mumtaz in lead roles.
