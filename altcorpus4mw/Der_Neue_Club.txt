Der Neue Club &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''''Der Neue Club''''' was an Expressionist club founded in the Hackesche Höfe courtyards, Berlin by Kurt Hiller and Jakob van Hoddis. &&&&& &&&&& The Neopathetic Cabaret &&&&& The Neopathetic Cabaret was a short-lived by influential event held at ''Der Neue Club'' with a total of nine events over two years. The cabaret soon attracted a group of poets and writers such as Georg Heym Erich Unger, Ernst Blass and Else Lasker-Schüler. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
