Delimobil &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Delimobil''' () is one the largest carsharing organizations in Russia with a fleet of over 3200 vehicles and more than 1 million members. Its vehicle park consists of Hyundai Solaris, Renault Kaptur and Volkswagen Polo. Delimobil's carsharing service is available in Moscow, Saint-Petersburg, Ufa, Nizhny Novgorod, Yekaterinburg, Samara, Novosibirsk, Krasnodar and Grozny. &&&&& History &&&&& The company was founded in 2015 with a fleet of 100 cars in Moscow.
