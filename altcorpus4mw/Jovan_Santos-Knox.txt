Jovan Santos-Knox &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jovan Santos-Knox''' (born July 5, 1994) is a professional Canadian football linebacker for the Edmonton Eskimos of the Canadian Football League (CFL). Santos-Knox was signed by the Blue Bombers after driving nine hours with his father to a tryout camp which his father had found in Charlotte, North Carolina. He started his rookie season on special teams but by the end of the regular season was a starting linebacker and was being praised by coach Mike O'Shea and media for big plays at big moments of games.
