Endless (private equity) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Endless''' is a British private equity firm. &&&&& &&&&& Endless is headquartered at 3 Whitehall Quay, Leeds, with an office at 102 Park Street, London W1. &&&&& &&&&& Endless has invested in West Cornwall Pasty Company, Jones Bootmaker, Crown Paints, and Theo Fennell. In April 2006, Endless acquired GlynWebb, which they sold on four months later. In March 2011, Endless acquired T. J. Hughes for an undisclosed sum. &&&&& &&&&& In July 2017, Endless bought the luggage company Antler Luggage, from fellow private equity firm Lloyds Development Capital (LDC). In July 2018, it announced that The Works, which it bought out of administration in May 2008 for around £17 million, would have an IPO and a valuation of £100 million. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& External links &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
