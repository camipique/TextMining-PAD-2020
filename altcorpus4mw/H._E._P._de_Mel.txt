P. de Mel &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Hugh Edmund Peter de Mel''' (17 June 1907 - 27 September 1992) was a Ceylonese politician. &&&&& &&&&& Hugh Edmund Peter de Mel, was born 17 June 1907 in Moratuwa, the son of Cornelis Francis de Mel, and brother of Joseph Ford Francis, all of whom were key figures in the country's safety match industry. In 1929 de Mel established the Lanka Light Match factory in Moratuwa. He was unsuccessful in his bid, losing to the Lanka Sama Samaja Party candidate, Meryl Fernando, by 7,718 votes.
