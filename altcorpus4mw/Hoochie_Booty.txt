Hoochie Booty &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& "'''Hoochie Booty'''" is a song by British boy band Ultimate Kaos, from their eponymous debut album. The song was released as the second single from the album, and was a Top 20 hit, peaking at #17 on the UK Singles Chart in January 1995.
