Finnish Standards Association &&&&& Introduction &&&&& The '''Finnish Standards Association''' ('''SFS''', , ) is the central standards organization in Finland. It is a member organization of the ISO and CEN, representing Finland in these organizations. SFS is responsible for both international and national standards, with the former being translated from ISO standards.
