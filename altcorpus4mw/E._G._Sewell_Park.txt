E. G. Sewell Park &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''E. G. Sewell Park''' is a riverfront park located close to the 17th Street Bridge over the Miami River, in Miami, Florida, United States. It is named for former Miami mayor E. G.
