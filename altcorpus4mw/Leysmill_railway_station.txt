Leysmill railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Leysmill railway station''' served the village of Leysmill, Angus, Scotland from 1848 to 1955 on the Arbroath and Forfar Railway. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& The station opened in 1848 by the Aberdeen Railway. It was situated north of and south of station.
