56	Igarkiella
Igarkiella &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Igarkiella''''' is an extinct genus of trilobite containing a single described species '''''Igarkiella igarkaensis'''''. The species is known from fossils dating to 505 to 501 million years ago during the Cambrian Period. The fossils were described from an outcrop of the Labaz Formations Saami Member along the Kulyumbe River in Krasnoyar, Russia. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
