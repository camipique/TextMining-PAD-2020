Lean Into It (song) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& "'''Lean Into It '''" is a song by American R&B singer Joe. It was written by Joe along with Brandon Hodge, Jolyon Skinner, Derek "D.O.A." Allen, Gerald Isaac, and Damien R. Farmer III for his twelfth studio album ''My Name Is Joe Thomas''. Production was helmed by Farmer, Isaac and Allen.
