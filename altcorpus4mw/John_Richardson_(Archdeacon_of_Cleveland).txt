John Richardson (Archdeacon of Cleveland) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''John Richardson''' (b North Bierley 6 February 1676; d York 28 October 1735) was Archdeacon of Cleveland from 3 August 1711 to his death on 28 October 1735. &&&&&  &&&&& Richardson was educated at Christ's College, Cambridge.
