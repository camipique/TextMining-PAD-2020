Dongfeng Fengdu MX5 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& Dongfeng Fengdu MX5 rear &&&&& Dongfeng Fengdu MX5 side profile &&&&& The '''Dongfeng Fengdu MX5''' is a compact CUV produced by Dongfeng Motor Corporation under the Dongfeng Fengdu sub-brand. The Dongfeng Fengdu MX5 CUV debuted during the 2016 Chengdu Auto Show with the MX5 being available to the market in late 2016 with prices ranging from 103,500 yuan to 135,500 yuan.
