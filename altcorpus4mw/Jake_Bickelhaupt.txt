Jake Bickelhaupt &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Jake Bickelhaupt''' is an American chef based in Chicago, Illinois. His two-Michelin-star restaurant 42 Grams was the subject of a 2017 documentary of the same name. The film focuses on Bickelhaupt as he turns an illegal restaurant out of his home into brick and mortar 42 Grams in less than a year. He currently runs the underground restaurant Konro.
