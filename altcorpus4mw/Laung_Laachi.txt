Laung Laachi &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Laung Laachi''''' ( Clove, Cardamom) is a Punjabi language movie starring Neeru Bajwa and Ammy Virk was released on 9 March 2018, the film was directed by Amberdeep Singh and produced by Neeru Bajwa, The "title track song" and "Rooh De Rukh" of Laung Laachi became very popular. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& &&&&& A recently married husband and wife play a strange game with each other. They decide to live under the same roof as strangers and the husband tries winning her love back. But will he be successful in his endeavor or do they remain alienated forever, Amberdeep Singh has played the husband's character Mehnga and Neeru Bajwa is his wife Laachi. Both like each other as Mehnga is very caring and love his wife and wants to keep his wife happy in all situations. Laachi is a loving girl who likes singing and dancing and even on their wedding night, she ends up singing songs for the guests. &&&&& &&&&& Cast &&&&& * Neeru Bajwa as Laachi &&&&& * Amberdeep Singh as Mehnga &&&&& * Ammy Virk as Ajaypal &&&&& * Gurpreet Bhangu as Bebe Tej Kaur &&&&& * Baninder Bunny as Neighbor &&&&& * Nirmal Rishi as Ajaypal's daadi &&&&& * Amrit Maan as Singer Jagtaar Maan &&&&& * Gurinder Makna as Mehnga's Uncle &&&&& * Veet Baljit as Geetkar Kaonkeaan Wala &&&&& * Labh Chatamliwala as Gaama Singh &&&&& &&&&& Soundtrack &&&&& &&&&& Soundtrack album of the film consists of 8 tracks sung by singers like Mannat Noor, Ammy Virk and Prabh Gill.
