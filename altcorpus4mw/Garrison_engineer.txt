Garrison engineer &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& India &&&&& A garrison engineer holds the rank of executive engineer in the Indian Defense Service of Engineers. Garrisson Engineer is a designation which is held either by a civilian officer of M.E.S. or by a military officer of the rank of Lt Col. The Garrison engineer's staff includes at least three assistant garrison engineers, a barrack stores officer, an accounts officer, and junior engineers. Responsibilities of the position include procuring contracts and executing them.
