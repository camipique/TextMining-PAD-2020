Eau de Paris &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Eau de Paris''' (Paris Water) is the publicly owned company responsible for the public water supply and waste water collection for the city of Paris. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& &&&&& The company was created in 2008 after a ballot initiative from then Mayor Bertrand Delanoë. The city's water was then substantially managed by two private companies (Veolia and Suez) which the municipal government bought out. &&&&& &&&&& Operations &&&&& &&&&& The company distributes 563,000 m³ of drinking water a day.
