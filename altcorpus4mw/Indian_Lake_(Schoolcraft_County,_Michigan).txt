Indian Lake (Schoolcraft County, Michigan) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Indian Lake''' is a lake in Schoolcraft County, Michigan, United States. Measuring six miles long and three miles wide, and covering , it is the fourth largest inland lake on Michigan's Upper Peninsula.
