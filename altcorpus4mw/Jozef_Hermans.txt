Jozef Hermans &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jozef Hermans''' was a Belgian rower. He competed at the 1908 Summer Olympics in London with the men's single sculls where he was eliminated in the quarter final.
