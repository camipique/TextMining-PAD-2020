79	Hotel_Bell_(Alva,_Oklahoma)
Hotel Bell (Alva, Oklahoma) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Hotel Bell''' in Alva, Oklahoma was built in 1927. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2013. &&&&& &&&&& It is a six-story three-part vertical building with frame of reinforced concrete and with floors and walls of hollow clay tiles and brick. It has been the tallest building northwestern Oklahoma. &&&&& &&&&& An extensive renovation was completed in 2017 and it was reopened, after being closed for four decades. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
