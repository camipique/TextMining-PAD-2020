Lorenzo Senatore &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Lorenzo Senatore,''' ICG, AIC (born March 2, 1974) is an Italian cinematographer. He studied filmmaking in Rome, and at the age of 25 became the youngest steadicam and camera operator in Italy. He has served as a camera operator and 2nd unit director of photography on numerous blockbuster films including ''300: Rise of an Empire, Spectre, London Has Fallen, Beauty and the Beast,'' and ''Wonder Woman''.
