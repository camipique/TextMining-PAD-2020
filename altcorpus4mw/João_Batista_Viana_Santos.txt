João Batista Viana Santos &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''João Batista Viana Santos''' (born 20 July 1961 in Uberlândia, Brazil), or simply '''João Batista''' or '''Batista''', is a Brazilian former footballer who played as a defender. He won the silver medal at the 1988 Summer Olympics with Brazil.
