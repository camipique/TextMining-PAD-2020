Hygrophorus meridionalis &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Hygrophorus meridionalis''''' is a species of basidiomycete fungus in the family Hygrophoraceae. Described as new to science in 2018, this waxcap is found in abundance on the island of Cyprus and southern Greece, where it grows in association with ''Pinus brutia'' and ''Pinus halepensis'' on calcareous substrates. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
