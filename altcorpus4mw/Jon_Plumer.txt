Jon Plumer &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Jon Plumer''' is an American politician from the state of Wisconsin. He serves in the Wisconsin State Assembly from the 42nd district. He is a member of the Republican Party. &&&&& &&&&& Plumer currently serves on the town board for Lodi, Wisconsin and the Columbia County Board of Supervisors. Plumer ran in a special election, held on June 12, 2018, against Democrat Ann Groves Lloyd.
