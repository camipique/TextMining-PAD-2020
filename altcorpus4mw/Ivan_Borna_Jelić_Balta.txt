Ivan Borna Jelić Balta &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Ivan Borna Jelić Balta''' (born 17 September 1992) is a Croatian football defender. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& Born in Zagreb, Jelić Balta is a native of Bjelovar where he went through the school of the local NK Bjelovar, joining the ranks of the third-tier first team in 2010. After two seasons, he joined local rivals NK Mladost Ždralovi in the same tier, which ended up winning the 2012/13 season of Treća HNL North. &&&&& &&&&& After that, he played in the Germany lower leagues clubs, until his return to Croatia.
