Javaid Rehman &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Javaid Rehman''' (born 1967 in Faisalabad, Pakistan) is a British-Pakistani legal scholar and Professor of Islamic Law and International Law at Brunel University. &&&&& &&&&& Special Rapporteur on human rights in Iran &&&&& On 7 July 2018, he was appointed as the United Nations Special Rapporteur on the human rights situation in the Islamic Republic of Iran. &&&&& On 6 August 2018, Javaid Rehman, wrote to the Iranian Government expressing his interest in visiting Iran. He said he had already received a number of reports raising concerns about alleged violations of human rights in the country.
