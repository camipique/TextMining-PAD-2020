Displaying the Body of Saint Bonaventure &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& thumb &&&&& '''''Displaying the Body of Saint Bonaventure''''' (French - ''Exposition du corps de saint Bonaventure'') is a 1629 painting by Francisco de Zurbarán, now in the Louvre. Around the body of saint Bonaventure are figures including James I of Aragon and Pope Gregory X, shown in conversation. It formed part of a series of paintings on the saint's life - the other works are ''Saint Bonaventure at the Council of Lyon'' (Louvre), ''Saint Bonaventure and the Angel'' (Dresden) and ''Saint Bonaventure and Saint Thomas Aquinas before a Crucifix'' (Berlin). &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& category:Paintings of the Louvre by Spanish artists &&&&& category:1629 paintings &&&&& category:Paintings by Francisco de Zurbarán &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
