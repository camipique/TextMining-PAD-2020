Janīna Kursīte-Pakule &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Janīna Kursīte-Pakule''' (born 2 March 1952 in Arendole) is a Latvian literary scholar, linguist, writer, publicist, and politician. She has served as deputy of the 9th, 10th, 11th, and 12th Saeimas.
