Henry Martin Farm &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Henry Martin Farm''', near Ripley, Ohio, is a historic farm which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1975. &&&&& &&&&& The land was purchased in 1798 by Alexander Martin, an American Revolutionary War veteran. He built a log cabin first, and in 1816 built "Stonehurst", a stone house. Two barns built by his son Henry Martin are close by. The property had not left the Martin family up to 1999. &&&&& &&&&& Photo of some farm land, but not of the listed area with the three contributing buildings &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& 		 &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
