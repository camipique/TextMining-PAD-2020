158	Gorm's
Gorm's &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Gorm's''' is a chain of pizza restaurants headquartered in Copenhagen, Denmark. &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& Gorm's traces its history back to 2003 when Gorm Wisweh began to bake pizzas in his mother's café in Svaneke on the island of Bornholm. He later opened I pizzeria at Magstræde 16 in Copenhagen together with a group of friends. It was later followed by several other pizza restaurants in the city. The first Gorm's outside Copenhagen opened in Brandts Klædefabrik in Odense in May 2017. &&&&& &&&&& A 67 % share of the company was acquired by Orkla in 2018.
