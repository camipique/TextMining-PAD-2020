60	Lobispa
Lobispa &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Lobispa''''' is a genus of leaf beetles in the subfamily Eumolpinae. It is known from the Neotropical realm. The genus superficially resembles the subfamily Hispinae. &&&&& &&&&& The genus name comes from ''lobus'' (Greek for "an elongated projection") plus ''-ispa'' from the subfamily name Hispinae. &&&&& &&&&& Species &&&&& * ''Lobispa callosa'' (Baly, 1885) &&&&& * ''Lobispa expansa'' Staines, 2001 &&&&& * ''Lobispa sentus'' Staines, 2001 &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
