Lamesa Farm Workers Community Historic District &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Lamesa Farm Workers Community Historic District''' in Los Ybanez in Dawson County, Texas, was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1993. It is a historic district which included 32 contributing buildings. &&&&& &&&&& It includes a number of farm worker houses, a number of quadruplex shelters, a community center, a manager's house, a liquor store, a water tank, and other structures. &&&&& &&&&& It is located about 1 mile north of the junction of US 87 and US 180 in Los Ybanez. &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& &&&&& *National Register of Historic Places listings in Dawson County, Texas &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
