Heat shock protein family a (hsp70) member 12b &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Heat shock protein family A (Hsp70) member 12B''' is a protein that in humans is encoded by the HSPA12B gene. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Function &&&&& &&&&& The protein encoded by this gene contains an atypical heat shock protein 70 (Hsp70) ATPase domain and is therefore a distant member of the mammalian Hsp70 family. This gene may be involved in susceptibility to atherosclerosis.
