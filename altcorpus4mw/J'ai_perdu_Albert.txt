J'ai perdu Albert &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''J'ai perdu Albert''''' is a French comedy directed and written by Didier Van Cauwelaert. This was the last film to be composed by Michel Legrand, who died in 2019. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& Chloe, a young medium that the great business leaders, politicians and the jet set snapped up, shelters in her since childhood the spirit of Albert Einstein. Overworked, the information does not "pass" anymore. So Albert decides to move ... For better or for worse, he settles in Zac, a depressive Cartesian, beekeeper routed and waiter.
