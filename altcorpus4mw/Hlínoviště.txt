51	Hlínoviště
Hlínoviště &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Hlínoviště''' is a village in the Mladá Boleslav District, Czech Republic. It is an administrative part of Bělá pod Bezdězem, and located about 4.3 km west of the town.
