Fencing at the 2019 Pan American Games &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Fencing''' competitions at the '''2019 Pan American Games''' in Lima, Peru are scheduled to be held between August 5th and 10th, 2019 at the Escuela Militar de Chorrilos. &&&&& &&&&& 12 medal events are scheduled to be contested, in the three disciplines of épée, foil and sabre. In each discipline an individual and team event will be held for each gender. The number of athletes a nation entered is in parentheses beside the name of the country. &&&&& &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& * &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Qualification &&&&& &&&&& A total of 156 fencers will qualify to compete. Each nation may enter a maximum of 18 athletes (nine per gender). The top seven teams at the 2018 Pan American Championships, along with the top two individuals not qualified through the team event will qualify for each respective discipline per gender. The host nation, Peru, automatically qualifies the maximum number of fencers (18).
