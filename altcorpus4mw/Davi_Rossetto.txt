Davi Rossetto &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Davi Rossetto de Oliveira Athayde''' (born July 27, 1992), is a Brazilian professional basketball player who currently plays with Flamengo in the Novo Basquete Brasil (NBB). At a height of 6'1" (1.85 m) tall, he plays at the point guard position. &&&&& &&&&& Youth career &&&&& Rossetto started playing with São Paulo's Hebraica in 2003, when he was 10. He also played with Círculo Militar, until joining the Pinheiros youth system, in 2005. With the Pinheiros youth teams, Rossetto won some titles, and earned a good reputation. He was called up for the U-17 national team in 2009. &&&&& &&&&& Professional career &&&&& ===Pinheiros=== &&&&& Rossetto started to play with the men's first team of Pinheiros in the 2010 season, and was a part of the club's Paulista State Championship winning side in 2011. He left aiming for more gametime. &&&&& &&&&& ===Solar Cearense=== &&&&& Rossetto signed with Solar Cearense, in 2012. In his first season in Fortaleza, he struggled to adapt, and didn't play a lot. In his second season, he alternated between the first team, in the NBB, and the Under-22 side in the Development League. The team lost to Bauru in the NBB's playoff quarterfinals. In the LDB, despite being eliminated in the final round, he finished as the competition's assist leader. &&&&& &&&&& In the 2014–15 season, Rossetto improved his play, and won the NBB's Most Improved Player award, despite his team missing the league's playoffs. He also won the LDB title with the club's Under-22 side, in a perfect season campaign (28–0). He was named the development league's Finals MVP. &&&&& &&&&& ===Flamengo=== &&&&& On July 10, 2018, Rossetto signed with Flamengo, for the 2018–19 NBB season. &&&&& &&&&& National team career &&&&& Rossetto has been a member of the senior Brazilian national basketball team.
