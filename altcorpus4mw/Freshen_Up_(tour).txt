Freshen Up (tour) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Freshen Up''' is a concert tour by English musician Paul McCartney that began on 17 September 2018 with the first four shows in Canada. The tour is McCartney's first tour after the release of his album, ''Egypt Station''. Prior to the announcement of the tour, McCartney revealed he would headline the 17th Austin City Limits Music Festival at Zilker Park, in October 2018. &&&&& &&&&& Tour band &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
