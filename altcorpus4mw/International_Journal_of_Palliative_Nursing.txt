International Journal of Palliative Nursing &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''''International Journal of Palliative Nursing''''' is a monthly peer-reviewed medical journal covering palliative care nursing. It was established in 1995 and is published by the Mark Allen Group. The editor-in-chief is Brian Nyatanga (of the University of Worcester).
