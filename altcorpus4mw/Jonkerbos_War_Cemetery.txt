Jonkerbos War Cemetery &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Jonkerbos War Cemetery and Memorial''' is located in the town of Nijmegen, Netherlands. The cemetery contains 1,643 British Commonwealth and foreign service personnel of World War II. It was built to a design by Commission architect Philip Hepworth. &&&&& &&&&& Background &&&&& On the site of this cemetery the preparation camp was stationed for the Waal Crossing during Operation Market Garden.
