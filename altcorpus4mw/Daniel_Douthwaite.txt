Daniel Douthwaite &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Daniel Alexander Douthwaite''' (born 8 February 1997) is an English cricketer. He made his List A debut for Warwickshire against the West Indies A team in a tri-series warm-up match on 17 June 2018. He made his Twenty20 debut for the Marylebone Cricket Club in the 2018 MCC Tri-Nation Series against the Netherlands on 29 July 2018.
