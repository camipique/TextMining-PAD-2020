Houssam Ghacha &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Houssam Eddine Ghacha''' (born October 22, 1997 in Ouled Djellal) is an Algerian footballer who currently plays for ES Sétif in the Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 1. &&&&& &&&&& Club career &&&&& On July 17, 2018, Ghacha made his ES Sétif debut as a starter in the 2018 CAF Champions League group stage against Difaâ El Jadidi.
