Greigs Flat, New South Wales &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Greigs Flat''' is a locality in the Bega Valley Shire of New South Wales, Australia. At the , Greigs Flat had a population of 151. &&&&& &&&&& Greigs Flat Public School opened in November 1869 and closed in December 1951. It was half-time with the school at Lochiel until January 1873, a provisional school from February to December 1888, and a public school from January 1889 to February 1944 and from November 1946 to December 1951. It was known as Yowaka Public School after 1942.
