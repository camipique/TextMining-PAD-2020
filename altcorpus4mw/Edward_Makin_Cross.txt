Edward Makin Cross &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Edward Makin Cross''' (1880 - 1965) was a bishop of Spokane in The Episcopal Church. He had previously been rector of St. John the Evangelist in St. Paul, Minnesota. He was instrumental in the development of a summer camp on Lake Coeur d'Alene which is named for him. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
