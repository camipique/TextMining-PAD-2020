Iraq at the 2018 Asian Games &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Iraq''' participated in the '''2018 Asian Games''' in Jakarta and Palembang, Indonesia from 18 August to 2 September 2018. Iraq first competed at the Asian Games in 1974 Tehran, and the best achievement was in 1982 Delhi, when the country was ranked 11th with a total 9 medals, 2 gold, 3 silver, and 4 bronze medals.
