Ivan Nikulin: Russian Sailor &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Ivan Nikulin: Russian Sailor''', () is a 1944 Soviet drama film directed by Igor Savchenko. &&&&& &&&&& Plot &&&&& The film takes place in the summer of 1942. The film tells about the sailors of the Black Sea fleet Ivan Nikulin and Vasily Klevtsov, returning to their carriages.
