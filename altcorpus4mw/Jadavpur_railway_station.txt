Jadavpur railway station &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Jadavpur railway station''' is a Kolkata Suburban Railway Station on the Sealdah–Sonarpur line with an approximate distance from Sealdah railway station. It is under the jurisdiction of Eastern Railway zone. Jadavpur railway station is one of the busiest railway stations of Sealdah railway division. More than 115 pairs of E.M.U. Local Trains ply through the railway station on a daily basis. It is situated in Kolkata in the Indian State of West Bengal.
