In Search of a Future &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''In Search of a Future: The Story of Kashmir''''' is a book written by Indian journalist and author David Devadas, first published in 2007 by Viking Penguin. The book discusses the history of Kashmir, starting with the revolt of the Muslim majority populace against their ruler, and traces the roots of the insurgency in the state of Jammu and Kashmir.
