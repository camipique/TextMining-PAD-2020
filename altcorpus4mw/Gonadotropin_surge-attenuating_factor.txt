Gonadotropin surge-attenuating factor &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Gonadotropin surge-attenuating factor''' ('''GnSAF''') is a nonsteroidal ovarian hormone which is involved in the regulation of gonadotropin secretion and the menstrual cycle. A marked decline in GnSAF activity in the late follicular phase allows for the expression of the mid-cycle gonadotropin surge and ovulation in women. &&&&& &&&&& See also &&&&& * Hypothalamic–pituitary–gonadal axis &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
