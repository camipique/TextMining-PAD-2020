Expo 2019 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& An Air China Airbus A350 in Expo 2019 special livery &&&&& '''Expo 2019''' is an upcoming world horticultural exposition presented by the Bureau International des Expositions and will be held in Yanqing District, Beijing, People's Republic of China. Expo 2016 in Antalya, Turkey was the previous one. The exposition will begin on April 29, 2019 and close October 7 2019.
