Harmadiya &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Harmadiya''' () is a village / panchayat located in the Gir Gadhada Taluka of Gir Somnath district in Gujarat State, India. Earlier, until August 2013, Harmadiya was part of Kodinar Taluka and Junagadh district. The latitude 20.927632 and longitude 70.841389 are the geo-coordinate of the Village Harmadiya. Gandhinagar is the state capital of Harmadiya village which is located around 400 kilometres away from Harmadiya. &&&&& &&&&& According to Census 2011, with the 1167 families, the population of this village is 6439. Out of this, 3265 are males and 3174 are females. Most residents are dependent on agriculture. &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& Demographics &&&&& &&&&& According to the 2011 census of India, Harmadiya has 1167 households.
