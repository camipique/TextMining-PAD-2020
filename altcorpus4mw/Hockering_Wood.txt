Hockering Wood &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Hockering Wood''' is a biological Site of Special Scientific Interest east of Dereham in Norfolk. &&&&& &&&&& This is one the largest areas of ancient, semi-natural woodland in the county. It has many rare species, especially of bryophytes, and there are ponds which have populations of great crested newts, a protected species under the Wildlife and Countryside Act 1981. &&&&& &&&&& The site is private with no public access. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
