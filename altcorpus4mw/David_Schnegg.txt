David Schnegg &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''David Schnegg''' (born 29 September 1998) is an Austrian professional footballplayer. He plays for FC Liefering in the Erste Liga as striker. &&&&& &&&&& Club career &&&&& Schnegg started playing football for FG Schönwies/Mils and for the Academy Tyrol. 2017– 2018 he played for SC Imst in the Tyrol League, the 4th level in Austrian football.
