Jubilo &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''''Jubilo''''' is a 1919 American comedy film directed by Clarence G. Badger and written by Robert F. Hill. The film stars Will Rogers, Josie Sedgwick, Charles K. French, Willard Louis and James Mason.
