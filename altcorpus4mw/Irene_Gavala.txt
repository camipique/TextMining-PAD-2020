Irene Gavala &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Irene Gavala''', also '''Irene Gavala-Chardalia''' (born Athens) is a Greek broadcast and radio director. &&&&& &&&&& She graduated from Law School of National and Kapodistrian University of Athens. She started her work at ERT in 1976. Between 1980 and 1995 she worked as Head of the Greek delegation to the Eurovision Song Contest. Between 1984 and 1995 Gavala was Head of ERT International Relations Dept. In 2002 she was a member of the international jury of the International Prize for the best Mediterranean Doc and Reportage. Further Gavala worked as a Deputy Director of Public and International Relations, Chief of International Co-productions, Programme Consultant and finally as General Manager of ERT World.
