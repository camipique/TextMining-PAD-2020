Evangelia Andreadaki &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Evangelia Andreadaki''' (Greek:Ευαγγελία Ανδρεαδάκη) is a Greek actress. She is sometimes credited as Vangelio Andreadaki. Her film credits include ''Smac'', ''Little England'' and ''Black Field''.
