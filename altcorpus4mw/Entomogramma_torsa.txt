Entomogramma torsa &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''''Entomogramma torsa''''' is a moth of the family Noctuidae first described by Achille Guenée in 1852. It is found in India, Sri Lanka, and Java. &&&&& &&&&& The wingspan of the male is 56 mm. Body reddish brown. Head blackish. Forewing without a fulvous subcostal streak. A black spot found at end of cell. Postmedial line dentate. Both wings with fine marginal ochreous line.
