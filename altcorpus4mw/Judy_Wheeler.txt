Judy Wheeler &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Judith Roderick Wheeler''' (born 1944 in Cardiff, Wales) is an Australian herbarium botanist. After receiving an honours degree in botanical science, she was employed at the State Herbarium of South Australia, before moving to Western Australia's Murdoch University and later the West Australian Herbarium.
