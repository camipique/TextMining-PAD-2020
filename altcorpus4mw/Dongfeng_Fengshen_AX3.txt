Dongfeng Fengshen AX3 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& Fengshen AX3 rear &&&&& The '''Dongfeng Fengshen AX3''' is a subcompact crossover produced by Dongfeng Motor Corporation under the Dongfeng Fengshen sub-brand. The Dongfeng Fengshen AX3 was unveiled on the 2015 Shanghai Auto Show with the market launch in January 2016 revealing prices ranging from 69,700 yuan to 87,700 yuan.
