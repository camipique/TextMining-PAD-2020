Josué Mitchell &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Josué Mitchell''' (born November 11, 1989), is a Costa Rican professional football forward. He currently plays for A.D. Municipal Pérez Zeledón of the Primera División de Costa Rica. &&&&& &&&&& International career &&&&& Mitchell was called up by coach Oscar Ramirez for friendly matches in March 2018 against Scotland and Tunisia in preparation for the 2018 FIFA World Cup. &&&&& He made his debut for the Costa Rica national football team on March 27, 2018 in the friendly match against Tunisia at the Allianz Riviera Stadium in Nice, France.
