Ewald Bonzet &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& George Groenewald (Ewald) Bonzet (25 December 1951 – 7 September 2016) was a South African Track and Road runner. At his time he was the National record holder in various distances. &&&&& &&&&& Early days &&&&& &&&&& Bonzet was born in Caledon, Western Cape South Africa. His father was a Train Station Master. He attend Epping High School (today known as Goodwood College) He qualified as a Health Inspector at Cape Technikon Later the Technikon merged and became the Cape Peninsula University of Technology. He is married to Rene and have one child.
