Indira Devi (actress) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Indira Devi''', born '''Effie Hippolet''', was an actress of early silent Indian cinema. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& &&&&& Indira Devi was born from Culcutta's Anglo-Indian community. She was a leading actress in early silent films for the Indian cinema. She paused her career in the early 1930s after her marriage. She played the lead role opposite Prithviraj Kapoor in ''Sher-e-Arab'' / ''Arabian Knights'' (1930).
