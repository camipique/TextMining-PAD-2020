LG-2527 &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''LG-2527''' is a nonsteroidal progestin which was under development by Ligand Pharmaceuticals and Wyeth for the treatment of menopausal symptoms and breast cancer but was never marketed.
