Justin Hill (politician) &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Justin Hill''' is an American businessman and politician from the state of Missouri. A Republican, Hill was first elected to the Missouri House of Representatives from Missouri's 108th District in November 2014. He represents a portion of Saint Charles County in the Lake St. Louis area, south of I-70 and straddling U.S. Route 40/61.
