Luis Quiñónez &&&&& Introduction &&&&& '''Luis Manuel Quiñónez Castillo''' (born 5 October 1968) is a retired Colombian football midfielder. &&&&& &&&&& He was born in Tumaco. Playing for Millonarios from 1988 through 1991, he then played for Once Caldas from 1992 to mid-1996 and Deportes Tolima through 1999. He stayed the first half of 2000 in Deportivo Pasto, then in Atlético Bucaramanga through 2002.
