Foster-Fair House &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& The '''Foster-Fair House''' is a historic two-story house in Louisville, Mississippi. It was &&&&& built in 1842 by Samuel Washington Smyth. It was acquired by Dr. Edward Foster, a settler and physician who was related to Vice President John C. Calhoun, in 1852. &&&&& &&&&& The property was designed as an I-house, with Greek Revival features.
