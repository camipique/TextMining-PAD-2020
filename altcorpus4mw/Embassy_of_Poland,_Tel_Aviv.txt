Embassy of Poland, Tel Aviv &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& The '''Embassy of Poland''' in Tel Aviv () is the diplomatic mission of the Republic of Poland to the Israel.  &&&&& &&&&& History &&&&& Poland originally opened embassy in Israel in 1962, but together with several other Warsaw Pact countries closed it in 1967. The embassy was reopened in 1990.
