Jordin Canada &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Jordin Elizabeth Canada''' (born August 11, 1995) is an American professional women's basketball player with the Seattle Storm of the WNBA. She was selected by the Storm with the 5th overall pick in the 2018 WNBA draft. Born and raised in Los Angeles, Canada played collegiately with the UCLA Bruins. She is known for her playmaking, speed and crossover skills.
