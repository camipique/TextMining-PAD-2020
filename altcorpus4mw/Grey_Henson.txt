Grey Henson &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''Grey Henson''' (born July 2, 1990) is an American actor, dancer, and singer. He currently is cast as Damian Hubbard in the Broadway production of ''Mean Girls'', for which he earned a Tony Award nomination for Best Featured Actor in a Musical.
