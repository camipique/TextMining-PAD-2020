Isabel Toua &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''Isabel Toua''' is a Papua New Guinean cricketer. In July 2018, she was named in Papua New Guinea's squad for the 2018 ICC Women's World Twenty20 Qualifier tournament.
