FC Nizhny Novgorod &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''FC Nizhny Novgorod''' () is a Russian football club from Nizhny Novgorod, founded in 2015. In the 2015–16 season, it began to play in the third-tier Russian Professional Football League. It was founded as a farm club for FC Volga Nizhny Novgorod. On 15 June 2016, the parent club Volga was dissolved, with Olimpiyets becoming the top club in Nizhny Novgorod. At the end of the 2016–17, Olimpiyets won their PFL zone and were promoted to the second-level Russian National Football League. &&&&& &&&&& "Olimpiyets" means Olympian in Russian. &&&&& &&&&& Team
