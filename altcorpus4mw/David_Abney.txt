David Abney &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& '''David Abney''' (born c. 1956) is an American business executive. He graduated from Delta State University in 1976. &&&&& &&&&& Career &&&&& Abney began his career for UPS in college. He was chief operating officer from 2007 to 2014. He has served as its chairman and chief executive officer since 2014. &&&&& &&&&& Abney formerly served on the board of directors Johnson Controls. &&&&& &&&&& Personal life &&&&& Abney is married to Sherry, his college sweetheart.
