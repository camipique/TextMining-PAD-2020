DTaP-IPV-HepB vaccine &&&&& Introduction &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& '''DTaP-IPV-HepB vaccine''' is a combination vaccine whose generic name is diphtheria and tetanus toxoids and acellular pertussis adsorbed, hepatitis B (recombinant) and inactivated poliovirus vaccine or DTaP-IPV-Hep B. It protects against the infectious diseases diphtheria, tetanus, pertussis, poliomyelitis, and hepatitis B. &&&&& &&&&& A branded formulation is marketed in the USA as Pediarix by GlaxoSmithKline. &&&&& &&&&& References &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&& &&&&&
